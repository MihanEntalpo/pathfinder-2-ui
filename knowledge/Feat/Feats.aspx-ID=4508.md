# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Call of the Green Man](./Feats.aspx-ID=4508.md) Feat 13

Leshy
Source [Player Core pg. 69](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

You can call forth nearby nature spirits to augment your body. Once per day, you can cast plant form as a 5th-rank primal innate spell. When you reach 17th level, you can heighten the spell to 6th rank when you cast it.

## Traits

Leshy: A creature with this trait is a member of the leshy ancestry. These small plant or fungus creatures are humanoid in form. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by leshies. A weapon with this trait is created and used by leshies.
