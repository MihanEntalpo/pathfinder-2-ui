# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Staff of ControlItem 6+

[Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Staff](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=700.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 279](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk 1

---

An array of dazzling gemstones lines the twisting head of the golden staff. While wielding the staff, you gain a +1 status bonus to Diplomacy checks to make a Request.

Activate Cast a Spell; Effect You expend a number of charges from the staff to cast a spell from its list.

Craft Requirements Supply one casting of all listed ranks of all listed spells.
