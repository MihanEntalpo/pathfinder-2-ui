# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Plunderer of the Hive’s Riches](./Epithets.aspx-ID=17.md)

Source [Pathfinder #217: Death Sails a Wine-Dark Sea pg. 78](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=273.md)
You take what’s yours, even if an army should stand in your way. You gain the Energized Spark feat, with your choice of either [poison](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=669.md) or [sonic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=697.md) damage. When you critically succeed at a Strike, the taste of honey fills your mouth as you plunder part of your foe’s vitality for yourself, gaining temporary Hit Points equal to half the creature’s level that last for 1 minute.

When you Spark Transcendence, a mass of buzzing insects fill the air around you, granting you [concealment](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=62.md) until the beginning of your next turn. You can’t use this concealment to [Hide](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2404.md) or [Sneak](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2405.md), as normal for concealment when your position is still obvious.
