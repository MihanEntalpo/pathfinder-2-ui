# [Fey-Touched](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=22.md)

Source [Core Rulebook pg. 196](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)
Your blood ties you to the magic of the First World and the otherworldly fey. Like them, you’re capricious, charming, and hard to pin down.

## Ability Scores

Improve your Charisma first. Good Dexterity, Constitution, and Wisdom provide solid defenses.

## Skills

[Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38&Redirected=1.md), Diplomacy, Nature, [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48&Redirected=1.md)

## Bloodline

Fey

## Higher-Level Feats

Reach Spell (1st), Primal Evolution (4th), Advanced Bloodline (6th), Greater Bloodline (10th), Bloodline Focus (12th)

## Spell Repertoire

1st charm, fleet step, summon fey; Cantrips [dancing lights](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=58.md), daze, detect magic, ghost sound, tanglefoot
