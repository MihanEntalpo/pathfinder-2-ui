# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Pushing Gust [two-actions] Focus 1

Uncommon Air [Cleric](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=554.md) [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 372](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Domain [air](../Domain/Domains.aspx-ID=65.md)
Range 500 feet; Targets 1 creature
Defense Fortitude

---

Giving the air a push, you buffet the target with a powerful gust of wind; it must attempt a Fortitude save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.
Success The target is pushed 5 feet away from you.
Failure The target is pushed 10 feet away from you.
Critical Failure The target is pushed 10 feet away from you and knocked [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88&Redirected=1.md).
