# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Shared Warding](./Feats.aspx-ID=8377.md) Feat 12

[Thaumaturge](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=895.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 46](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

Prerequisites [Esoteric Warden](./Feats.aspx-ID=8360.md)

---

You ward your allies from the attacks of your foes whenever you apply those protections to yourself. When you gain a status bonus to AC and saves from Esoteric Warden, you can choose to grant the same benefit to all allies within 30 feet.

## Traits

Thaumaturge: This indicates abilities from the [thaumaturge](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=69.md) class.
