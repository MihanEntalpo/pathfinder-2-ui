You have sworn the Aldori swordpact and studied the art of Aldori dueling, a famed school of bladecraft that has been passed down for over a millennium from the teachings of Baron Sirian Aldori. The Aldori Academy, widely regarded as the region’s finest war college, is the pride and joy of Restov. You’re filled with pride and determined to challenge yourself at every opportunity. One day, you hope to demonstrate your skill at swordplay to become acknowledged as a true swordlord.

Aldori duelists have much in common with [swashbucklers](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=63.md), and many Aldori duelists have the swashbuckler class or [archetype](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=235.md). Much like a swashbuckler’s daring deeds, classic Aldori duelist maneuvers are often showy feats intended to impress or intimidate.

Many feats in this section refer to being in a duel or dueling actions. For more information about duels and dueling actions, see the [dueling subsystem](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=3084.md).

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Aldori Duelist Dedication Feat 2

Uncommon Archetype Dedication
Source [Battlecry! pg. 54](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)
Archetype [Aldori Duelist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=324.md)
Prerequisites trained in martial weapons
Access You are from the Broken Lands region.

---

Your Aldori duelist training teaches you martial techniques and increases your dedication to the Aldori dueling sword. You become trained in your choice of [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md) or [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md); if you were already trained in that skill, you become an expert instead. You gain the [Additional Lore](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=5114.md) feat for Dueling Lore; if you were already trained in Dueling Lore, you also become trained in a Lore skill of your choice.

You have familiarity with Aldori dueling swords, treating them as martial weapons for the purposes of proficiency. You gain access to Aldori dueling swords.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Dueling Acumen Feat 2

Archetype Skill
Source [Battlecry! pg. 54](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)
Archetype [Aldori Duelist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=324.md)
Prerequisites Aldori Duelist Dedication

---

You're always ready for a new [duel](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=3084.md), and you've been trained to start and end fights quickly. When you're in a duel or can see exactly one creature, you can always roll Dueling [Lore](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=41.md) for initiative. If you do so while in a duel, you must choose from either Deception, Intimidation, or Perception when you roll for initiative; you're considered to have rolled the chosen skill for initiative for the purpose of dueling actions.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Duelist's Form [one-action] Feat 16

Archetype Stance
Source [Battlecry! pg. 55](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)
Archetype [Aldori Duelist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=324.md)
Prerequisites Aldori Duelist Dedication
Requirements You are wielding only an Aldori dueling sword and have your other hand or hands free; you are not [fatigued](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=73.md).

---

Many assume that a duelist's habit of fighting with one hand behind their back or on their hip is an insult or self-restriction. This could not be further from the truth. Fighting with a single hand dictates mastery and heightens the duelist's focus by narrowing points of interest, allowing them to move and react to changing situations with unnatural speed. While in this stance, you are [quickened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=89.md). You can use this extra action only to [Step](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2304.md) or use a single-action activity gained from the Aldori duelist archetype. If you use your free hand for anything, this stance ends. When this stance ends, you become [fatigued](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=73.md) until you rest for at least 10 minutes.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Devastating Duelist [one-action] Feat 18

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Aldori Swordlord Feat 20

Archetype
Source [Battlecry! pg. 55](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)
Archetype [Aldori Duelist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=324.md)
Prerequisites Aldori Duelist Dedication; you have taken the last name of Aldori, sworn to the Aldori swordpact, and demonstrated skill at swordplay by winning a duel using only a sword in the presence of an Aldori swordlord.

---

You are an Aldori swordlord. You can always obtain free room and board at the Aldori Academy for yourself and your allies. You can use Dueling [Lore](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=41.md) instead of Diplomacy to [Make an Impression](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2392.md) or [Request](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2393.md) provided that the target you are Making an Impression on would be aware of the Aldori swordlords or your Request is related to your standing as a swordlord. Whenever you successfully Strike a creature with an Aldori dueling sword, you gain 5 temporary Hit Points (or 10 temporary Hit Point on a critical success) that last for 1 round.

Special You can’t take both this feat and Swordlord Exile.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Swordlord Exile Feat 20

Archetype
Source [Battlecry! pg. 55](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)
Archetype [Aldori Duelist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=324.md)
Prerequisites Aldori Duelist Dedication; you have broken or forsaken the Aldori swordpact.

---

You are a swordlord exile. You have abandoned or been abandoned by the Aldori Academy. Aldori swordlords are honor-bound to challenge you on sight. Additionally, your unbridled skill strikes terror in those around you. You can use Dueling [Lore](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=41.md) instead of Intimidation to [Coerce](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2394.md) and [Demoralize](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2395.md) while your Aldori dueling sword is visible to the target of your attempt.

With broken pride, your blade is now a disgraced tool used to remove obstacles between you and your goals, whatever they may be. The first time you hit a creature with an Aldori dueling sword each round, you deal 5 additional precision damage.

Special You can’t take both this feat and Aldori Swordlord.
