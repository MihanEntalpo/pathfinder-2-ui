# [Immobilized](./Conditions.aspx-ID=81.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 444](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
You are incapable of movement. You can't use any actions that have the [move](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=658&Redirected=1.md) trait. If you're immobilized by something holding you in place and an external force would move you out of your space, the force must succeed at a check against either the DC of the effect holding you in place or the relevant defense (usually Fortitude DC) of the monster holding you in place.
