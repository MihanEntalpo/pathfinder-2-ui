# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Grasping Reach](./Feats.aspx-ID=4493.md) Feat 1

Leshy
Source [Player Core pg. 68](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

You can extend a tangle of vines or tendrils to support your arms and extend your reach. When you wield a melee weapon that requires two hands, doesn’t have reach, and deals at least 1d6 damage, you can change between a typical two-handed grip and an extended two-handed grasp using an [Interact](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2297&Redirected=1.md) action. Weapons wielded in your extended grasp gain reach of 10 feet. This grasp is less stable and powerful than a typical grip, reducing the weapon’s damage die by 1 step.

## Traits

Leshy: A creature with this trait is a member of the leshy ancestry. These small plant or fungus creatures are humanoid in form. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by leshies. A weapon with this trait is created and used by leshies.
