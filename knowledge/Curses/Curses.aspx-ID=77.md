# [Curse of Nightmares](./Curses.aspx-ID=77.md)Curse 2

[Curse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=566.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 86](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
This curse plagues you with terrible nightmares whenever you sleep, preventing you from resting properly.

Saving Throw DC 16 Will; Effect You must rest for 12 hours instead of 8 to avoid becoming [fatigued](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=73.md) and can't gain any benefits from resting or long-term rest. You can still make your daily preparations.
