# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Focusing Hum [one-action] Spell 3

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 105](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Traditions [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Area 15-foot emanation centered on you
Duration sustained up to 1 minute

---

You infuse the air around you with a sustained hum that allows creatures within it to resist distractions. The DC of any flat check to Cast a Spell while [stupefied](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=94.md) for creatures within the area is 2 lower. [Confused](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=63.md) creatures in the area don't attack anyone, including themselves, and instead lose the ability to speak coherently. You take a –2 penalty to Stealth checks to [Hide](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2404.md) and [Sneak](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2405.md).
