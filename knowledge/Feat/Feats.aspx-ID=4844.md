# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Improved Twin Riposte (Fighter)](./Feats.aspx-ID=4844.md) Feat 14

[Fighter](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=601.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 150](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetypes [Avenger](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=282.md)*, [Dual-Weapon Warrior](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=247.md)*
Prerequisites [Twin Riposte](./Feats.aspx-ID=4831.md)
* This archetype offers Improved Twin Riposte (Fighter) at a different level than displayed here.

---

Your weapons are a blur, blocking and biting at your foes. You can use Twin Riposte even if you aren't benefiting from Twin Parry (though you must be wielding two melee weapons, one in each hand). At the start of each of your turns, you gain an additional reaction that you can use only to make a Twin Riposte.

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more archetypes in addition to the listed classes. When selected this way, the feat does not have its class traits.
