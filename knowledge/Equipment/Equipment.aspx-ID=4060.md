# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Dragonfire HalfbowItem 12

## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Uncommon Fire Magical
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 296](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 1,800 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk 1
Base Weapon [Composite Shortbow](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=435.md)

---

Crafted by layering bamboo with strips of sliced scales harvested from a fire-breathing dragon, this bow has a significant draw strength. This design is commonly seen in Hongal, as the shorter limb allows for easier maneuvering on horseback. While the wielder of this [+2 striking](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2861.md) [composite shortbow](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=435.md) is mounted, they apply the bow’s item bonus to [Nature](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=43.md) checks to [Command](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2400.md) their mount.

Activate—Dragon's Arrow [two-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect You draw the bow back to its maximum draw length without nocking an arrow, and as you release the string, the dragonfire halfbow casts [fireball](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1530.md) (heightened to 5th rank; DC 29 Reflex save), targeted at a point of your choosing within the spell’s range. You and your mount are immune to the effects of this fireball.
