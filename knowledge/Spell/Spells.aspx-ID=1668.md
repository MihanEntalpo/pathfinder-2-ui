# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Shape Wood [two-actions] Spell 2

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Plant [Wood](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=508.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 356](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Deities Hearth and Harvest, Sovyrian Conclave, Yuelral
Range touch; Targets an unworked piece of wood up to 20 cubic feet in volume

---

You shape the wood into a rough shape of your choice. The shaping power is too crude to produce with intricate parts, fine details, moving pieces, or the like. You can't use this spell to enhance the value of the wooden object you are shaping.
