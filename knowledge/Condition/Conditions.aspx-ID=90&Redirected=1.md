# [Restrained](./Conditions.aspx-ID=90.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 446](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
You're tied up and can barely move, or a creature has you pinned. You have the [off-guard](./Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) and [immobilized](./Conditions.aspx-ID=81&Redirected=1.md) conditions, and you can't use any [attack](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=540&Redirected=1.md) or [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) actions except to attempt to Escape or Force Open your bonds. Restrained overrides [grabbed](./Conditions.aspx-ID=77&Redirected=1.md).
