# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Irrepressible](./Feats.aspx-ID=4469.md) Feat 9

Halfling
Source [Player Core pg. 61](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

You are easily able to ward off attempts to play on your fears and emotions. When you roll a success on a saving throw against an emotion effect, you get a critical success instead. If your heritage is gutsy halfling, when you roll a critical failure on a saving throw against an emotion effect, you get a failure instead.

## Traits

Halfling: A creature with this trait is a member of the halfling ancestry. These small people are friendly wanderers considered to be lucky. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by halflings. A weapon with this trait is created and used by halflings.
