# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Blood-Feasting Breath [three-actions] Spell 5

Uncommon [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Healing [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) [Vitality](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=509.md) [Void](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=510.md)
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 224](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Traditions [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Area two 30-foot cones
Defense [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Fortitude

---

Like a vorpal dragon, you absorb life with an inhale and release it with an exhale. All living creatures in the first cone take 5d6 void damage with a basic Fortitude save. You and all living creatures in the second cone regain Hit Points equal to the greatest damage a single creature took from the first cone.

---

Heightened (+1) The damage and healing increase by 1d6.
