# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Impose Order (Psychic)](./Feats.aspx-ID=8334.md) [one-action] Feat 10

[Fortune](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=612.md) [Psyche](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=901.md) [Psychic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=894.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 27](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

Requirements [precise discipline](../SubconsciousMinds/SubconsciousMinds.aspx-ID=7.md) subconscious mind

---

You bring discipline to your mind and magic, ensuring that you don't under-perform. Until the end of your turn, instead of rolling dice for damage, count each damage die as average damage rounded up (3 for d4, 4 for d6, 5 for d8, 6 for d10, and 7 for d12).

## Traits

Fortune: A fortune effect beneficially alters how you roll your dice. You can never have more than one fortune effect alter a single roll. If multiple fortune effects would apply, you have to pick which to use. If a fortune effect and a misfortune effect would apply to the same roll, the two cancel each other out, and you roll normally.

Psyche: Your psyche must be unleashed to use abilities that have the psyche trait, and they end automatically when your unleashed psyche subsides.

Psychic: This indicates abilities from the [psychic](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=68.md) class.
