## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Rare Magical
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 308](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 360 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Base Weapon [Fighting Fan](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=284.md)

---

This elegant black, gold-tipped [+1 striking](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2861.md) [fighting fan](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=284.md) is adorned with images of three golden leaves and a red rope tassel. If a creature is reduced to 0 Hit Points by a spirit fan, the golden leaves on the fan light up, providing illumination equal to that of a [torch](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2760.md) for 1 minute.

Activate—Release Life Energy [two-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md), [vitality](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=509.md)); Frequency once per hour; Requirements The spirit fan’s leaves are illuminated; Effect You sweep the spirit fan in the direction of a single target you can see within 30 feet, releasing the life energy in the form of a streak of golden light. The spirit fan goes dark. If the target is a living creature, the energy restores 3d8+8 Hit Points. If the target is [undead](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=722.md), it takes 2d8+8 vitality damage (DC 23 [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Fortitude save).
