This +2 greater striking flaming longsword has an ornate brass hilt and a blade shaped like stylized flames. When wielded, the blade projects illumination resembling shimmering firelight, emitting dim light in a 10-foot radius.

Activate—Shoot Fire [two-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md), [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)); Effect You cast the [ignition](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1565.md) cantrip from the sword as a 7th-rank arcane spell, using your melee attack modifier with searing blade as your spell attack modifier.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Searing Blade (Greater)Item 17

Source [GM Core pg. 243](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Price 13,800 gp
This is a +3 greater striking greater flaming longsword. When you activate the sword to cast ignition, the spell is 9th rank. This weapon also has the Radiate Flames activation.

Activate—Radiate Flames [one-action] (aura, concentrate, fire); Frequency once per day; Effect A 10-foot emanation of flame radiates from the greater searing blade for 1 minute. All weapon and unarmed attacks by you and your allies within the area gain the effect of the flaming property rune.
