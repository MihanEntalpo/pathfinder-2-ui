# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Expert Skysage Divination](./Feats.aspx-ID=8119.md) Feat 10

Archetype
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 238](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Archetype [Oatia Skysage](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=335.md)
Prerequisites [Basic Skysage Divination](./Feats.aspx-ID=8113.md)

---

Your studies of the stars have taught you about the mysteries of the cosmos. You choose one of the following 4th-rank spells to add to your spell repertoire: [clairvoyance](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1466.md) or [read omens](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1647.md). At 12th level, choose one of the following 5th-rank spells to add to your spell repertoire: [mind probe](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1601.md) or [scouting eye](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1661.md). At 14th level, choose one of the following 6th-rank spells to add to your spell repertoire: [scrying](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1662.md) or [truesight](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1727.md). You become an expert with spell attack rolls and spell DCs for occult spells.
