# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Tomorrow's Dawn [two-actions] Spell 4

## This Spell may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Rare [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Light [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 309](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Traditions [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Defense Will; Duration [sustained](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2317.md) up to 1 minute

---

The golden light of a new dawn and the promise of life beyond death radiates from your body. You radiate bright light in a 20-foot radius (and dim light for the next 20 feet) and gain mental resistance 5. Any nindoru [fiend](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=600.md) or [undead](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=722.md) that begins its turn adjacent to you must attempt a Will save with the following results.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected and immune to this spell’s effects for the remainder of the spell’s duration.
Success The creature is [sickened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=91.md) 1 until the end of its turn.
Failure The creature is sickened 1. In addition, it becomes [slowed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=92.md) 1 until the end of its next turn.
Critical Failure As failure, but sickened 2 and the creature remains slowed 1 for the rest of the spell’s duration.

---

Heightened (6th) You gain mental resistance 10.
Heightened (8th) You gain mental resistance 15.
