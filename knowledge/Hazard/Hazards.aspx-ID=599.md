# [Sacred Geyser](./Hazards.aspx-ID=599.md)Hazard 3

[Environmental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=591.md) [Fire](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=604.md) [Water](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=732.md)
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 62](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 19 (trained)
Description A geyser of boiling water erupts at any character who attempts to take water from the oasis.

---

Disable DC 21 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) (trained) to concoct a mechanism for indirectly harvesting the water from a safe distance
Boiling Geyser [reaction] Trigger A creature dips a vessel or otherwise attempts to remove water from the oasis; Effect A jet of magical boiling water targets the triggering creature and affects all creatures within 5 feet of the target. Creatures caught in the burst take 2d10+13 fire damage ([basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) DC 23 Reflex save).

---

Reset The geyser resets automatically and adapts to previous circumvention, requiring a new check to disable it each time.
