# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Planar Seal [two-actions] Spell 7

Uncommon [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 350](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Spell List [elemental](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=5.md)
Range 120 feet; Area 60-foot burst
Duration until your next daily preparations

---

You create a visible magical barrier that attempts to counteract teleportation effects and planar travel into or out of the area, including items that allow access to extradimensional spaces. Planar seal tries to counteract any attempt to [summon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=520.md) a creature into the area but doesn't stop the creature from departing when the summoning ends.
