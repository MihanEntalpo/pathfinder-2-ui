# [Haunted Forge](./Hazards.aspx-ID=620.md)Hazard 14

[Unique](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=723.md) [Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) [Haunt](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=622.md)
Source [Pathfinder Adventure Path #219: Lord of the Trinity Star pg. 20](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=283.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth 28
Description The rundown furnaces blaze to life, filling with metal and fire, while the wall of hardened lava to the east appears to revert to red hot molten rock.

---

Disable DC 38 [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md) (master) to exorcise the spirits or DC 41 [Intimidation](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=40.md) to quell them with furious anger; three total successes are required to disable the haunt
AC 36, Fort +30, Ref +22
Forge Hardness 24, HP 94 (BT 47); Immunities critical hits, fire, object immunities, precision damage;, Weaknesses cold 10
Searing Heat [reaction] (divine, fire) Trigger A creature comes within 30 feet of either forge; Effect The eastern wall appears to transform into molten lava, and dozens of shrieking human spirits made of fire fly out of the lava to spiral and scorch through the room. Creatures in area must attempt a DC 34 Reflex save.
Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
Success The creature takes 4d6 fire damage
Failure The creature takes 8d6 fire damage, 1d6 persistent fire damage, and is [frightened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=76.md) 1.
Critical Failure The creature takes 16d6 fire damage, 2d6 persistent fire damage, and is frightened 2.

---

Routine (4 actions) The burning spirits shriek and howl, swooping in and out of the forges to the south, which roar into scorching life. The spirits scoop up gobs of phantasmal molten dawnsilver and hurl them at different targets in the room. Up to four randomly determined creatures are targeted in this way with molten dawnsilver Strikes (the spirits can target any creature in the area). A single creature may be targeted only once per round, so if there are fewer than four different targets, the haunt’s extra actions are lost. The haunt also loses two actions from its routine if one of the two forges are destroyed.

On a critical hit from a molten dawnsilver Strike, the target is [slowed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=92.md) 1 for 1 round as the molten dawnsilver swiftly cools and encases the target. Molten dawnsilver produced by the haunt vanishes entirely 1 round after it’s been thrown.

Ranged molten dawnsilver +29, Damage 3d10+11 bludgeoning plus 2d6 fire
