# [Violet Mister](./Hazards.aspx-ID=591.md)Hazard 2

[Mechanical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=646.md) [Trap](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=715.md)
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 27](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 20 (trained) to notice the trap’s trigger-thread attached to the door
Description Opening the door pulls a thread, which causes a plant mister to spray a cloud of poison into the triggering creature’s face.

---

Disable DC 18 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) to safely cut the thread
AC 16, Fort +10, Ref +10
Hardness 7, HP 25
Poisoned Welcome [reaction] Trigger A creature attempts to open the door; Effect Every creature within 10 feet of the door is exposed to violet venom poison.
Violet Venom ([contact](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=565.md), [poison](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=669.md)) Saving Throw DC 17 Fortitude; Onset 1 minute; Maximum Duration 6 rounds; Stage 1 1d6 poison plus [enfeebled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=71.md) 1 (1 round); Stage 2 1d6 poison plus [drained](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=68.md) 1 (1 round); Stage 3 2d6 poison plus enfeebled 1 (1 round)
