# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Masquerade ScarfItem 2+

[Invested](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=637.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 292](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage worn; Bulk &mdash;

---

This delicately embroidered scarf matches with every outfit and can even complete a costume or disguise with illusions.

Activate—Masquerade 1 minute ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect You arrange the scarf over your lower face, and it casts a 1st-rank [illusory disguise](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1568.md) spell on you, which ends immediately if the scarf is removed. You can alter the scarf's appearance or make it invisible as part of the illusory disguise, but it can still be felt if touched.
