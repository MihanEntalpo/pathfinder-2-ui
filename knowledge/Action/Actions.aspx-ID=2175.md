Activate—Rusting Disarm [reaction] Trigger You critically succeed at a Disarm attempt with the flail against a metal weapon; Effect Flakes of acid-laden rust are deposited on the disarmed weapon. The weapon takes 2d6 damage, ignoring the weapon's Hardness.
