# Flawless Celestial ShawlItem 9

[Rare](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=683.md) [Artifact](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=537.md) [Divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md) [Invested](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=637.md)

Usage worn garment; Bulk &mdash;

---

Each flawless celestial shawl functions only for a specific tennin and is the culmination of their immortal cultivation. The sash is made of celestial silken threads stronger than [adamantine](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2915.md) yet lighter than gossamer. The tennin can draw thread from the flawless celestial shawl to serve as a [sterling artisan’s toolkit](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2703.md) and workshop to [Craft](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2385.md). The sash can be used to work with precious materials of any grade. On any [Crafting](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=37.md) check the tennin attempts using the shawl, they use the outcome one degree of success better than the result of their roll.

Activate—Reweave [three-actions] ; Effect The tennin uses the sash to [Repair](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2384.md) an item. This Repair activity loses the [exploration](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=595.md) trait.
