# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Laughing Fit [two-actions] Spell 2

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Emotion [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Mental
Source [Player Core pg. 340](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Bloodline fey
Deities Bes, Chaldira, Cong, Kofusachi, The Lantern King, The Perplexing Jest, [Upion and Warrik](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=705.md)
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 living creature
Defense Will; Duration sustained

---

The target is overtaken with uncontrollable laughter. It must attempt a Will save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.
Success The target is plagued with uncontrollable laughter. It can't use reactions.
Failure The target is slowed 1 and can't use reactions.
Critical Failure The target falls [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88&Redirected=1.md) and can't use actions or reactions for 1 round. It then takes the effects of a failure.
