# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Slow [two-actions] Spell 3

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 357](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Bloodline demonic
Deities Embaral, Korada, Lorthact, Matravash, Sithhud, Thalaphyrr
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature
Defense Fortitude; Duration varies

---

You dilate the flow of time around the target, slowing its actions.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.
Success The target is slowed 1 for 1 round.
Failure The target is slowed 1 for 1 minute.
Critical Failure The target is slowed 2 for 1 minute.

---

Heightened (6th) You can target up to 10 creatures.
