# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Undaunted](./Feats.aspx-ID=4499.md) Feat 1

Leshy
Source [Player Core pg. 68](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

Your spirit has endured many challenges over its long existence. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saves against emotion effects. If you roll a success on a saving throw against an emotion effect, you get a critical success instead.

## Traits

Leshy: A creature with this trait is a member of the leshy ancestry. These small plant or fungus creatures are humanoid in form. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by leshies. A weapon with this trait is created and used by leshies.
