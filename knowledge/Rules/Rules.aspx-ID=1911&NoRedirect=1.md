Treasure Vault

Momentary Magic

Source [Treasure Vault pg. 96](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=191.md)

The [spellgun](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=491.md) trait appears on items that can generate a magical effect that flies out like a bullet. You can make either a spell attack roll with a spellgun or a ranged attack roll using your proficiency with simple firearms. Spellguns have a range increment, which applies regardless of which type of attack roll you make. Though you can use your modifier for simple firearms, a spellgun isn't actually a firearm. You don't get the damage of a firearm Strike, nor other benefits like critical specialization. Similarly, you can't load or reload a spellgun, affix [talismans](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-Category=15&Subcategory=19.md) to one, attach scopes, affix attached weapons like [bayonets](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=186.md) or [reinforced stocks](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=187.md), and so on.
