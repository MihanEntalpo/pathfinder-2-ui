# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Dominate [two-actions] Spell 6

Uncommon [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Incapacitation [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Mental
Source [Player Core pg. 326](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Deities Droskar, The Godclaw
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature
Defense Will; Duration until your next daily preparations

---

You take command of the target, forcing it to obey you. If you issue an obviously self-destructive order, the target doesn't act until you issue a new order. The effect depends on its Will save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.
Success The target is stunned 1 as it fights off your commands.
Failure You control the target. It gains the [controlled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=64.md) condition, but it can attempt a Will save at the end of each of its turns. On a success, the spell ends.
Critical Failure As a failure, but the target receives a new save only if you give it a new order that is against its nature, such as killing its allies.

---

Heightened (10th) The duration is unlimited.
