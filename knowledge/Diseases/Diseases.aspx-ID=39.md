# [Crimson Ooze](./Diseases.aspx-ID=39.md)Disease 15

[Disease](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=578.md) [Virulent](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=726.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 89](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
This devastating fungus infects your hand and can be cured by amputating the limb before you reach stage 4.

Saving Throw DC 34 Fortitude; Stage 1 [clumsy](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=61.md) 1 (1 day); Stage 2 clumsy 2, and using the infected hand deals 3d6 persistent bleed damage (1 day); Stage 3 clumsy 2, [stupefied](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=94.md) 2, and the infected hand is unusable (1 day); Stage 4 clumsy 3, stupefied 3, and infected hand is permanently unusable (1 day); Stage 5 [confused](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=63.md), and damage does not end the confused condition (1 day); Stage 6 death
