# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Gunslinger](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=114.md)

Source [Guns & Gears pg. 127](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=98.md)
[PFS Note](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md) All Pathfinder Society agents have access to the Gunslinger archetype.

You like a weapon that makes an impression almost as much as you like making an impression.

### Multiclass Gunslinger Characters

Gunslinger is a great fit for dexterous characters who want a weapon that hits hard from a distance, especially [rangers](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=36.md) and [rogues](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=37.md). Spellcasters with [sure strike](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1709.md), especially [witches](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=38.md) and [bards](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=32.md) with their one-action cantrips, might favor firearms with the [fatal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=597.md) property and gunslinger abilities that improve options for supporting their allies.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Gunslinger Dedication Feat 2

Uncommon Archetype Dedication Multiclass
Source [Guns & Gears pg. 127](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=98.md)
Archetype [Gunslinger](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=114.md)
Class [Gunslinger](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=20.md)
Prerequisites Dexterity +2

---

You excel in using specific types of ranged weapons. You have familiarity with martial crossbows and firearms, treating them as simple weapons for the purposes of proficiency. You gain access to uncommon martial and simple crossbows and firearms that do not have an ancestry trait. You become trained in gunslinger class DC.

Choose a gunslinger [way](../Ways/Ways.aspx.md). You become trained in your way's associated skill; if you were already trained in this skill, you become trained in a skill of your choice. You don't gain any other abilities from your choice of way.
