# [Jotunborn](./Traits.aspx-ID=860.md)

Source [Battlecry! pg. 220](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)
A creature with this trait is a member of the [jotunborn](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=95.md) ancestry. Jotunborn are large people forged from the blood of an ancient group of giants called titans and usually have low-light vision. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by jotunborn. A weapon with this trait is created and used by jotunborn.
