## A1. The Atrium (Levels 1-2)

### Magnetic GeckoCreature 1

Small [Animal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=531.md)
Source [Howl of the Wild pg. 152](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=226.md)
[Recall Knowledge](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2638.md) DC 15 &bull; Animal ([Nature](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=43.md))
Perception +10; [low-light vision](../MonsterAbility/MonsterAbilities.aspx-ID=69.md), greater electrolocation 20 feet
Skills [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md) +6, [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) +6 ((+8 to Climb)), [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48.md) +6
Str +2, Dex +3, Con +4, Int -4, Wis +2, Cha -1
Greater Electrolocation A magnetic gecko can sense minute electrical charges in living creatures, which it can use as a precise sense at a range of 20 feet. This distance increases to 100 feet against any creature that has used an [electricity](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=586.md) effect within the last minute.
Uncanny Climber A magnetic gecko's feet allow it to climb virtually any surface, no matter how slick or sheer. If a gecko attempts an [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) check to [Climb](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2374.md) and critically fails, it gets a failure instead.

---

AC 15; Fort +10, Ref +7, Will +4
HP 20; Immunities [electricity](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=586.md)

---

Speed 30 feet, climb 30 feet
Melee [one-action] jaws +7 [+2/-3], Damage 1d6+2 piercingMelee [one-action] tongue +7 [+2/-3] (electricity, finesse, reach 10 feet), Damage 2d4 electricity plus static clingLaunch Metal [two-actions] The gecko repulses the metal attached to its body in all directions, dealing 2d6 bludgeoning damage (DC 17 basic Reflex save) to all creatures in a 10-foot emanation. The gecko can't use Launch Metal again for 1d4 rounds.Repel [one-action] The gecko manipulates its magnetic field to repel metal, humming audibly and gaining resistance 2 to damage from metal weapons and [metal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=505.md) effects until the beginning of its next turn.Static Cling If the gecko hits Small or smaller creature with its tongue, and the target is made of metal or is wearing metallic armor, the gecko's tongue latches on to the creature. The creature must attempt a DC 17 Reflex save or become [grabbed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=77.md). While the gecko is Grabbing a creature in this way, it can attempt an [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) check against the target's Fortitude DC to pull the creature to a space adjacent to the gecko. A creature grabbed in this way can [Escape](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2296.md) normally.
