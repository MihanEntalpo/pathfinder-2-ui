# [Nosois' Vengeance](./Hazards.aspx-ID=660.md)Hazard 11

[Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Trap](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=715.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 201](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth +23 (master)
Description The sparrow on the scroll animates, speaking to the PCs in Noguchi’s voice (one of the nosois from Event 11: Nosois’ Brunch): “They’re the ones! They’re the blasphemers who can’t even properly serve tea! Send them along to the Lady!”

---

Disable DC 36 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) (to quickly destroy the scroll without triggering the hazard), DC 33 [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md) (to apologize to [Pharasma](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=290.md) and the nosois for the previous insult), or [dispel magic](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1493.md) (6th rank; counteract DC 29).
Summon Vanths [reaction] ([divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md), [summon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=520.md)) Trigger One of the PCs enters the room and is noticed by the hazard; Effect The trap summons four vanths, who then roll initiative and remain for up to 1 minute before the spell ends and the vanths disappear. The summoned vanths can use 3 actions each round and can use reactions, unlike most summoned creatures.
