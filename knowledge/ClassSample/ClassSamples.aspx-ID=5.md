# [Animal Rager](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=5.md)

Source [Core Rulebook pg. 90](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)
The animal within you breaks free, turning you into a feral creature of nature when you rage. You follow your instincts rather than reason, and you find yourself at home in the wilderness.

## Ability Scores

Increase your Strength as high as you can, followed by your Constitution and Dexterity. A high Wisdom can improve your senses.

## Skills

[Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md), Intimidation, Nature, Survival

## Instinct

Animal Instinct

## Starting Feat

Raging Intimidation

## Higher-Level Feats

No Escape (2nd), Animal Skin (6th), [Animal Rage](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=151.md) (8th), Terrifying Howl (10th), Predator's Pounce (12th)
