# [Demonblood](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=23.md)

Source [Core Rulebook pg. 200](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)
The grotesque and chaotic creatures of the Abyss left their mark on your family’s bloodline. You have a vicious streak and find wanton destruction thrilling

## Ability Scores

Your Charisma is most important, and boosting Strength, Dexterity, and Constitution make you a better combatant.

## Skills

[Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md), [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38&Redirected=1.md), Intimidation, Religion

## Bloodline

Demonic

## Higher-Level Feats

[Dangerous Sorcery](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=603.md) (1st), Divine Evolution (4th), Advanced Bloodline (6th), Bloodline Resistance (8th), Greater Bloodline (10th), Bloodline Focus (12th)

## Spell Repertoire

1st bane, fear, harm; Cantrips acid splash, chill touch, detect magic, light, shield
