# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Riding Dragonet](./AnimalCompanions.aspx-ID=122.md)

Uncommon Dragon Minion
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 221](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Your companion is a flightless dragonet whose larger-than-average size keeps it grounded. Though typically cagey around other creatures, they have inexplicably attached themself to you, becoming a loving companion. A riding dragonet has the [dragon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=581.md) trait instead of the [animal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=531.md) trait, but it otherwise functions normally as an animal companion.
Size Small
Melee [one-action] claw ([finesse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=602.md), [magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)), Damage 1d8 bludgeoning
Melee [one-action] tail ([finesse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=602.md), [magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md), [agile](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=526.md)), Damage 1d6 slashing
Str +1, Dex +3, Con +2, Int -4, Wis +1, Cha +3
Hit Points 8
Skill [Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md)
Senses [darkvision](../Familiar/Familiars.aspx-ID=142.md)
Speed 30 feet
Special mount
Support Benefit Your dragonet’s wings flutter rapidly, pulsating the air around you both. Until the start of your next turn, each time you Strike while riding the dragonet and hit a creature in the dragonet’s reach, the creature takes 1d4 bludgeoning damage from the force of the dragonet’s wings. If your dragonet is nimble or savage, the bludgeoning damage increases to 2d4.
Advanced Maneuver Enamoring Exhalation

### Enamoring Exhalation [two-actions]

Olfactory
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 221](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Frequency once per 10 minutes

---

Your riding dragonet enraptures those nearby, exhaling a 15-foot cone of pheromones that fascinates those within it. Each creature in the area is [fascinated](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=72.md) with you and your dragonet until the end of your next turn unless it succeeds at a Fortitude save. This uses a trained DC using the dragonet’s Charisma modifier, or an expert DC if the dragonet is specialized.
