Powerful winds carry you through the air, giving you a fly Speed equal to your Speed. When this spell's duration would end, if you're still flying, you float to the ground, as [gentle landing](./Spells.aspx-ID=1542.md).

---

Heightened (6th) When you fly using stormwind flight, you ignore difficult terrain from wind.
