# Destiny Tempest

Before the Jaathoom Empire, the [Plane of Air](../Plane/Planes.aspx-ID=29.md) was ruled by destiny tempests from their Reverient Empire of Lost Nights. When the jaathoom armies crushed the Reverient Empire, they trapped the defeated destiny tempests in bronze spheres scattered across the Plane of Air, prisons of time and everlasting nightmare.

### Hushed Voices

On the [Plane of Air](../Plane/Planes.aspx-ID=29.md), many creatures communicate through telepathy, shared dreams, or illusory rebuses. Though destiny tempests are telepathic and can magically understand most languages, they prefer to convey their thoughts and ideas metaphorically by shaping the clouds and currents of the Plane of Air to act out elaborate, moving stories and plays. Sometimes these animated clouds retain a bit of magic, becoming Pictures-in-Clouds.
