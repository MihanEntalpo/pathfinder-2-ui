### [Familiar Shadows (4)](../Monster/Monsters.aspx-ID=3186.md)pg. 157

Infest Shadow [two-actions] ([curse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=566.md), [divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md)) Requirement The familiar shadow is adjacent to the PC whose image it duplicates, that PC is not in darkness or invisible, and the familiar shadow is aware of the PC; Effect The familiar shadow attempts to infest the shadow of the PC whose appearance it has. The familiar shadow twists and writhes against the PC's shadow before pulling back, and the targeted PC must attempt a DC 21 Will save with the following results. This ability replaces the standard Shadow Spawn ability possessed by the typical undead shadow.
Critical Success The PC is unaffected and is immune to further attempts to Infest Shadow.
Success The PC is unaffected.
Failure The familiar shadow leaves its unholy influence behind in the PC's shadow, infesting it. The PC's shadow writhes and twists on its own, as if willfully ignoring the actions of the character to whom it's attached. The infested shadow has a habit of making distracting and embarrassing motions and imparts a –2 status penalty to its attached PC's [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38.md), [Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md), and [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48.md) checks. If the PC is in a situation where they aren't casting a shadow (such as when they're in complete darkness or are [invisible](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=83.md)), these penalties are suppressed, but they return as soon as their infested shadow does. If a PC with an infested shadow gains the dying condition, the shadow breaks free and becomes a shadow spawn (which functions the same as a shadow spawn created by a typical shadow). This shadow spawn won't attack the creature it spawned from, but any other targets are fair game. If the dying character recovers, their shadow spawn immediately dies, and their shadow returns to normal—otherwise, the only way to be rid of this strange curse is through the use of a spell like cleanse affliction, as it has an unlimited duration.
Critical Failure As failure, but the penalties apply to all checks made by the PC.
Steal Shadow [one-action] ([divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md)) As shadow, but this ability only functions against the PC who the familiar shadow appears to duplicate. If a familiar shadow manages to separate the target's shadow from its body by increasing the PC's [enfeebled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=71.md) value to 3 or more by using this ability, the target's shadow automatically becomes infested (see Infest Shadow above) as if the PC got a critical failure on the saving throw to resist this ability. The enfeebled value from Steal Shadow decreases by 1 every hour.
