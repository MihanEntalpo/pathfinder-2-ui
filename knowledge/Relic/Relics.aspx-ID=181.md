# [Grappling Vine](./Relics.aspx-ID=181.md)Major Gift

[Plant](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=668.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 317](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Aspect plant

---

Activate [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)); Effect Your relic launches a sticky, multi-stranded vine at a target within 50 feet. Make an attack roll as you would when using a grappling hook, but if you roll a critical failure on the check to secure the vine, you get a failure instead. Once the vine is anchored, creatures receive a +1 status bonus to [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md) checks to Climb the vine, and they gain a +5-foot status bonus to the distance they move with a successful check while Climbing using the vine. The vine lasts for 1 day or until you use grappling vine again, at which point your older vine withers.
