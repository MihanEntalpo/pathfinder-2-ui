# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Thaumaturgic Ritualist](./Feats.aspx-ID=8366.md) Feat 4

[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Thaumaturge](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=895.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 44](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Archetype [Palatine Detective](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=306.md)*
* This archetype offers Thaumaturgic Ritualist at a different level than displayed here.

---

Your studies into the supernatural have resulted in an especially strong knowledge of [rituals](../Ritual/Rituals.aspx.md). You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to all primary checks to perform a ritual. You learn two uncommon rituals with a rank no higher than half your level; you must meet all prerequisites for casting the ritual to choose it. You can cast these as the primary caster, but you can't teach them to anyone else or allow someone else to serve as primary caster unless they know the ritual as well.

At 8th level and every 4 levels thereafter, you learn another uncommon ritual with a rank no higher than half your level and for which you meet the prerequisites.

## Traits

Uncommon: Something of uncommon rarity requires special training or comes from a particular culture or part of the world. Some character choices give access to uncommon options, and the GM can choose to allow access for anyone. Less is known about uncommon creatures than common creatures. They typically can't be summoned. The DC of Recall Knowledge checks related to these creature is increased by 2.

Thaumaturge: This indicates abilities from the [thaumaturge](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=69.md) class.
