# [Microgravity](./Traits.aspx-ID=651.md)

Source [GM Core pg. 172](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
There’s little to no gravity on this plane. Creatures float in space unless they can push off a surface or use some force to propel themselves throughout the plane.
