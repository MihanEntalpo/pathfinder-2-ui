# [Nonlethal](./Traits.aspx-ID=661.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 282](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Attacks with this weapon are [nonlethal](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2311.md), and are used to knock creatures unconscious instead of kill them. You can use a nonlethal weapon to make a lethal attack with a –2 circumstance penalty.
