# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Oatia Skysage](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=335.md)

Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 238](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
The tradition of Oatia skysages stems from a small sect of dedicated stargazers on Castrovel. To better study the celestial mantle, these eccentric astronomers broke away from their Sovyrian kin and migrated to the wild continent of Ukulam, where the night skies were clear of light pollution. Though reclusive, Oatia skysages have been known to pass on their esoteric knowledge and unique worldview to similar-minded adventurers. Such adventuring skysages search for secrets and omens in the stars and can even call down stars’ light to aid them in battle.

# Additional Feats

Source [Player Core pg. 215](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Some archetypes include a list of “Additional Feats” that appear in other sources. The list includes each feat’s level, which might be different than normal when gained from the archetype. You can take the feat as an archetype feat of that level, meaning it counts toward the number of feats required by the archetype’s dedication feat. When selected this way, a feat that normally has a class’s trait (such as the fighter trait) doesn’t have that class trait.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Oatia Skysage Dedication](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8112.md) Feat 2

### This Feat may contain spoilers from the Gatewalkers Adventure Path

Uncommon Archetype Dedication
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 238](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Archetype [Oatia Skysage](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=335.md)
Prerequisites trained in [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md)

---

Your study of the stars unlocks supernatural insights and magic. You either become trained in [Astronomy Lore](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=41.md) or an expert in [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md).

You learn to cast spontaneous spells and gain the [Cast a Spell](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2734.md) activity. You gain a spell repertoire with two of the following cantrips of your choice: [detect magic](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1485.md), [guidance](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1549.md), [know the way](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1582.md), or [read aura](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1646.md). You cast these spells as occult spells as you draw on the stars and night sky. You’re trained in occult spell attack rolls and spell DCs. Your key spellcasting ability for these spells is Intelligence.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Stargazer's Eyes](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8114.md) Feat 4

Long hours in darkened observatories have sharpened your senses. If you don’t have low-light vision or darkvision, you gain low-light vision, or if you already have low-light vision, you gain darkvision.

The stars and moon lend you their light. You gain your choice of the [moonbeam](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1820.md) or zenith star domain spell. Like your Oatia skysage spells, you cast this [focus spell](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2228.md) as an occult spell. It costs 1 Focus Point to cast a focus spell. This feat grants a focus pool of 1 Focus Point that you can recover by using the Refocus to reflect upon or gaze at the stars.

Special You can take this feat a second time, gaining the focus spell that you didn’t gain the first time. Increase the number of Focus Points in your pool by 1.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Night's Warning](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8121.md) [reaction] Feat 14

Archetype [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Occult
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 239](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Archetype [Oatia Skysage](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=335.md)
Frequency once per hour
Prerequisites [Oatia Skysage Dedication](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8112.md)
Trigger A creature targets you with an attack and you can see the attacker.
Requirements You can see the night sky.

---

Portents written in the night sky show you how to avoid an attack. The triggering attack roll targets your Occultism DC instead of your AC. Though this allows you to avoid taking penalties to your AC, it doesn’t remove any conditions or other effects causing such penalties. For example, an enemy with sneak attack would still deal extra damage to you for being [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md), even though you wouldn’t take the –2 circumstance penalty when defending against the attack.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Starlight Armor](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8122.md) [three-actions] Feat 14

Archetype Occult
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 239](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Archetype [Oatia Skysage](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=335.md)
Frequency once per day
Prerequisites [Oatia Skysage Dedication](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8112.md)

---

You armor yourself in the light of distant suns, shielding yourself from harm. The armor grants you resistance 7 to piercing, bludgeoning, and slashing damage. The armor also sheds bright light for 20 feet (and dim light for the next 20 feet).

Whenever an adjacent creature attacks you, the attacker must attempt a Will save against your spell DC at the end of its action. On a failure, it becomes [dazzled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=65.md) until the end of its next turn. Regardless of the result of the save, the attacker is temporarily immune until the end of its next turn. The dazzling effect has the [light](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=640.md) and [visual](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=727.md) traits.

When you reach 17th level, the resistance increases to 10.
