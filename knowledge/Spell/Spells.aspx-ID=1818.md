Domain [might](../Domain/Domains.aspx-ID=83.md)
Duration 1 round

---

Your body fills with physical power and skill. You gain a +10- foot status bonus to Speed and a +2 status bonus to [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md) checks. As a part of Casting this Spell, you can use a Stride, Leap, Climb, or Swim action. The spell's bonuses apply during that action.
