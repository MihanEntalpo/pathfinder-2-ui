# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Pharimia (The Horizon Flame)](./Deities.aspx-ID=704.md)

Source [Pathfinder #216: The Acropolis Pyre pg. 78](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=271.md), [Myth-Speakers Players Guide pg. 11](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=270.md)
Patron of lighthouses and ports, Pharimia believes in guiding Iblydos and her fellow hero-gods to achieve unity and common purposes. Especially with the hero-god population slowly dwindling without news replacements, she works with any hero-gods she can—from the holiest to the most sinister—so long as they can set aside any malice to preserve the archipelago's pantheon. In turn, she favors priests who adopt equally difficult challenges that require negotiation and leadership.

Category Iblydos Hero-Gods
Edicts Guide others between places of safety, maintain travel infrastructure, unite contentious parties to achieve greater goals
Anathema Deny directions to the lost, extinguish a lighthouse or similar beacon, bring destruction upon Iblydos
Areas of Concern Ports and lighthouses
Religious Symbol three stars above a lighthouse
Sacred Animal rattlesnake
Sacred Color(s) red, yellow
