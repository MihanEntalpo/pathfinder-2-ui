# [Planar Rift](./Hazards.aspx-ID=476.md)Hazard 13

Magical Trap
Source [GM Core pg. 103](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 35 (trained)
Description A rift attempts to draw creatures into another plane (the GM chooses the specific plane).

---

Disable DC 33 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) (master) to assemble a rift seal using objects strongly grounded to your plane, or [dispel magic](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1493.md) (7th rank; counteract DC 31) to counteract the rift
Into the Great Beyond [reaction] ([occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md), [teleportation](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=710.md)) Trigger A creature moves within 10 feet of the rift; Effect The triggering creature and all creatures within 30 feet of the rift are drawn into another plane. Each creature can attempt a DC 33 Reflex save to avoid this fate.
