# [Mindshift](./Traits.aspx-ID=900.md)

Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 221](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
An action with this trait can always be altered to use the raw power of your mind. When you take this action, you can choose to replace any damage it deals with mental damage, give it the [mental](./Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) trait, and replace any save it requires with a Will save. The action loses any traits matching damage types it no longer deals.
