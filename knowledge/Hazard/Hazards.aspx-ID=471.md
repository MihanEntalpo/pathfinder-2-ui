# [Hallucination Powder Trap](./Hazards.aspx-ID=471.md)Hazard 6

Magical Trap
Source [GM Core pg. 102](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 24 (expert)
Description A tube of hallucinogenic powder armed with a miniature explosive is connected to a doorknob or similar latch.

---

Disable DC 26 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) (expert) to disable the hammer that strikes the percussion cap
AC 24, Fort 0, Ref 0
Hardness 0, HP 1; Immunities critical hits, object immunities, precision damage
Powder Burst [reaction] ([mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md), [poison](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=669.md)) Trigger The latch is opened or the tube is broken; Effect The tube explodes, spraying hallucinogenic powder in a 30-foot cone. Any creature in the cone must succeed at a DC 24 Will save or be [confused](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=63.md) for 1 round and take a –2 status penalty to Perception checks and saves against mental effects for 1d4 hours. On a critical failure, the penalty is instead –4.
