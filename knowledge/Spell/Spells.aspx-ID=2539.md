## This Spell may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Uncommon [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Linguistic [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Mental
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 287](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Access Character follows the ways of Sangpotshi or studies texts and lore containing Sangpotshi secrets
Traditions [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Range 30 feet; Targets creatures who are [hostile](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=80.md) toward each other
Duration 5 rounds

---

You speak about the consequences of actions people take against each other and how it’s possible to break cycles of violence simply by making a different choice. The targets regain 1d4 Hit Points at the end of each of their own turns while the spell is in effect. If a target uses a hostile action against the other target, the spell ends for the target that used the hostile action.

---

Heightened (+1) The targets regain an additional 1d4 Hit Points at the end of their own turns.
