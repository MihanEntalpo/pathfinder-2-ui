# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Orc Sight](./Feats.aspx-ID=4572.md) Feat 1

[Dromaar](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=516.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 83](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites low-light vision

---

Your orc blood grants you the keen vision of your forebears. You gain darkvision, allowing you to see in darkness and dim light just as well as you can in bright light. However, in darkness, you see in black and white only.

Special You can take this feat only at 1st level, and you can’t retrain out of this feat or into this feat.

## Traits

Dromaar: A creature with this trait has the dromaar versatile heritage. Dromaars are of mixed ancestry, including orcs or other dromaars. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by dromaars.
