# [Poisoned Dart Gallery](./Hazards.aspx-ID=492.md)Hazard 8

[Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) Mechanical Trap
Source [GM Core pg. 107](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth +16 (expert) or DC 31 (master) to notice the control panel
Description Countless holes to launch poison darts from line a long hallway with a hidden control panel on the far end.

---

Disable DC 21 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) (expert) on the control panel deactivates the trap
AC 27, Fort +13, Ref +17
Hardness 14, HP 56 (BT 28); Immunities critical hits, object immunities, precision damage
Dart Volley [reaction] Trigger A creature enters the hallway or ends its turn in the hallway; Effect The trap makes a poisoned dart [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) against the triggering creature, then rolls initiative.

---

Routine (1 action) The trap launches one dart against every creature in the gallery as 1 action. Because it launches darts continuously, the trap can also use the Continuous Barrage free action (see below) to launch darts at each creature during that creature’s turn.

Ranged poisoned dart +21, Damage 3d4 piercing plus flesset poison; no multiple attack penalty

Continuous Barrage [free-action] Trigger A creature within the active gallery finishes an action; Effect The trap makes a poisoned dart Strike against the triggering creature.

Flesset Poison (poison) Saving Throw DC 22 Fortitude; Maximum Duration 6 rounds; Stage 1 1d6 poison damage and [clumsy 1](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=61.md) (1 round); Stage 2 2d6 poison damage and clumsy 2 (1 round); Stage 3 3d6 poison damage and clumsy 3 (1 round)

---

Reset The trap deactivates and resets after 1 minute.
