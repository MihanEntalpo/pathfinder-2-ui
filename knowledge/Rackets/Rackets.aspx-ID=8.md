# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Scoundrel](./Rackets.aspx-ID=8.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 166](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
You use fast-talking, flattery, and a silver tongue to avoid danger and escape tricky situations. You might be a grifter or con artist, or you might use your skills in certain reputable professions, like a barrister or diplomat

When you successfully Feint, the target is off-guard against your melee attacks until the end of your next turn. On a critical success, the target is off-guard against all melee attacks until the end of your next turn, not just yours. If you Feint while wielding an agile or finesse melee weapon, you can [Step](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2304&Redirected=1.md) immediately after the Feint as a free action.

You’re trained in [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38&Redirected=1.md) and Diplomacy. You can choose Charisma as your key ability score.
