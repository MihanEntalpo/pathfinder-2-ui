# Critical Specialization Effects

Source [Player Core pg. 283](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Certain effects can grant you benefits when you make a Strike with certain weapons and get a critical success. This is called a critical specialization effect. The exact effect depends on which weapon group your weapon belongs to, as listed below. You can always decide not to add the critical specialization effect of your weapon.

Click here for the full rules on Critical Specialization Effects.

## Axe

Source [Player Core pg. 283](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Choose one creature adjacent to the initial target and within reach. If its AC is lower than your attack roll result for the critical hit, you deal damage to that creature equal to the result of the weapon damage die you rolled (including extra dice for its striking rune, if any). This amount isn't doubled, and no bonuses or other additional dice apply to this damage.

Weapons Adze, [Battle Axe](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=371.md), Battle Saddle, Boarding Axe, Butchering Axe, [Dwarven Waraxe](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=420.md), [Greataxe](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=376.md), Hand Adze, [Hatchet](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=382.md), Mambele, [Orc Necksplitter](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=422.md), Palstave, Panabas

## Bomb

Source [Player Core pg. 283](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Increase the radius of the bomb's splash damage (if any) to 10 feet.

Weapons [Alchemical Bomb](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=431.md)

## [Bow](./WeaponGroups.aspx-ID=3.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 283](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
If the target of the critical hit is adjacent to a surface, it gets stuck to that surface by the missile. The target is [immobilized](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=81&Redirected=1.md) and must spend an Interact action to attempt a DC 10 [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md) check to pull the missile free; it can't move from its space until it succeeds. The creature doesn't become stuck if it is incorporeal, is liquid (like a water elemental or some oozes), or could otherwise escape without effort.

Weapons Alchemical Crossbow, [Arrows](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=443.md), Backpack Ballista Bolts, [Bolts](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=441.md), Bolts (Phalanx Piercer), Bow Staff, [Composite Longbow](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=434.md), [Composite Shortbow](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=435.md), [Crescent Cross](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=345.md), Daikyu, Gakgung, Hongali Hornbow, Lancer, [Longbow](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=436.md), Mikazuki, Phalanx Piercer, Repeating Crossbow Magazine, Repeating Hand Crossbow Magazine, Repeating Heavy Crossbow Magazine, Rotary Bow, Shield Bow, [Shortbow](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=437.md), Taw Launcher, Wooden Taws
