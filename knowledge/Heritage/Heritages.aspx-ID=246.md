# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Fey-touched Gnome](./Heritages.aspx-ID=246.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 51](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
The blood of the fey flows through your veins, saturating you with their magic and making you truly one of them. You gain the fey trait, in addition to the gnome and humanoid traits. Choose one cantrip from the [primal](../SpellLists/SpellLists.aspx-Tradition=4.md) spell list. You can cast this spell as a primal innate spell at will. A cantrip is heightened to a spell rank equal to half your level rounded up. You can change this cantrip to a different one from the same list once per day by meditating to realign yourself with the First World; this is a 10-minute activity that has the [concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) trait.
