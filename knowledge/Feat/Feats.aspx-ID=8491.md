# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Guide the Timeline](./Feats.aspx-ID=8491.md) [one-action] Feat 6

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 186](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Archetype [Chronoskimmer](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=349.md)
Frequency once per day
Prerequisites [Chronoskimmer Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8489.md)

---

You know the result you want and subtly nudge the timeline to your intended destination. Choose an ally or a foe. If you choose an ally, the next time within the next round that ally makes an attack roll or skill check, they roll it twice and take the higher result; this is a [fortune](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=612.md) effect. If you choose a foe, the next time within the next round that foe makes an attack roll or skill check, they must roll twice and take the lower result unless they succeed at a Will save against your chronoskimmer DC; this is a [misfortune](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=654.md) effect. Regardless of your choice, the target becomes temporarily immune for 24 hours.

## Traits

Archetype: This feat belongs to an archetype.

Concentrate: An action with this trait requires a degree of mental concentration and discipline.
