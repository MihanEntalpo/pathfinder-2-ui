# Mummy Adjustments

Source [Book of the Dead pg. 72](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=118.md)

All types of creatures can have their corpses preserved and rise as mummies.

- Add the mummy and undead traits.
- Add the [Necril](../Language/Languages.aspx-ID=20.md) language.
- Add the following immunities: death effects, disease, paralyzed, poison, unconscious.
- Add weakness to fire, with a value depending on the creature's level.
- Add the following abilities.
Darkvision
Negative Healing
Lesser Despair (aura, divine, emotion, [enchantment](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=61.md), fear, mental) 30 feet. Living creatures of the mummy creature's level or lower are frightened 1 while in its despair aura. They can't naturally recover from this fear while in the area but recover instantly once they leave.
