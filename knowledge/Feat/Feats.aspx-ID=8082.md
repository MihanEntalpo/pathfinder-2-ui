# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Voice of the Elements](./Feats.aspx-ID=8082.md) Feat 5

[Dragonblood](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=794.md)
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 216](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md), [Rage of Elements pg. 21](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=205.md)

Prerequisites Primal Dragonblood

---

You inherit a primal dragon’s bond to base elements. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to [Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md) checks to [Make an Impression](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2392.md) on creatures with the [elemental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=587.md) trait. You can also ask questions of, receive answers from, and use the Diplomacy skill with elementals that don’t speak any language.

## Traits

Dragonblood: A creature with this trait has the dragonblood versatile heritage. These creatures are born of draconic lineage and often have draconic appearances.
