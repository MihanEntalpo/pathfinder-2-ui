# [Mirror Door](./Hazards.aspx-ID=626.md)Hazard -1

[Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Trap](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=715.md) [Visual](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=727.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 122](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 15
Description The reflection of a wall shows a phantom door where none exists. This door can only be manipulated while viewed through the mirror's reflection.

---

Disable DC 18 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) to open the door while viewing the reflection
Appear [reaction] ([arcane](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=534.md), [illusion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=629.md)) Trigger A creature views the wall through a mirror's reflection; Effect A door appears in the mirror's reflection of the wall. The triggering creature can open and manipulate this door only as long as they view it through the mirror and only by succeeding at a DC 18 Thievery check to disable the trap.
