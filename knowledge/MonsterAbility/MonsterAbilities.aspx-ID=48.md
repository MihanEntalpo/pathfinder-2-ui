# [Reactive Strike](./MonsterAbilities.aspx-ID=48.md) [reaction]

Source [Rage of Elements pg. 233](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=205.md)
Trigger A creature within the monster's reach uses a [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) action or a [move](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=658&Redirected=1.md) action, makes a ranged attack, or leaves a square during a move action it's using; Effect The monster attempts a melee Strike against the triggering creature. If the attack is a critical hit and the trigger was a manipulate action, the monster disrupts that action. This Strike doesn't count toward the monster's multiple attack penalty, and its multiple attack penalty doesn't apply to this Strike.
