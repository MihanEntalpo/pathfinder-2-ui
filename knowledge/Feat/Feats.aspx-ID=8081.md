# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Tenacious Jaws](./Feats.aspx-ID=8081.md) Feat 5

[Dragonblood](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=794.md)
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 216](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

Prerequisites Draconic Aspect; [jaws unarmed attack](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=356.md)

---

Your teeth are curved or hooked to hold on to your enemies. Your jaws unarmed attack gains the [grapple](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=619.md) trait. When you use your [Breath of the Dragon](./Feats.aspx-ID=5730.md), an opponent [grabbed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=77.md) or [restrained](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=90.md) in your jaws uses the outcome one degree of success worse than the result of their saving throw.

## Traits

Dragonblood: A creature with this trait has the dragonblood versatile heritage. These creatures are born of draconic lineage and often have draconic appearances.
