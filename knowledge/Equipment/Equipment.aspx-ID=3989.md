## This Item may contain spoilers from the Myth-Speakers Adventure Path

[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Consumable](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=564.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Talisman](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=709.md)
Source [Pathfinder #216: The Acropolis Pyre pg. 81](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=271.md)
Usage affixed to armor; Bulk &mdash;
Activate [free-action] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md)) Trigger You are about to roll an [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md) or [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) check, and you are trained or better in the skill; Requirements You can perceive at least 12 allies or friendly bystanders encouraging you.

---

This elegant crown of laurels can be worn on the head, atop a helmet, or even wrapped around the neck as a torc, signaling the wearer's athletic achievements in a past competition. When you Activate the laurels, you draw strength from your adoring crowd and gain a status bonus to the triggering check. If you critically succeed at the check, you gain temporary Hit Points equal to the item's level that last for 1 minute.
