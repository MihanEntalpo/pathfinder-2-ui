# [Distant Wandering](./Feats.aspx-ID=8045.md) [one-action] Feat 10

## This Feat may contain spoilers from the Gatewalkers Adventure Path

Rare Deviant Magical
Source [Gatewalkers Player's Guide (Remastered) pg. 13](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=287.md), [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 103](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

---

Your body falls unconscious and your spirit projects out of it. While in spirit form, you can’t consciously move your body, and you are [invisible](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=83.md) and inaudible, though a creature still might notice the signs of your presence and passing, just like for an invisible creature affected by [silence](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1674.md), or a [scouting eye](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1661.md). You can freely move about, though you can’t touch or move anything, cast spells, attack, or otherwise affect anything around you. Despite your ghostly form, you are not [incorporeal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=632.md) and can’t pass through barriers you couldn’t in your body. Most effects can’t harm your spirit form, though some spells, like [spirit blast](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1685.md) and spirit song, explicitly damage a creature’s spirit.

You can return to your body as a free action. If you are in spirit form at the beginning of your turn, you must attempt another backlash check for your deviation, returning to your body if you fail.

Awakening Your spirit moves at the speed of thought. You gain a fly Speed and a +20-foot status bonus to your Speed while in spirit form.

Awakening Time seems to pass more slowly while you are projecting your spirit. When you enter spirit form, you can [Recall Knowledge](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2638.md) or [Seek](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2301.md). You are [quickened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=89.md) while in spirit form and can use the extra action only to Recall Knowledge or Seek.

They typically can't be summoned. The DC of Recall Knowledge checks related to these creatures is increased by 5.

Deviant: This trait describes strange supernatural or paranormal abilities

Magical: Something with the magical trait is imbued with magical energies not tied to a specific tradition of magic. Some items or effects are closely tied to a particular tradition of magic. In these cases, the item has the arcane, divine, occult, or primal trait instead of the magical trait. Any of these traits indicate that the item is magical.
