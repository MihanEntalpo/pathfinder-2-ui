# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Reverse Curse](./Feats.aspx-ID=8477.md) Feat 12

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 169](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Archetype [Curse Maelstrom](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=347.md)
Prerequisites [Counter Curse](./Feats.aspx-ID=8475.md)

---

When you counter a [curse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=566.md) or [misfortune](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=654.md) effect, you send it back to destroy its originator. Whenever you succeed at the counteract check to Counter Curse, the creature is affected by its own curse or misfortune effect, attempting a saving throw against its own DC if the effect allows a saving throw. In the case of a constant ability, such as a misfortune aura, the creature is affected only until the beginning of its next turn, when the ability reasserts itself.
