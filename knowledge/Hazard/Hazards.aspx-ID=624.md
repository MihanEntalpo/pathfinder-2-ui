# [Shattered Runewell of Pride](./Hazards.aspx-ID=624.md)Hazard 13

[Unique](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=723.md) [Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) [Environmental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=591.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Mythic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=835.md)
Source [Pathfinder Adventure Path #219: Lord of the Trinity Star pg. 60](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=283.md)
Complexity Complex
27 (expert) to realize that the shattered runewell is dangerous; noticing the hazard has a DC of 0 27
Description Plumes of magenta and violet light and vapor shift and writhe out of a hole in reality surrounded by the fragments of a runewell suspended mid-explosion.

---

Disable DC 37 [Arcana](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=35.md) (master) or DC 40 [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md) (master) to redirect the magic back in on itself and cancel out the danger, or DC 40 [Crafting](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=37.md) (legendary) to reposition runewell fragments so they’re not dangerous; three total successes are required to disable the shattered runewell
AC 34, Fort +29, Ref +26
Shattered Runewell Hardness 23, HP 90 (BT 45); Immunities critical hits, object immunities, precision damage
Frightful Shriek [reaction] (emotion, fear, mental) Trigger Intruders enter the Eye of Arrogance; Effect All of the illusory peacocks in the demiplane emit loud shrieks of anger. All intruders to the Eye of Arrogance must attempt a DC 37 Will save or become [frightened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=76.md) 1 (frightened 2 on a critical failure). The shattered runewell then rolls initiative.

---

Routine (3 actions) When the shattered runewell takes its f irst action, a towering, 30-foot-tall image of Runelord Xanderghul formed from scintillating magenta and violet light and fire looms up from the runewell. On each of the hazard’s three actions, this manifestation of Runelord Xanderghul scowls down at the PCs, and tendrils of strangely colored fire lash out of the runewell, each targeting a different PC in the area. The effects of these tendrils vary according to their color; these ranged Strikes can target anyone in the Eye of Arrogance and accrue multiple attack penalties normally.

Ranged iridescent fire +27 (mental), Damage 3d10+6 mental damage (plus the struck creature is [slowed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=92.md) 1 for 1 round if they fail a DC 37 Will save)
Ranged black fire +27 (void), Damage 3d10+6 void damage plus 2d6 persistent acid damage
Ranged crimson fire +27 (agile), Damage 3d10+6 slashing damage plus 2d6 persistent bleed
