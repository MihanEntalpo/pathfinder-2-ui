# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Splint Mail](./Armor.aspx-ID=48.md)Item 1

Source [Player Core pg. 273](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Price 13 gp; AC Bonus +5; Dex Cap +1; Check Penalty -3; Speed Penalty -10 ft.
Strength +3; Bulk 3; Category Heavy; Group Composite

---

This type of armor is chain mail reinforced with flexible, interlocking metal plates, typically located on the wearer's torso, upper arms, and legs. A suit of this armor comes with an undercoat of padded armor (see above) and a pair of gauntlets.

## Armor Specialization Effects

Source [Core Rulebook pg. 275](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)
Certain class features can grant you additional benefits with certain armors. This is called an armor specialization effect. The exact effect depends on which armor group your armor belongs to, as listed below. Only medium and heavy armors have armor specialization effects.

Composite: The numerous overlapping pieces of this armor protect you from piercing attacks. You gain resistance to piercing damage equal to 1 + the value of the armor’s potency rune for medium armor, or 2 + the value of the armor’s potency rune for heavy armor.

## Specific Magic Armor

Life-Saver Mail, Mail of Luck, Mariner's Splint, Psychic Brigandine
