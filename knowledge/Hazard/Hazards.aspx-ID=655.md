# [Fiendish Reflection](./Hazards.aspx-ID=655.md)Hazard 3

[Unique](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=723.md) [Haunt](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=622.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 134](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 23 (expert)
Description Reflections viewed in the water here appear monstrous and fiendish; a PC who views themself in these waters sees a distorted version of themself looking back at them, sneering and mocking.

---

Disable DC 20 [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md) to exorcise the haunt, or DC 23 [Intimidation](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=40.md) to turn the fear back on the haunt and overwhelm it with its own terror
Frightening Visage [reaction] ([emotion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=590.md), [fear](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=598.md), [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md), [occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md)) Trigger A creature looks at the water; Effect The creature sees its reflection on the water’s surface distort into a fiendish version of itself; it must succeed at a DC 20 Will save or become [frightened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=76.md) 2 (and [fleeing](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=74.md) for as long as it remains frightened on a critical failure).

---

Reset The haunt resets at the end of the round automatically, ready to trigger again the next tome someone looks into the water.
