# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Divine Dragon's Watch Spell 5

Uncommon [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Contingency](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=385.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) [Sanctified](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=519.md) Spirit
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 224](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Traditions [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md)
Cast 10 minutes
Range touch; Targets 1 creature
Duration 24 hours

---

You summon an invisible divine draconic spirit to watch over your target. When the spell is complete, you gain the Dragon’s Protection reaction; once you use the reaction, the spell ends.

Dragon’s Protection [reaction] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [sanctified](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=519.md), [spirit](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=737.md)) Trigger An enemy you can see would reduce this spell’s target to 0 Hit Points; Effect The dragon intercepts, preventing the spell’s target from being knocked out; the spell’s target remains at 1 Hit Point. As the dragon disappears, it flies over the triggering enemy and releases its retributive breath, dealing 5d4 spirit damage to it with a [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Reflex save.

---

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 1d4.
