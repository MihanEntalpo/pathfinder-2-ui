Ancestry Page [Dromaar](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=70.md)
Orcish strength emboldens your bloodline. You have a green tinge to your skin and other indicators of orc heritage. You gain the orc trait, the [dromaar](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=516.md) trait, and low-light vision. When you gain an ancestry feat, you can choose from dromaar and orc feats in addition to those from your ancestry.
