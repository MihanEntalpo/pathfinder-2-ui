# [Constricting Hall](./Hazards.aspx-ID=639.md)Hazard 8

[Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Trap](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=715.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 124](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth DC 31
Description A hall's four walls become fluid and elastic when a creature reaches the hall's midpoint.

---

Disable DC 28 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) (trained) to stabilize each wall, or [dispel magic](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1493.md) (4th level; counteract DC 26) to counteract each wall
Lose Form [reaction] ([occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md)) Trigger A creature reaches the hall's midpoint; Effect The hall's walls lose their rigidity, becoming fluid and elastic. The hall becomes difficult terrain, and each creature in the hall is knocked [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88.md) unless they succeed at a DC 26 Reflex save. The trap then rolls initiative.

---

Routine (4 actions) This trap loses 1 action each round for each wall that has been disabled. Each wall uses 1 action to slam back and forth rapidly, pummeling one creature in the hall with a wall Strike. On a hit, the target is additionally knocked prone. The walls distribute the attacks as evenly as possible among creatures in the hall, and the Strikes don't apply the trap's multiple attack penalty.
Melee [one-action] wall +20, Damage 2d10+11 bludgeoning

---

Reset 1 hour
