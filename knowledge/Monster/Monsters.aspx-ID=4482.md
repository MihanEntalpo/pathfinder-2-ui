# Giant Mosquito

These horrifically enlarged versions of the common mosquito often prey upon megafauna like dinosaurs and other large creatures, but they won't turn down a chance to drink the blood of a smaller target—such as a humanoid.

# Mosquito

Source [Monster Core 2 pg. 228](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=276.md)
While the common mosquito is a pest capable of spreading deadly diseases, their giant kin and ravenous clouds of mosquito swarms are even more dangerous.

### Malaria Strains

The most common version of [malaria](../Diseases/Diseases.aspx-ID=30.md)—a pernicious disease often associated with mosquitoes—is presented within [GM Core](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md), and while the variants inflicted by mosquito swarms and giant mosquitoes are deadlier, they don’t have the chance to recur after 1d4 months. Pyrexic malaria also causes weakness and fevers, while septic malaria causes painful and infected lesions to form on the body. Regardless of the strain you contract, you can’t reduce your sickened condition while you’re affected with malaria.
