# [Occultism (Int)](./Skills.aspx-ID=44.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 243](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
You know a great deal about ancient philosophies, esoteric lore, obscure mysticism, and supernatural creatures.

- [Recall Knowledge](./Skills.aspx-ID=24&General=true.md) about ancient mysteries; obscure philosophies; creatures of occult significance (like [aberrations](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=523.md), spirits, and [oozes](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=665.md)); and esoteric planes like the Astral Plane, [Creation's Forge](../Plane/Planes.aspx-ID=27.md), the Ethereal Plane, [the Netherworld](../Plane/Planes.aspx-ID=36.md), and [the Void](../Plane/Planes.aspx-ID=28.md).

## Occultism Trained Actions

- [Decipher Writing](./Skills.aspx-ID=20&General=true.md) on occult topics, including complex metaphysical systems, syncretic principles, obscure philosophies, and incoherent ramblings.
- [Identify Magic](./Skills.aspx-ID=22&General=true.md), particularly magic of the occult tradition.
- [Learn a Spell](./Skills.aspx-ID=23&General=true.md) from the occult tradition.
