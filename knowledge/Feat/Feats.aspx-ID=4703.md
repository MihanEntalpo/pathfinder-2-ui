# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Shared Clarity](./Feats.aspx-ID=4703.md) Feat 18

[Cleric](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=554.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 121](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetype [Mortal Herald](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=305.md)*
Prerequisites [Premonition of Clarity](./Feats.aspx-ID=4691.md)
* This archetype offers Shared Clarity at a different level than displayed here.

---

You can project your premonitions of clarity to your allies. When you use Premonition of Clarity, allies within 15 feet of you who failed the same saving throw against the same mental effect can also spend their reactions to reroll the failed saving throw with a +2 circumstance bonus.

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more archetypes in addition to the listed classes. When selected this way, the feat does not have its class traits.
