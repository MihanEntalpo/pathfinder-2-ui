# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Vine Leshy](./Heritages.aspx-ID=271.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 68](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Your prehensile vines grant you unmatched skill at climbing. You do not need to have any hands free to Climb. Additionally, if you roll a success on an [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md) check to Climb, you get a critical success instead.
