# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Dwarven Doughtiness](./Feats.aspx-ID=4387.md) Feat 1

Dwarf
Source [Player Core pg. 43](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

You are naturally calm and collected in the face of imminent danger. At the end of your turn, reduce your frightened condition by 2 instead of 1.

## Traits

Dwarf: A creature with this trait is a member of the dwarf ancestry. Dwarves are stout folk who often live underground and typically have darkvision. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by dwarves. An item with this trait is created and used by dwarves.
