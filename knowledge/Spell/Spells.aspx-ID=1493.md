# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Dispel Magic [two-actions] Spell 2

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 325](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Spell List [elemental](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=5.md)
Bloodline imperial
Range 120 feet; Targets 1 spell effect or unattended magic item

---

You unravel the magic behind a spell or effect. Attempt a counteract check against the target. If you successfully counteract a magic item, it becomes a mundane item of its type for 10 minutes. This doesn't change the item's non-magical properties. If the target is an artifact or similar item, you automatically fail.
