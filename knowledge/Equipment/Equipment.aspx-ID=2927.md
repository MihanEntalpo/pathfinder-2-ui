# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Spellstrike AmmunitionItem 3+

[Consumable](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=564.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 256](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Ammunition any; Bulk &mdash;
Activate [two-actions] Cast a Spell

---

Mystic patterns create a magic reservoir within this ammunition. You activate spellstrike ammunition by Casting a Spell on the ammunition. The spell must be of a spell rank the ammunition can hold, must take 1 or 2 actions to cast, and must be able to target a creature other than the caster. A creature hit by activated spellstrike ammunition is targeted by the spell. If the creature isn't a valid target for the spell, the spell is lost.
The ammunition affects only the target hit, even if the spell would normally affect more than one target. If the spell requires a spell attack roll, use the result of your ranged attack roll to determine the degree of success of the spell. If the spell requires a save, the target attempts the save against your spell DC.
The maximum rank of spell the ammunition can hold determines its item level and Price.
