# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Heart of Earth](./Feats.aspx-ID=8088.md) Feat 13

[Dragonblood](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=794.md)
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 217](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

---

The earth parts to allow you passage. You gain a [burrow Speed](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2348.md) of 15 feet. While burrowing, your tremorsense becomes precise up to 15 feet. When you [Burrow](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2310.md), you can choose to leave behind a tunnel of your size or smaller for others to traverse through.

## Traits

Dragonblood: A creature with this trait has the dragonblood versatile heritage. These creatures are born of draconic lineage and often have draconic appearances.
