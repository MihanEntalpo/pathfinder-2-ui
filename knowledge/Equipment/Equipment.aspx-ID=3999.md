# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Shield of SnakesItem 10

## This Item may contain spoilers from the Myth-Speakers Adventure Path

Unique Magical [Mythic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=835.md)
Source [Pathfinder #218: Titanbane pg. 69](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=274.md)
Price 1,000 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk 1

---

A shield of snakes (Hardness 10, HP 90, BT 45) is built to resemble a withering tangle of snakes. These serpents slither and hiss whenever you [Raise the Shield](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2316.md).

Activate—Snap Up [reaction] Trigger You roll initiative; Effect The shield of snakes moves into position, allowing you to [Raise the Shield](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2316.md).

Activate—Snakebite [two-actions] ([attack](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=540.md)); Effect You thrust one of the snake heads at a target, attempting a [shield spikes](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=397.md) [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md). If you spend 1 [Mythic Point](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=3323.md), you attempt this Strike at [mythic proficiency](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=3322.md). On a hit, you inflict damage normally, and then the snakes poison the target, inflicting an additional 2d6 poison damage (DC 27 [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Fortitude save).
