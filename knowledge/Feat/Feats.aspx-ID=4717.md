# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Enhanced Familiar](./Feats.aspx-ID=4717.md) Feat 2

[Animist](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=836.md) [Druid](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=583.md) [Magus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=389.md) [Sorcerer](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=824.md) [Thaumaturge](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=895.md) [Witch](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=734.md) [Wizard](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=735.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 129](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md), [Player Core 2 pg. 153](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetypes [Elementalist](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=207.md)*, [Familiar Master](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=250.md)*, [Rivethun Emissary](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=308.md)*, [Shadowcaster](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=101.md)*
Prerequisites a [familiar](../Familiar/Familiars.aspx.md)
* This archetype offers Enhanced Familiar at a different level than displayed here.

---

You infuse your familiar with additional primal energy, increasing its abilities. You can select four familiar or master abilities each day, instead of two.

Special [Witch] Add the bonus familiar abilities you gain for being a witch to this amount. [Wizard] If your arcane thesis is improved familiar attunement, your familiar’s base number of familiar abilities, before adding any extra abilities from the arcane thesis, is four.

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more archetypes in addition to the listed classes. When selected this way, the feat does not have its class traits.
