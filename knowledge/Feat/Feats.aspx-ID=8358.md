# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Scroll Thaumaturgy](./Feats.aspx-ID=8358.md) Feat 1

[Thaumaturge](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=895.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 42](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

---

Your multidisciplinary study of magic means you know how to activate the magic in [scrolls](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2962.md) with ease. You can activate scrolls of any magical tradition, using your thaumaturge class DC for the scroll's DC, rather than a particular spell DC. If a spell is on the spell list for multiple traditions, you choose which tradition to use at the time you activate the scroll. You can draw and activate scrolls with the same hand holding an implement, much like you can for esoterica.
