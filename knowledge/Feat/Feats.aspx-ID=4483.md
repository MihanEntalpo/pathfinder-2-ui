# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Sense Allies](./Feats.aspx-ID=4483.md) Feat 5

Human
Source [Player Core pg. 64](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

Like many humans raised in a close-knit community, you have always been strongly attuned to the presence of others. Willing allies that you are aware of within 60 feet that would otherwise be [undetected](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=96&Redirected=1.md) by you are instead [hidden](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=79&Redirected=1.md) from you. The flat check for you to target willing allies within 60 feet that are hidden from you is 5 instead of 11.

## Traits

Human: A creature with this trait is a member of the human ancestry. Humans are a diverse array of people known for their adaptability. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by humans.
