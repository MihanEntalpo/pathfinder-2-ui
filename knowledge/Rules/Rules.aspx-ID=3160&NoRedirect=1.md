# Important Items

Source [GM Core pg. 223](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)

Some magical items in the game are an important part of the game's progression, so ensure your players are getting enough of the following items.

- Fundamental runes. Potency runes for [weapons](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2830.md) and [armor](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2785.md), [resilient](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2786.md) runes for armor, [reinforcing](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2811.md) runes for shields, and [striking](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2829.md) runes for weapons are all important to ensure characters have the attacks, damage, and defenses suited to their level. Read more about these runes here.
- Skill items. Players seeking true mastery of a skill will be happy to get items that give bonuses to that skill. These are mostly in the categories of held items and worn items.
- Spellcaster items. A staff is as important to a spellcaster as a magic sword is to a fighter. Learn more about staves here. Scrolls and wands provide extra spells in a more limited capacity.
- Weapon alternatives. Characters using unarmed attacks need [handwraps of mighty blows](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=3086.md) or similar items instead of weapon runes.
- Healing potions. Keep them alive!
