# [Cast a Spell](./Actions.aspx-ID=2734.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 299](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

Spells can vary in how many actions they take, as shown in the spell’s stat block. You cast [cantrips](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2227.md), spells from [spell slots](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2222.md), and [focus spells](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2228.md) using the same process, but must expend the spell when casting a spell from a spell slot and must spend 1 Focus Point to cast a focus spell. Some rules will refer to the Cast a Spell activity, such as “if the next action you use is to Cast a Spell.” Any spell qualifies as a Cast a Spell activity, and any characteristics of the spell use those of the specific spell you’re casting.

See rules for [Casting Spells](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2233.md) for more information.
