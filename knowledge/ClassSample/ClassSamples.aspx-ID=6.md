# [Fury](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=6.md)

Source [Core Rulebook pg. 92](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)
You keep things simple, because you need only one thing to drive you: your own inner rage.

## Ability Scores

Prioritize your Strength, then your Constitution and Dexterity. Wisdom can make you more perceptive.

## Skills

[Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34&Redirected=1.md), [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md), Intimidation, [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48&Redirected=1.md)

## Instinct

Fury Instinct

## Starting Feat

Sudden Charge

## Higher-Level Feats

Furious Finish (2nd), [Fast Movement](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=140.md) (4th), Attack of Opportunity (6th), Whirlwind Strike (14th)
