# [Athlete's Ally](./Feats.aspx-ID=8179.md) [one-action] Feat 14

Magical [Mythic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=835.md)
Source [Pathfinder Adventure Path #219: Lord of the Trinity Star pg. 85](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=283.md)
Archetype [Warshard Warrior](../MythicDestiny/MythicDestinies.aspx-ID=341.md)
Prerequisites [Warshard Warrior Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8178.md)
Requirements You're wielding your warshard weapon.

---

You channel the power of your warshard through your weapon to better perform athletic maneuvers. Spend a [Mythic Point](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=3323.md). For 1 minute, when you attempt Athletics checks to [Disarm](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2383.md), [Grapple](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2376.md), [Reposition](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2379.md), [Shove](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2380.md), or [Trip](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2382.md) with your warshard weapon, you do so at mythic proficiency. Note that this ability doesn’t grant additional weapon traits to your weapon, so your options for which of these maneuvers you can attempt might be limited by your weapon’s existing traits.

## Traits

Magical: Something with the magical trait is imbued with magical energies not tied to a specific tradition of magic. Some items or effects are closely tied to a particular tradition of magic. In these cases, the item has the arcane, divine, occult, or primal trait instead of the magical trait. Any of these traits indicate that the item is magical.

Mythic: Options with this trait grant or utilize mythic power. Feats with the mythic trait can only be taken by mythic characters, who are typically characters with a mythic Calling.

Monsters with the mythic trait have access to a pool of Mythic Points and are particularly strong for creatures of their level. Many mythic monsters are either resistant or entirely immune to attacks from non-mythic creatures and weapons.

Spells with the mythic trait require the expenditure of a Mythic Point in order to be cast, and items with the mythic trait require the expenditure of a Mythic Point in order to use their activated abilities.

Weapons with the mythic trait overcome the resistances and immunities of mythic monsters.
