# [Nindoru](./Traits.aspx-ID=904.md)

Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 328](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Nethys Note: This page contains HUGE SPOILERS for the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path. Read ahead at your own risk. Nindorus are [unholy](./Traits.aspx-ID=521.md) [fiends](./Traits.aspx-ID=600.md) who rise from corruptions to the cycle of reincarnating souls and live within the Universe. Although they often have elements in their appearance that make them seem undead, they’re actually living beings. Most nindorus have [darkvision](../MonsterAbility/MonsterAbilities.aspx-ID=59.md), are immune to death effects, have weakness to [silver](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2920.md), and possess the power to manifest objects or creatures from their thoughts.
