# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Ritual Researcher](./Feats.aspx-ID=4593.md) Feat 4

Uncommon [Bard](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=546.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 103](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites [enigma muse](../Muses/Muses.aspx-ID=1.md); expert in Occultism

---

Careful research into the art of rituals has made you better at performing them. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to all primary and secondary checks you attempt as part of casting a ritual.

## Traits

Uncommon: Something of uncommon rarity requires special training or comes from a particular culture or part of the world. Some character choices give access to uncommon options, and the GM can choose to allow access for anyone. Less is known about uncommon creatures than common creatures. They typically can't be summoned. The DC of Recall Knowledge checks related to these creature is increased by 2.
