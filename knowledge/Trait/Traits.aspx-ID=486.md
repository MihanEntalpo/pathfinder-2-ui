# [Ponderous](./Traits.aspx-ID=486.md)

Source [Treasure Vault pg. 220](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=191.md)
The armor has moving parts or other complications that lengthen the wearer's initial reaction time. While wearing the armor, you take a –1 penalty to initiative checks. If you don't meet the armor's required Strength modifier, this penalty increases to be equal to the armor's check penalty if it's worse.
