# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Hunter's Aim](./Feats.aspx-ID=4867.md) [two-actions] Feat 2

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Ranger](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=682.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 158](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetype [Game Hunter](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=169.md)*
* This archetype offers Hunter's Aim at a different level than displayed here.

---

When you focus on aiming, your attack becomes particularly accurate. Make a ranged weapon Strike against your hunted prey. On this Strike, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to the attack roll and ignore your prey’s concealed condition and any lesser cover.

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more archetypes in addition to the listed classes. When selected this way, the feat does not have its class traits.
