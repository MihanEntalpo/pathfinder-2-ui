# [Storm Druid](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=63.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 132](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
You call upon the power of nature to let loose bolts of lightning, intense weather, and elemental spells.

## Ability Scores

Prioritize Wisdom. You typically stay at range, so take a high Dexterity. You can identify creatures from the safety of the air with high Intelligence.

## Skills

[Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md), [Medicine](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=42.md), [Nature](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=43.md), [Survival](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=49.md)

## Order

[storm](../DruidicOrders/DruidicOrders.aspx-ID=10.md)

## Higher-Level Feats

[Widen Spell](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=4715.md) (1st), [Storm Retribution](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=4732.md) (6th), [Wind Caller](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=4740.md) (8th), [Primal Focus](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=4751.md) (12th), [Invoke Disaster](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=4762.md) (18th)

## Prepared Spells
