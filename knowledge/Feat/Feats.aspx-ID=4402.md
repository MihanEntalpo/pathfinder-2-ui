# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Telluric Power](./Feats.aspx-ID=4402.md) Feat 13

Dwarf
Source [Player Core pg. 45](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

You channel strength from the earth beneath your feet to pummel your enemies. When making a melee [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306&Redirected=1.md) against a target who is standing on the same earth or stone surface as you are, you gain a circumstance bonus to the damage roll equal to the number of weapon damage dice.

## Traits

Dwarf: A creature with this trait is a member of the dwarf ancestry. Dwarves are stout folk who often live underground and typically have darkvision. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by dwarves. An item with this trait is created and used by dwarves.
