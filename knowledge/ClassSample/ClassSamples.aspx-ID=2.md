# [Bomber](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=2.md)

Source [Core Rulebook pg. 78](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)
You can mix an explosive out of just about anything!

## Ability Scores

Prioritize Intelligence and Dexterity. Constitution helps you survive explosions gone wrong, and Wisdom prevents you from bombing the wrong target.

## Skills

[Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34&Redirected=1.md), Arcana, Crafting, [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38&Redirected=1.md), Intimidation, Society, [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48&Redirected=1.md), [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50&Redirected=1.md)

## Research Field

Bomber

## Starting Feat

Quick Bomber

## Higher-Level Feats

Smoke Bomb (2nd), [Calculated Splash](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=99.md) (4th), Directional Bombs (6th), Sticky Bomb (8th), Expanded Splash (10th), Mega Bomb (20th)
