# [Trickster](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=101.md)

Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 40](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
You use mirrors to confound and control areas.

## Ability Scores

Raise Charisma, Constitution, and Dexterity or Strength, depending on your weapon.

## Skills

[Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38.md), [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48.md), [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md)
