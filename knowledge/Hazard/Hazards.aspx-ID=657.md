# [Unholy Hunger](./Hazards.aspx-ID=657.md)Hazard 6

[Unique](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=723.md) [Haunt](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=622.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 137](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 25 (expert)
Description An unspeakable miasma of foul-smelling vapor with shrieking faces barely visible in the steam bubbles up from the soup.

---

Disable DC 19 [Cooking Lore](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=41.md) to neutralize the haunted soup with the addition of a few select spices from nearby shelves, or DC 25 Religion to exorcise the haunt.
Worse Than it Smells [reaction] ([occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md), [poison](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=669.md)) Trigger A non-nindoru creature takes the lid off the cooking pot; Effect All creatures within 15 feet of the pot must succeed at a DC 27 Fortitude save or become [sickened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=91.md) 2. The triggering creature instead must attempt a DC 24 Will save to resist the compulsion to dip their face into the stew and swallow a mouthful of the stuff (this effect has the [emotion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=590.md) and [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) traits). On a failure, the triggering creature takes 5d6 poison damage (DC 24 [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Fortitude save).

---

Reset The haunt resets 1 round after the lid is replaced on the cooking pot.
