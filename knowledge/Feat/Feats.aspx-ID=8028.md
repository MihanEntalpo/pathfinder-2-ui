# [Storming Breath](./Feats.aspx-ID=8028.md) [two-actions] Feat 6

## This Feat may contain spoilers from the Gatewalkers Adventure Path

Rare Deviant Magical
Source [Gatewalkers Player's Guide (Remastered) pg. 9](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=287.md), [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 100](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

---

You spit out a freezing breath or sonic scream. You deal 4d6 damage, plus 1d6 damage for every level you have beyond 6th, to all creatures in a 30-foot cone, with a [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Reflex save.

Awakening Your blast powerfully batters your foes. A creature that critically fails its save is knocked [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88.md).
Awakening The kickback of your blast helps you make a speedy escape. When you use this ability, you [Fly](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2312.md) backward 15 feet in a straight line directly opposite your blast. This movement doesn’t trigger reactions based on movement.
