# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Titan Slinger](./Feats.aspx-ID=4462.md) Feat 1

Halfling
Source [Player Core pg. 60](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

You have learned how to use your sling to fell enormous creatures. When you hit on an attack with a weapon of the sling group against a Large or larger creature, increase the size of the weapon damage die by one step (details on increasing weapon damage die sizes can be found on page 276).

## Traits

Halfling: A creature with this trait is a member of the halfling ancestry. These small people are friendly wanderers considered to be lucky. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by halflings. A weapon with this trait is created and used by halflings.
