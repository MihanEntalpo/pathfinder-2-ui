# [Blackfrost](./Curses.aspx-ID=96.md)Curse 10

Uncommon Cold Cursed Disease Occult
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 236](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
This affliction can’t be reduced below stage 1, nor the damage from it healed, until successfully treated with [cleanse affliction](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1467.md) or a similar effect; the affliction can then be removed as a normal disease. A creature that dies from cold damage while cursed with blackfrost rises as a blackfrost dead after 24 hours.

Saving Throw DC 27 Fortitude; Effect Stage 1 4d6 cold damage (1 minute); Stage 2 [weakness](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2312.md) to cold 5 and [drained](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=68.md) 1 (1 day); Stage 3 weakness to cold 10 and drained 2 (1 day)
