# [Razing](./Traits.aspx-ID=784.md)

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 274](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Razing weapons are particularly good at damaging objects, structures, and vehicles. Whenever you deal damage to an object (including [shields](../Shield/Shields.aspx.md) and [animated objects](../MonsterFamily/MonsterFamilies.aspx-ID=360.md)), structure, or vehicle with a razing weapon, the object takes an amount of additional damage equal to double the number of weapon damage dice.
