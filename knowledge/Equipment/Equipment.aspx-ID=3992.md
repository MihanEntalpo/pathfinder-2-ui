## This Item may contain spoilers from the Myth-Speakers Adventure Path

[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Invested](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=637.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [Pathfinder #216: The Acropolis Pyre pg. 81](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=271.md)
Price 50 gp
Usage worn cloak; Bulk L

---

The red and dark-orange feathers of a [pyrefowl](../Monster/Monsters.aspx-ID=3951.md) have been woven into a beautiful cloak.

Activate—Deflect Flame [reaction] (manipulate); Trigger You would be affected by a [fire](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=604.md) effect; Effect You flourish the cloak, using the feathers to reduce the incoming heat. You gain fire resistance 5 and a +1 circumstance bonus to saving throws against the triggering effect until the beginning of your next turn. If you are taking [persistent fire damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86&Redirected=1.md), you can immediately attempt a flat check to end the persistent damage.
