# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Very, Very Sneaky](./Feats.aspx-ID=4453.md) Feat 13

Goblin
Source [Player Core pg. 57](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites [Very Sneaky](./Feats.aspx-ID=4446.md)

---

You can move up to your Speed when you use the Sneak action, and you no longer need to have cover or greater cover or be concealed to Hide or Sneak.

## Traits

Goblin: A creature with this trait is a small, frantic goblinoid. Goblins tend to have darkvision. An ability with this trait can be used or chosen only by goblins. A weapon with this trait is created and used by goblins.
