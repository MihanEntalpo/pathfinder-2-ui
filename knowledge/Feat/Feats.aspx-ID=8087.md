# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Debilitating Breath](./Feats.aspx-ID=8087.md) Feat 13

[Dragonblood](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=794.md)
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 217](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

Prerequisites [Breath of the Dragon](./Feats.aspx-ID=5730.md)

---

Your breath causes lasting hindrance to those it harms. Creatures who fail their saves against your Breath of the Dragon gain one of the following conditions, based on the damage type of your breath: [clumsy](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=61.md) 1 ([electricity](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=586.md), [cold](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=555.md), [fire](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=604.md), or [sonic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=697.md) damage), [enfeebled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=71.md) 1 ([acid](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=524.md), physical, or [poison](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=669.md) damage), or [stupefied](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=94.md) 1 ([force](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=610.md), [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md), [spirit](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=737.md), [vitality](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=509.md), or [void](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=510.md) damage). The condition lasts until the end of your next turn.

## Traits

Dragonblood: A creature with this trait has the dragonblood versatile heritage. These creatures are born of draconic lineage and often have draconic appearances.
