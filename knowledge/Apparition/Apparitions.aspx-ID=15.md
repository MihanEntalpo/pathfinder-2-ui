# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Speaker in Sibilance](./Apparitions.aspx-ID=15.md)

Source [Pathfinder #216: The Acropolis Pyre pg. 83](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=271.md)
Whether witnessed in hissing serpents or the whisper of pungent fumes wafting up from a forlorn cave, a speaker in sibilance offers forbidden knowledge and revels in the anguish of an unprepared mind. These apparitions often take medusa-like forms, each serpent providing different (and often contradictory) advice.
