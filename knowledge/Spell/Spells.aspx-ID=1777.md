# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Symphony of the Unfettered Heart [two-actions] Focus 5

Uncommon [Bard](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=546.md) Composition [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 371](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Range 30 feet; Targets you or 1 ally

---

Your symphony lifts listeners from their worldly concerns. Attempt a Performance check to counteract an effect applying one of the following conditions to the target: [grabbed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=77&Redirected=1.md), [immobilized](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=81&Redirected=1.md), paralyzed, [restrained](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=90&Redirected=1.md), slowed, or stunned. If you fail, you can't target the same effect on the target for 1 day. Use the condition's source to determine the counteract DC (for example, the Escape DC for grabbed).

---

Heightened (9th) You can target up to four creatures.
