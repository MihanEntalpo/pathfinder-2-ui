# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Warp Step [two-actions] Cantrip 1

[Cantrip](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=550.md) [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 107](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)

---

When you walk, the earth warps beneath your feet—your steps extend, distance contracts, and everything is just a little bit closer. You gain a +5-foot status bonus to your Speed until the end of your turn. You then Stride twice. You can use warp step to [Burrow](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2310.md), [Climb](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2374.md), [Fly](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2312.md), or [Swim](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2381.md) instead of Stride if you have the corresponding movement type.
