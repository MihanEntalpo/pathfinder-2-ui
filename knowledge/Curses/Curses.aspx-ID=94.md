# [Amnerian Amnesia](./Curses.aspx-ID=94.md)Curse 7

[Curse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=566.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md)
Source [Pathfinder #217: Death Sails a Wine-Dark Sea pg. 45](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=273.md)
The Iblydan underworld slowly saps inhabitants’ memories before shunting them to their final afterlife.

Saving Throw DC 24 Will; Effect Onset 1 day; Stage 1 [stupefied](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=94.md) 1 (1 day); Stage 2 [drained](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=68.md) 1 and stupefied 1 (1 day); Stage 3 drained 1 and stupefied 2 (1 week); Stage 4 drained 1, stupefied 2, and whenever the creature would fail a save against this curse, the value of their stupefied condition increases by 1 (1 month);

Special A creature who becomes stupefied 5 by this curse cannot recall anything from before they entered Amneros, and over the next week they meld with Amnaia’s landscape and are transported to [Pharasma’s](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=290.md) Court for judgment.
