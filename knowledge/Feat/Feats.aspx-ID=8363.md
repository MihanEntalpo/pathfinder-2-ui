Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 43](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

Prerequisites Exploit Vulnerability

---

You can find the one weak point in a creature's scales, wards, or armor to get past its resistances. When you succeed at Exploit Vulnerability, you learn about the highest of the creature's resistances that can be bypassed (for example, if the creature has resistance to physical damage except silver), if the creature has one. If you prefer, you can choose the following benefit instead of one of the usual two benefits from Exploit Vulnerability%END>.TraitsThaumaturge: This indicates abilities from the thaumaturge class.
