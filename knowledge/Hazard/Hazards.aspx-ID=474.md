# [Hidden Pit](./Hazards.aspx-ID=474.md)Hazard 0

Mechanical Trap
Source [GM Core pg. 102](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 18 (or 0 if the trapdoor is disabled or [broken](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=60.md))
Description A wooden trapdoor covers a pit that’s 10 feet square and 20 feet deep.

---

Disable DC 12 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) to remove the trapdoor
AC 10, Fort +1, Ref +1
Trapdoor Hardness 3, Trapdoor HP 12 (BT 6); Immunities critical hits, object immunities, precision damage
Pitfall [reaction] Trigger A creature walks onto the trapdoor; Effect The triggering creature falls in and takes falling damage (typically 10 bludgeoning damage). That creature can use the [Grab an Edge](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2313.md) reaction to avoid falling.

---

Reset Creatures can still fall into the trap, but the trapdoor must be reset manually for the trap to become hidden again.
