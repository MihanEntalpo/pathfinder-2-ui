# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Dancing ScarfItem 3+

[Invested](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=637.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Visual](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=727.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 288](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage worn belt; Bulk &mdash;

---

This long and billowing scarf is typically woven of silk or sheer fabric and adorned with bells or other jangling bits of shiny metal. It grants a +1 item bonus to Performance checks to dance.

Activate—Swirling Scarf [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)); Requirements On your most recent action, you succeeded at a Performance check to dance; Effect You become concealed until the beginning of your next turn.
