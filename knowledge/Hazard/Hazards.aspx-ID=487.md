# [Confounding Betrayal](./Hazards.aspx-ID=487.md)Hazard 8

[Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) Haunt
Source [GM Core pg. 105](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth +21 (expert)
Description Allies appear to shed their disguises and reveal themselves to be malevolent monsters.

---

Disable DC 28 [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38.md) (expert) twice to confound the haunt with your own deceptions, or DC 28 [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md) (trained) twice to create a ward against the haunt’s mental influence
Unmask [reaction] ([illusion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=629.md), [occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md)) Trigger Two or more creatures enter the haunt’s area; Effect Each creature sees the forms of nearby creatures shift and change, appearing to transform into fiendish or aberrant beings with a thirst for blood. Each creature in the area must attempt a DC 30 Will save. The haunt then rolls initiative.

Critical Success The creature sees through the illusions entirely and is temporarily immune to the haunt’s routine for 1 minute.
Success The creature is unaffected by the strange images.
Failure The creature believes the illusions to be true; if they become [confused](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=63.md) by the haunt’s routine, they can’t attempt flat checks to end the confused condition when they take damage.
Critical Failure As failure, but the creature is left with a lingering suspicion of others and can’t benefit from Aid reactions for 24 hours.

---

Routine (1 action; [illusion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=629.md), [incapacitation](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=631.md), [occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md)) The haunt continues to confound victims’ senses and inspire them to commit violence against each other. Each creature in the haunt’s area must attempt a DC 26 Will save.
Critical Success The creature is unaffected and temporarily immune for 1 minute.
Success The creature is unaffected.
Failure The creature is [confused](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=63.md) for 1 round.
Critical Failure The creature is confused for 1 minute.

---

Reset The haunt deactivates 1 minute after all creatures leave the area but resets immediately thereafter
