## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Uncommon Consumable Magical Potion
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 299](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 160 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Activate [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md))

---

Vengeful demon’s tears is an infamously noxious spirit mixed into a potent magical cocktail and traditionally packaged in a gourd adorned with an engraving of a terrifying demonic face. Drinking it feels akin to lighting your throat on fire and putting it out with an herbal tonic. When you activate this potion, you gain the [quickened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=89.md) condition for 3 rounds and can use the extra action each round to Strike, [Stride](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2305.md), or to take the following action.

Activate—Release the Demon [one-action] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [healing](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=623.md)); Effect You focus on the sensation of life burning within your flesh and gain 2d8 temporary Hit Points for 1 round.
