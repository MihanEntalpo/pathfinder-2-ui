# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Sleep [two-actions] Spell 1

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Incapacitation [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Mental Sleep
Source [Player Core pg. 357](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Deities Atropos, Bes, Charg, [Desna](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=281.md), Kagia, Kitumu, Luhar, Radiant Prism, Rhan-Tegoth, Thremyr, Vavaalrav, Vudravati, Zugero
Lesson [lesson of dreams](../Lesson/Lessons.aspx-ID=16.md)
Patron Theme paradox of opposites
Range 30 feet; Area 5-foot burst
Defense Will

---

Each creature in the area becomes drowsy, possibly nodding off. A creature that falls unconscious from this spell doesn't fall [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88&Redirected=1.md) or release what it's holding. This spell doesn't prevent creatures from waking up due to a successful Perception check, limiting its utility in combat.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
Success The creature takes a –1 status penalty to Perception checks for 1 round.
Failure The creature falls unconscious. If it's still unconscious after 1 minute, it wakes up automatically.
Critical Failure The creature falls unconscious. If it's still unconscious after 1 hour, it wakes up automatically.

---

Heightened (4th) The creatures fall unconscious for 1 round on a failure or 1 minute on a critical failure. They fall [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88&Redirected=1.md) and release what they're holding, and they can't attempt Perception checks to wake up. When the duration ends, the creature is sleeping normally instead of automatically waking up.
