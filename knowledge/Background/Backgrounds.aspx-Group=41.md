# Revenge of the Runelords Backgrounds

The rare backgrounds presented here are available to all PCs in the Revenge of the Runelords Adventure Path. Note that while some of these rare backgrounds grant unique abilities, those that do so do not include a bonus skill feat—in effect, the unique ability replaces that benefit of a common background.
