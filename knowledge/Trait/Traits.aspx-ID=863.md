# [Gigas](./Traits.aspx-ID=863.md)

Source [Pathfinder #217: Death Sails a Wine-Dark Sea pg. 43](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=273.md)
One of the first creations of the titans. The few existing stories of their origin suggest they were created from the blood of a titan who was injured in a mighty battle. Amnerion then carved their own domain from the caves beneath Iblydos and guards it jealously.
