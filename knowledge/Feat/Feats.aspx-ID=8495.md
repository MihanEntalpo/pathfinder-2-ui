# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Steal Time](./Feats.aspx-ID=8495.md) [two-actions] Feat 10

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 186](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Archetype [Chronoskimmer](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=349.md)
Frequency once per hour
Prerequisites [Chronoskimmer Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8489.md)

---

You reach into another creature's timeline and attempt to alter their flow of time. Select a creature within 30 feet. That creature attempts a Fortitude saving throw against your chronoskimmer DC. The creature takes the effects of [slow](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1677.md) based on the result of its saving throw.
