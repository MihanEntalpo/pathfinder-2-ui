# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Energized Font](./Feats.aspx-ID=4430.md) [one-action] Feat 5

Gnome
Source [Player Core pg. 52](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Frequency once per day
Prerequisites focus pool, at least one innate spell from a gnome heritage or ancestry feat that shares a tradition with at least one of your focus spells

---

You can focus your mind more quickly thanks to the magic that flows within you. You regain 1 Focus Point, up to your usual maximum.

## Traits

Gnome: A creature with this trait is a member of the gnome ancestry. Gnomes are small people skilled at magic who seek out new experiences and usually have low-light vision. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by gnomes. A weapon with this trait is created and used by gnomes.
