Even in death, a dragon’s gaze is terrible to behold. When used to cast [breathe fire](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1457.md) at 3rd rank or higher, an eye from a dragon causes the spell to manifest some of the creature’s fearsome aura and inspire terror. Creatures in the spell’s area must attempt a [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Will save instead of a basic Reflex save, and in addition to taking the spell’s normal damage on a failure, they’re [frightened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=76.md) 1. This adds the [emotion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=590.md), [fear](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=598.md), and [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) traits to the spell.
