# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Intimidating Strike](./Feats.aspx-ID=4782.md) [two-actions] Feat 2

[Barbarian](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=813.md) Emotion Fear [Fighter](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=601.md) Mental
Source [Player Core pg. 141](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md), [Player Core 2 pg. 77](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetypes [Battle Harbinger](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=304.md)*, [Blackjacket](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=316.md)*
* This archetype offers Intimidating Strike at a different level than displayed here.

---

Your blow not only wounds creatures but also shatters their confidence. Make a melee Strike. If you hit and deal damage, the target is frightened 1, or frightened 2 on a critical hit.

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more archetypes in addition to the listed classes. When selected this way, the feat does not have its class traits.

## Traits

Emotion: This effect alters a creature’s emotions. Effects with this trait always have the mental trait as well. Creatures with special training or that have mechanical or artificial intelligence are immune to emotion effects.

Fear: Fear effects evoke the emotion of fear. Effects with this trait always have the mental and emotion traits as well.

Mental: A mental effect can alter the target’s mind. It has no effect on an object or a mindless creature.
