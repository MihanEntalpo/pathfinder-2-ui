# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Draconic Paragon](./Feats.aspx-ID=8074.md) Feat 9

Kobold
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 215](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

Prerequisites [Benefactor's Strike](./Feats.aspx-ID=8069.md); [Dracomancer](./Feats.aspx-ID=8073.md), or [Kobold Breath](./Feats.aspx-ID=8068.md)

---

Your connection to your draconic benefactor becomes especially pronounced. You gain additional effects for the prerequisite kobold feat. If you have more than one of the prerequisite feats, you gain the appropriate additional effects.

- Benefactor’s Strike Your unarmed strikes made with your [jaws, claws, or tail](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=356.md) gain the [deadly d6](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=570.md) trait.
- Dracomancer Increase the number of times per day you can cast each of the granted 1st and 2nd-rank innate spells by 1.
- Kobold Breath Creatures that critically fail their save against your Kobold Breath take 3d4 [persistent damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86.md) of the type dealt by your draconic benefactor.

## Traits

Kobold: A creature with this trait is a member of the [kobold](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=80.md) ancestry, reptilian humanoids who are usually Small and typically have [darkvision](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2410.md). An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by kobolds.
