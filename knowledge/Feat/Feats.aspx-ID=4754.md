# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Reactive Transformation](./Feats.aspx-ID=4754.md) [reaction] Feat 14

[Druid](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=583.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 134](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites [Untamed Form](./Feats.aspx-ID=4713.md); [Dragon Shape](./Feats.aspx-ID=4749.md), [Elemental Shape](./Feats.aspx-ID=4741.md), [Plant Shape](./Feats.aspx-ID=4744.md), or [Soaring Shape](./Feats.aspx-ID=4739.md)
Trigger varies

---

You transform reflexively when in danger. You cast [untamed form](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1861.md) to transform into one shape granted by one of the prerequisite feats you have, depending on the trigger. Your shape’s resistances and weaknesses apply against the triggering damage.

- Trigger You fall 10 feet or more; Effect Choose a shape from [aerial form](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1437.md).
- Trigger You take bludgeoning or poison damage; Effect Choose a primal dragon shape from [dragon form](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1502.md) that resists the triggering damage.
- Trigger You take fire damage; Effect Choose a fire elemental shape from [elemental form](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1510.md).
- Trigger You take poison damage; Effect Choose a shape from [plant form](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1637.md).
