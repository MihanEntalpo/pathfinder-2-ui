# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Flicker [two-actions] Spell 4

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Teleportation
Source [Player Core pg. 332](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Deities Black Butterfly, Guardians of the Sacred Self, [Nethys](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=288.md), Ng, Phi Deva
Duration 1 minute

---

You flicker quickly between your current plane and another. You gain resistance 5 to all damage, except force. At the end of each of your turns, you automatically teleport 10 feet in a random direction, as determined by the GM. You can Sustain the spell to teleport in this way.

---

Heightened (+2) The resistance increases by 3.
