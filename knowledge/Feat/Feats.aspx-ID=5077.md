# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Advanced Wilding](./Feats.aspx-ID=5077.md) Feat 6

Archetype
Source [Player Core pg. 218](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetype [Druid](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=213.md)
Class [Druid](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=34.md)
Prerequisites [Basic Wilding](./Feats.aspx-ID=5075.md)

---

You gain one druid feat. For the purpose of meeting its prerequisites, your druid level is equal to half your character level.

Special You can select this feat more than once. Each time you select it, you gain another druid feat.

## Traits

Archetype: This feat belongs to an archetype.
