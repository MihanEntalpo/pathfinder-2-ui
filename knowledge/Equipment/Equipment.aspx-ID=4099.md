## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Unique Magical
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 305](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 675 gp
Usage held in 2 hands; Bulk &mdash;

---

This net was woven by the jorogumo Injigo, who presented it as a gift to her [gnome](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=61.md) sweetheart, Mara, to keep her safe in their mountain cottage whenever Injigo left home to go on hunting expeditions.

Injigo’s Loving Embrace functions as a typical [net](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2738.md). You gain a +1 item bonus to [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) checks to [Grapple](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2376.md) with the net.

Activate—Ensnare Ghost [two-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect You spin the net in the direction of an [incorporeal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=632.md) creature within 30 feet that you can see. Lines of silken force whip out to lash around the incorporeal creature, who must succeed at a DC 25 Reflex save or become [immobilized](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=81.md) until the end of your next turn. You can [Sustain](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2317.md) this activation for up to 1 minute, and the creature must succeed at a DC 25 check to [Escape](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2296.md) the net.

Activate—A Night of Melancholic Dreams 10 minutes ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)); Frequency once per week; Effect You wrap the net around you like a blanket as you prepare to sleep. As long as you get 8 hours of sleep or analogous rest, you dream of writing love letters to someone you adore: this could be an actual paramour, someone you’re interested in, or someone unknown whose identity you don’t recall after waking. When you wake, you’re filled with a sense of melancholia about those unsent letters, but these feelings distract you from other, more unpleasant thoughts, granting you [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) resistance 5 and a +1 item bonus to saving throws against mental effects for the next 8 hours.
