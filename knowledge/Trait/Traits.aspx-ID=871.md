# [Soulrider](./Traits.aspx-ID=871.md)

Source [Monster Core 2 pg. 365](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=276.md)
Soulriders are nigh-parasitic [aberrations](./Traits.aspx-ID=523.md) that latch on to souls as they move through the Great Beyond. They sometimes take on the traits of planes.
