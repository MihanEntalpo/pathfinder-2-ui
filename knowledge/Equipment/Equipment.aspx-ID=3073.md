# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Crown of WitchcraftItem 10+

[Focused](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=609.md) [Invested](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=637.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 288](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage worn headwear; Bulk &mdash;

---

An elegant accoutrement for a witch who has come into the higher echelons of power, a crown of witchcraft typically looks like a garland of flowering twigs, a jeweled circlet, or a tall hat of fine fabric. You gain a +1 item bonus to Intimidation checks, and if you're a witch, you gain a +2 item bonus to your patron skill.
If you have a familiar, you can attach a small portion of the crown's material to your familiar, such as a strip of fabric from a hat tied around its tail or a sprig of natural material linked to its collar; the familiar doesn't need to invest the item itself. If you do this, your familiar gains the tough pet ability as long as the crown is invested by you.

Activate—Defiant Hex [free-action] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect Gain 1 Focus Point, which you can spend only to cast a witch hex spell. If you don't spend this point by the end of this turn, it is lost.

Craft Requirements You are a witch.
