# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Become Thought](./Feats.aspx-ID=8348.md) Feat 20

[Psychic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=894.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 29](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

---

You shed some of your material form, becoming a being of pure thought. This has the following effects.

- You gain resistance 10 to physical damage but weakness 5 to mental damage.
- You can change your appearance on a whim. This takes a single action, which has the [concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) trait, has the effects of [illusory disguise](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1568.md), and lasts until you change your appearance again.
- Once per year, when you die, you automatically return to life the next day as long as one living sentient creature on your plane remembers you; the memories enable you to reconstruct yourself from their thoughts. You appear in the vicinity of the creature that remembers you most strongly.

## Traits

Psychic: This indicates abilities from the [psychic](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=68.md) class.
