# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Fey Dragonet](./Heritages.aspx-ID=422.md)

Source [Draconic Codex pg. 210](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
You have a brightly colored body with gleaming scales that become more lustrous as you age, and iridescent butterfly wings. You can change the coloration and pattern of your wings with a single action to let you blend into an area. When your wings match your environment, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48.md) checks until your surroundings shift in coloration or pattern. You gain the [fey](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=599.md) trait and add [Fey](../Language/Languages.aspx-ID=119.md) to your list of known languages.
