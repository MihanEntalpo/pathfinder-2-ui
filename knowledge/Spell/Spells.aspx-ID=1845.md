Domain [tyranny](../Domain/Domains.aspx-ID=96.md)
Requirements Your most recent action dealt damage to a target
Range 100 feet; Targets a creature you dealt damage to on your most recent action
Defense Will; Duration until the end of the target's next turn

---

With the threat of more pain, you compel a creature you've recently harmed. You issue a command to the target, with the effects of the spell [command](./Spells.aspx-ID=1470.md). If the target is frightened, stupefied, or taking [persistent damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86&Redirected=1.md), it takes a –2 circumstance penalty to the save. Regardless of the result, the target is then temporarily immune for 1 hour.
