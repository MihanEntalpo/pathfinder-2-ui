# [Dreams of Vengeance](./Backgrounds.aspx-ID=575.md)Background

Rare
Source [Gatewalkers Player's Guide (Remastered) pg. 6](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=287.md)
You’ve known strife your whole life, whether on the battlefield, in the arena, or simply living your day-to-day life. When you’re not thinking about your next brawl, you’re turning over memories of traitorous rivals and troublesome betrayers who’ve wronged you in the past. The sight of your enemies broken and maimed, then, greatly appealed to you when you glimpsed it through a mysterious glowing archway during the Missing Moment. But when you stepped through the gate, you found yourself back where you started, with no indications of your exacted revenge anywhere in sight. You were nearly consumed by frustrated rage, realizing only afterward the magnitude of the new power you wielded. Fiery rays of energy you can shoot from your eyes or the ability to swat away your foes with the flick of your wrist— these, no doubt, would come in handy.

Your deviant abilities are of either the dragon or the troll classification. If you choose dragon, you gain the [Blasting Beams](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8026.md) deviant feat with the fire type. If you choose troll, you gain the [Titan Swing](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8039.md) deviant feat. As a quirk, your pupils are shaped like curved arches. When you use a deviant ability, the centers of your pupils shine with light, like the glowing gateways of the Missing Moment.

Choose two attribute boosts. One must be to Strength or Constitution, and one is a free attribute boost.

You're trained in the [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) skill, and the Gladiator Lore skill. You gain the [Titan Wrestler](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=5226.md) skill feat.
