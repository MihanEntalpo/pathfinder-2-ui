# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Pearl Dragonet](./Heritages.aspx-ID=425.md)

Source [Draconic Codex pg. 210](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
You have pearlescent scales and a round body, with short legs, small wings, and a long tail. The shape of your body lets you absorb a blow and roll away.

Bounce Away [reaction] Frequency once per 10 minutes; Trigger A melee Strike hits you and deals bludgeoning damage; Effect You gain [resistance](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2312.md) equal to your level to bludgeoning damage from the triggering Strike, then [Stride](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2305.md) up to half your Speed. You can [Fly](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2312.md) instead of Striding if you have a fly Speed.
