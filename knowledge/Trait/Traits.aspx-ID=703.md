# [Strange Gravity](./Traits.aspx-ID=703.md)

Source [GM Core pg. 172](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
All bodies of mass are centers of gravity with roughly the same force. A creature can stand on any solid objects that’s as large as or larger than themself.
