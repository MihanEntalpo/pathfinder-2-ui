# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Mist Child](./Feats.aspx-ID=4538.md) Feat 5

Changeling
Source [Player Core pg. 77](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

You blend in with unnatural subtlety. When you are concealed or [hidden](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=79&Redirected=1.md), increase the DC of the [flat check](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=333.md) to target you to 6 if you’re concealed or 12 if you’re hidden.

## Traits

Changeling: A creature with this trait has the changeling versatile heritage. Changelings are the children of hags and members of other humanoid ancestries. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by changelings.
