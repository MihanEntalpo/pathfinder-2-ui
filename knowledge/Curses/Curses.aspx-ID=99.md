# [Unraveling Skin](./Curses.aspx-ID=99.md)Curse 5

[Rare](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=683.md) [Curse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=566.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 162](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Often used to protect ancient tomes, this curse causes your skin to shed like weathered paper, cracking and bleeding at the slightest touch.

Saving Throw DC 20 Fortitude; Effect Seeping wounds cover your body as this curse makes your skin brittle. Any time you would gain the [wounded](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=99.md) condition, increase the value by 1. Any time you take bleed damage, increase the amount of damage by 1.

---

Tempted Curse Your skin falls from your body and exposes your raw flesh beneath, but the curse holds your flesh together as its magic increases. You gain resistance [to persistent bleed damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86&Redirected=1.md) equal to half your level and are [doomed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=67.md) 1.
