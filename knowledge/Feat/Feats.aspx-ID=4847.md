# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Whirlwind Strike](./Feats.aspx-ID=4847.md) [three-actions] Feat 14

[Barbarian](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=813.md) [Fighter](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=601.md) Flourish
Source [Player Core pg. 150](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md), [Player Core 2 pg. 84](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetype [Wylderheart](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=318.md)*
* This archetype offers Whirlwind Strike at a different level than displayed here.

---

You lash out in a blur of motion, attacking all nearby adversaries. Make a melee Strike against each enemy within your melee reach. Each attack counts toward your multiple attack penalty, but you do not increase your penalty until you have made all your attacks.

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more archetypes in addition to the listed classes. When selected this way, the feat does not have its class traits.

## Traits

Flourish: Actions with the flourish trait are special techniques that require too much exertion for you to perform frequently. You can use only one action with the flourish trait per round.
