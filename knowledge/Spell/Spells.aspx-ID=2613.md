# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Temporal Distortion [one-action] Focus 1

[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 189](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md), [Divine Mysteries pg. 287](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=234.md)
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature
Defense Will

---

You share some of your own temporal distortions with a foe, altering their mind and body unpredictably as they're thrown backward or forward in time. Roll 1d4. On a 1, the foe becomes [clumsy](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=61.md); on a 2, it becomes [enfeebled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=71.md); on a 3, it becomes [stupefied](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=94.md); and on a 4, you choose which condition applies.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.
Success The target is either clumsy 1, enfeebled 1, or stupefied 1 for 1 round, depending on the result of the d4.
Failure The target is either clumsy 1, enfeebled 1, or stupefied 1 for 4 rounds, depending on the result of the d4.
Critical Failure As failure, but the time warp is stronger, increasing the condition’s effects but making it run its course faster. The condition’s value is 3, and the condition lasts for 2 rounds.
