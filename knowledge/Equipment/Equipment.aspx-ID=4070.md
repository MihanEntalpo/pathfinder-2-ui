## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Uncommon Consumable Divine Magical [Vitality](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=509.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 298](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 250 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Activate [two-actions] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md))

---

These roasted soybeans are blessed by a priest to ward against all various forms of otherworldly influence, but are most effective against [incorporeal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=632.md) evils, such as wraiths or path maidens. They can be safely eaten by living creatures and taste delicious when paired with rice wine. When you activate this item, choose an adjacent square to exhale into. You fill that square with soothing vapors that harm any [fiend](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=600.md), [fey](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=599.md), [ghost](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=753.md), [spirit](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=737.md), or [undead](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=722.md) creature that ends their turn in that square for the next minute, causing them to take 4d8+4 spirit damage (DC 28 [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Will save). An incorporeal creature that takes this damage must also attempt a DC 28 Fortitude save to resist being affected as follows.
