## This Item may contain spoilers from the Myth-Speakers Adventure Path

Rare Alchemical Consumable Injury [Mythic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=835.md) Poison
Source [Pathfinder #218: Titanbane pg. 38](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=274.md)
Price 120 gp
Usage held in 1 hands; Bulk L
Activate [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md))

---

Crafted from nightshade plants that grow near sites of [mythic power](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=3320.md), this poison wreaks havoc on those with a [mythic calling](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=3330.md).

Saving Throw DC 28 Fortitude; Onset 1 round; Maximum Duration 5 rounds; Stage 1 3d6 poison damage and lose 1 [Mythic Point](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=3323.md) (1 round); Stage 2 4d6 poison and [stupefied](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=94.md) 1 (1 round); Stage 3 6d6 poison damage, stupefied 2, and lose all Mythic Points (1 round)
