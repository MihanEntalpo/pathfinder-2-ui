Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 49](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Archetype [Thaumaturge](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=343.md)
Class [Thaumaturge](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=69.md)
Prerequisites [Thaumaturge Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8398.md); trained in at least one of [Arcana](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=35.md), [Nature](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=43.md), [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md), or [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md) and expert in at least one other

---

Increase your proficiency rank in one of Arcana, Nature, Occultism, or Religion from expert to master and in another from trained to expert. You gain a skill feat associated with each of the skills you chose.

Archetype: This feat belongs to an archetype.
