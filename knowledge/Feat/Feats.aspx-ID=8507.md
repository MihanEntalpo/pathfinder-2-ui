# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Just the Tool](./Feats.aspx-ID=8507.md) [one-action] Feat 4

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 205](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Archetype [Mind Smith](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=351.md)
Prerequisites [Mind Smith Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8504.md)

---

You temporarily change your weapon's shape to assist you in the field. You morph your weapon into a single simple [tool](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2759.md), such as a shovel or [crowbar](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2718.md), to help with a mundane task. You can't replicate entire toolkits with this ability. You can use this action again to change your mind weapon back to a weapon.
