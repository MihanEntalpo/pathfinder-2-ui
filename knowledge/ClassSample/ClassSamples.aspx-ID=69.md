# [Ruffian](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=69.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 174](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
You rely on brute force and underhanded deeds to get the job done.

Prioritize Dexterity, plus Strength to deal extra damage. Charisma helps you intimidate others.
