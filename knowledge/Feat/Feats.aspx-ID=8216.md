## This Feat may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Rare [Reincarnated](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=903.md) [Universal Ancestry](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=467.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 321](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)

---

It’s one thing to remember your previous lives. It’s quite another to remember how you died in each of those lives. You can recall dozens, if not more, of excruciating, humiliating, and devastating ends you’ve suffered over the eons, and you can inflict upon your enemies a vision of these pains. You gain [phantasmagoria](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1629.md) as an occult innate spell that you can cast once per day. When you gain this feat, choose any two of the following damage types: [acid](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=524.md), bludgeoning, [cold](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=555.md), [electricity](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=586.md), [fire](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=604.md), piercing, [poison](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=669.md), slashing, or [sonic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=697.md). These damage types caused your deaths more often than not in your previous lives, and whenever a creature takes damage from a phantasmagoria spell you cast, you can swap the [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) damage that spell normally causes for one of the two damage types you chose.

## Traits

Rare: This rarity indicates that a rules element is very difficult to find in the game world. A rare feat, spell, item or the like is available to players only if the GM decides to include it in the game, typically through discovery during play. Creatures with this trait are rare. They typically can't be summoned. The DC of Recall Knowledge checks related to these creatures is increased by 5.

Reincarnated: These rare ancestry feats are available to any character who has reincarnated at least once either as part of their backstory or because of a reincarnation that took place during play.

Universal Ancestry: A feat with this trait is available to all ancestries.
