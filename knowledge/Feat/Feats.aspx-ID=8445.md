# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Malleable Form](./Feats.aspx-ID=8445.md) [one-action] Feat 13

[Reflection](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=902.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 121](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

Prerequisites ability to cast at least one [polymorph](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=670.md) spell
Requirements You're [polymorphed](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=670.md) by a spell that has multiple choices of battle form.

---

You adroitly shift your form, taking on another appearance. Choose a different battle form of the [polymorph](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=670.md) spell affecting you, and change to that form instead of the current one. This destabilizes the spell's magic, reducing its duration by 1 round. Use the rank of the spell to determine the statistics, as normal.

## Traits

Reflection: A creature with this trait has the [reflection](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=97.md) versatile heritage. Reflections are beings who are copies of living people, whether through cloning, planar duplication, or another mechanism. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by reflections.
