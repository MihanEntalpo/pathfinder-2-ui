# [Shattered Earth](./Relics.aspx-ID=150.md)Minor Gift

[Earth](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=585.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 313](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Aspect earth

---

Activate [two-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md), [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)); Frequency once per hour; Effect Seismic pulses from your relic ripple through the nearby ground, shaking sharp fragments to the surface. A 10-foot-radius patch of stone or earth within 60 feet is transformed into [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366&Redirected=1.md). A creature that enters an affected square during a move action must succeed at an [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34&Redirected=1.md) check or Reflex save or take 1d6 piercing damage; it needs to roll only once per [move](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=658&Redirected=1.md) action even if it moves through several squares. At 6th level and every 4 levels thereafter, increase the damage by 1d6.
