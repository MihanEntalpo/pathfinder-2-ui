# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[All in your Head](./Feats.aspx-ID=8346.md) [reaction] Feat 18

[Psyche](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=901.md) [Psychic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=894.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 29](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

Trigger You’re about to attempt a Fortitude save or a Reflex save.

---

Flames scouring at your body, venom coursing through your veins—you refuse to believe they exist, and nothing can affect you if you don't believe in it. You attempt a Will save against the triggering effect, instead of a Reflex or Fortitude save. You don't gain the benefits of fortress of will or walls of will on this save.

## Traits

Psyche: Your psyche must be unleashed to use abilities that have the psyche trait, and they end automatically when your unleashed psyche subsides.

Psychic: This indicates abilities from the [psychic](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=68.md) class.
