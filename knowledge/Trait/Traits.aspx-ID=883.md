# [Sahkil](./Traits.aspx-ID=883.md)

Source [Monster Core 2 pg. 365](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=276.md)
Sahkils are fiends that delight in spreading fear and unease among mortal creatures. They typically have [darkvision](../MonsterAbility/MonsterAbilities.aspx-ID=59.md), immunity to [fear](./Traits.aspx-ID=598.md), and weakness to [holy](./Traits.aspx-ID=517.md).
