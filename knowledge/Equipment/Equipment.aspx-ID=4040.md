## This Item may contain spoilers from the Gatewalkers Adventure Path

Uncommon Consumable Magical Talisman
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 237](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Price 175 gp
Usage affix to armor; Bulk &mdash;
Activate [free-action] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md)); Trigger You attempt a Will save against a [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) spell, but you haven’t rolled yet; Requirements You have master proficiency in Will saves.

---

Found throughout Golarion, these star-shaped talismans of whalebone scrimshaw are carved by the Erutaki people from the Crown of the World. They are popular with adherents to the cult of [Desna](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=281.md), who believe the talismans protect their dreams.

When you activate this talisman, you gain a +2 status bonus to saves against spells with the [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) trait for 1 minute. On the triggering save, if the outcome of the roll is a failure, you get a success instead, or if the outcome is a critical failure, you get a failure instead.
