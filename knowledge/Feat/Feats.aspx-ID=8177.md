# [Timelessness](./Feats.aspx-ID=8177.md) Feat 20

[Mythic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=835.md)
Source [Pathfinder Adventure Path #219: Lord of the Trinity Star pg. 83](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=283.md)
Archetype [Timewracked](../MythicDestiny/MythicDestinies.aspx-ID=340.md)
Prerequisites [Timewracked Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8166.md)

---

Time travelers are rare, but even among them your isolation from time is unique. As long as you remain timewracked, you do not age and cannot die of natural causes. You’re immune to hostile spells and effects related to time or aging, as well as the [slowed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=92.md) condition (except for when you gain the slowed condition through this mythic destiny). You reduce the DC of recovery checks by 1. Whenever you roll a flat check to end persistent damage, you roll twice and choose your desired result; this is a [fortune](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=612.md) effect. In addition, your intimate knowledge of the flow of time gives you a measure of influence over the attempt of another to manipulate it. Whenever an enemy creature that you are aware of and who is within 60 feet of you gains the [quickened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=89.md) condition, you gain the quickened condition for 1 round. The actions available to you from the acquired condition are the same as those granted by the ability that quickens your enemy.

## Traits

Mythic: Options with this trait grant or utilize mythic power. Feats with the mythic trait can only be taken by mythic characters, who are typically characters with a mythic Calling.

Monsters with the mythic trait have access to a pool of Mythic Points and are particularly strong for creatures of their level. Many mythic monsters are either resistant or entirely immune to attacks from non-mythic creatures and weapons.

Spells with the mythic trait require the expenditure of a Mythic Point in order to be cast, and items with the mythic trait require the expenditure of a Mythic Point in order to use their activated abilities.

Weapons with the mythic trait overcome the resistances and immunities of mythic monsters.
