# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Veil of Dreams [one-action] Focus 1

Uncommon [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md) Hex [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Mental [Witch](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=734.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 386](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Lesson [lesson of dreams](../Lesson/Lessons.aspx-ID=16.md)
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature
Defense Will; Duration sustained up to 1 minute

---

Your patron draws the target into a drowsy state, causing daydreams and sluggishness. The target must attempt a Will save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.
Success The target takes a –1 status penalty to Perception, attack rolls, and Will saves. This penalty increases to –2 for Will saves against sleep effects.
Failure As success, and any time the target uses a [concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) action, it must succeed at a DC 5 flat check or the action is disrupted.
