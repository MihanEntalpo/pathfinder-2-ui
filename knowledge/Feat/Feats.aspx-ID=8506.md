You alter your weapon's phase so it can more easily strike incorporeal creatures. Your mind weapon gains the effects of a [ghost touch](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2840.md) property rune for 1 minute.

## Traits

Archetype: This feat belongs to an archetype.

Concentrate: An action with this trait requires a degree of mental concentration and discipline.
