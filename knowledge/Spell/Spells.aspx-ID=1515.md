# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Entangling Flora [two-actions] Spell 2

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Plant [Wood](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=508.md)

Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Catalysts Bougainvillea Blossom, Wemmuth Trinket
Deities [Green Man](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=666.md), Immaculate Growth, Kzininn, Nhimbaloth, Sicva
Range 120 feet; Area all squares in a 20-foot burst
Duration 1 minute

---

Plants and fungi burst out or quickly grow, entangling creatures. All surfaces in the area are [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366&Redirected=1.md). Each round that a creature starts its turn in the area, it must attempt a Reflex save. On a failure, it takes a –10-foot circumstance penalty to its Speeds until it leaves the area, and on a critical failure, it's also [immobilized](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=81&Redirected=1.md) for 1 round. Creatures can attempt to Escape to remove these effects.
