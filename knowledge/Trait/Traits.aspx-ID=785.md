# [Venomous](./Traits.aspx-ID=785.md)

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 119](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
These weapons inject poison into every hit. When you hit a creature with this weapon, it deals an additional 1 [persistent poison damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86.md). This increases to 2 persistent poison damage if the weapon has a [greater striking rune](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2829.md).
