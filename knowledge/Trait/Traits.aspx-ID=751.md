# [Duskwalker](./Traits.aspx-ID=751.md)

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 316](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
A creature with this trait has the duskwalker versatile heritage. Duskwalkers are planar scions infused with the supernatural energy of psychopomps.
