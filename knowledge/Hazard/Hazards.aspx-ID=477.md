# [Poisoned Lock](./Hazards.aspx-ID=477.md)Hazard 1

Mechanical Trap
Source [GM Core pg. 103](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 17 (trained)
Description A spring-loaded, poisoned spine is hidden near the keyhole of a lock. Disabling or breaking the trap does not disable or break the lock.

---

Disable DC 17 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) (trained) on the spring mechanism
AC 15, Fort +8, Ref +4
Hardness 6, HP 24 (BT 12); Immunities Critical hits, object immunities, precision damage
Spring [reaction] Trigger A creature tries to unlock or [Pick the Lock](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2412.md); Effect A spine extends to [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) the triggering creature.

Melee spine +13, Damage 1 piercing plus cladis poison

Cladis Poison ([poison](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=669.md)) Saving Throw DC 19 Fortitude; Maximum Duration 4 hours; Stage 1 1d6 poison damage and [drained](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=68.md) 1 (1 hour); Stage 2 2d6 poison damage and drained 2 (1 hour); Stage 3 3d6 poison damage and drained 2 (1 hour)
