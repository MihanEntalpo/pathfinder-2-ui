# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Vicious Swing](./Feats.aspx-ID=4775.md) [two-actions] Feat 1

[Fighter](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=601.md) Flourish
Source [Player Core pg. 141](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetypes [Battle Harbinger](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=304.md)*, [Mauler](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=256.md)*
* This archetype offers Vicious Swing at a different level than displayed here.

---

You unleash a particularly powerful attack that clobbers your foe but leaves you a bit unsteady. Make a melee Strike. This counts as two attacks when calculating your multiple attack penalty. If this Strike hits, you deal an extra die of weapon damage. If you’re at least 10th level, increase this to two extra dice, and if you’re at least 18th level, increase it to three extra dice.

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more archetypes in addition to the listed classes. When selected this way, the feat does not have its class traits.

## Vicious Swing Leads To...

## Traits

Flourish: Actions with the flourish trait are special techniques that require too much exertion for you to perform frequently. You can use only one action with the flourish trait per round.
