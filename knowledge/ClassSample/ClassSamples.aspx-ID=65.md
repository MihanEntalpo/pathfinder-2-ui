# [Archer](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=65.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 148](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
You take out your opponents from a distance with ranged weapons, and you excel at dispatching flying or other hard-to-reach enemies.

## Ability Scores

Prioritize Dexterity. Constitution and Wisdom helps with survivability, and Strength adds damage with propulsive weapons.

## Skills

[Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md), [Medicine](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=42.md), [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48.md), [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md)

## Starting Feat

[Point Blank Stance](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=4771.md)

## Higher-Level Feats
