# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Phantasmagoria [two-actions] Spell 9

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Death Illusion [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Mental
Source [Player Core pg. 349](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Bloodline hag
Deities Charon, Nyarlathotep (The Crawling Chaos), Nyarlathotep (The Faceless Sphinx), Nyarlathotep (The Veiled Voice)
Range 120 feet; Targets any number of creatures
Defense Will

---

You fill the targets' minds with endless images, like countless dreams and lives colliding with each other. The onrushing information deals 16d6 mental damage to each target, depending on its Will save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.
Success The target takes half damage and can't use reactions until the start of your next turn.
Failure The target takes full damage and is confused until the start of your next turn.
Critical Failure The target takes double damage and is confused for 1 minute.
