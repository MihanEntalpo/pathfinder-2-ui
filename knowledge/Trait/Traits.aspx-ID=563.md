# [Construct](./Traits.aspx-ID=563.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 454](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
A construct is an artificial creature empowered by a force other than vitality or void. Constructs are often [mindless](./Traits.aspx-ID=652.md); they’re immune to [bleed damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86.md), death effects, [disease](./Traits.aspx-ID=578.md), [healing](./Traits.aspx-ID=623.md), [nonlethal attacks](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2311.md), [poison](./Traits.aspx-ID=669.md), [vitality](./Traits.aspx-ID=509.md), [void](./Traits.aspx-ID=510.md), and the [doomed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=67.md), [drained](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=68.md), [fatigued](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=73.md), [paralyzed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=85.md), [sickened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=91.md), and [unconscious](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=95.md) conditions; and they might have Hardness based on the materials used to construct their bodies. Constructs are not living creatures, nor are they undead. When reduced to 0 Hit Points, a construct creature is destroyed.
