# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Eerie Environs](./Feats.aspx-ID=8419.md) Feat 8

[Druid](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=583.md) [Ranger](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=682.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 65](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

Prerequisites trained in [Intimidation](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=40.md) and [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48.md)

---

The natural world can be scary to those not used to it—and you make it scarier still. If you're [hidden](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=79.md) from a creature, you can attempt to [Demoralize](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2395.md) it without losing your hidden condition— imitating the sounds of strange beasts or causing the foliage to rustle menacingly. When you do so, you don't take a penalty to your check if the target doesn't understand your language.
