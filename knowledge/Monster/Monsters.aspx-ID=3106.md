# Nightmare

Source [Monster Core pg. 238](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=221.md)
Nightmares are flaming equine harbingers of death.

### Nightmare Steeds

Nightmares allow only the evilest of creatures to ride them and are willing partners in the destruction those creatures inflict. Powerful dullahans are particularly well known to associate with nightmares.
