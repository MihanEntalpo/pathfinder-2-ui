# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Charhide Goblin](./Heritages.aspx-ID=250.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 55](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Your ancestors have always had a connection to fire and a thicker skin, which allows you to resist burning. You gain fire resistance equal to half your level minimum 1). You can also recover from being on fire more easily. Your flat check to remove [persistent fire damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86&Redirected=1.md) is DC 10 instead of DC 15, which is reduced to DC 5 if another creature uses a particularly appropriate action to help.
