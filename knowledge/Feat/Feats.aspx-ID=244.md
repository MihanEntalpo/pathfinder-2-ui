# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Blade of Justice](./Feats.aspx-ID=244.md) [two-actions] Feat 12

### Legacy Content

Champion
Source [Core Rulebook pg. 113](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)

Prerequisites [tenets of good](../Tenets/Tenets.aspx-ID=1.md)

---

You call upon divine power and make a weapon or unarmed Strike against a foe you have witnessed harming an ally or innocent. The Strike deals two extra weapon damage dice if the target of your Strike is unholy. Whether or not the target is unholy, you can convert all the physical damage from the attack into spirit damage, and if you are a paladin, the Strike applies all effects that normally apply on a Retributive Strike (such as divine smite).
