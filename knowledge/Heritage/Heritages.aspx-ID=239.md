# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Ancient Elf](./Heritages.aspx-ID=239.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 47](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
In your long life, you've dabbled in many paths and many styles. A typical ancient elf is at least 100 years old, though you might be younger at the GM's discretion. Choose a class other than your own. You gain the [multiclass dedication](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=659.md) feat for that class, even though you don't meet its level prerequisite. You must still meet its other prerequisites to gain the feat.
