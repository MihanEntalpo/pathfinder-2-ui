Source [GM Core pg. 247](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md), [Player Core 2 pg. 287](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md); There is a more recent version of this item. Click here to view.
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Activate [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md))

---

After you drink this elixir, you notice subtle visual details. For the next hour, you gain an item bonus to Perception checks that is greater when attempting to find secret doors and traps.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Eagle-Eye Elixir (Lesser)Item 1

Source [GM Core pg. 247](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Price 4 gp
The bonus is +1, or +2 to find secret doors and traps.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Eagle-Eye Elixir (Major)Item 16

Source [GM Core pg. 247](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
