# [Mindhammer Mushrooms](./Hazards.aspx-ID=597.md)Hazard 3

[Environmental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=591.md) [Fungus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=614.md)
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 48](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 20 (trained) to notice small animal skeletons among the mushrooms
Description The mushrooms emit powerful psychic blasts that overwhelm potential predators with exhausting psychedelic visions.

---

Disable DC 20 [Survival](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=49.md) (trained) to navigate a safe path without triggering the mushrooms
AC 16, Fort +10, Ref +8
HP 20; Immunities critical hits, object immunities, precision damage, Weaknesses [fire](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=604.md) 10
Psychic Blast [reaction] Trigger A creature walks through the mushrooms; Effect The mushrooms release a loud hum of psychic energy. The triggering creature takes 2d8+8 mental damage (DC 23 [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Will save). On a critical failure, the creature is [fatigued](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=73.md).

---

Reset The mushrooms must rest for 24 hours before they can emit another Psychic Blast
