A dragon chooses its rider.

Only a few are worthy. “Having the soul of a dragon” is an apt Avistani saying for individuals who show indomitable charisma, unfaltering purpose, and an iron will. Such qualities mark every drake rider, while a proud or noble dragon also demands respect from any who would ride upon their back. A dedicated rider who doesn’t confuse violence with strength and shows no hesitation or fear earns a deep bond that grows from the dragon’s mutual respect. In the air or on the ground, this bond forms a lethal union of dragon and rider.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Drake Rider Dedication](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8102.md) Feat 2

Uncommon Archetype Dedication
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 220](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Archetype [Drake Rider](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=334.md)
Prerequisites trained in [Nature](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=43.md); Charisma +1

---

You gain the service of a young [riding drake](../AnimalCompanions/AnimalCompanions.aspx-ID=77.md), [riding dragonet](../AnimalCompanions/AnimalCompanions.aspx-ID=122.md), or another animal companion with the [dragon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=581.md) trait you have access to. Your bond with your dragon companion lets you communicate and issue commands to it telepathically with a range of 100 feet. You must still use the [Command an Animal](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2400.md) action to direct it but don’t require words or gestures to do so.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Mature Dragon Companion](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8103.md) Feat 4

Archetype
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 220](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Archetype [Drake Rider](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=334.md)
Prerequisites [Drake Rider Dedication](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8102.md)

---

Your dragon companion matures and the bond you share is strengthened. The dragon companion you gained with your Drake Rider Dedication is now a mature animal companion.

Your dragon companion gains greater independence. During an encounter, even if you don’t use the [Command an Animal](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2400.md) action, your dragon companion can still use 1 action that round on your turn to [Stride](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2305.md) or [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md). It can do this at any point during your turn, as long as you aren’t currently taking an action. If it does, that’s all the actions it gets that round—you can’t Command it later.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Winged Leap](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8104.md) Feat 4

Archetype
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 220](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Archetype [Drake Rider](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=334.md)
Prerequisites dragon companion with the mount special ability; [Drake Rider Dedication](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8102.md)

---

While drakes and dragons are able flyers, they’re uncomfortable carrying a rider in the air without special training. Through hard work, nerves of steel, and the deep bond you share with your draconic companion, your companion has learned to carry you skyward, if only briefly. Your dragon companion gains the Winged Leap action.

Winged Leap [one-action] Frequency once per round; Effect Your dragon companion [Flies](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2312.md). If it doesn’t normally have a fly Speed, it gains a fly Speed of 25 feet for this movement. If it isn’t on solid ground at the end of this movement, it falls.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Flair Rider Stance](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8105.md) [one-action] Feat 6

Archetype Stance
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 220](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Archetype [Drake Rider](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=334.md)
Prerequisites [Drake Rider Dedication](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8102.md); trained in [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md)

---

You spend little time in your saddle as you flip unpredictably around your dragon mount, dodging attacks. While mounted and in this stance, your mount always provides you with [lesser cover](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2372.md) and you don’t take the –2 circumstance penalty to Reflex saves while mounted.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[War Rider Stance](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8106.md) [one-action] Feat 6

Archetype Aura Stance
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 220](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Archetype [Drake Rider](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=334.md)
Prerequisites [Drake Rider Dedication](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8102.md); expert in martial weapons; dragon companion with a support benefit that includes damage

---

Your dragon’s constant breath threatens your foes. While in this stance, your dragon companion has an aura in a 5-foot [emanation](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2387.md). Each creature that starts its turn in the aura takes the damage listed under the dragon’s support benefit with a [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Reflex save against your mount’s [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) DC.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Death Dive](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8109.md) [three-actions] Feat 12

Archetype
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 220](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Archetype [Drake Rider](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=334.md)
Prerequisites [Flair Rider Stance](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8105.md); [Wing Rider](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8108.md)
Requirements You are riding your dragon companion and are airborne.

---

You guide your mount into a plummet, diving headlong at a single foe. You command your mount to [Fly](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2312.md) twice. It must end this movement at a lower altitude than it began its turn at. You and your mount gain a +4 circumstance bonus to AC against reactions triggered by this movement. At the end of this movement, your mount attempts a melee [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) against one creature in its reach. If the Strike hits, the target is knocked [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88.md) if it’s smaller than your mount; if it’s a critical hit, the target is knocked prone if it’s the same size as your mount or smaller.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Strafing Breath](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8110.md) [three-actions] Feat 12

Archetype
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 221](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Archetype [Drake Rider](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=334.md)
Prerequisites [War Rider Stance](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8106.md)
Requirements War Rider Stance is active.

---

You urge your dragon mount forward, wreathed in magical power. Your mount [Strides](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2305.md) three times. If your mount has a fly Speed, it can substitute a [Fly](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2312.md) action for any of these Stride actions. As it moves, your dragon companion’s magical breath billows around you. The aura from War Rider Stance increases to a 10-foot [emanation](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2387.md) during this movement, and each creature that’s in the emanation at any point during your movement takes the damage from it (with the normal save). A creature can take damage only once, even if you move past it multiple times.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Guided Hover](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8111.md) [reaction] Feat 14

Archetype
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 221](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Archetype [Drake Rider](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=334.md)
Prerequisites [Wing Rider](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8108.md)
Trigger Your turn ends.
Requirements Your airborne dragon mount has not used the [Fly](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2312.md) action this turn.

---

You guide your draconic companion, whose outstretched wings fill with unseen thermals. Your mount hovers without needing to use a [Fly](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2312.md) action to stay in place.
