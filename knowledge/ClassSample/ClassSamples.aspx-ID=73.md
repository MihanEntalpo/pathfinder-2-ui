# [Student of the Void](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=73.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 200](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Your magic summons spirits, transports you, and lets you channel the energy of other planes. You know about odd creatures and distant realms.

## Ability Scores

Prioritize Intelligence. Dexterity, Constitution, and Wisdom round out your defenses.
