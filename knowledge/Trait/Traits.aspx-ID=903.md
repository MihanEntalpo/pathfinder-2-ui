# [Reincarnated](./Traits.aspx-ID=903.md)

Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 318](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
These rare ancestry feats are available to any character who has reincarnated at least once either as part of their backstory or because of a reincarnation that took place during play.
