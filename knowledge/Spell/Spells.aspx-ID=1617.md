# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Oaken Resilience [two-actions] Spell 2

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Plant [Wood](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=508.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 347](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Catalysts Wood-Rotted Root
Deities Blooms of the Spreading Weald, Gendowyn, Uirch, Verilorn
Range touch; Targets 1 willing creature
Duration 10 minutes

---

The target's skin becomes tough, with a consistency like bark or wood. The target gains resistance 2 to bludgeoning and piercing damage and weakness 3 to fire. After the target takes fire damage, it can Dismiss the spell as a free action triggered by taking the damage; doing so doesn't reduce the fire damage the target was dealt.

---

Heightened (+2) The resistances increase by 2, and the weakness increases by 3.
