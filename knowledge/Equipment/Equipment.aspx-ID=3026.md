# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Maestro's InstrumentItem 3+

[Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 275](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage held in 2 hand; Bulk 1

---

A maestro's instrument can be crafted in the form of any variety of handheld musical instruments. A maestro's instrument grants you a +1 item bonus to Performance checks while playing music with the instrument.

Activate—Charming Performance [two-actions] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect You play the instrument, causing it to cast a DC 17 charm spell.
