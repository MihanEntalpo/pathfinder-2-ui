# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Tangling Creepers [three-actions] Spell 6

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Plant [Wood](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=508.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 362](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Deities [Green Man](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=666.md), Immaculate Growth, Kagia, Osiris, Telvrys, Treerazer, Yuelral, Zibik
Range 500 feet; Area 40-foot burst
Duration 10 minutes

---

Dense, twitching creepers sprout from every surface and fill any bodies of water in the area. Any creature moving on the land, or Climbing or Swimming within the creepers, takes a –10-foot circumstance penalty to its Speeds while in the area. Once per round, you can Sustain the spell to make a vine lash out from any square within the expanse of creepers. This vine has a 15- foot reach. Make a melee spell attack roll against the target; on a success, the vine pulls the target into the creepers and makes it [immobilized](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=81&Redirected=1.md) for 1 round or until the creature Escapes (against your spell DC), whichever comes first.
