# [High-Speed Regeneration](./Feats.aspx-ID=8040.md) [free-action] Feat 6

## This Feat may contain spoilers from the Gatewalkers Adventure Path

Rare Deviant Healing Magical
Source [Gatewalkers Player's Guide (Remastered) pg. 13](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=287.md), [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 101](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

Trigger Your turn begins

---

Your wounds knit together with barely a thought. You regain Hit Points equal to double your level. When you gain this deviation, the GM secretly selects one type of energy damage or precious material, such as [fire](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=604.md) or [cold iron](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2916.md). When you take damage of that type, your wound smokes until the end of your next turn, preventing you from using High-Speed Regeneration.

Awakening Your regeneration can automatically save you from the brink of death. Once per day, you can use High-Speed Regeneration when your Hit Points would be reduced to 0 instead of the usual trigger. You avoid being knocked out and remain at the number of HP you regained.
Awakening Regrowth invigorates you. You gain a +10-foot status bonus to your Speed until the end of your turn.
