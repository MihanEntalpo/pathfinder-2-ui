# Hollow RobesItem 13

## This Item may contain spoilers from the Revenge of the Runelords Adventure Path

Rare Illusion Invested [Mythic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=835.md)
Source [Pathfinder Adventure Path #219: Lord of the Trinity Star pg. 70](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=283.md)
Price 300 gp
Usage worn garment; Bulk 1

---

These heavy cloth garments function as a set of +2 [resilient](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2786.md) [explorer’s clothing](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2715.md). They feature multiple stylish layers, voluminous sleeves, and long strings of Thassilonian runes along the hems. The robes employ potent illusion magic, sufficient to convince the wearer and any attackers that a devastating blow is nothing more than a flesh wound.

### (concentration) [one-action]

Source [Pathfinder Adventure Path #219: Lord of the Trinity Star pg. 70](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=283.md)
Frequency once per hour

---

The wearer becomes invisible inside the robes for one minute. Since the wearer’s gear (including the robes) remain visible, they don’t become concealed, but they can’t be visually identified unless the viewer can see invisible creatures. The unsettling nature of this effect grants the wearer a +2 item bonus to Intimidation checks made while the robes appear to be empty.

### (concentration) [reaction]

Source [Pathfinder Adventure Path #219: Lord of the Trinity Star pg. 70](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=283.md)
Trigger You take persistent damage or a critical hit from a Strike;
Requirements Empty Robes is in effect

---

Spend 1 Mythic Point. You momentarily transform yourself into an illusory duplicate, rendering yourself “hollow” to the triggering damage or Strike. The damage from a critical hit Strike is reduced to 0. All persistent damage you’re currently taking ends.

### (spellshape) [one-action]

Source [Pathfinder Adventure Path #219: Lord of the Trinity Star pg. 70](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=283.md)
Requirements Empty Robes is in effect

---

If your next action is to Cast a Spell that doesn’t have the manipulate trait, that spell gains the subtle trait.

Craft Requirements You must be a mythic arcane spellcaster capable of casting [invisibility](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1577.md).
