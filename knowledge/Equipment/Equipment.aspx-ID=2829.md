# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)StrikingItem 4+

[Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 236](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage etched onto a weapon; Bulk &mdash;

---

A striking rune stores destructive magic in the weapon, increasing the weapon damage dice it deals to two instead of one. For instance, a +1 striking dagger would deal 2d4 damage instead of 1d4 damage.
You can upgrade the striking rune already etched on a weapon to a stronger version, increasing the values of the existing rune to those of the new rune. The Price of the upgrade is the difference between the two runes' Prices.
