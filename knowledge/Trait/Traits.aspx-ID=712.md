# [Time](./Traits.aspx-ID=712.md)

Source [GM Core pg. 334](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Time creatures are natives of the Dimension of Time. They don’t age, and while some might be driven by supernatural hungers, they don’t need to eat or drink to survive. They can survive the basic environmental effects of the Dimension of Time.
