# Specific Magic Weapons

Source [Core Rulebook pg. 600](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)
These weapons have abilities far different from what can be gained by simply etching runes. A specific magic weapon lists its [fundamental runes](./Equipment.aspx-Category=23&Subcategory=25.md), which you can upgrade, add, or transfer as normal. You can't etch or transfer any [property runes](./Equipment.aspx-Category=23&Subcategory=27.md) onto a specific weapon that it doesn't already have, and you can't remove its property runes.
