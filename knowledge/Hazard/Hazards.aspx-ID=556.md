# [Temptation of Glory](./Hazards.aspx-ID=556.md)Hazard 8

[Rare](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=683.md) [Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) [Trap](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=715.md)
Source [Pathfinder #217: Death Sails a Wine-Dark Sea pg. 60](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=273.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth +18 (expert)
Description Ghostly images of your heart’s desire appear and beckon you toward them.

---

Disable [Arcana](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=35.md) or [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md) DC 25 (expert) to disrupt the images; [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md) DC 26 (expert) to shout a countering chant; [Intimidation](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=40.md) DC 27 (expert) to scare the images away; Will DC 28 (expert) to refuse to listen
Tempt [reaction] ([mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md), [occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md)) Trigger A PC moves down the exit hallway; Effect The ghostly images turn cruel and hungry, grasping and slashing at the PC

---

Routine (1 per PC) The hazard targets a PC and uses cruel temptation
Cruel Temptation [one-action] claw +20, Damage 2d10+10 mental

---

Special The hazard continues each round until defeated, and the hallway’s end cannot be reached until the hazard is stopped.
