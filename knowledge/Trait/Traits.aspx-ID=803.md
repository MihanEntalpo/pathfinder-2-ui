# [Vigilante](./Traits.aspx-ID=803.md)

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 218](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Your class feats and [vigilante](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=267.md) feats are associated with your vigilante identity, and using them while in your social identity may risk exposing you as a vigilante. If your identity is exposed to the public, you lose the benefits of Vigilante Dedication to disguising yourself, but you can spend 1 week of downtime to create a new social identity.
