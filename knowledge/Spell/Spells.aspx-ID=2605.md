# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Stagnate Time [two-actions] Spell 5

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 182](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Deities Atropos, Charg
Range 120 feet; Area 20-foot burst
Duration 1 minute

---

You examine the time stream in a particular area, then proceed to stick a few metaphorical pins into it, thickening and slowing down the flow of time until it rumbles along like a river of molasses. Any creature that begins its turn in the area must attempt a Will save.

Success The creature is unaffected.
Failure The creature is [slowed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=92.md) 1 this turn.
Critical Failure The creature is slowed 2 this turn.
