# [Grabbed](./Conditions.aspx-ID=77.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 444](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
You're held in place by another creature, giving you the [off-guard](./Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) and [immobilized](./Conditions.aspx-ID=81&Redirected=1.md) conditions. If you attempt a [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) action while grabbed, you must succeed at a DC 5 flat check or it is lost; roll the check after spending the action, but before any effects are applied.
