# [Kugaptee's Tree](./Hazards.aspx-ID=656.md)Hazard 6

[Unique](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=723.md) [Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) [Haunt](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=622.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 135](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth +15 (expert)
Description The branches of the stone tree statue animate, extend, and lash out at intruders.

---

Disable DC 25 [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md) to exorcise the haunt, or DC 28 [Intimidation](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=40.md) to appear overwhelmingly strong of body and mind so that the haunt recoils and fades
AC 22, Fort +18, Ref +12
Hardness 14, HP 50 (BT 25); Immunities critical hits, precision damage, Weaknesses [vitality](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=509.md) 10
Pulse of Fear [reaction] ([emotion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=590.md), [fear](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=598.md), [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md), [occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md)) Trigger An intruder begins their turn in area E3; Effect The stone statue animates, its branches whipping through the air as a horrifying roar rips through the monastery—a roar that seems to come from underground. All intruders within the monastery must attempt a DC 21 Will save. The haunt then rolls initiative.
Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
Success The creature is [frightened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=76.md) 1.
Failure The creature is frightened 2.
Critical Failure The creature is frightened 2 and [fleeing](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=74.md) for as long as it remains frightened.

---

Routine (3 actions) Kugaptee's tree lashes out with its branches, using its actions to make three branch Strikes. Targets that are within reach but are outside of area E3 have [cover](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2372.md) from the surrounding archways. The haunt favors targets in area E3 but otherwise selects its targets randomly.
Melee branch +17 ([magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md), [reach 40 feet](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=684.md)), Damage 1d8+9 bludgeoning plus 1d8 mental

---

Reset 1 hour
