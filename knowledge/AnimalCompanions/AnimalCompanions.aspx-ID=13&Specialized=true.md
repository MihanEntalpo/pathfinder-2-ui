# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Ambusher](./AnimalCompanions.aspx-ID=13&Specialized=true.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 211](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
In your companion’s natural environment, it can use a [Sneak](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2405.md) action even if it’s currently [observed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=84.md). Its proficiency rank in [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48.md) increases to expert (or master if it was already an expert from its type), and its Dexterity modifier increases by 1. Its proficiency rank for unarmored defense increases to expert.
