[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 365](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Deities Dachzerul, Kalekot, Kelinahat, Ketephys, Ng, Skode, Tanagaar, Ulon, Zarongel
Duration 1 hour

---

You obscure the tracks you leave behind. The DC of checks to Track you gains a +4 status bonus or is equal to your spell DC, whichever results in a higher DC.

---

Heightened (2nd) The duration increases to 8 hours.
Heightened (4th) The duration increases to 8 hours. The spell has a range of 20 feet and targets up to 10 creatures.
