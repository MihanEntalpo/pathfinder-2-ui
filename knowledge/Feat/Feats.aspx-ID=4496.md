# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Leshy Superstition](./Feats.aspx-ID=4496.md) [reaction] Feat 1

Leshy
Source [Player Core pg. 68](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Trigger You attempt a saving throw against a spell or magical effect, but haven’t rolled yet

---

You notice spirits that inhabit objects, learning which bring good fortune and which are unlucky. You focus on the power of a lucky object, granting you a +1 circumstance bonus to your saving throw against the triggering effect.

## Traits

Leshy: A creature with this trait is a member of the leshy ancestry. These small plant or fungus creatures are humanoid in form. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by leshies. A weapon with this trait is created and used by leshies.
