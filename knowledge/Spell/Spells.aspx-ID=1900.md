# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Earthworks [one-action] to [three-actions] Focus 1

Uncommon [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Earth [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) [Wizard](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=735.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 388](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Range 60 feet; Area 5-foot burst or more
Duration 1 minute

---

With a ripple of earth, you raise small barriers from the ground. The ground in the area becomes [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366&Redirected=1.md). The spell's area is a 5-foot burst if you spent 1 action to cast it, a 10-foot burst if you spent 2 actions, or a 15-foot burst if you spent 3 actions. A creature can Interact to clear the barriers from one 5-foot square adjacent to it.

---

Heightened (4th) You pull the barriers to float in the air, causing the spell to function as difficult terrain for flying creatures.
