# [Living Storm](./Relics.aspx-ID=129.md)Grand Gift

[Air](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=527.md) [Electricity](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=586.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 310](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Aspect air

---

The relic grants you a fly Speed equal to your Speed or 60 feet, whichever is greater. In addition, a creature that Strikes you with a melee attack takes 5 electricity damage, and you can cast 5th-rank [lightning bolt](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1586.md) as an innate spell.
