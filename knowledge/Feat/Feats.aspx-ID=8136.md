# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Ilverani Purist](./Feats.aspx-ID=8136.md) Feat 8

Archetype Skill
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 243](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Archetype [Twilight Speaker](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=337.md)
Prerequisites [Twilight Speaker Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8132.md)

---

All twilight speakers are trained to resist the alluring customs of younger peoples, but you have made it your mandate to keep the Ilverani way unchanged. Such dedication has given you a trained eye for subtlety and deception. When you attempt to [Sense the Motive](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2302.md) of a non-elf humanoid creature and you roll a critical failure, you fail instead.

## Traits

Archetype: This feat belongs to an archetype.

Skill: A general feat with the skill trait improves your skills and their actions or gives you new actions for a skill. A feat with this trait can be selected when a class grants a skill feat or general feat. Archetype feats with the skill trait can be selected in place of a skill feat if you have that archetype’s dedication feat.
