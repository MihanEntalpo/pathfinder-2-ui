[Invested](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=637.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 231](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Price 360 gp
Usage worn armor; Bulk 1
Base Armor [Chain Shirt](../Armor/Armor.aspx-ID=43.md)

---

This +1 silver chain shirt has a collar adorned with stitched images of the phases of the moon. You can see in moonlight as though you had low-light vision.

Activate—Dim Sight [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect You touch the stitched image of the new moon on the armor's collar and suppress the dazzled condition for 1 minute.

Craft Requirements The initial raw materials must include 33 gp of silver.
