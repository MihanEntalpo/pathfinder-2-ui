# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Inspired Stratagem](./Feats.aspx-ID=4952.md) Feat 8

[Rogue](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=687.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 173](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

During your daily preparations, you can review stratagems for the day’s adventures with up to five allies. Later, you can quickly advise them on your schemes using the below reaction.

Inspired Stratagem [reaction] (fortune, linguistic) Trigger An ally you reviewed stratagems with is about to attempt an attack roll or skill check; Effect The ally rolls the triggering check twice and takes the better of the two results. That ally then becomes temporarily immune to your Inspired Stratagem until your next daily preparations.
