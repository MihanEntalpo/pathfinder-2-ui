# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Kobold Breath](./Feats.aspx-ID=8068.md) [two-actions] Feat 1

Kobold
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 215](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md), [Advanced Player's Guide pg. 14](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=39.md)

Prerequisites dragonscaled kobold heritage

---

You channel the power of your [draconic benefactor](../DraconicBenefactors/DraconicBenefactors.aspx.md) to expel a torrent of energy from your mouth that manifests as a 30-foot line or a 15-foot cone, dealing 1d4 damage. Each creature in the area must attempt a [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) saving throw against the higher of your class DC or spell DC. You can’t use this ability again for 1d4 rounds.

At 3rd level and every 2 levels after that, the damage increases by 1d4. The breath shape, damage type, and the saving throw match those of your draconic benefactor, such as those shown on the Draconic Benefactors table here. This activity has the traits associated with the magical tradition of your draconic benefactor and the type of damage the breath deals.

## Traits

Kobold: A creature with this trait is a member of the [kobold](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=80.md) ancestry, reptilian humanoids who are usually Small and typically have [darkvision](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2410.md). An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by kobolds.
