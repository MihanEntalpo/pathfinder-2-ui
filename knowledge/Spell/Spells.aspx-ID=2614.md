# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Time Skip [one-action] Focus 3

[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 189](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md), [Divine Mysteries pg. 287](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=234.md)
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 willing creature other than yourself
Duration 1 round

---

You manipulate time around your ally, pushing them forward a few moments in time. The target is [quickened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=89.md) on its next turn and can use the action only to Step, Stride, or Strike.

---

Heightened (6th) You can affect up to four willing creatures other than yourself within range.
