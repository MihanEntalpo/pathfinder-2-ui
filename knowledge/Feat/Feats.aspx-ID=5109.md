Archetype

Archetypes [Wizard](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=218.md), [Spellshot](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=122.md)

You gain the basic spellcasting benefits. Each time you gain a spell slot of a new rank from the wizard archetype, add two common spells of that rank to your spellbook.

[Arcane Breadth](./Feats.aspx-ID=5111.md), Expert Beast Gunner Spellcasting, [Expert Wizard Spellcasting](./Feats.aspx-ID=5112.md)
