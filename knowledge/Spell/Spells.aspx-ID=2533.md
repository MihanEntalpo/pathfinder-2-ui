# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Lashunta's Life Bubble [one-action] Spell 5

## This Spell may contain spoilers from the Gatewalkers Adventure Path

Uncommon Air [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 231](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Targets 1 creature
Duration 8 hours

---

You create a replenishing protective bubble of fresh, temperate air around the target. The shell of air enables the target to breathe even underwater or in a vacuum, protects from severe cold and heat, and grants a +1 status bonus to the target’s saves against inhaled dangers such as inhaled poisons and [toxic cloud](./Spells.aspx-ID=1722.md).

---

Heightened (6th) The bubble lasts until your next preparation.
Heightened (8th) The bubble lasts until your next preparation, protects against extreme cold and heat, and grants a +2 status bonus instead of +1.
