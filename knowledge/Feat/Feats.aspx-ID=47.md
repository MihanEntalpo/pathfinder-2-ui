### Legacy Content

Goblin
Source [Core Rulebook pg. 49](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)

Prerequisites Goblin Weapon Familiarity

---

You know how to wield your people's vicious weapons. Whenever you score a critical hit using a goblin weapon, you apply the weapon's critical specialization effect.

## Traits

Goblin: A creature with this trait is a small, frantic goblinoid. Goblins tend to have darkvision. An ability with this trait can be used or chosen only by goblins. A weapon with this trait is created and used by goblins.
