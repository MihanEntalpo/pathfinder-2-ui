This [+1 striking](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2861.md) [longspear](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=361.md) of polished white bone features a tip of layered reptilian scales, compressed to form a sharp edge. Akki crafted this spear herself after she and her allies tangled with a hydra plaguing the marshes outside Aelyosos. She pressed the scales of the creature down into a spearpoint mostly through sheer stubbornness, granting it a unique ability.

Activate—Hydra Swipe [two-actions] Frequency once per hour; Effect The spearhead unravels and elongates into a group of snake heads. Make up to three melee Strikes with the Hydra Spear, each at a different creature within 20 feet. These attacks do not count toward your multiple attack penalty until all attacks have been resolved.
