# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Charlatan's GlovesItem 3+

[Invested](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=637.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 286](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage worn gloves; Bulk L

---

Tiny silver hooks decorate these fine silk gloves. They grant a +1 item bonus to Thievery and allow you to cast [telekinetic hand](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1715.md) as an innate occult spell.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Charlatan's Gloves (Greater)Item 9

Source [GM Core pg. 286](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Price 600 gp
The gloves' bonus increases to +2. If you are also wearing a [charlatan's cape](./Equipment.aspx-ID=3063.md), whenever you would move an object using [telekinetic hand](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1715.md), you may instead have it disappear in a puff of smoke and reappear hovering in a space adjacent to yours. This is a [teleportation](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=710.md) effect. The item hovers until the end of your turn or until retrieved with an [Interact](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2297.md) action.
