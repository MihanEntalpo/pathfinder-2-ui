# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Summon Monitor [three-actions] Spell 5

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) [Summon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=520.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 361](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md)
Range 30 feet
Duration sustained up to 1 minute

---

You summon a creature that has the monitor trait and whose level is 5 or lower to fight for you. The GM might determine your deity restricts the specific types of monitors you can summon in certain cases. For instance, Urgathoa typically doesn't allow her followers to summon psychopomps.
