# [Living Statue](./Relics.aspx-ID=153.md)Grand Gift

[Earth](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=585.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 313](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Aspect earth

---

Your relic's energies have given you control over the boundary between flesh and stone. You can cast 6th-rank [petrify](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1628.md) and [sure footing](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1708.md) (to remove petrification only) as innate spells, each once per day. As long as you are standing on the earth, your body reflexively petrifies at the moment you are struck, granting you resistance 5 to physical damage (except adamantine). This increases to 8 in caves or subterranean environments.
