# [Counting Damage Dice](./Rules.aspx-ID=2304.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 406](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Effects based on a weapon's number of damage dice include only the weapon's damage die plus any extra dice from a [striking rune](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2829.md). They don't count extra dice from abilities, critical specialization effects, property runes, weapon traits, or the like.
