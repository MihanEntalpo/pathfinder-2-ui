Source [Player Core pg. 363](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Deities Apollyon, Cixyron, Isph-Aun-Vuln, Nergal, Nhimbaloth, Sicva, Vorasha, Ydersius
Mystery blight
Range 120 feet; Area 20-foot burst
Defense basic Fortitude; Duration 1 minute

---

You conjure a poisonous fog. This functions as [mist](./Spells.aspx-ID=1606.md), except the area moves 10 feet away from you each round. A breathing creature that starts its turn in the area takes 6d8 poison damage with a basic Fortitude save. You can Dismiss the spell.

---

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 1d8.
