# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Underhanded Assault](./Feats.aspx-ID=4929.md) [two-actions] Feat 2

[Rogue](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=687.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 171](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetype [Red Mantis Assassin](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=271.md)*
Prerequisites trained in [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48&Redirected=1.md)
* This archetype offers Underhanded Assault at a different level than displayed here.

---

You capitalize on the distraction provided by an ally. You Sneak up to a foe adjacent to one of your allies. You can roll against the foe you’re Sneaking up on, even if it’s currently observing you, as though you were hidden. You take a –2 penalty on your Stealth check. If your Stealth check against the chosen foe succeeds, you can make a melee Strike against that foe at the end of your Sneak.

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more archetypes in addition to the listed classes. When selected this way, the feat does not have its class traits.
