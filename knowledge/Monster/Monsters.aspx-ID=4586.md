# Totum Font

Before mortals, fey, or even genies, totum fonts were the first creatures born of six elements undivided and harmonious. From the fonts sprung new elemental children, and they aided the gods in the many acts of creation that would follow.

### Wellsprings Of One

Without access to the balance of all six elemental planes, a totum font becomes fractured and inundated in a single element. Most were healed when the [Planes of Metal](../Plane/Planes.aspx-ID=24.md) and [Wood](../Plane/Planes.aspx-ID=25.md) returned, but some of these so-called “wellsprings of one” still wander the universe, agitated and confused.
