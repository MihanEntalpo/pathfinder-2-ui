# [Protean](./Traits.aspx-ID=678.md)

Source [GM Core pg. 332](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
A family of monitors spawned within the Maelstrom, these creatures are guardians of disorder. They typically have darkvision, an amorphous anatomy.
