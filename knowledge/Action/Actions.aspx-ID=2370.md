# [Tumble Through](./Actions.aspx-ID=2370.md) [one-action]

[Move](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=658&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 233](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Skill [Acrobatics (Untrained)](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md)

---

You Stride up to your Speed. During this movement, you can try to move through the space of one enemy. Attempt an [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34&Redirected=1.md) check against the enemy's Reflex DC as soon as you try to enter its space. You can Tumble Through using Climb, [Fly](./Actions.aspx-ID=2312&Redirected=1.md), Swim, or another action instead of Stride in the appropriate environment.

Success You move through the enemy's space, treating the squares in its space as difficult terrain (every 5 feet costs 10 feet of movement). If you don't have enough Speed to move all the way through its space, you get the same effect as a failure.
Failure Your movement ends, and you trigger reactions as if you had moved out of the square you started in.
