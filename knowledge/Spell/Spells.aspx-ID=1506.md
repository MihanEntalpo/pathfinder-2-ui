# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Earthbind [two-actions] Spell 3

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Earth [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 327](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Deities Doloras, Jukha, [Torag](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=294.md), Treasures of the Eternal Delve
Range 120 feet; Targets 1 flying creature
Defense Fortitude; Duration varies

---

Using the weight of earth, you hamper a target's flight, with effects based on its Fortitude save. If the creature reaches the ground safely, it doesn't take falling damage.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.
Success The target falls safely up to 120 feet.
Failure The target falls safely up to 120 feet. If it hits the ground, it can't [Fly](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2312&Redirected=1.md), levitate, or otherwise leave the ground for 1 round.
Critical Failure The target falls safely up to 120 feet. If it hits the ground, it can't Fly, levitate, or otherwise leave the ground for 1 minute.
