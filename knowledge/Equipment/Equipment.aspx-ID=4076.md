## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Uncommon Consumable Magical Potion [Tea](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=738.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 300](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 20 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Activate [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md))

---

This sweet and refreshing tea is made with a mixture of honey, lemon slices, and sliced ginger. This is considered to be the bare minimum when serving energizing tea, and many go above and beyond by adding additional citrus fruits or berries to the mix or refusing to serve the tea at all without also serving a full platter of spiced pastries and sweets. This golden tea has energizing properties and, when consumed, grants you a +1 item bonus to [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) and [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md) checks for 10 minutes.

---

Activate—Tea Ceremony 10 minutes ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)) The duration increases to 1 hour.
