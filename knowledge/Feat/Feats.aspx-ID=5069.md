# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Advanced Dogma](./Feats.aspx-ID=5069.md) Feat 6

Archetype
Source [Player Core pg. 217](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetype [Cleric](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=212.md)
Class [Cleric](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=33.md)
Prerequisites [Basic Dogma](./Feats.aspx-ID=5068.md)

---

You gain one cleric feat. For the purpose of meeting its prerequisites, your cleric level is equal to half your character level.

Special You can select this feat more than once. Each time you select it, you gain another cleric feat.

## Traits

Archetype: This feat belongs to an archetype.
