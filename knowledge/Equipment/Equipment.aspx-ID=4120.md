[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Alchemical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=528.md) [Consumable](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=564.md) [Ingested](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=633.md) [Poison](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=669.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 82](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Price 10 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk 1
Access Member of a secret society
Activate [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md))

---

These innocuous, colorless drops can be poured directly into a victim's mouth, or slipped into their food or drink. They initially haze a victim's mind slightly, making them easier to fool; in later stages, they can lead to the victim entering a murderous confused state. Secret societies use these drops to befuddle a target or to frame them for violence.

Saving Throw DC 18 Fortitude; Onset 5 minutes; Maximum Duration 1 hour; Stage 1 [stupefied](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=94.md) 1 (10 minutes); Stage 2 stupefied 1 and [clumsy](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=61.md) 1 (20 minutes); Stage 3 stupefied 1, clumsy 1, and [confused](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=63.md) (30 minutes)
