This grisly molar hangs from a cord threaded through a hole just above its dried, exposed root. When you activate this talisman, make a melee Strike against an adjacent creature. That creature is [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) against the Strike and until the end of your turn.
