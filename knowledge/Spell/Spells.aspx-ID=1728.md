# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Truespeech [two-actions] Spell 5

Uncommon [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 364](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Range touch; Targets 1 creature
Duration 1 hour

---

The target can understand all words regardless of language and also speak the languages of other creatures. When in a mixed group of creatures, each time the target speaks, it can choose a creature and speak in a language that creature understands, even if the target doesn't know what language that is.

---

Heightened (7th) The duration is 8 hours.
