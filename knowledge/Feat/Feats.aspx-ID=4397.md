# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Echoes in Stone](./Feats.aspx-ID=4397.md) [one-action] Feat 9

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Dwarf
Source [Player Core pg. 45](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Requirements You are standing on a stone or earthen surface

---

You pause a moment to attune your senses to the stone around you. Until the start of your next turn, you gain imprecise tremorsense with a range of 20 feet.

## Traits

Concentrate: An action with this trait requires a degree of mental concentration and discipline.

Dwarf: A creature with this trait is a member of the dwarf ancestry. Dwarves are stout folk who often live underground and typically have darkvision. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by dwarves. An item with this trait is created and used by dwarves.
