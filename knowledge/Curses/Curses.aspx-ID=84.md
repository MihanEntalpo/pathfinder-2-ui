# [Sellswords's Folly](./Curses.aspx-ID=84.md)Curse 9

[Curse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=566.md) [Emotion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=590.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 87](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Even the most experienced combat veteran becomes as reckless as a rookie when suffering from sellsword's folly.

Saving Throw DC 26 Will; Effect The chaos of combat overwhelms you. Each time you roll initiative for a combat encounter, you must attempt a new saving throw against the curse; on a failure, you become [confused](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=63.md) for 1 round. This is an [incapacitation](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=631.md) effect.
