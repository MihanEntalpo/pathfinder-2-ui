You have trained in the martial arts, making your unarmed strikes lethal. You seek neither mysticism nor enlightenment, and you don't view this training as some greater path to wisdom. Yours is the way of the fist striking flesh, the hand turning aside the blade, and the devastating kick taking your enemy down. Your training is focused and practical, and since you have turned every part of your body into a weapon, you never find yourself without one. While you might have learned your techniques in a dojo or school focused on training the body, you might just as easily have learned them in street brawls and bar fights, combining instincts with intense athleticism for a deadly combination.

Regardless of your origins, your path is one of iron, transforming your body into a deadly weapon. The techniques available are many, but the results are all the same. Whether you attack with your fist, fang, or foot, your prowess is undeniable. Many martial artists seek to master a stance to put form to their flurry of attacks. Such techniques allow one to channel their strength. However, some prefer freedom in form and reject stances altogether. You may find your own style or master one of the forms used by legendary martial artists.

# Additional Feats

Source [Player Core pg. 215](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Some archetypes include a list of “Additional Feats” that appear in other sources. The list includes each feat’s level, which might be different than normal when gained from the archetype. You can take the feat as an archetype feat of that level, meaning it counts toward the number of feats required by the archetype’s dedication feat. When selected this way, a feat that normally has a class’s trait (such as the fighter trait) doesn’t have that class trait.

Source [Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 120](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=230.md)
Archetype [Martial Artist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=255.md)
Prerequisites ; Twisting Petal Stance
Trigger An enemy within your reach targets you with a melee attack, and you're aware of the attack.
Requirements You’re in Twisting Petal Stance.

---

You evade an incoming attack by twisting your enemy’s tactics in your favor. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to AC against the triggering attack. If the attack misses you, you can immediately attempt a [Feint](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2390.md) or [Shove](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2380.md) against the triggering attacker; if you roll a success, you get a critical success instead.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Five-gods Ram [three-actions] Feat 14

[Monk](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=656.md)
Source [Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 120](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=230.md)
Archetype [Martial Artist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=255.md)
Prerequisites Rushing Goat Stance
Requirements You’re in Rushing Goat Stance.

---

You attack in an unceasing flurry of charging headbutts. Stride or [Climb](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2374.md) and then make a ramming horn Strike. If the Strike is successful, you immediately push the target back 10 feet, then Stride or Climb and make a second ramming horn Strike against them. If the second Strike is successful, you immediately knock the target [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88.md), but if the second Strike is a failure or if you’re unable to complete all the required actions, you become [stunned](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=93.md) 1. Both Strikes count toward your multiple attack penalty, but the penalty doesn’t increase until you’ve made both of them.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Path of Iron [three-actions] Feat 14

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Flourish](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=606.md)
Source [Player Core 2 pg. 206](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetype [Martial Artist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=255.md)
Frequency once per minute
Prerequisites Martial Artist Dedication

---

With a burst of effort and speed, you weave a nimble path through your many enemies, striking each in turn as you move past them. You [Stride](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2305.md); this movement doesn’t trigger reactions. You can attempt a melee [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) up to three times at any point during your movement, each against a different enemy. Each attack counts toward your [multiple attack penalty](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2289.md), but your multiple attack penalty doesn’t increase until you have made all your attacks.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Whirling in the Summer Storm [three-actions] Feat 14

[Monk](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=656.md)
Source [Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 121](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=230.md)
Archetype [Martial Artist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=255.md)
Prerequisites Twisting Petal Stance
Requirements You’re in Twisting Petal Stance.

---

Your hands move in a hypnotic and distracting flurry that throws your foes off-balance before you cast them away with a violent pirouette. You [Step](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2304.md). Then each enemy within your reach must attempt a Will save against the higher of your class DC or your Deception DC; on a failure, they’re [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) to melee attacks from you and your allies until the end of your next turn. Then, [Shove](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2380.md) up to three adjacent enemies; each Shove counts toward your multiple attack penalty, but you don’t increase your penalty until after you’ve resolved all the Shove attempts.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Mountain Quake [one-action] Feat 16

[Monk](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=656.md)
Source [Player Core 2 pg. 126](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetypes [Martial Artist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=255.md)
Prerequisites Mountain Stronghold
Requirements You are in Mountain Stance.

---

You stomp, shaking the earth beneath you. Creatures on the ground within a 20-foot emanation take damage equal to your Strength modifier (minimum 0), with a basic Fortitude save against your class DC. On a failure, they also fall [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88.md). After you use this action, you can't use it again for 1d4 rounds.

Special If you have this feat, the Dexterity modifier cap to your AC while using Mountain Stance increases from +1 to +2.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Echoing Violence [free-action] Feat 18

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Death](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=571.md) [Incapacitation](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=631.md)
Source [Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 121](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=230.md)
Archetype [Martial Artist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=255.md)
Frequency once per minute
Prerequisites Martial Artist Dedication
Trigger You critically succeed with an unarmed [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) against an opponent.

---

Your powerful attack causes damage that reverberates through your opponent’s body, shaking muscle from bone. The creature takes 10d6 bludgeoning damage with a basic Fortitude save against your class DC. If it critically fails, it immediately dies as your blow tears apart its body internally.
