# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Insect Shape](./Feats.aspx-ID=4730.md) Feat 6

[Druid](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=583.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 131](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites [Untamed Form](./Feats.aspx-ID=4713.md)

---

Your understanding of life expands, allowing you to mimic a wider range of creatures. Add the shapes in [insect form](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1575.md) to your untamed form list. Whenever you use untamed form to polymorph into the non-flying insect shapes listed in pest form, the duration is 24 hours instead of 10 minutes.
