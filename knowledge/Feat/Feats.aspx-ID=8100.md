# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Call Draconic Ally](./Feats.aspx-ID=8100.md) Feat 14

Archetype
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 219](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Archetype [Draconic Acolyte](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=333.md)
Prerequisites [Draconic Acolyte Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8090.md)

---

Either by beseeching a dragon or bending it to your will, you conjure a mighty creature to assist you. Once per day while you are Channeling Draconic Essence, you can cast [summon dragon](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1697.md) as an innate spell with a tradition matching your draconic exemplar, heightened to half your level rounded up.
