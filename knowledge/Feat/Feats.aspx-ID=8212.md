# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Boneyard Acquaintance](./Feats.aspx-ID=8212.md) Feat 17

## This Feat may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Rare Force [Reincarnated](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=903.md) [Universal Ancestry](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=467.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 321](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)

---

Before your soul returned from death, you interacted with a host of other souls on their way to the [Boneyard](../Plane/Planes.aspx-ID=39.md). One of these acquaintances owes you a favor. You can temporarily summon it to come to your aid, and it appears as a swirling mass of ectoplasm with indiscernible features and a translucent [dagger](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=358.md), the favored weapon of [Pharasma](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=290.md). This functions as [spiritual guardian](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1688.md) that you can cast once per day as a 9th-rank [divine](../SpellLists/SpellLists.aspx-Tradition=2.md) innate spell.

## Traits

Rare: This rarity indicates that a rules element is very difficult to find in the game world. A rare feat, spell, item or the like is available to players only if the GM decides to include it in the game, typically through discovery during play. Creatures with this trait are rare. They typically can't be summoned. The DC of Recall Knowledge checks related to these creatures is increased by 5.

Force: Effects with this trait deal force damage or create objects made of pure magical force.

Reincarnated: These rare ancestry feats are available to any character who has reincarnated at least once either as part of their backstory or because of a reincarnation that took place during play.

Universal Ancestry: A feat with this trait is available to all ancestries.
