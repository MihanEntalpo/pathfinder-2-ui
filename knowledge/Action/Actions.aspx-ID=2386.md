# [Identify Alchemy](./Actions.aspx-ID=2386.md)

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Exploration](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=595.md) [Secret](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=690&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 237](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Skill [Crafting (Trained)](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=37.md)
Requirements You are holding or wearing an [alchemist's toolkit](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2702.md).

---

You can identify the nature of an alchemical item with 10 minutes of testing using your alchemist's toolkit. If your attempt is interrupted in any way, you must start over.

Success You identify the item and the means of activating it.
Failure You fail to identify the item but can try again.
Critical Failure You misidentify the item as another item of the GM's choice.
