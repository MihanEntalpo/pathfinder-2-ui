# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Advanced General Training](./Feats.aspx-ID=4489.md) Feat 13

Human
Source [Player Core pg. 65](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

Over the long course of your adventuring life, your adaptability has let you pick up numerous useful abilities. You gain a [general feat](./Feats.aspx-Traits=78.md) of 7th level or lower. You must meet the feat’s prerequisites.

Special You can select this feat multiple times, choosing a different feat each time.

## Traits

Human: A creature with this trait is a member of the human ancestry. Humans are a diverse array of people known for their adaptability. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by humans.
