# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Ring of SigilsItem 1+

[Arcane](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=534.md) [Invested](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=637.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 294](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage worn; Bulk &mdash;

---

This silver band is carved with the personal sigils of different individuals, adding one to represent you when you invest it. The ring allows you to cast sigil as an arcane innate cantrip.

Activate—Track Sigil [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)); Frequency once per 10 minutes; Effect You detect the general direction toward the most recent sigil you created using the ring. This activation fails if the sigil is more than 5 miles away or if there's lead or running water between you and the sigil.
