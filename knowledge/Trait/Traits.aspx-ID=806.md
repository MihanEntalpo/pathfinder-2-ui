# [Hampering](./Traits.aspx-ID=806.md)

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 274](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
A weapon with the hampering trait includes a disruptive limb or flange. After you hit with the weapon, you can use an [Interact](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2297.md) action to give the target a –10-foot circumstance penalty to all Speeds. The penalty ends after the target takes a [move](./Traits.aspx-ID=658.md) action, at the start of your next turn, if you attack with the weapon, or if you move out of reach of the target, whichever comes first.
