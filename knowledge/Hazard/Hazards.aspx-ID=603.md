# [Memory of Osoyo](./Hazards.aspx-ID=603.md)Hazard 5

[Unique](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=723.md) [Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) [Environmental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=591.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 100](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth +12 (expert)
Description The party’s memories of Osoyo coalesce into a titanic mirage in the storm clouds above.

---

Disable DC 22 [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md) (trained) or DC 24 [Arcana](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=35.md) (trained) to divert the memory’s rage; three total successful checks are required to disable the hazard
Glacial Gust [reaction] Trigger The characters’ shared vision during Event 15 subsides; Effect The memory of Osoyo unleashes a tremendous sigh of freezing wind, coating the deck of the Mermaid’s Tears in ice in a 60-foot cone. (The hazard can choose any point for the cone to originate.) The area becomes [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366.md). Creatures in the area must succeed at a DC 26 Fortitude save or take 1d6 [persistent cold damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86.md) as the freezing wind cuts to the bone. The hazard then rolls initiative.

---

Routine (1 action) The memory of Osoyo gives a keening scream that shatters the layer of ice coating the deck of the Mermaid’s Tears. Any creature on [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366.md) created by the memory’s Glacial Gust takes 2d8+7 cold damage (DC 26 [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Reflex save) from the icy shrapnel.
