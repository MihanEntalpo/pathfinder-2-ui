[Dragonet](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=893.md)

Prerequisites [versatile heritage](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-Versatile=true.md)

You’ve awoken the power of your draconic heritage. You gain all the mechanical benefits of the [dragonet](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=96.md) heritage you selected at first level, allowing you to take feats and gain any benefits that require a specific dragonet heritage.

## Traits

Dragonet: Dragonets are miniature dragons, complete with wings, dagger-like teeth, and magical breath. They can be fiercely independent and proud, at times protective or avaricious, and they delight in the finer things in life.
