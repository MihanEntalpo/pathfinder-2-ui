# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Grown of Oak](./Feats.aspx-ID=4729.md) Feat 6

[Druid](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=583.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 131](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites leaf order

---

You can make your skin take on the woody endurance of an ancient tree and have your familiar follow suit. You can cast [oaken resilience](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1617.md) at will as an innate primal spell with a spell rank one lower than the highest rank of spells you can cast. This spell targets you, as well as your leshy familiar if it’s within 30 feet of you.
