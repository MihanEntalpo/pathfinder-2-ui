# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Wylderheart](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=318.md)

Source [Shining Kingdoms pg. 146](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=265.md)
You’re a defender of nature, called to protect the world from the corrupting influence of demons. You might use a bow in the vein of your organization’s patron deity Ketephys, wield a sword in the style of valiant knights, or harness the [primal](../SpellLists/SpellLists.aspx-Tradition=4.md) magic of nature itself against your foes. No matter your chosen tool, your greatest assets in the battle against evil are the strength of your heart and your bond with your allies.

# Additional Feats

Source [Player Core pg. 215](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Some archetypes include a list of “Additional Feats” that appear in other sources. The list includes each feat’s level, which might be different than normal when gained from the archetype. You can take the feat as an archetype feat of that level, meaning it counts toward the number of feats required by the archetype’s dedication feat. When selected this way, a feat that normally has a class’s trait (such as the fighter trait) doesn’t have that class trait.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Wylderheart Dedication Feat 2

Uncommon Archetype Dedication
Source [Shining Kingdoms pg. 146](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=265.md)
Archetype [Wylderheart](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=318.md)
Prerequisites member of the Wylderhearts
Access You’re from Kyonin.

---

You’re primed to face off against threats from the [Outer Rifts](../Plane/Planes.aspx-ID=45.md). You gain the [Additional Lore](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=5114.md) skill feat for [Demon Lore](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=41.md). If you were already trained in Demon Lore, you also become trained in a Lore skill of your choice. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to initiative rolls in encounters against [fiends](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=600.md), and if you tie with a fiend’s initiative roll, you go first.

Certain wylderheart feats give you focus spells. The rules for focus spells appear [here](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2228.md). When you gain your first wylderheart focus spell, you become trained in the spell attack modifier and spell DC statistics. Your key spellcasting attribute for the wylderheart archetype spells is Wisdom, and they’re [primal](../SpellLists/SpellLists.aspx-Tradition=4.md) spells. You can Refocus by celebrating life or spending time in nature.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Demon-Hunting Companion Feat 4

Archetype
Source [Shining Kingdoms pg. 146](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=265.md)
Archetype [Wylderheart](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=318.md)
Prerequisites Wylderheart Dedication

---

You gain a young animal companion that has been specially trained to help you fight demons. You can choose any common animal companion, which gains [scent](../MonsterAbility/MonsterAbilities.aspx-ID=74.md) as an imprecise sense with a range of 30 feet that can be used only to smell fiends. If the animal companion already has scent, it gains a +2 circumstance bonus to Perception when using scent against [fiends](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=600.md).

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Barreling Charge [two-actions] Feat 6

[Barbarian](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=813.md) [Fighter](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=601.md) Flourish
Source [Player Core pg. 143](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md), [Player Core 2 pg. 78](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetypes [Rivethun Invoker](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=309.md), [Wylderheart](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=318.md)
Prerequisites trained in [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md)

---

You rush forward, moving enemies aside to reach your foe. You [Stride](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2305.md), attempting to move through your enemies’ spaces. Roll an [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) check and compare the result to the Fortitude DC of each creature whose space you attempt to move through during your Stride, moving through its space on a success but ending your movement before entering its space on a failure. You can then [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md), regardless of how your Stride ended. You can use Barreling Charge to [Burrow](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2310.md), [Climb](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2374.md), [Fly](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2312.md), or [Swim](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2381.md) instead of Stride if you have the corresponding movement type.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Blessed Sentinel [two-actions] Feat 6

Archetype Flourish
Source [Shining Kingdoms pg. 146](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=265.md)
Archetype [Wylderheart](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=318.md)
Prerequisites Wylderheart Dedication

---

You infuse a weapon with the blessing of Ketephys and attack. Make a [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md). This Strike gains the [holy](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=517.md) trait and deals an additional 1d4 [spirit](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=737.md) damage, or 2d4 spirit damage against [fiends](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=600.md). This counts as two attacks for your multiple attack penalty.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Mature Animal Companion (Ranger) Feat 6

[Ranger](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=682.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 159](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetype [Wylderheart](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=318.md)
Prerequisites [Animal Companion](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=4708.md)

---

Your animal companion becomes a mature animal companion and gains additional capabilities.

Your animal companion has greater independence. During an encounter, even if you don't use the Command an Animal action, your animal companion can still use 1 action that round on your turn to Strike or Stride (or Burrow, Climb, or Swim if it has that Speed). It can do this at any point during your turn, as long as you aren’t currently taking an action. If it does, that’s all the actions it gets that round—you can’t Command it later.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Wild Dance [one-action] Feat 12

Archetype Flourish
Source [Shining Kingdoms pg. 146](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=265.md)
Archetype [Wylderheart](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=318.md)
Prerequisites Wylderheart Dedication

---

The combination of your passion for both life and the battle against evil makes you graceful and unpredictable. [Stride](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2305.md) up to your Speed. A creature that attempts a reaction triggered by this movement must first attempt a Will save against your class DC or spell DC, whichever is higher.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
Success The creature is [fascinated](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=72.md) by you until the end of your next turn.
Failure The creature is fascinated by you until the end of your next turn, but your movements might be too hard to follow; if the reaction requires an attack roll, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to AC against that attack roll.
Critical Failure The creature is fascinated by you until the end of your next turn, and it loses its reaction.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Fiend Slayer [one-action] Feat 16

Archetype Death [Void](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=510.md)
Source [Shining Kingdoms pg. 147](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=265.md)
Archetype [Wylderheart](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=318.md)
Prerequisites Wylderheart Dedication
Requirements Your last action was a successful [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) against a [fiend](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=600.md) that dealt damage.

---

Your attack tears through a fiend, possibly sending it back to where it came from. The fiend you just struck takes 80 [void](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=510.md) damage with a [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Fortitude save against your class DC or spell DC, whichever is higher. On a critical failure, the target is also [stunned](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=93.md) 1. Regardless of the results of the saving throw, the target is then temporarily immune to Fiend Slayer for 24 hours.
