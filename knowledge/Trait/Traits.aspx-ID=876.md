# [Fetchling](./Traits.aspx-ID=876.md)

Source [Monster Core 2 pg. 364](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=276.md)
A creature with this trait is a member of the fetchling ancestry. Fetchlings were once [human](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=64.md), but generations of life in [the Netherworld](../Plane/Planes.aspx-ID=36.md) have transformed them.
