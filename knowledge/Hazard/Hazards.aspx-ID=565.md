# [Sudden Geysers](./Hazards.aspx-ID=565.md)Hazard 10

Unique [Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) Environmental Fire Water
Source [Pathfinder #218: Titanbane pg. 61](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=274.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth +17 (expert)
Description The pooling seawater burbles and hisses.

---

Disable DC 32 [Arcana](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=35.md) or [Nature](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=43.md) (master) to dissipate the fire-aligned energies in the water, or DC 28 [Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md) or [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md) to beseech Sangeh and convince her to momentarily tamp down her mythic power; two successes are required to disable the hazard
Scalding Gout [reaction] ([fire](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=604.md), [water](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=732.md)) Trigger A creature within the hazard’s area rolls initiative; Effect An enormous column of superheated steam rises from the water, dealing 4d12+26 fire damage to all creatures in the area (DC 28 [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Reflex save). On a critical failure, the creature also takes 1d12 [persistent fire damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86.md) as the water continues to burn their skin. The hazard then rolls initiative.

---

Routine (2 actions) The hazard uses each of its actions to create a Blistering Plume centered on a space chosen by the GM. Each successful check to disable the hazard removes one action from this routine.
Blistering Plume [one-action] A 30-foot-tall geyser erupts from the boiling water. Creatures in a 5-foot [emanation](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2387.md) of the geyser take 2d12+13 fire damage (DC 30 [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Reflex save).
