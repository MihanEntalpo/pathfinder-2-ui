# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Ferocious Will](./Feats.aspx-ID=8058.md) [reaction] Feat 9

[Dragonet](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=893.md)
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 213](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

Frequency once per hour
Trigger You succeed at a saving throw against a [magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) effect.

---

You send a blast of magical feedback at the effect's source, dealing 5d6 mental damage with a [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Will save against your class DC or spell DC, whichever is higher. If the target critically fails, it’s also [slowed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=92.md) 1 for 1 round. At 11th level and every 2 levels thereafter, the damage increases by 1d6.

## Traits

Dragonet: Dragonets are miniature dragons, complete with wings, dagger-like teeth, and magical breath. They can be fiercely independent and proud, at times protective or avaricious, and they delight in the finer things in life.
