# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Toppling Dance](./Feats.aspx-ID=4472.md) Feat 13

Halfling
Source [Player Core pg. 61](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites [Dance Underfoot](./Feats.aspx-ID=4467.md)

---

While sharing a creature's space using Dance Underfoot, your melee weapons and unarmed attacks gain the trip trait, but only against the creature whose space you share. You can be in the same space as a Large or larger prone creature, even if it's not your ally.

## Traits

Halfling: A creature with this trait is a member of the halfling ancestry. These small people are friendly wanderers considered to be lucky. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by halflings. A weapon with this trait is created and used by halflings.
