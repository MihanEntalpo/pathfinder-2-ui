# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Dueling Riposte](./Feats.aspx-ID=4811.md) [reaction] Feat 8

[Fighter](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=601.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 146](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetypes [Duelist](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=248.md)*
Prerequisites [Dueling Parry (Fighter)](./Feats.aspx-ID=4781.md)
Trigger A creature within your reach critically fails a Strike against you
Requirements You are benefiting from Dueling Parry
* This archetype offers Dueling Riposte at a different level than displayed here.

---

You elegantly counterattack against your flailing enemy. Make a melee Strike against or attempt to Disarm the triggering creature.

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more archetypes in addition to the listed classes. When selected this way, the feat does not have its class traits.
