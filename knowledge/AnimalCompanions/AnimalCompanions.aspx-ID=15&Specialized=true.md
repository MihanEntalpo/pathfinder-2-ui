# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Daredevil](./AnimalCompanions.aspx-ID=15&Specialized=true.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 211](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Your companion joins the fray with graceful leaps and dives. It gains the deny advantage ability, so it isn’t [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) to [hidden](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=79.md), [undetected](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=96.md), or [flanking](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2375.md) creatures unless such a creature’s level is greater than yours. Its proficiency rank in [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md) increases to master, and its Dexterity modifier increases by 1. Its proficiency rank in unarmored defense increases to expert.
