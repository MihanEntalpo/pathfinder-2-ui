# [Fly](./Actions.aspx-ID=2312.md) [one-action]

[Move](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=658&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 419](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Requirements You have a fly Speed.

---

You move through the air up to your fly Speed. Moving upward (straight up or diagonally) uses the rules for moving through [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366&Redirected=1.md). You can move straight down 10 feet for every 5 feet of movement you spend. If you Fly to the ground, you don't take falling damage. You can use an action to Fly 0 feet to hover in place. If you're airborne at the end of your turn and didn't use a Fly action this round, you fall.
