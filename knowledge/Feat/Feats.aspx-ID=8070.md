# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Kobold Momentum](./Feats.aspx-ID=8070.md) Feat 5

Kobold
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 215](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

Prerequisites [Kobold Breath](./Feats.aspx-ID=8068.md)

---

You possess the confidence of a dragon, and your success emboldens you to draw on power reserves you didn’t know you had. You recharge your Kobold Breath whenever you score a critical hit with a Strike. In addition, if you score a critical hit with a Strike and then use your Kobold Breath on the same turn, you can reroll any 1s on the damage dice. You must use the new result, even if it’s a 1.

## Traits

Kobold: A creature with this trait is a member of the [kobold](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=80.md) ancestry, reptilian humanoids who are usually Small and typically have [darkvision](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2410.md). An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by kobolds.
