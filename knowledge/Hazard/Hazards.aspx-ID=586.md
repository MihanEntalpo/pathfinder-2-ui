# [Haunted Aiudara](./Hazards.aspx-ID=586.md)Hazard 6

[Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) [Haunt](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=622.md)
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 211](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth +15 (expert)
Description The spiritual imprints of long-dead explorers haunt this aiudara, manifesting as a trio of astral wraiths who guard the gateway jealously.

---

Disable DC 25 [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md) (trained) to exorcise one of the wraiths or DC 25 [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md) (expert) to erase one of the wraith’s astral sigils; three successful checks are required to disable the haunt
Forceful Screech [reaction] ([divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md)) Trigger A creature comes within 30 feet of the aiudara; Effect Three aiudara wraiths screech in unison and manifest to protect the arch. Each creature within 30 feet must attempt a DC 27 Will save. The haunt rolls initiative.
Success The creature is unaffected.
Failure The creature takes 2d8+9 force damage.
Critical Failure The creature takes 4d8+9 force damage and is [fatigued](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=73.md).

---

Routine (3 actions; [divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md), [teleportation](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=710.md)) With spectral claws, the aiudara wraiths hurl trespassers through time and space. Each of the three wraiths has 1 action which it can use to make a forceful hand [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) against a creature within 60 feet. The haunt loses 1 action for each successful check to disable the haunt.
When the haunt rolls a critical hit to Strike a creature, the creature is instantly shunted to an empty space within 30 feet. The haunt chooses which space the creature is transported to.
Melee [one-action] forceful hand +17 ([magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)), Damage 2d8+9 force; no multiple attack penalty

---

Reset The aiudara wraiths disperse after 1 minute, but the haunt resets after 24 hours.
