# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Leshy Familiar](./Feats.aspx-ID=4710.md) Feat 1

[Druid](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=583.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 127](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites leaf order

---

You call a minor spirit of nature into a plant body, creating a leshy companion to aid you in your spellcasting. You gain a familiar, which has your choice of either the [plant](../Familiar/Familiars.aspx-ID=120.md) or [fungus](../Familiar/Familiars.aspx-ID=114.md) familiar ability; this doesn't count against your usual limit of familiar abilities (typically 2). The spirit you call has a more tenuous connection to its plant body than fully independent leshies, so it is Tiny in size like other familiars.
