# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Deepening Devotion](./Feats.aspx-ID=8097.md) Feat 10

Archetype
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 219](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Archetype [Draconic Acolyte](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=333.md)
Prerequisites [Draconic Acolyte Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8090.md)

---

Your investment in your draconic gift has unlocked greater power. When your spectral dragon shares your space, the status bonus to saves against [sleep](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=696.md) and [paralysis](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=85.md) increases to +2.

In addition, you can use Draconic Salvation if you succeeded or failed on your saving throw, not only if you critically failed.
