# [Titanic Flytrap](./Hazards.aspx-ID=485.md)Hazard 4

Environmental
Source [GM Core pg. 105](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 25 (trained)
Description On the surface, a titanic flytrap appears to be a patch of the more common flytrap plant, but beneath murky waters it hides a far larger set of jaws, reaching 10 feet across, reinforced with woody branches and lined with paralytic hairs.

---

Disable DC 22 [Survival](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=49.md) (trained) to mislead the flytrap’s sense of weight and pressure
AC 21, Fort +15, Ref +8
HP 56 (BT 28); Immunities [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md), Resistances [acid 20](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=524.md), [fire 10](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=604.md)
Snap Shut [reaction] Trigger A Small or Medium creature moves into a square that is within reach of the flytrap’s hidden jaws; Effect The flytrap’s jaws snap shut, making a jaws [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) against the triggering creature.

Melee [one-action] jaws +17, Effect devour

Devour The target is trapped by the flytrap’s jaws, gaining the [grabbed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=77.md) condition until it [Escapes](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2296.md) (DC 21). Additionally, it is exposed to the titanic flytrap toxin from the hundreds of tiny hairs that line the inside of its leaves. If the flytrap’s jaws Strike was a critical success, the target takes a –2 circumstance penalty to its saving throws against this poison. At the end of each of the target’s turns that it remains grabbed, the target takes 3d6 [acid](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=524.md) damage.

Titanic Flytrap Toxin ([contact](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=565.md), [poison](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=669.md)) Saving Throw DC 21 Fortitude; Maximum Duration 4 rounds; Stage 1 2d6 poison damage and [stunned](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=93.md) 1 (1 round); Stage 2 3d6 poison damage and stunned 2 (1 round); Stage 3 4d6 poison damage and [paralyzed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=85.md) (1 round)

---

Reset 1 hour (or longer, after a large meal)
