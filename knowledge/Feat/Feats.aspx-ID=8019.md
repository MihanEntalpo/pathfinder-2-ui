# [Grief's Fury](./Feats.aspx-ID=8019.md) [reaction] Feat 10

## This Feat may contain spoilers from the Myth-Speakers Adventure Path

Source [Pathfinder #218: Titanbane pg. 68](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=274.md)
Archetype [Warrior Of Legend](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=286.md)
Class [Fighter](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=35.md)
Frequency once per day
Prerequisites [Hero-God's Bond](./Feats.aspx-ID=8017.md)
Trigger Your bonded partner gains the [dying](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=69.md) condition or their dying value would increase.

---

A lost love. A broken [spear](../WeaponGroups/WeaponGroups.aspx-ID=14.md). Rage burning through a hero-god. When a myth speaks of a wounded partner, the hero-god often rejects the future. Too many find themselves on a battlefield, clutching a body that they swore to protect. In that moment, the wellspring of power ignites.

Increase the value of your [doomed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=67.md) condition by 1, to a maximum of doomed 3. You gain a +2 status bonus to attack rolls against any creature that attacked or dealt damage to your partner in the last minute. If you reduce a creature to 0 HP that attacked or damaged your partner, you gain temporary Hit Points equal to twice their level. These benefits last until your bonded partner is no longer [unconscious](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=95.md) or for 1 minute, whichever duration is shorter.
