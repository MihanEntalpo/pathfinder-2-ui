# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Betraying Shank](./Feats.aspx-ID=8135.md) [one-action] Feat 6

Archetype
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 243](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Archetype [Twilight Speaker](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=337.md)
Prerequisites [Twilight Speaker Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8132.md)
Requirements You are within melee reach of a target, but it is not in combat with you.

---

While your usual diplomacy is well intentioned, you know that sometimes those same skills will need to be turned to deception. In a flash, you draw a sheathed or concealed [agile](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=526.md) or [finesse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=602.md) weapon and make a melee Strike. The target is [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) against your Strike. You then roll [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38.md) for initiative.
