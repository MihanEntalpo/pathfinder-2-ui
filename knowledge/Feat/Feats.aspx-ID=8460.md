You connect with the fey trickster within yourself to momentarily vanish from sight. This has the effects of [invisibility](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1577.md).

## Traits

Archetype: This feat belongs to an archetype.

Illusion: Effects and magic items with this trait involve false sensory stimuli.

Primal: This magic comes from the primal tradition, connecting to the natural world and instinct. Anything with this trait is magical.

A creature with this trait is primarily constituted of or has a strong connection to primal magic.

Subtle: A spell with the subtle trait can be cast without incantations and doesn't have obvious manifestations.
