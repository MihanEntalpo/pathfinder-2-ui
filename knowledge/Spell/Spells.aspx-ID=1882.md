# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Patron's Puppet [free-action] Focus 1

Uncommon [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md) Hex [Witch](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=734.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 385](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Trigger Your turn begins

---

At your unspoken plea, your patron temporarily assumes control over your familiar. You Command your familiar, allowing it to take its normal actions this turn. Your Command does not have the [auditory](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=541&Redirected=1.md) or concentrate traits; your patron simply moves its agent directly.
