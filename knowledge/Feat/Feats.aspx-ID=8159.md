# [Let Death Be My Weapon](./Feats.aspx-ID=8159.md) Feat 16

[Mythic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=835.md)
Source [Pathfinder Adventure Path #219: Lord of the Trinity Star pg. 78](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=283.md)
Archetype [Heroic Scion](../MythicDestiny/MythicDestinies.aspx-ID=339.md)
Prerequisites [Heroic Scion Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8151.md)

---

You recall your death in vivid detail, and the fear, pain, and uncertainty you suffered are blunted by the truth of your reincarnation. Pain and agony brought on by your foes can be endured and even turned back upon them when you feel yourself at death’s door. Whenever you have the [doomed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=67.md), [dying](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=69.md), or [wounded](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=99.md) condition, you gain that condition’s value as a status bonus to damage rolls when you make melee or ranged weapon Strikes. If you have multiple conditions, only the highest value condition counts for setting the amount of this damage bonus. You also gain the following free action.

### Share Death's Promises [free-action]

Source [Pathfinder Adventure Path #219: Lord of the Trinity Star pg. 78](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=283.md)
Trigger Your turn begins;
Requirements You are gaining a bonus to damage from Let Death Be My Weapon;

---

Spend a [Mythic Point](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=3323.md).

You gain a status bonus to damage equal to double the bonus you're receiving from Let Death Be My Weapon that lasts for 1 minute. During this time, this status bonus doesn’t decrease or increase if the value of your [doomed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=67.md), [dying](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=69.md), or [wounded](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=99.md) condition decreases or increases.

## Traits

Mythic: Options with this trait grant or utilize mythic power. Feats with the mythic trait can only be taken by mythic characters, who are typically characters with a mythic Calling.

Monsters with the mythic trait have access to a pool of Mythic Points and are particularly strong for creatures of their level. Many mythic monsters are either resistant or entirely immune to attacks from non-mythic creatures and weapons.

Spells with the mythic trait require the expenditure of a Mythic Point in order to be cast, and items with the mythic trait require the expenditure of a Mythic Point in order to use their activated abilities.

Weapons with the mythic trait overcome the resistances and immunities of mythic monsters.
