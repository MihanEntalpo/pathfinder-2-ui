## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Unique [Grimoire](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=387.md) Magical
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 306](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 700 gp
Bulk L

---

This well-worn manuscript is side stitched with fading lilac-colored thread. It’s filled with poems written long ago by a philosopher named Seiji Juubun, containing a lifetime of wisdom and presenting information and advice on a tremendous breadth of topics. Additional rules for grimoires appear here.

Activate—Poetic Spellcasting [one-action] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [emotion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=590.md), [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md), [spellshape](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=513.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect If your next action is to [Cast a Spell](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2734.md) that has the linguistic trait and that targets 1 creature, you’re overtaken by Juubun’s spirit, and the spoken elements of your spell become impressively poetic. If the target is an ally, your words inspire them and they gain 10 temporary Hit Points that last for 1 minute. If the targeted creature is an enemy and the spell requires a saving throw, and if the enemy fails its saving throw, they become [sickened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=91.md) 1 as a result of despair and sadness.
