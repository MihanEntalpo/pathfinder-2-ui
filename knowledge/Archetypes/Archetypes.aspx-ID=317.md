# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Splinter of Finality](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=317.md)

Source [Shining Kingdoms pg. 124](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=265.md)
During the events of the [Night of the Gray Death](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=99.md), the [final blade](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=1094.md) known as Silent Lenore was overwhelmed by a surge of psychic energy that shattered it into 13 fragments. While many final blades since recovered have been rendered inert and harmless by means of the [blunt the final blade](../Ritual/Rituals.aspx-ID=69.md) ritual, the remnants of Silent Lenore—and perhaps some other final blades still unaccounted for—were recovered in secret and crafted into powerful talismans of cruelty.

The splinter of finality is an archetype artifact, a type of powerful item that’s described [here](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=1961.md).

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Spectral Dagger [one-action] Feat 2

Archetype [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Shining Kingdoms pg. 124](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=265.md)
Archetype [Splinter of Finality](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=317.md)
Requirements You have a hand free and aren’t already wielding a spectral dagger.

---

By touching the splinter of finality in your neck, you conjure a spectral dagger into your hand that resembles the splinter wrapped in a crude hilt. This weapon acts like a [+1](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2830.md) [ghost touch](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2840.md) [dagger](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=358.md), and each successful [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) with it deals 1 additional [spirit](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=737.md) damage. If the attack is a critical hit, this damage increases to 1d6. If the spectral dagger ever leaves your hand, it vanishes and you must spend an action to conjure it again. If this occurs as the result of you making a ranged thrown Strike with the dagger, you resolve the attack before the weapon vanishes.

You can increase the power of your spectral dagger with runes like you can other weapons, transferring the rune from a runestone or other weapon by meditating over the rune as a downtime activity that takes 1 day.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Soul Bleed Feat 6

Archetype
Source [Shining Kingdoms pg. 125](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=265.md)
Archetype [Splinter of Finality](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=317.md)
Prerequisites Spectral Dagger

---

Your blade can rupture an enemy’s very soul, draining away its essence to empower you. When you score a critical hit with your spectral dagger, you deal 1d6 [persistent spirit damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86.md). If the target wasn’t already taking persistent spirit damage when you scored the critical hit, you gain temporary Hit Points equal to the spirit damage dealt by the attack that last until the beginning of your next turn.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Soul Well [two-actions] Feat 8

Archetype [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Occult
Source [Shining Kingdoms pg. 125](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=265.md)
Archetype [Splinter of Finality](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=317.md)
Frequency once per hour

---

Your brush your fingers across your splinter of finality, momentarily creating a metaphysical vortex from which mortal spirits struggle to escape. For the next minute, [incorporeal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=632.md) [undead](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=722.md) treat all squares within 30 feet of you as [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366.md) and living creatures within the same area die from the [dying](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=69.md) condition at dying 5 rather than dying 4. As it comes close to tasting a living soul, the splinter of finality is empowered. If a living creature’s dying value increases while within your Soul Well, your spectral dagger deals one additional weapon die of damage until the end of your next turn.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Soul Oubliette [reaction] Feat 20

Archetype [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Occult [Unholy](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=521.md)
Source [Shining Kingdoms pg. 125](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=265.md)
Archetype [Splinter of Finality](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=317.md)
Prerequisites Spectral Dagger
Trigger You reduce a creature to 0 Hit Points with an attack from your spectral dagger.
Requirements Your splinter of finality's doesn’t contain a trapped soul.

---

Your mortal strike wrenches the soul from your victim’s body and imprisons it in your splinter of finality, with the effects of [seize soul](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1664.md). The splinter of finality can only hold one soul at a time, but you can release a trapped soul as a free action. If you don’t free a soul willingly, it escapes automatically after 24 hours. However, if you touch the splinter of finality to an unbroken final blade while a soul is trapped within, the soul is transferred to the [final blade](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=1094.md) and imprisoned indefinitely.

While your splinter of finality contains a trapped soul, you gain fast healing 15.
