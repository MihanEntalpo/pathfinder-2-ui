# Swashbuckler Styles

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 160](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Your own distinctive style lets you gracefully handle any situation. Choose a swashbuckler’s style. This style determines the additional actions you use to gain panache and makes you trained in the skill related to that action.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Battledancer

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 160](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
To you, a fight is a kind of performance art, and you command your foes’ attention with mesmerizing motions. You are trained in [Performance](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=45.md) and gain the [Fascinating Performance](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=5147.md) skill feat. When you [Perform](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2401.md), the action gains the [bravado](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=801.md) trait.

### Exemplary Finisher Level 9

You can [Step](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2304.md) as a free action immediately after the finisher.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Braggart

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 160](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
You boast, taunt, and psychologically needle your foes. You are trained in [Intimidation](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=40.md). When you [Demoralize](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2395.md), the action gains the [bravado](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=801.md) trait.

### Exemplary Finisher Level 9

If the foe was temporarily immune to your
Demoralize, their temporary immunity ends.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Fencer

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 161](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
You move carefully, feinting and creating false openings to lead your foes into inopportune attacks. You are trained in [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38.md). When you [Create a Diversion](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2387.md) or [Feint](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2390.md), the action gains the [bravado](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=801.md) trait.

### Exemplary Finisher Level 9

The foe is [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) until your next turn.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Gymnast

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 161](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
You reposition, maneuver, and bewilder your foes with daring feats of physical prowess. You are trained in [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md). When you [Grapple](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2376.md), [Reposition](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2379.md), [Shove](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2380.md), or [Trip](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2382.md), the action gains the [bravado](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=801.md) trait.

### Exemplary Finisher Level 9

If the foe is [grabbed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=77.md), [restrained](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=90.md), or [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88.md), you gain a circumstance bonus to the damage roll equal to double the number of weapon damage dice.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Rascal

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 161](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
You aren’t afraid to use underhanded tactics to get the edge over your opponents. You are trained in [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) and gain the Dirty Trick general feat. When you use Dirty Trick, the action gains the [bravado](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=801.md) trait.

### Exemplary Finisher Level 9

The foe takes a –10 circumstance penalty to its speed until the start of your next turn.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Wit

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 161](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
You are friendly, clever, and full of humor, always knowing just what to say. Your witticisms leave foes unprepared for the skill and speed of your attacks. You are trained in [Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md) and gain the Bon Mot skill feat. When you use Bon Mot, the action gains the [bravado](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=801.md) trait.

### Exemplary Finisher Level 9

The foe takes a –2 circumstance penalty to attack rolls against you until the start of your next turn.
