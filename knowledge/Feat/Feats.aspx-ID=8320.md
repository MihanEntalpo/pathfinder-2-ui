# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Thoughtform Summoning](./Feats.aspx-ID=8320.md) Feat 4

[Psychic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=894.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 25](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

---

You can create a prototypical creature from the depths of your imagination instead of calling one from elsewhere. Whenever you summon a creature, you can choose to instead create a psychic construct of that creature. This grants it resistance to physical damage equal to half its level, weakness 5 to force and mental damage, and the ability to pass through enemies' spaces without needing to [Tumble Through](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2370.md) (though it can't end its turn in an occupied space).

## Traits

Psychic: This indicates abilities from the [psychic](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=68.md) class.
