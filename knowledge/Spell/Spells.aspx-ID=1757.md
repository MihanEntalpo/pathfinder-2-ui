# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Wave of Despair [two-actions] Spell 5

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Emotion [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Mental
Source [Player Core pg. 369](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Bloodline diabolic
Deities [Ahriman](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=563.md), Chamiaholom, Charon, [Gaasham](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=716.md), Groetus, Haborym, Mrtyu, Nyarlathotep (The Faceless Sphinx), Ozranvial, Sifkesh, The Lost Prince
Area 30-foot cone
Defense Will; Duration 1 or more rounds

---

You inflict despair on creatures in the area. The effects for each creature are determined by its Will save.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
Success For 1 round, the creature can't use reactions and must attempt another save at the start of its turn; on a failure, it is slowed 1 for that turn as it sobs uncontrollably.
Failure As success, but the duration is 1 minute.
Critical Failure As failure, and the creature is automatically slowed 1 for 1 minute.

---

Heightened (7th) The area increases to a 60-foot cone.
