## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Uncommon Illusion Magical
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 302](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 1,300 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L

---

This folding handheld paper fan appears blank when first found, but if the wielder unfolds its leaves as an [Interact](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2297.md) action, they display an ink-brush painting of the wielder’s choice. The fan must be closed and opened again to display a different painting. While held, the fan of falling words grants you a +2 item bonus to [Performance](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=45.md) checks to act or perform comedy, orate, or sing.

Activate—Whispered World [two-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect You manifest the tapestry of your words, causing the image you depict on the fan to leap off its leaves. The fan casts [illusory scene](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1570.md) to your specifications (DC 27 Will save). While this only takes 3 actions to [perform](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2401.md) (as opposed to the spell’s normal 10 minutes of casting), the effect has no range, and the 50-foot burst it creates is centered on you.
