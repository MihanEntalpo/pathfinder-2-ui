The sturdy head of this +3 major striking flaming shock orichalcum warhammer is shaped like a blazing comet.

Activate—Comet Fall [reaction] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md)); Trigger Your attack roll with the sky hammer is a critical success; Effect A 6th-rank arcane [fireball](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=604.md) spell explodes, centered on the sky hammer. The spell DC is 45. You are immune to the fireball's effect, though your allies are not.
