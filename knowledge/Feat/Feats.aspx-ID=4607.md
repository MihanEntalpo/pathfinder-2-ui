# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Know-It-All](./Feats.aspx-ID=4607.md) Feat 8

[Bard](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=546.md) [Thaumaturge](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=895.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 104](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetypes [Oatia Skysage](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=335.md)*
Prerequisites [Bard] [enigma muse](../Muses/Muses.aspx-ID=1.md)
* This archetype offers Know-It-All at a different level than displayed here.

---

When you succeed at a check to Recall Knowledge, you gain additional information or context. When you critically succeed at a check to Recall Knowledge, you get additional information or context or can ask an additional follow-up question (the GM chooses which).

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more archetypes in addition to the listed classes. When selected this way, the feat does not have its class traits.

## Traits

Thaumaturge: This indicates abilities from the [thaumaturge](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=69.md) class.
