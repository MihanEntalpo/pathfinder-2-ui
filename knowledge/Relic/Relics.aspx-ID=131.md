# [Call of the Wild](./Relics.aspx-ID=131.md)Minor Gift

Source [GM Core pg. 310](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Aspect beast

---

Activate [two-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md), [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect Your relic summons a creature to fight at your side, casting summon animal with a spell rank of half the item's level rounded up. You can Sustain this activation as you would the spell.
