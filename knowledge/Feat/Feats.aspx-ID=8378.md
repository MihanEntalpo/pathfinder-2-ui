# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Thaumaturge's Demesne](./Feats.aspx-ID=8378.md) Feat 12

[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Arcane](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=534.md) [Thaumaturge](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=895.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 46](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

---

You have claimed an area or location as your demesne, granting you the ability to ward and protect it. When you select this feat, choose a demesne, an area of no more than 2,000 square feet. You must legitimately own the area or, if its a public area, you must lay claim to the area without anyone contesting it.

Once you've chosen your demesne, it's automatically protected by an arcane [peaceful bubble](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1624.md) spell with an unlimited duration, heightened to half your level rounded up and using your thaumaturge class DC in place of a spell DC, if necessary. Additionally, the demesne is attended by three [phantasmal minions](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1631.md) (Player Core 349). These phantasmal minions have an unlimited duration; you don't need to concentrate on them, and they aren't summoned minions. You can spend an action, which has the [concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) trait, to command one to perform a basic task; it continues to perform the task until the task is completed or it's commanded again.
