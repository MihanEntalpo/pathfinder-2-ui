Consisting of long steel spikes projecting from light barding, anti-dragon barding helps to protect vulnerable mounts and livestock from massive aerial predators, including dragons, griffons, and rocs. An [animal companion](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2113.md) wearing anti-dragon barding gains a +2 item bonus to its Fortitude DC and Reflex DC against attempts to [Grapple](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2376.md), [Reposition](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2379.md), [Shove](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2380.md), or [Trip](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2382.md) it, and a creature that successfully Grapples the animal companion takes 1d4 piercing damage.
