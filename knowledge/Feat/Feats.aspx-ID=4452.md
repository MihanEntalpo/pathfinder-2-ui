# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Skittering Scuttle](./Feats.aspx-ID=4452.md) Feat 9

Goblin
Source [Player Core pg. 57](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites [Goblin Scuttle](./Feats.aspx-ID=4441.md)

---

You can scuttle farther and faster when maneuvering alongside allies. When you use Goblin Scuttle, you can [Stride](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2305&Redirected=1.md) up to half your Speed instead of [Stepping](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2304&Redirected=1.md).

## Traits

Goblin: A creature with this trait is a small, frantic goblinoid. Goblins tend to have darkvision. An ability with this trait can be used or chosen only by goblins. A weapon with this trait is created and used by goblins.
