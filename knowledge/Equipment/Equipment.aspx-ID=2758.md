You need a thieves' toolkit to Pick Locks or Disable Devices (of some types) using the [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50&Redirected=1.md) skill. If your thieves' toolkit is broken, you can repair it by replacing the lock picks with replacement picks appropriate to your toolkit; this doesn't require using the Repair action. If you wear your thieves' toolkit, you can draw and replace it as part of the action that uses it.
