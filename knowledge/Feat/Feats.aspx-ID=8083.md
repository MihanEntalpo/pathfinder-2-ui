# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Energize Bite](./Feats.aspx-ID=8083.md) [free-action] Feat 9

[Dragonblood](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=794.md)
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 217](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

Prerequisites [Breath of the Dragon](./Feats.aspx-ID=5730.md); [jaws unarmed attack](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=356.md)
Requirements Your most recent action was to use Breath of the Dragon.

---

The deadly energy of your breath remains in your jaws to empower your attacks. Until the end of your turn, your [jaws](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=356.md) unarmed attack deals an extra 1d6 damage of a type matching that of your [draconic benefactor.](../DraconicBenefactors/DraconicBenefactors.aspx.md) This ability has the trait associated with the type of damage it deals.

## Traits

Dragonblood: A creature with this trait has the dragonblood versatile heritage. These creatures are born of draconic lineage and often have draconic appearances.
