# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Ki Strike](./Feats.aspx-ID=433.md) Feat 1

### Legacy Content

Monk
Source [Core Rulebook pg. 158](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)

---

Your study of the flow of mystical energy allows you to harness it into your physical strikes. You gain the ki strike ki spell and a focus pool of 1 Focus Point. The rules for ki spells are summarized in the Key Terms section on the monk class page, and the full rules for focus spells appear here.

## Ki Strike Leads To...

Elemental Fist, Ki Cutting Sight, Sacred Ki
