# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Rock Runner](./Feats.aspx-ID=4391.md) Feat 1

Dwarf
Source [Player Core pg. 44](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

Your innate connection to stone makes you adept at moving across uneven surfaces. You can ignore difficult terrain caused by stone (such as rubble or gravel) and uneven ground made of stone and earth. In addition, when you use the [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34&Redirected=1.md) skill to Balance on narrow surfaces or uneven ground made of stone or earth, you aren't [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md), and when you roll a success at one of these Acrobatics checks, you get a critical success instead.
