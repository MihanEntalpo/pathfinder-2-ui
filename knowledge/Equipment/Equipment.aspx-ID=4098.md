## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Rare Invested Magical [Tattoo](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=262.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 305](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 60 gp
Usage tattooed on the body; Bulk &mdash;

---

The spider lily tattoo marks you as a trusted member of Granny Hu’s network. This crimson tattoo fades and becomes invisible within a day of being applied, reappearing only when you Activate it, when you gain the [doomed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=67.md) condition, or when you die. The higher the doomed value, the more vivid the color. Additional rules for magical tattoos appear here.

If your tattoo is plainly visible, you gain a +1 item bonus to [Intimidation](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=40.md) checks against all creatures that can see the tattoo, but you take a –1 item penalty to [Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md) checks to [Make an Impression](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2392.md) on those who understand the actual meaning of the spider lily tattoo (including all of Willowshore’s citizens).

Activate—Reveal Tattoo [one-action] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md)); Requirements Your tattoo isn't currently visible; Effect You make the tattoo visible. You can use this activation again to make the tattoo not visible, unless you have the [doomed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=67.md) condition.

Activate—Dead Like You [reaction] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md)); Frequency once per day; Trigger An [undead](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=722.md) creature detects you for the first time, and you’re aware of the undead creature; Effect The spider lily tattoo manipulates your life force to make you appear to be [undead](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=722.md) for a short time. Attempt a [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38.md) check against the triggering undead creature’s Perception DC. On a success, the triggering undead believes you’re undead as well—a mindless undead is likely to ignore you, while a sapient undead might react with curiosity or confusion. You can continue attempting Deception checks each round as a single action to [Sustain](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2317.md) the effect for up to 1 minute.
