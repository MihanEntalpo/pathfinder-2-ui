# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Implement's Assault](./Feats.aspx-ID=8386.md) [three-actions] Feat 18

[Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Thaumaturge](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=895.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 47](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

Requirements You're holding an [implement](../Implements/Implements.aspx.md).

---

Your implement supercharges your weapon to shoot an impossible volley or carve through your foes. Make a Strike with your weapon against each enemy within 30 feet of you. You don't increase your multiple attack penalty until after making all the attacks. If your weapon is a melee weapon and any of the attacks are outside your reach, you [Release](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2300.md) the weapon before the Strikes, and it returns to your grasp after all of them. If your hands are full when the weapon returns, it falls to the ground in your space.

## Traits

Magical: Something with the magical trait is imbued with magical energies not tied to a specific tradition of magic. Some items or effects are closely tied to a particular tradition of magic. In these cases, the item has the arcane, divine, occult, or primal trait instead of the magical trait. Any of these traits indicate that the item is magical.

Thaumaturge: This indicates abilities from the [thaumaturge](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=69.md) class.
