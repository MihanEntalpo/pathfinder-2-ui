# Additional Feats

Source [Player Core pg. 215](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Some archetypes include a list of “Additional Feats” that appear in other sources. The list includes each feat’s level, which might be different than normal when gained from the archetype. You can take the feat as an archetype feat of that level, meaning it counts toward the number of feats required by the archetype’s dedication feat. When selected this way, a feat that normally has a class’s trait (such as the fighter trait) doesn’t have that class trait.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Mauler Dedication Feat 2

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Dedication](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=572.md)
Source [Player Core 2 pg. 207](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetype [Mauler](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=256.md)
Prerequisites Strength +2

---

You specialize in melee weapons that require two hands. You have familiarity with all melee weapons that require two hands to wield or have the [two-hand](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=718.md) trait. For the purposes of proficiency, you treat any of these that are martial weapons as simple weapons and any that are advanced weapons as martial weapons. If you are at least an expert in such a weapon, you gain access to the critical specialization effect with that weapon.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Slam Down [two-actions] Feat 4

[Fighter](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=601.md) Flourish
Source [Player Core pg. 143](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetypes [Mauler](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=256.md), [Rivethun Invoker](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=309.md), [Thlipit Contestant](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=224.md)
Prerequisites trained in [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md)

---

You make an attack to knock a foe off balance, then follow up immediately with a sweep to topple them. Make a melee Strike. If it hits and deals damage, you can attempt an Athletics check to Trip the creature you hit. If you’re wielding a two-handed melee weapon, you can ignore Trip’s requirement that you have a hand free. Both attacks count toward your multiple attack penalty, but the penalty doesn’t increase until after you’ve made both of them.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Vicious Swing [two-actions] Feat 4

[Fighter](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=601.md) Flourish
Source [Player Core pg. 141](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetypes [Battle Harbinger](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=304.md), [Mauler](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=256.md)

---

You unleash a particularly powerful attack that clobbers your foe but leaves you a bit unsteady. Make a melee Strike. This counts as two attacks when calculating your multiple attack penalty. If this Strike hits, you deal an extra die of weapon damage. If you’re at least 10th level, increase this to two extra dice, and if you’re at least 18th level, increase it to three extra dice.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Clear the Way [two-actions] Feat 6

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Player Core 2 pg. 207](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetypes [Mauler](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=256.md), [Warrior Of Legend](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=286.md)
Prerequisites Mauler Dedication
Requirements You’re wielding a melee weapon in two hands.

---

You put your body behind your massive weapon and swing, shoving enemies to clear a wide path. You attempt to [Shove](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2380.md) up to five creatures adjacent to you, rolling a separate [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) check for each target and ignoring the requirement that you have a hand free. Then [Stride](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2305.md) up to half your Speed. This movement doesn’t trigger reactions from any of the creatures you successfully Shoved. Each attack counts toward your [multiple attack penalty](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2289.md), but don’t increase your penalty until you have made all your attacks.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Shoving Sweep [reaction] Feat 8

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Player Core 2 pg. 207](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetype [Mauler](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=256.md)
Prerequisites Mauler Dedication; expert in [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md)
Trigger An enemy within your reach leaves a square during a [move](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=658.md) action it’s using.
Requirements You’re wielding a melee weapon in two hands.

---

You swing your weapon at your foe, rebuffing them back. You attempt to [Shove](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2380.md) the triggering creature, ignoring the requirement that you have a hand free. Unless you critically succeed at your check, the creature continues its movement after the Shove.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Crashing Slam Feat 12

[Fighter](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=601.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 147](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetypes [Mauler](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=256.md), [Rivethun Invoker](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=309.md), [Thlipit Contestant](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=224.md)
Prerequisites [Slam Down](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=4794.md)

---

You can dash your foe to the ground with a single blow. When you use Slam Down, instead of making a Strike followed by a Trip, you can attempt a single Strike. If the Strike you make with Slam Down hits, you can automatically get a critical success on your Trip instead of rolling a check. (Both the Strike and Trip still count toward your multiple attack penalty.) If you used a two-handed melee weapon for the Strike, you can use the weapon’s damage die size instead of the regular die size for the damage from a critical Trip.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Brutal Finish [one-action] Feat 14

[Fighter](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=601.md) Press
Source [Player Core pg. 148](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetypes [Mauler](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=256.md)
Requirements You are wielding a melee weapon in two hands

---

Your final blow can make an impact even if it rebounds off a foe’s defenses. Make a Strike with a melee weapon you’re wielding in two hands. After the Strike, your turn ends. The Strike deals one additional weapon damage die, or two additional weapon damage dice if you’re at least 18th level. The Strike also gains the following failure effect.

Failure You deal damage equal to one weapon damage die of the required weapon. Increase this to two dice if you’re at least 18th level.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Hammer Quake [three-actions] Feat 14

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Flourish](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=606.md)
Source [Player Core 2 pg. 207](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetype [Mauler](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=256.md)
Prerequisites Mauler Dedication
Requirements You’re wielding a non-[finesse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=602.md) melee weapon in two hands.

---

You smash the ground with your weapon, knocking nearby creatures to the ground. Choose a square within your reach, including your own space. If there’s an enemy in the chosen square, you can [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) that enemy with your two-handed weapon. Regardless of whether you attempted a Strike, you then attempt to [Trip](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2382.md) every enemy in the chosen square plus each square adjacent to that square, ignoring Trip’s requirement that you have a hand free.

Hammer Quake counts as three attacks toward your [multiple attack penalty](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2289.md), but the penalty doesn’t increase until after you’ve made the Strike, if any, and all the Trip attempts.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Unbalancing Sweep [three-actions] Feat 14

[Barbarian](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=813.md) [Flourish](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=606.md)
Source [Player Core 2 pg. 83](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetypes [Mauler](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=256.md), [Warrior Of Legend](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=286.md)

---

You make a great sweep with your weapon or fists about yourself, knocking creatures off their feet and away from you. Choose up to three enemies within your reach and choose whether to [Shove](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2380.md) or [Trip](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2382.md) all three of them. Whichever of the two options you choose, roll a separate [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) check against each enemy, performing the same action against each enemy. Each attempt counts toward your multiple attack penalty, but don't increase your penalty until you have made all the attempts.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Avalanche Strike [three-actions] Feat 16

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Flourish](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=606.md)
Source [Player Core 2 pg. 207](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetype [Mauler](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=256.md)
Prerequisites Mauler Dedication
Requirements You are wielding a melee weapon in two hands.

---

You attack all nearby adversaries. Make a melee [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) with your two-handed weapon against each enemy within its melee reach. Each attack counts toward your [multiple attack penalty](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2289.md), but don’t increase your penalty until you have made all your attacks.
