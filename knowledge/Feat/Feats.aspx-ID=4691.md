# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Premonition of Clarity](./Feats.aspx-ID=4691.md) [reaction] Feat 14

[Cleric](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=554.md) Fortune
Source [Player Core pg. 120](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetypes [Mortal Herald](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=305.md)*, [Seneschal](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=284.md)*
Frequency once per hour
Trigger You fail a saving throw against a mental effect
* This archetype offers Premonition of Clarity at a different level than displayed here.

---

Your deity sends you a vision of faith to steel you against mental attacks. Reroll the triggering saving throw with a +2 circumstance bonus. You must use the second result, even if it’s worse.

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more archetypes in addition to the listed classes. When selected this way, the feat does not have its class traits.

## Premonition of Clarity Leads To...

[Shared Clarity](./Feats.aspx-ID=4703.md)

## Traits

Fortune: A fortune effect beneficially alters how you roll your dice. You can never have more than one fortune effect alter a single roll. If multiple fortune effects would apply, you have to pick which to use. If a fortune effect and a misfortune effect would apply to the same roll, the two cancel each other out, and you roll normally.
