# [Shadow Guards](./Hazards.aspx-ID=595.md)Hazard 2

[Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Shadow](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=693.md) [Trap](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=715.md)
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 40](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth +11 (trained) to notice the runes concealed among the drawings
Description Shadowy caricatures of elves peel themselves from the floor and attack everyone in the room.

---

Disable DC 16 [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md) to move through the room without touching the runes, followed by DC 18 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) (trained) to carve them out without triggering them; or [dispel magic](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1493.md) (1st rank; counteract DC 16) to counteract the runes
AC 18, Fort +11, Ref +11
Hardness 8, HP 32
Shadow Rush [reaction] Trigger A creature enters the room; Effect After waiting 1 round, the trap animates its shadowy stick figures and makes a painted spear Strike against each creature in the room. The trap rolls initiative.

---

Routine (1 action) The drawings stab at each character in the room with spears drawn from sharp lines.
Melee [one-action] painted spear +11, Damage 1d10+4 piercing

---

Reset The trap deactivates and resets after 1 minute. The drawings cannot pursue characters out of the room.
