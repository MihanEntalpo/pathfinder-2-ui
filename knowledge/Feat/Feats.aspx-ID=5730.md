# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Breath of the Dragon](./Feats.aspx-ID=5730.md) [two-actions] Feat 1

[Dragonblood](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=794.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [Player Core 2 pg. 45](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)

---

Tapping into the physiology of your draconic ancestor, you can exhale a torrent of energy in a 15-foot cone or a 30-foot line, dealing 1d4 damage. Each creature in the area must attempt a basic saving throw against the higher of your class DC or spell DC. You can't use this ability again for 1d4 rounds.

At 3rd level and every 2 levels thereafter, the damage increases by 1d4. The shape of the breath, the damage type, and the saving throw match those of your draconic exemplar (see above). This ability has the trait associated with the type of damage it deals.

## Traits

Dragonblood: A creature with this trait has the dragonblood versatile heritage. These creatures are born of draconic lineage and often have draconic appearances.

Magical: Something with the magical trait is imbued with magical energies not tied to a specific tradition of magic. Some items or effects are closely tied to a particular tradition of magic. In these cases, the item has the arcane, divine, occult, or primal trait instead of the magical trait. Any of these traits indicate that the item is magical.
