# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Boots of BoundingItem 7+

[Invested](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=637.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 286](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage worn shoes; Bulk L

---

The springy soles of these sturdy leather boots cushion your feet and make each step lighter. These boots give you a +5- foot item bonus to your Speed and a +2 item bonus to Athletics checks to [High Jump](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2377&Redirected=1.md) and [Long Jump](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2378&Redirected=1.md). In addition, when you use the Leap action, you can move 5 feet further if jumping horizontally or 3 feet higher if jumping vertically.
