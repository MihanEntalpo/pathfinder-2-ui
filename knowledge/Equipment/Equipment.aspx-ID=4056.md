## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Uncommon Alchemical Consumable Healing [Processed](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=487.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 295](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 28 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Activate 10 minutes ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md))

---

This slender bar of pine-scented soap can only be activated when you’re immersed in water. Upon activation, the soap covers you in a sudsy foam that quickly fades, filling you with energy and soothing away aches and pains. It immediately restores 10 Hit Points and removes the [fatigued](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=73.md) condition. If you begin an 8-hour period of rest immediately after using invigorating soap, you regain an additional 10 Hit Points from resting.
