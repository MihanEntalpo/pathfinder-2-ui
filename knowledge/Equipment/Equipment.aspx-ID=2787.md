# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)AntimagicItem 15

[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 226](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Price 6,500 gp
Usage etched onto armor; Bulk &mdash;

---

This intricate rune displaces spell energy, granting you a +1 status bonus to saving throws against magical effects.

Activate—Antimagic Armor [reaction] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md)); Frequency once per day; Trigger A spell targets you or includes you in its area; Effect The armor attempts to counteract the triggering spell with the effect of a 7th-rank dispel magic spell and a counteract modifier of +26.

Craft Requirements Supply one casting of dispel magic.
