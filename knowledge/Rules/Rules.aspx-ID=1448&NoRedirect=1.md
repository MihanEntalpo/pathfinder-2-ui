Advanced Player's Guide

Chapter 6: Items

Source [Advanced Player's Guide pg. 247](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=39.md)

The second portion of this chapter consists of a catalog of new alchemical items, snares, and both consumable and permanent magic items, including runes.

## Alchemical Items

Alchemical items are consumable, non-magical items that rely on the properties of volatile alchemical reagents to produce their effects. Rules for alchemical items, including the rules for [bombs](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-Category=6&Subcategory=7.md), [elixirs](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-Category=6&Subcategory=8.md), and [poisons](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-Category=6&Subcategory=9.md), are located here.

## Snares

[Snares](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-Category=31.md) are small, simple traps to damage or hamper your foes that you can create and deploy quickly enough to utilize on a battlefield. Snares are nonmagical consumable items. The full rules for creating and using snares are located here.

## Magic Items

The remainder of this chapter contains the descriptions of dozens of new magic items. These items are divided into two subcategories for ease of reference: consumable magic items, including [ammunition](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-Category=15&Subcategory=16.md), [oils](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-Category=15&Subcategory=17.md), [potions](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-Category=15&Subcategory=18.md), and [talismans](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-Category=15&Subcategory=19.md). Permanent items consist of armor, [held items](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-Category=21.md), [runes](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-Category=23.md), shields, [staves](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-Category=32.md), [wands](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-Category=34.md), weapons, and [worn items](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-Category=41.md). Items are organized alphabetically within each section. Rules for activating and investing items, for sustaining and dismissing activations, and for reading an item's stat block are located here.
