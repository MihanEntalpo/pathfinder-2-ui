# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Gouging Claw [two-actions] Cantrip 1

[Attack](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=540&Redirected=1.md) [Cantrip](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=550.md) [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Morph
Source [Player Core pg. 333](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Range touch; Targets 1 creature
Defense AC

---

You temporarily morph your limb into a clawed appendage. Make a melee spell attack roll against your target's AC. If you hit, you deal your choice of 2d6 slashing damage or 2d6 piercing damage, plus 2 [persistent bleed damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86&Redirected=1.md). On a critical success, you deal double damage and double bleed damage.

---

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 1d6 and the persistent bleed damage increases by 1.
