# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Musagani](./Deities.aspx-ID=726.md)

Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 292](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Nethys Note: This deity entry contains spoilers for Season of Ghosts. Read at your own risk.

Immense Musagani, the largest of all known nindorus, led a ninsat that infiltrated the nation of Zi Ha in 6778 ic, during which Musagani claimed hundreds of victims before finally meeting an end at the hands of the City of Bone and Juniper’s giants. Prior to this banishment, Musagani absorbed the souls of 66 giants, swelling to behemoth proportions that no nindoru has replicated in the centuries since.

Category Nindoru Ascetics
Edicts disrupt organizations, slay creatures larger than you, prevent resurrection or reincarnation
Anathema provide aid to a [samsaran](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=88.md), restore a non-nindoru creature to life, willingly reduce your size
Areas of Concern chaos, consumption of souls, immense monsters, warfare
Religious Symbol fist clenching a shattering skull
