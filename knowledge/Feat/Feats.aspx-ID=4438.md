# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Burn It!](./Feats.aspx-ID=4438.md) Feat 1

Goblin
Source [Player Core pg. 56](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

Fire fascinates you. Your spells and alchemical items that deal fire damage gain a status bonus to damage equal to half the spell's rank or one-quarter the item's level (minimum bonus +1). You also gain a +1 status bonus to any persistent fire damage you deal.

## Traits

Goblin: A creature with this trait is a small, frantic goblinoid. Goblins tend to have darkvision. An ability with this trait can be used or chosen only by goblins. A weapon with this trait is created and used by goblins.
