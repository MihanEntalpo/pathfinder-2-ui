# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Torrential Backlash](./Feats.aspx-ID=8476.md) [two-actions] Feat 10

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 169](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Archetype [Curse Maelstrom](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=347.md)
Prerequisites [Curse Maelstrom Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8470.md)
Requirements You’re in a curse maelstrom state.

---

Calling upon forbidden practices, you release the curse from within yourself, allowing it free rein to destroy everything around you. All creatures within your curse maelstrom emanation except you take 1d6 void damage for each level you have, with a [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Fortitude save, using the higher of your class DC or spell DC. Your curse maelstrom state then ends.
