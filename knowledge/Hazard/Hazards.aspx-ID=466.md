# [Bloodthirsty Urge](./Hazards.aspx-ID=466.md)Hazard 10

Haunt
Source [GM Core pg. 100](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 31 (trained)
Description An object haunted by the echoes of a vicious mind attempts to kill someone who comes near.

---

Disable DC 29 [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md) (master) to exorcise the spirit or DC 31 [Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md) (expert) to talk it down
Quietus [reaction] ([death](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=571.md), [emotion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=590.md), [fear](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=598.md), [illusion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=629.md), [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md), [occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md)) Trigger A creature moves within 10 feet of the haunted object; Effect The haunt takes control of the triggering creature, forcing it to attack itself. The creature must attempt a DC 29 Will save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.
Success The target makes a [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) against itself and automatically hits; the target also becomes [frightened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=76.md) 1.
Failure The target makes a Strike against itself and automatically scores a critical hit; the target also becomes frightened 2.
Critical Failure The target attempts a Fortitude save. If the target succeeds, it is subject to the effects of a failure instead. If the target fails, it is reduced to 0 HP and dies.
