# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Tidepool Dragonet](./Heritages.aspx-ID=426.md)

Source [Draconic Codex pg. 210](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
You have a slender, eel-like body with azure scales. Your wings sweep along your body like massive fins, making you well suited for watery terrain. You gain the [amphibious](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=529.md) trait, and a swim Speed of 15 feet. You add [Thalassic](../Language/Languages.aspx-ID=113.md) to your list of known languages.
