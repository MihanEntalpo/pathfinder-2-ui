# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Planar Tether [two-actions] Spell 4

Source [Player Core pg. 350](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature
Defense Will; Duration varies

---

You stitch the target to its current plane. While the target is affected by planar tether, the spell attempts to counteract any teleportation effect that would move the target, or any effect that would transport it to a different plane. Planar tether's duration is determined by the target's Will save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.
Success The duration is 1 minute.
Failure The duration is 10 minutes.
Critical Failure The duration is 1 hour.
