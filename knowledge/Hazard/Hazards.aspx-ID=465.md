# [Armageddon Orb](./Hazards.aspx-ID=465.md)Hazard 23

Rare Magical Trap
Source [GM Core pg. 100](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 10 or [detect magic](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1485.md)
Description A roiling red orb, forged from a drop of the god [Rovagug](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=291.md)’s blood, rains fire from the sky when a specified condition is met.

---

Disable DC 48 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) (legendary) to imbue a [thieves’ toolkit](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2758.md) with aspects representing [Asmodeus](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=278.md) and [Sarenrae](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=292.md) and use them to drain away the orb’s power over 10 minutes; the character attempting this check takes 5 fire damage each round until the orb is depleted
Burn It All [reaction] ([death](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=571.md), [divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md), [fire](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=604.md)) Trigger A special condition set by the trap’s creator occurs, typically the event of their death; Effect Fire rains from the sky in a 100-mile radius, dealing 10d6 fire damage to creatures and objects in the area. Each creature or object can attempt a DC 46 basic Reflex save. Any creature reduced to 0 Hit Points by this damage dies instantly. This is not enough damage to completely burn away a forest or level an entire mountain or city, but it typically kills most creatures in the area.
