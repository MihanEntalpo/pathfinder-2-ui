# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Shifting Form [one-action] Focus 4

Uncommon [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md) Morph [Wizard](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=735.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 388](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Duration 1 minute

---

You change your body to better suit the moment. You gain one of the following abilities of your choice. You can Dismiss this spell.

- You gain a 20-foot status bonus to your Speed.
- You gain a climb or swim Speed equal to half your Speed.
- You gain darkvision.
- You gain a pair of claws. These are agile finesse unarmed attacks that deal 1d8 slashing damage.
- You gain scent 60 feet ([imprecise](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2407&Redirected=1.md)).
