# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Scoundrel's Surprise](./Feats.aspx-ID=4937.md) [one-action] Feat 4

[Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) [Rogue](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=687.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 172](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

You drop your disguise at the right moment and surprise your target. After you Impersonate someone else using a disguise kit, you can remove your disguise with a dramatic gesture. Any creatures that previously failed to see through your disguise are off-guard against the next attack you make before the end of your turn.

## Traits

Manipulate: You must physically manipulate an item or make gestures to use an action with this trait. Creatures without a suitable appendage can’t perform actions with this trait. Manipulate actions often trigger reactions.
