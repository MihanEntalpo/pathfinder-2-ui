# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Charming Push [one-action] Focus 1

Uncommon [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md) Incapacitation Mental [Wizard](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=735.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 388](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature
Defense Will; Duration until the start of your next turn

---

You push at the target's mind to deflect their ire. The target must attempt a Will save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.
Success The target takes a –1 circumstance penalty to attack rolls and damage rolls against you.
Failure The target can't use hostile actions against you.
Critical Failure The target is stunned 1 and can't use hostile actions against you.
