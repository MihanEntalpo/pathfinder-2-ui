# [Pharaoh's Ward](./Hazards.aspx-ID=475.md)Hazard 7

Magical Trap
Source [GM Core pg. 103](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 25 (expert)
Description A curse is imbued on an entryway’s threshold.

---

Disable DC 27 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) (master) to painstakingly remove the lintel without triggering the magic, or [dispel magic](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1493.md) (4th rank; counteract DC 25) to counteract the rune
Curse the Intruders [reaction] ([curse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=566.md), [divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md)) Trigger The seal on the tomb is broken from the outside; Effect Each living creature within 60 feet must succeed at a DC 23 Will save or be subjected to the pharaoh’s curse. A cursed creature takes a –2 status penalty to Fortitude saves, and any natural or magical [healing](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=623.md) it receives is halved. The curse remains until removed by [cleanse affliction](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1467.md) or similar magic.

---

Reset The trap resets when the door is shut.
