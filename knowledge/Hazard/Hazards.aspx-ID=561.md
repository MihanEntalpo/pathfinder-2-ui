# [Shaken Loose](./Hazards.aspx-ID=561.md)Hazard 10

Unique Elemental [Mythic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=835.md)
Source [Pathfinder #218: Titanbane pg. 54](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=274.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 10
Description The Granite Ox shakes off centuries of accumulated earth, flinging the PCs toward its hindquarters.

---

Shake [reaction] Trigger The Granite Ox awakens from slumber; Effect Creatures in area F2 are thrown hundreds of feet from the peak of the Ox's horn onto its back. Each creature takes falling damage as if they fell 60 feet (30 bludgeoning damage), with a 31 Reflex save. Creatures can arrest their fall with spells like gentle landing, as normal. In addition, the result determines how far the PC must travel in order to escape the Ox's tail in the Swatted Like Flies hazard.
Critical Success The PC must travel 20 feet.
Success The PC must travel 40 feet.
Failure The PC must travel 60 feet.
Critical Failure As failure, and the PC is [stunned](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=93.md) 1.
