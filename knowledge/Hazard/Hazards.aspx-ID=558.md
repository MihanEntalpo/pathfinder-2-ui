# [The Ketos](./Hazards.aspx-ID=558.md)Hazard 7

Unique [Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) Environmental [Mythic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=835.md)
Source [Pathfinder #218: Titanbane pg. 10](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=274.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth +19 (expert)
Description A shadow the size of an island appears in the water beneath the ship.

---

Disable DC 27 [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md), [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md), or [Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md) or DC 23 [Sailing Lore](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=41.md) to help the crew of the Tide Hunter escape the Ketos; three total successes are required to disable the hazard
AC 25, Fort +20, Ref +12
Tentacle HP 60
Grasp of the Depths [reaction] Trigger The Tide Hunter enters the Ketos’s hunting grounds; Effect The Ketos uses its eight tentacles to seize the ship, holding it in place. The hazard then rolls initiative.

---

Routine (3 actions) The Ketos uses its actions to make tentacle Strikes against creatures aboard the Tide Hunter or within 30 feet of the ship.
Melee tentacle +18, Damage 2d10+9 slashing; no multiple attack penalty

---

Reset If three or more of its tentacles are destroyed, the Ketos releases the Tide Hunter and disappears into the ocean’s depths to nurse its wounds. The monster does not return to this area for 24 hours.
