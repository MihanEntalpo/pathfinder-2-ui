Divine grace ensure that nothing can keep you prisoner or hold you back. You immediately escape from every magical effect that has you [immobilized](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=81&Redirected=1.md) or [grabbed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=77&Redirected=1.md) unless the effect is of a higher rank than your unimpeded stride spell. You then Stride. During this movement, you ignore [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366&Redirected=1.md) and any circumstance or status penalties to your Speed.
