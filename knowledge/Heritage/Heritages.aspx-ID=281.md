Ancestry Page [Aiuvarin](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=69.md)
You have elves, or possibly other aiuvarins, in your family tree. You have pointed ears and other telltale signs of elf heritage. You gain the elf trait, the [aiuvarin](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=515.md) trait, and low-light vision. In addition, when you gain an ancestry feat, you can choose from aiuvarin and elf feats in addition to those from your ancestry.
