# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Lion's ArmorItem 12+

[Invested](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=637.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 230](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage worn armor; Bulk 3
Base Armor [Half Plate](../Armor/Armor.aspx-ID=49.md)

---

Various parts of this +2 resilient [half plate](../Armor/Armor.aspx-ID=49.md) are forged into the shape of a lion’s head. The golden lion heads grant you a commanding presence and seem to actively growl at your enemies, granting you a +2 item bonus to Intimidation.

The lions on the armor work in tandem with the lion on a [lion's shield](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2823.md). If you critically hit with the lion’s shield using Lion Bite, the lions on the armor roar in support of the shield, causing the target of the shield’s Strike to become frightened 1.

Activate—Roar of the Pride [two-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect The lions on your armor roar, attempting to cow your enemies. Attempt a check to Demoralize each enemy within 30 feet of you. On a critical success, the target is also fleeing for 1 round.
