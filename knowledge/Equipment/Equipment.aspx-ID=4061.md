## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Uncommon Magical
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 296](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 1,250 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk 1

---

A bright, octagonal frame surrounds this circular [dawnsilver shield](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2814.md), which itself is polished to a mirrorlike sheen that reflects clear and true. While you have this standard-grade dawnsilver shield (Hardness 5, HP 20 [BT 10]) raised, you gain its circumstance bonus to saving throws against spells with the [unholy](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=521.md) or [void](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=510.md) trait.

Activate—Reflect Evil [reaction] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [fortune](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=612.md)); Frequency once per day; Trigger You attempt a saving throw against an effect with the [unholy](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=521.md) or [void](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=510.md) traits; Effect Your saving throw result is considered one degree better than the actual result. If this effect turns a success into a critical success, you can reflect some of the effect’s power as a wave of magical backlash back to the source of the triggering effect, as long as the source is within 30 feet. When you do so, the target must succeed at a DC 28 Fortitude save or become [stunned](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=93.md) 1.
