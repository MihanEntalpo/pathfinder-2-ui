# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Aerekostes (The Mentor in Blades)](./Deities.aspx-ID=699.md)

Source [Pathfinder #216: The Acropolis Pyre pg. 76](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=271.md), [Myth-Speakers Players Guide pg. 11](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=270.md)
The hero-god Ximakter carried this favorite sword into battle against the monstrous beast Ousmariku, which consumed him. Shortly after being recovered by onlookers, the sword awoke with magical intelligence and mythic power. It's unclear whether Aerekostes is a unique entity, the trapped soul of Ximakter awaiting resurrection, or something else entirely. The mystery doesn't seem to interest Aerekostes, who would rather lend themself out to aspiring heroes.
