# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Rock Dwarf](./Heritages.aspx-ID=237.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 43](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Your ancestors lived and worked among the ancient stones of the mountains or the depths of the earth. This makes you solid as a rock when you plant your feet. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to your Fortitude or Reflex DC against attempts to [Reposition](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2379.md), Shove, or Trip you. This bonus also applies to saving throws against spells or effects that attempt to force you to move or knock you [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88&Redirected=1.md). In addition, if any effect would force you to move 10 feet or more, you are moved only half the distance.
