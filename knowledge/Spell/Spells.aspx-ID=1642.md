# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Pummeling Rubble [two-actions] Spell 1

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Earth [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 351](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Deities Rivethun, The Devourer, Treasures of the Eternal Delve, Xoveron
Area 15-foot cone
Defense Reflex

---

A spray of heavy rocks flies through the air in front of you. The rubble deals 2d4 bludgeoning damage to each creature in the area. Each creature must attempt a Reflex save.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
Success The creature takes half damage.
Failure The creature takes full damage and is pushed 5 feet away from you.
Critical Failure The creature takes double damage and is pushed 10 feet away from you.

---

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 2d4.
