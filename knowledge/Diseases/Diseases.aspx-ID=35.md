# [Blinding Sickness](./Diseases.aspx-ID=35.md)Disease 7

[Disease](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=578.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 89](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Endemic to jungles of the Mwangi Expanse, blinding sickness is transmitted by dirty water or the bites of certain creatures.

Saving Throw DC 23 Fortitude; Stage 1 carrier with no effects (1 day); Stage 2 [enfeebled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=71.md) 1 (1 day); Stage 3 enfeebled 2 (1 day); Stage 4 enfeebled 2 and permanently [blinded](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=59.md) (1 day); Stage 5 enfeebled 4 (1 day); Stage 6 [unconscious](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=95.md) (1 day); Stage 7 death
