# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Cloud PouchItem 6

[Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Water](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=732.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 273](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Price 225 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk 1

---

This small bag is filled with a fine, silvery powder that feels silky to the touch.

Activate—Disperse [two-actions] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)); Frequency once per hour; Effect You scatter the dust into the air around you, causing it to condense into a cloud in a 20-foot burst within 10 feet, as the [mist](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1606.md) spell. You can Sustain the activation to make the cloud Fly 20 feet. The cloud lasts 1 minute, and you can Dismiss it.
