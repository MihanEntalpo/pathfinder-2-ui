# [Poisonous Atmosphere](./Hazards.aspx-ID=600.md)Hazard 1

[Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) [Environmental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=591.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) [Poison](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=669.md)
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 65](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth +5 (expert) to identify the source of sudden nausea
Description Aucturn’s poisonous, magical atmosphere has strange effects on the planet’s creatures.

---

Disable DC 20 [Survival](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=49.md) (trained) or [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md) (trained) to breathe pockets of safe air trapped in bags and clothing
Poisonous Atmosphere [reaction] ([illusion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=629.md), [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md), [occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md)) Trigger A non-native creature breathes the atmosphere; Effect The triggering creature must attempt a DC 17 Fortitude save. The hazard then rolls initiative.
Critical Success The creature is invigorated by the atmosphere and is [quickened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=89.md) for 1 round.
Success The creature is unaffected.
Failure The creature is [clumsy](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=61.md) 1 for 1 round.
Critical Failure The creature is [confused](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=63.md) for 1 round.

---

Routine (1 action; [illusion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=629.md), [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md), [occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md)) The atmosphere continues to affect all creatures in the area, requiring a new Fortitude save each round against its Poisonous Atmosphere (above). A creature holding its breath gains a +2 circumstance bonus to the save.
