# [Static](./Traits.aspx-ID=702.md)

Source [GM Core pg. 173](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Visitors can’t affect living residents of the plane or objects the denizens carry in any way. Any spells that would affect those on the plane have no effect unless the static trait is somehow removed or suppressed.
