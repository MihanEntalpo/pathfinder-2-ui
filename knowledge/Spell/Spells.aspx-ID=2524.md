# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Armor of Thorn and Claw [two-actions] Spell 1

Uncommon [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Morph
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 224](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Traditions [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Duration 1 minute

---

Razor-sharp thorns and claws erupt from your skin or scales. Whenever a creature touches you or hits you with a melee unarmed attack, it takes 1 piercing damage. Additionally, if you become [grabbed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=77.md), [restrained](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=90.md), or otherwise held [immobilized](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=81.md) in a creature’s grasp, such as by being engulfed or swallowed, the creature takes 1d4 [persistent bleed damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86.md).

---

Heightened (+2) The piercing damage increases by 2, and the persistent bleed damage increases by 1d4.
