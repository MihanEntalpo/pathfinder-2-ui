# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Opportune Backstab](./Feats.aspx-ID=4954.md) [reaction] Feat 8

[Rogue](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=687.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 174](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetype [Red Mantis Assassin](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=271.md)*
Trigger A creature within your melee reach is hit by a melee attack from one of your allies
* This archetype offers Opportune Backstab at a different level than displayed here.

---

When your enemy is hit by your ally, you capitalize upon the distraction. Make a Strike against the triggering creature.

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more archetypes in addition to the listed classes. When selected this way, the feat does not have its class traits.
