## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Rare Consumable [Fulu](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=378.md) Magical Talisman
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 301](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 7 gp
Usage affixed to armor; Bulk &mdash;
Activate [free-action] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md)) Trigger You attempt to [Sense Direction](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2406.md); Requirements You’re trained in [Survival](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=49.md)

---

This fulu shows a series of constellations and arrows depicting astronomical movements through the night sky. When you activate a traveler’s fulu, the magic infuses your mind with sensations of deja vu, as if you’d been in this region before. Your attempt to [Sense Direction](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2406.md) functions as if you have a compass, and you use the outcome one degree of success better than the result of your [Survival](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=49.md) check to Sense Direction, or by two degrees of success if you’re a master in Survival.
