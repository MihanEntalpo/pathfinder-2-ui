# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Elaborate Talisman Esoterica](./Feats.aspx-ID=8371.md) Feat 8

[Thaumaturge](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=895.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 45](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

Prerequisites [Talisman Esoterica](./Feats.aspx-ID=8361.md)

---

As you continue to collect talismanic esoterica, you improve your ability to create temporary talismans. You can create four temporary talismans each day instead of two.

## Traits

Thaumaturge: This indicates abilities from the [thaumaturge](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=69.md) class.
