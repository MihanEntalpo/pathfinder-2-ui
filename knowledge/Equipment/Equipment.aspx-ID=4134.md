Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 114](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Price 30 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Activate [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md))

---

This wine, prepared using the blood of a creature with overwhelming vitality, soothes the body, mind, and spirit with a temporary sense of euphoria and rest. When you drink wine of the blood, you recover 2d10 Hit Points. Additionally, the wine attempts to counteract every negative [emotion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=590.md) effect affecting you, with a counteract rank of 3 and a counteract modifier of +9. Lastly, you gain a +1 status bonus to all Will saves and a +2 status bonus against emotion effects for 1 minute.
