Leathery wings sprout from your back, giving you a fly Speed of 60 feet or your Speed, whichever is faster. If you're still flying when this spell's duration would end, you float to the ground, with the effects of [gentle landing](./Spells.aspx-ID=1542.md).

Heightened (8th) The duration increases to 10 minutes.
