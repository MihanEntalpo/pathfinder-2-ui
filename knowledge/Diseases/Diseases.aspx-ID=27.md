# [Scarlet Fever](./Diseases.aspx-ID=27.md)Disease 1

[Disease](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=578.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 88](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
The relatively simple sore throat caused by this disease leads many victims to initially dismiss it as a mild illness, but scarlet fever can be deadly if left untreated. You can’t reduce your [sickened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=91.md) condition while affected with scarlet fever.

Saving Throw DC 13 Fortitude; Onset 2 days; Stage 1 [sickened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=91.md) 1 (1 day); Stage 2 sickened 2 (1 day); Stage 3 sickened 3 and can’t speak (1 day); Stage 4 death
