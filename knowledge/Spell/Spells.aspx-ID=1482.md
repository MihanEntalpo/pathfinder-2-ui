# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Daze [two-actions] Cantrip 1

Cantrip [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Mental Nonlethal
Source [Player Core pg. 322](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Bloodlines aberrant, hag
Range 60 feet; Targets 1 creature
Defense Will; Duration 1 round

---

You push into the target's mind and daze it with a mental jolt. The jolt deals 1d6 mental damage, with a basic Will save. If the target critically fails the save, it is also stunned 1.

---

Heightened (+2) The damage increases by 1d6.
