# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Untamed Form](./Feats.aspx-ID=4713.md) Feat 1

[Druid](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=583.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 128](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites untamed order

---

You are one with the wild, always changing and adapting to meet any challenge. You gain the [untamed form](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1861.md) order spell, which lets you transform into a variety of shapes that you can expand with druid feats.
