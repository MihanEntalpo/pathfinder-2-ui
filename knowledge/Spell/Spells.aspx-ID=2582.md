# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Telekinetic Bombardment [two-actions] Spell 7

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 107](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Range 500 feet; Area 10-foot burst or 30-foot line
Defense Reflex

---

You grasp a piece of the landscape—such as a tree, carriage, or piece of masonry—and lob it in your foe's direction. Your missile crashes down in either a 10-foot burst (for most missiles) or a 30-foot line (for something long and tall, like a tree or a ship's mast), dealing 14d6 bludgeoning damage and turning its area into [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366.md) as it breaks into rubble. All creatures in the area must attempt a Reflex save. If the area is a line, it doesn't have to start from your square but can instead start anywhere in range, as long as the entire area remains within range.

---

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 2d6.
