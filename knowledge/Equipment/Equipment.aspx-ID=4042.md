# Elixir of the PeaksItem 4+

## This Item may contain spoilers from the Revenge of the Runelords Adventure Path

Rare Alchemical Consumable Elixir
Source [Pathfinder Adventure Path #219: Lord of the Trinity Star pg. 69](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=283.md)
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L

---

With much of New Thassilon located among the rugged heights of the Kodar Mountains, these elixirs have grown in popularity among those frequently travel the region.

Activate [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)); Effect For the listed duration, drinking this elixir while in mountainous terrain protects you from the effects of severe cold and grants you an item bonus to Athletics checks made to [Climb](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2374.md), [High Jump](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2377.md), or [Long Jump](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2378.md), as well as to saving throws against the effects of high altitude.
