## This Item may contain spoilers from the Gatewalkers Adventure Path

Uncommon Consumable Magical Oil
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 237](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Price 150 gp
Usage held in 2 hands; Bulk L
Activate 1 minute ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md))

---

Adlet spiritual leaders use this thick purple gel during ancestral worship ceremonies, applying it that the dead so that their bodies can temporarily assume the appearance they had in life. Spreading it over the bones of an undead creature or a lifeless corpse causes the gel to congeal, forming a cosmetic layer that covers or restores any missing or compromised flesh until the body mimics its appearance in life. The dead creature’s flesh looks healthy and whole. An undead creature covered with the oil gains a +2 circumstance bonus on [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38.md) checks to look like a living creature. The gel does not restore life or Hit Points and the flesh quickly rots away 8 hours after application.
