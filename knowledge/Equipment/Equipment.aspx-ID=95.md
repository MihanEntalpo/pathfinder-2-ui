# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Leaper's ElixirItem 1+

### Legacy Content

Alchemical Consumable Elixir
Source [Core Rulebook pg. 549](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Activate [one-action] Interact

---

This tingly solution increases the elasticity and contraction of your leg muscles. For 1 minute after drinking this elixir, you can [High Jump](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2377&Redirected=1.md) or [Long Jump](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2378&Redirected=1.md) as a single action instead of 2 actions. If you do, you don’t perform the initial [Stride](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2305&Redirected=1.md) (nor do you fail if you don’t Stride 10 feet).
