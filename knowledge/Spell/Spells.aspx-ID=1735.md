# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Vampiric Exsanguination [two-actions] Spell 6

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Death [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) [Void](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=510.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 365](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Bloodline undead
Catalysts Unsullied Blood
Area 30-foot cone
Defense basic Fortitude

---

You draw in the blood and life force of other creatures through your outstretched arms. You deal 12d6 void damage to living creatures in the area with a basic Fortitude save.

You gain temporary Hit Points equal to half the damage a single creature takes from this spell; calculate these temporary Hit Points using the creature that took the most damage. You lose any remaining temporary Hit Points after 1 minute.

---

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 2d6.
