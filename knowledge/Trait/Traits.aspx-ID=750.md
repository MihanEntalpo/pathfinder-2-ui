# [Dhampir](./Traits.aspx-ID=750.md)

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 316](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
A creature with this trait has the [dhampir](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=85.md) versatile heritage. These humanoids are the offspring of [vampires](../MonsterFamily/MonsterFamilies.aspx-ID=480.md) and members of other ancestries.
