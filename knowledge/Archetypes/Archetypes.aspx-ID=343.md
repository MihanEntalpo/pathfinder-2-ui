# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Thaumaturge](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=343.md)

Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 49](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Scraps of knowledge have taught you how to exploit the vulnerabilities of creatures and the powers of mystical objects.

### Multiclass Thaumaturge Characters

Thaumaturge suits characters who want to glean strange knowledge. It's especially useful for those who tend to fight with one-handed weapons, like swashbucklers or rogues, since they have their other hand free for an implement.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Thaumaturge Dedication](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=8398.md) Feat 2

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Dedication](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=572.md) [Multiclass](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=659.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 49](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Archetype [Thaumaturge](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=343.md)
Class [Thaumaturge](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=69.md)
Prerequisites Charisma +2

---

You've uncovered basic thaumaturgy. You become trained in thaumaturge class DC. Choose an [implement](../Implements/Implements.aspx.md); you can use it to Glimpse Vulnerability, but don't gain its benefits. You also gain a few esoterica, allowing you to use [esoterica](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=899.md) actions. You become trained in your choice of [Arcana](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=35.md), [Nature](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=43.md), [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md), or [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md); if you were already trained in these, you become trained in a skill of your choice. You gain the Glimpse Vulnerability action.

Glimpse Vulnerability [one-action] ([esoterica](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=899.md), [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)) Frequency once per round; Requirement You are holding your implement; Effect You glimpse a hidden vulnerability and then strengthen it to aid you. Select a creature you can see. Until you Glimpse Vulnerability again, that target gains weakness 2 against your [unarmed](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=719.md) and weapon Strikes.
