# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Reflect Foe](./Feats.aspx-ID=8447.md) Feat 17

[Reflection](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=902.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 121](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

---

You create a copy of a foe by calling them forth from a nearby reflection, clonally growing them in an instant from one of your hairs or otherwise creating them in a way that matches your own nature as a reflection. You can cast [duplicate foe](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1505.md) once per day as an innate occult spell. At 20th level, your innate duplicate foe is heightened to 8th rank.

## Traits

Reflection: A creature with this trait has the [reflection](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=97.md) versatile heritage. Reflections are beings who are copies of living people, whether through cloning, planar duplication, or another mechanism. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by reflections.
