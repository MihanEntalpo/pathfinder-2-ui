# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Cat](./AnimalCompanions.aspx-ID=73.md)

[Animal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=531.md) [Minion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=653.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 208](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Your companion is a big cat, such as a leopard or tiger.
Size Small
Melee [one-action] jaws (finesse), Damage 1d6 piercing
Melee [one-action] claw (agile, finesse), Damage 1d4 slashing
Str +2, Dex +3, Con +1, Int -4, Wis +2, Cha +0
Hit Points 4
Skill [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48&Redirected=1.md)
Senses low-light vision, scent (imprecise, 30 feet)
Speed 35 feet
Special Your cat deals 1d4 extra precision damage against [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) targets.
Support Benefit Your cat throws your enemies off-balance when you create an opening. Until the start of your next turn, your Strikes that deal damage to a creature within your cat's reach make the target [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) until the end of your next turn.
Advanced Maneuver Cat Pounce

### Cat Pounce [one-action]

Flourish
Source [Player Core pg. 208](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

The cat Strides and then Strikes. If it was [undetected](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=96&Redirected=1.md) at the start of its Cat Pounce, it remains undetected until after the attack.
