# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Inhale, Exhale!](./Feats.aspx-ID=8054.md) [one-action] Feat 5

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Dragonet](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=893.md)
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 212](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

Prerequisites [Dragonet Breath](./Feats.aspx-ID=8047.md)

You take an especially deep breath as a prelude to unleashing your dragonet breath on your foes. If your next action is to use your Dragonet Breath, add 5 feet to the length of the cone or line. If your breath deals damage, you also deal one additional die of damage.

## Traits

Concentrate: An action with this trait requires a degree of mental concentration and discipline.

Dragonet: Dragonets are miniature dragons, complete with wings, dagger-like teeth, and magical breath. They can be fiercely independent and proud, at times protective or avaricious, and they delight in the finer things in life.
