### Multiclass Ranger characters

The ranger archetype has access to excellent options to improve a character’s monster knowledge and survival skills, but ranger is of particular interest to any character wanting to become a dedicated archer.

- [Bard](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=32.md) rangers support their allies and animal companions with expanded combat options. The bard’s knowledge can also complement the ranger’s investigation of their foes.
- [Cleric](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=33.md) rangers are particularly fitting for deities who connect thematically to nature or have a bow as their favored weapon. (Or, in the case of Erastil, both!)
- [Druids](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=34.md) have the most thematic overlap with rangers among the spellcasters, allowing you to create a true master of the wilderness.
- [Fighter](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=35.md) rangers are among the most fearsome archers, combining the feats from both classes to create a fighting style unique to each fighter ranger. Some warden spells can also be particularly helpful to a fighter.
- [Rogue](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=37.md) rangers make excellent snipers or ranged skirmishers, with their rogue skills naturally playing into the ranger’s strengths.
- [Witch](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=38.md) rangers add a new prowess scouting to the information they can gain through their familiar and patron.
- [Wizard](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=39.md) rangers benefit greatly from ranger’s feat support, backing up their spells with ranged weapon attacks.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Ranger Dedication](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=5087.md) Feat 2

Archetype Dedication Multiclass
Source [Player Core pg. 220](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetype [Ranger](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=215.md)
Class [Ranger](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=36.md)
Prerequisites Dexterity +2

---

You become trained in [Survival](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=49.md); if you were already trained in Survival, you instead become trained in another skill of your choice. You become trained in ranger class DC.

If you gain a warden spell through this archetype, you become trained in spell attacks and spell DCs. Your spellcasting attribute is Wisdom.

You can use the Hunt Prey action.
