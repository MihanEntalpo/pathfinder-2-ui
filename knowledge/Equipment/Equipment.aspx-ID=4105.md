## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Rare Invested Magical
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 307](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 360 gp
Usage worn; Bulk &mdash;

---

The flower that adorns this hairpin is a blooming lotus flower that varies in color; it regrows in a day after the item is activated. Whether or not the hairpin’s flower is in bloom, as long as you wear it and it’s invested, it grants a +1 item bonus to [Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md) checks.

Activate—Scatter Petals [two-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect You pluck the flower from the hairpin and scatter the petals, creating a flurry of color in a 10-foot burst centered on you. You become [concealed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=62.md) for 1 minute or until you move from your current location. Any creature within the 10-foot burst when you Activate the Item must succeed at a DC 23 Reflex save or become [dazzled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=65.md) until the end of its next turn (or [blinded](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=59.md) until the end of its next turn on a critical failure). The flower blooms again the next day.
