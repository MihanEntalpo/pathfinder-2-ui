# [Rage of the Sky Father](./Hazards.aspx-ID=557.md)Hazard 8

Unique [Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) Divine Trap
Source [Pathfinder #218: Titanbane pg. 7](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=274.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth +18 (expert)
Description Ominous storm clouds gather overhead, pulsating with flashes of blue-white lightning.

---

Disable DC 23 [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md) (trained) to petition [Gozreh](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=284.md) for safe passage or 28 [Arcana](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=35.md), [Nature](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=43.md), or [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md) (expert) to disperse the storm’s magical energies. A PC whose voice is exceptionally loud (such as using the bullhorn cantrip) can also attempt to [Disable](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2411.md) this hazard with a DC 25 [Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md) check to convince the Gozren priests that they mean no harm; the priests make a second prayer to dissipate the hazard on the following round, though they’re unable to stop the sargassum heaps.
Thunderclap [reaction] ([divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md), [sonic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=697.md)) Trigger A creature not designated by Katlanthe approaches within 10 feet of the circle of stone columns; Effect The storm clouds release a boom of thunder. Creatures atop the plateau take 2d10+9 sonic damage (DC 26 [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Fortitude save). On a critical failure, the creature is also [deafened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=66.md) for 1d4 rounds. The hazard rolls initiative.

---

Routine (1 action) ([divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md), [electricity](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=586.md)) A bolt of lightning strikes a 10-foot burst atop the plateau of the GM’s choosing. Creatures in the area take 2d10+9 electricity damage (DC 28 Reflex save). On a critical failure, the creature is also [stunned](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=93.md) 1. Creatures designated by Katlanthe within the area are infused with divine energy for 1 round. They gain the electricity trait and [immunity](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2312.md) to electricity damage, and all their attacks deal an additional 1d6 electricity damage.

---

Reset The storm clouds disperse once no creature other than those designated by Katlanthe are atop the plateau.
