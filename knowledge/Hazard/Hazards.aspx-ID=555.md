# [Precarious Footing](./Hazards.aspx-ID=555.md)Hazard 5

[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Environmental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=591.md) [Fire](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=604.md)
Source [Pathfinder #217: Death Sails a Wine-Dark Sea pg. 20](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=273.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 24 (trained)
Description The ground around the lava pit is crumbling and unstable.

---

Disable DC 18 [Survival](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=49.md) (trained) to weather the collapse safely; DC 20 [Crafting](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=37.md) (trained) to stabilize the ground
Lava Bath [reaction] Trigger A creature steps into the pit or within 5 feet of its edge; Effect The ground crumbles apart and the creature slides into the lava. That creature can use the [Grab an Edge](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2313.md) reaction to avoid falling. Otherwise it falls 20 feet (taking 10 fall damage) and is exposed to lava, taking 6d6 fire damage per round of contact. Climbing the crumbling rock face requires a successful DC 20 [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) check.
