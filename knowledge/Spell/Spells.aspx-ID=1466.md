# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Clairvoyance Spell 4

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Scrying
Source [Player Core pg. 320](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Deities Barbatos, Followers of Fate, Kaldemash, Ravithra, Shadow Cabinet
Cast 1 minute
Range 500 feet
Duration 10 minutes

---

You create an invisible floating eye at a location within range (even if it's outside your line of sight or line of effect). The eye can't move, but you can see in all directions from that point as if using your normal visual senses.
