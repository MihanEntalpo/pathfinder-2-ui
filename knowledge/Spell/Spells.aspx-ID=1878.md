Your patron uses its superior command of the elements, empowering them to undermine your foe. When you Cast this Spell, choose air, earth, [metal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=505.md), fire, water, or [wood](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=508.md). The target gains weakness 2 to that trait.

Heightened (+2) Increase the weakness by 1.
