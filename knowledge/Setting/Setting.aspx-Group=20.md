### [Quartz-Spawaned Shadow](../Monster/Monsters.aspx-ID=3186.md)pg. 153

replace shadow spawn and Steal Shadow with the Crystal Siphon activity.
Crystal Siphon [two-actions] ([death](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=571.md), [divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md), [void](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=510.md)) Requirements The shadow and the target are both within 40 feet of the crystal that spawned the shadow, and the crystal isn't covered or obstructed; Effect Shadow tendrils curl around the target, drawing a portion of the target's vitality into the crystal. The target takes 4d6 void damage (DC 21 basic Fortitude save). On a failure, it's also [enfeebled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=71.md) 1. This is cumulative with other enfeebled conditions from shadows, to a maximum of enfeebled 4. If Crystal Siphon increases the creature's enfeebled value to 3 or more, the target's shadow is pulled into the crystal. The creature feels an invisible tether linking it to the crystal, and becomes slowed 1 whenever it's more than 40 feet away from it. The enfeebled condition ends if the crystal is broken, but can't be reduced any other way.

- The 6-foot and 8-foot matryoshkas are Medium, not
- Huge, although they can Swallow Whole characters of
- up to Medium size (popping open and then engulfing

- Swallow Whole characters of up to Large size.

- damage dealt by wooden splinters. Fan the Flames
