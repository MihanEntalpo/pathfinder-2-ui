# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Ranger's Bramble [two-actions] Focus 3

Uncommon [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Plant [Ranger](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=682.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 384](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Range 100 feet; Area all squares that contain plants in a 5-foot burst
Duration 1 minute

---

You cause plants in the area to entangle your foes, with the effects of [entangling flora](./Spells.aspx-ID=1515.md). A creature that critically fails the save takes 2d4 [persistent bleed damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86&Redirected=1.md) in addition to being [immobilized](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=81&Redirected=1.md). Escaping your bramble doesn't end the bleed damage.

---

Heightened (+1) The bleed damage on a critical failure increases by 1d4.
