# [Ghostly Choir](./Hazards.aspx-ID=470.md)Hazard 6

Haunt
Source [GM Core pg. 102](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 20 (expert)
Description A choir of lost souls rises out of the floor, singing an eerie chant that terrifies its listeners and buffets their bodies with walls of sound.

---

Disable DC 28 [Performance](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=45.md) (trained) to disrupt the song’s resonance with another tune or DC 28 [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md) (trained) to ritually silence the spirits
Profane Chant [reaction] ([auditory](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=541.md), [emotion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=590.md), [fear](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=598.md), [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md), [occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md)) Trigger A creature [moves](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=658.md) within 10 feet of the section of floor from which the choir can arise; Effect The choir rises, and its song deals 4d8+18 mental damage to living creatures within 30 feet of the souls' spectral forms. Affected creatures must each attempt a DC 24 Will save.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
Success The creature takes half damage and becomes [frightened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=76.md) 1.
Failure The creature takes full damage and becomes frightened 2.
Critical Failure The creature takes double damage. It also becomes frightened 3 and [fleeing](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=74.md) for 1 round.
