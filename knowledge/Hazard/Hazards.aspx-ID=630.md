# [Reflected Desires](./Hazards.aspx-ID=630.md)Hazard 1

[Enchantment](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=61.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) [Trap](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=715.md) [Visual](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=727.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 123](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 17 (0 to notice the mirror)
Description This mirror reflects the viewer's deepest desires.

---

Disable DC 18 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) (trained) to shroud the mirror without looking at it, or DC 17 [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md) (trained) to erect mental barriers
AC 13, Fort +10, Ref +4
Hardness 6, HP 24 (BT 12); Immunities object immunities
Tempt [reaction] ([mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md), [occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md), [visual](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=727.md)) Trigger A creature looks into the mirror; Effect The triggering creature sees themselves enjoying their deepest desires in the mirror's reflection, becoming [fascinated](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=72.md) (Will DC 20 negates). This fascination ends automatically when the mirror is broken or covered up.
