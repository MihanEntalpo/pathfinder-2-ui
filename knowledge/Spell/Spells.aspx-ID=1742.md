# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Vision of Death [two-actions] Spell 4

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Death Emotion Fear [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Mental
Source [Player Core pg. 366](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Deities [Achaekek](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=325.md), Iggeret, Kalekot, [Norgorber](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=289.md), [Pharasma](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=290.md), Reymenda
Range 120 feet; Targets 1 living creature
Defense Will

---

You force the target to see a vision of its own death. It takes 8d6 mental damage with a Will save. If the target is reduced to 0 HP by this spell, its vision becomes reality and kills it instantly.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.
Success The target takes half damage and is frightened 1.
Failure The target takes full damage and is frightened 2.
Critical Failure The target takes double damage, is frightened 4, and is fleeing for as long as it's frightened.

---

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 2d6.
