# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Iapholi (Child of Bloodstone)](./Deities.aspx-ID=703.md)

Source [Pathfinder #218: Titanbane pg. 81](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=274.md), [Myth-Speakers Players Guide pg. 11](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=270.md)
Ever since the harpy Ekriathe led a ragtag army of beasts in the city-state's defense millennia ago, Pol-Dhuraxilis has welcomed monstrous citizens of all kinds. Ekriathe has periodically been reborn in Pol-Dhuraxilis, each time in a different body infused with the same, overwhelming mythic power. Iapholi is the latest incarnation, a nephilim who champions monstrous strength and acceptance. However, she lacks the same warmongering zeal as past incarnations, disappointing many monsters who had hoped a reborn Ekriathe might lead raids across Iblydos and beyond.

Category Iblydos Hero-Gods
Edicts Develop unique strengths, ally with those who appear different than you do, defend monsters who have been falsely vilified
Anathema Vilify a monster based on the reputation of their species, terrorize others for fun
Areas of Concern Monstrous heritage and acceptance
Religious Symbol Winged egg
Sacred Animal Hoatzin
Sacred Color(s) Crimson, green
