Shining, slippery eelskin covers the plates of this +1 resilient greater slick leather armor. The armor gives you the ability to breathe water and grants you a +2 item bonus to [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md) checks to Swim and [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48&Redirected=1.md) checks you attempt in the water.

Activate—Unleash Charge [two-actions] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)); Frequency once per hour; Effect You cast a 2nd-rank [thunderstrike](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1721.md) with a DC of 29.

Craft Requirements Supply one casting of thunderstrike.
