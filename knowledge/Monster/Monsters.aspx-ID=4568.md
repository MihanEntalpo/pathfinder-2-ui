# Strix

Source [Monster Core 2 pg. 307](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=276.md)
Strix, called itarii in their own language, are avian humanoids with sprawling, dark-feathered wings and large talons. They possess angular features and piercing eyes that are fixed facing forward.

### Strix Storytelling

Strix history and mythology are passed down through oral tradition. Every strix, even those from different tribes, knows nearly identical tales of their ancestral history and spiritual sermons. Always performing the tales in their own language, strix only share these ancestral stories with those they consider kin. Even among outsiders, strix hold the skill of storytelling in high regard and are fond of finely performed spoken narratives.
