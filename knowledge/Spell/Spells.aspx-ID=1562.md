# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Hydraulic Torrent [two-actions] Spell 4

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Water
Source [Player Core pg. 336](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Deities Apep, Hei Feng, Jin Li, Kelizandri, Matravash, Obari, Tresmalvos
Mystery tempest
Area 60-foot line
Defense Fortitude

---

A swirling torrent of water manifests along a straight line, battering creatures and unattended objects in its path and possibly pushing them away from you. The torrent deals 8d6 bludgeoning damage. Each creature in the area must attempt a basic Fortitude save; unattended objects automatically fail. Creatures and objects that fail are also knocked back 5 feet (10 feet on a critical failure).

---

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 2d6.
