# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Share Burden](./Feats.aspx-ID=8473.md) [reaction] Feat 6

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 169](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Archetype [Curse Maelstrom](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=347.md)
Prerequisites [Curse Maelstrom Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8470.md)
Trigger An ally within 60 feet would be affected by a [curse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=566.md) or [misfortune](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=654.md) effect, and you wouldn't be affected but are an eligible target for the effect.

---

You draw the misfortune or curse into yourself, potentially enabling you to enter your curse maelstrom state. Instead of affecting your ally, the triggering curse or misfortune effect affects you.
