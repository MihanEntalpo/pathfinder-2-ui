# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[House Drake](./Heritages.aspx-ID=424.md)

Source [Draconic Codex pg. 210](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
You have bright blue and purple scales, with silvery teeth and claws. Your jaws count as [silver](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2920.md) and you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to damage rolls against [fiends](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=600.md). You add [Diabolic](../Language/Languages.aspx-ID=117.md) to your list of known languages.
