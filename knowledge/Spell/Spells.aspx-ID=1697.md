# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Summon Dragon [three-actions] Spell 5

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) [Summon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=520.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 360](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Deities [Apsu](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=330.md), [Brixori](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=715.md), [Dahak](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=337.md), [Essence Dancers](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=723.md), [Sarshallatu](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=719.md)
Range 30 feet
Duration sustained up to 1 minute

---

You summon a creature that has the dragon trait and whose level is 5 or lower to fight for you. If the dragon has a magical tradition trait (arcane, divine, occult, or primal), you can summon it only if you're using that tradition to cast summon dragon.
