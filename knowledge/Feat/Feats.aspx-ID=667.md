You gain the Quick Alchemy action.

### Quick Alchemy [one-action]

Alchemist [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Core Rulebook pg. 72](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)
Cost 1 batch of infused reagents
Requirements You have the formula for the alchemical item you're creating, and you're either holding or wearing alchemist's tools.

---

You swiftly mix up a short-lived alchemical item to use at a moment's notice. You create a single [alchemical consumable item](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-Category=6.md) of your advanced alchemy level or lower that's in your formula book without having to spend the normal monetary cost in alchemical reagents or needing to attempt a Crafting check. This item has the infused trait, but it remains potent only until the start of your next turn.
