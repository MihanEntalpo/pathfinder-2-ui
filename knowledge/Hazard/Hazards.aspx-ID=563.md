# [By the Horns](./Hazards.aspx-ID=563.md)Hazard 10

Unique [Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) Environmental [Mythic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=835.md)
Source [Pathfinder #218: Titanbane pg. 55](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=274.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth +22 (master)
Description The head of the Granite Ox tosses back and forth as the creature charges toward Dhuraxilis.

---

Disable DC 27 [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) (master) to tug one of the ox’s ears, DC 32 [Arcana](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=35.md), [Nature](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=43.md), [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md), or [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md) (expert) to distract the Ox with a display of magic, or DC 35 [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md) (expert) to dangle in front of the Ox’s eyes and obscure its vision; three successes are required to disable the hazard
Toss [reaction] Trigger A creature reaches the Granite Ox’s head or attempts a skill check to disable the hazard; Effect The Granite Ox tosses its head. Creatures in the area 2d12+13 bludgeoning damage must attempt a DC 29 Fortitude saving throw. The hazard rolls initiative.
Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
Success The creature takes half damage.
Failure The creature takes full damage and is knocked [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88.md).
Critical Failure The creature takes double damage, is knocked prone, and takes 1d12 [persistent bleed damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86.md) as it scrapes across the Ox’s scales.

---

Routine (1 action) The Granite Ox bucks. Each creature on the Ox’s head must attempt a DC 33 Reflex save. [Prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88.md) creatures gain a +2 circumstance bonus to the save. On a failure, the creature falls prone. On a critical failure, the creature flies from the Ox’s back, falling 120 feet to the ground or into Kardaji Bay.

---

Reset The hazard ends automatically after 5 rounds or once all PCs are thrown from the Ox’s back, whichever comes first.
