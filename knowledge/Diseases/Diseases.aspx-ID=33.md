# [Bonechill](./Diseases.aspx-ID=33.md)Disease 5

[Disease](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=578.md) [Primal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=676.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 88](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
If you are [wounded](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=99.md) and exposed to [persistent](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86.md) cold, you might contract bonechill.

Saving Throw DC 20 Fortitude; Onset 1 day; Stage 1 [clumsy](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=61.md) 1 (1 day); Stage 2 clumsy 2 and can’t heal cold damage until this disease is cured (1 day); Stage 3 clumsy 3 and all cold temperature effects are one step more severe for the victim (1 day); Stage 4 [paralyzed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=85.md) and all cold temperature effects are one step more severe for the victim (1 day)
