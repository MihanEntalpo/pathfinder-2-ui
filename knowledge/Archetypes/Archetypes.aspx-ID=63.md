# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Horizon Walker](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=63.md)

### Legacy Content

Source [Advanced Player's Guide pg. 177](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=39.md)
Many long for the open road, but you prefer the trackless wild. You can find safe passage through the most inhospitable terrain in the world—icecaps, deserts, and similar environs are where you're comfortable. Your talents open untrod paths for you and help guide others through the wilderness.

# Additional Feats

Source [Player Core pg. 215](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Some archetypes include a list of “Additional Feats” that appear in other sources. The list includes each feat’s level, which might be different than normal when gained from the archetype. You can take the feat as an archetype feat of that level, meaning it counts toward the number of feats required by the archetype’s dedication feat. When selected this way, a feat that normally has a class’s trait (such as the fighter trait) doesn’t have that class trait.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Horizon Walker Dedication Feat 2

### Legacy Content

Archetype Dedication
Source [Advanced Player's Guide pg. 177](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=39.md)
Archetype [Horizon Walker](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=63.md)
Prerequisites trained in Survival

---

You've mastered travel within a specific type of terrain. You gain the Favored Terrain feat. When in your favored terrain, you gain a +10-foot circumstance bonus to your travel Speed. When other creatures Follow the Expert with you as a guide in your favored terrain, they gain both the ability to ignore non-magical difficult terrain for the purpose of their travel Speed and the bonus to travel Speed.

Special You can't select another dedication feat until you have gained two other feats from the horizon walker archetype.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Acclimatization Feat 4

### Legacy Content

Archetype
Source [Advanced Player's Guide pg. 177](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=39.md)
Archetype [Horizon Walker](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=63.md)
Prerequisites Horizon Walker Dedication

---

Your time spent in the hostile parts of the world has inured you to extreme climates. In your favored terrain, you gain resistance equal to half your level to all environmental damage and are affected by temperature effects as though they were one step less severe (incredible heat or cold becomes extreme, extreme heat or cold becomes severe, and so on). When other creatures Follow the Expert with you as a guide in your favored terrain, they too treat temperature effects as one step less severe.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Rebel's Map [free-action] Feat 4

### Legacy Content

Uncommon Archetype
Source Firebrands pg. 79
Archetypes [Horizon Walker](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=63.md), Scout
Prerequisites Horizon Walker Dedication, or Scout Dedication
Access Members of the Firebrands at the rank of second mark or higher have access to this feat.
Trigger You roll initiative.

---

Rebellions require an intricate knowledge of one's surroundings—because you might need to escape at any moment. You remember the locations of escape routes, ambush spots, and cover from a scouting expedition or a previous visit to the area. Roll a d20. At any point during the subsequent combat, you can substitute the result of this roll instead of rolling a d20 on one [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38&Redirected=1.md) check to Create a Diversion or on one [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48&Redirected=1.md) check to Hide.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Sure Foot Feat 4

### Legacy Content

Archetype Skill
Source [Advanced Player's Guide pg. 177](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=39.md)
Archetype [Horizon Walker](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=63.md)
Prerequisites Horizon Walker Dedication; expert in [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34&Redirected=1.md) and [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md)

---

You know the secrets of moving in your favored terrain. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to skill checks to move within your favored terrain, such as [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34&Redirected=1.md) checks to Balance on ice, [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md) checks to Swim in rough water, Acrobatics checks to [Fly](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2312&Redirected=1.md) in windy conditions, and so forth.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Perpetual Scout Feat 6

### Legacy Content

Archetype
Source [Advanced Player's Guide pg. 177](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=39.md)
Archetype [Horizon Walker](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=63.md)
Prerequisites Horizon Walker Dedication

---

You spot anything out of place in the areas you know best. While in your favored terrain, you always gain the benefits of the Scout exploration activity, even if you are performing another exploration activity. If you're legendary in Survival, you gain these benefits in any terrain.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Blind-Fight Feat 10

### Legacy Content

Fighter Investigator Ranger Rogue
Source [Advanced Player's Guide pg. 63](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=39.md), [Core Rulebook pg. 173](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)
Archetypes [Exorcist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=171.md), [Horizon Walker](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=63.md)
Prerequisites master in Perception

---

Your battle instincts make you more aware of concealed and invisible opponents. You don't need to succeed at a flat check to target concealed creatures. You're not flat-footed to creatures that are [hidden](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=79&Redirected=1.md) from you (unless you're flat-footed to them for reasons other than the hidden condition), and you need only a successful DC 5 flat check to target a hidden creature.

While you're adjacent to an [undetected](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=96&Redirected=1.md) creature of your level or lower, it is instead only hidden from you.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Terrain Master Feat 10

### Legacy Content

Ranger
Source [Core Rulebook pg. 174](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)
Archetype [Horizon Walker](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=63.md)
Prerequisites master in Survival; Favored Terrain

---

You adapt to your surroundings in any natural terrain. You can spend 1 hour practicing in your current terrain in order to make it your favored terrain, replacing your current favored terrain temporarily. If you spend a full day out of the new favored terrain, your favored terrain reverts back to your original choice when you took the Favored Terrain feat.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Wild Strider Feat 12

### Legacy Content

Archetype
Source [Advanced Player's Guide pg. 177](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=39.md)
Archetype [Horizon Walker](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=63.md)
Prerequisites Horizon Walker Dedication

---

Natural obstacles pose no obstacle to you. You gain the ranger's wild stride class feature. This allows you to ignore the effects of all non-magical difficult terrain, treat greater difficult terrain as merely difficult terrain, and gives you an additional benefit from Favored Terrain based on the terrain.
