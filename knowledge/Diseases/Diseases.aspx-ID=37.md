# [Nightmare Fever](./Diseases.aspx-ID=37.md)Disease 8

[Disease](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=578.md) [Occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 89](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Thought to be caused by a divine curse, nightmare fever inflicts you with terrible nightmares, and you awaken with the wounds you received in your dreams. Some versions cause you to dream of being wounded by bludgeoning or piercing weapons, in which case you take that type of damage instead. Damage and the [fatigued](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=73.md) condition caused by the disease can’t be healed until the disease is removed.

Saving Throw DC 25 Will; Stage 1 2d6 slashing damage and [fatigued](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=73.md) (1 day); Stage 2 4d6 slashing damage and fatigued (1 day); Stage 3 4d6 slashing damage, fatigued, and whenever you take slashing damage, you must succeed at a Will save against the disease’s DC or become [frightened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=76.md) 2 (1 day); Stage 4 6d6 slashing damage, fatigued, and whenever you take slashing damage, you must succeed at a Will save against the disease’s DC or become [paralyzed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=85.md) for 1 round (1 day); Stage 5 6d6 slashing damage and [unconscious](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=95.md)
