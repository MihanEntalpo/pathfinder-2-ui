## This Item may contain spoilers from the Myth-Speakers Adventure Path

Rare Magical Poison
Source [Pathfinder #217: Death Sails a Wine-Dark Sea pg. 78](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=273.md)
Price 150 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk 1
Base Weapon [Rapier](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=391.md)

---

Within hives of giant bees, some become protectors of their hive and etch runes onto their stingers via dance. Upon such a bee's death, its stinger can be removed from its body and used as [+1 striking](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2861.md) [rapier](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=391.md), though a hilt often needs to be attached for skilled swordsmanship. The imbued magic allows the stinger to slowly renew a small bit of its venom supply. The wielder can gather this and deliver it during a Strike. The victim is made to see prophetic flashes of their future as it courses through their system.

Activate—Apiprophecy Sting [free-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect The protector's final gift secrets apiprophecy venom, which coats the blade and affects the next creature successfully struck with the blade within the next minute.

Apiprophecy Venom ([poison](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=669.md)) Saving Throw DC 20 Will; Maximum Duration 4 rounds; Stage 1 2d6 mental damage and [dazzled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=65.md)
