# [Hidden](./Conditions.aspx-ID=79.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 444](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
While you're hidden from a creature, that creature knows the space you're in but can't tell precisely where you are. You typically become hidden by using [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48&Redirected=1.md) to Hide. When Seeking a creature using only [imprecise](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2407&Redirected=1.md) senses, it remains hidden, rather than [observed](./Conditions.aspx-ID=84&Redirected=1.md). A creature you're hidden from is [off-guard](./Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) to you, and it must succeed at a DC 11 flat check when targeting you with an attack, spell, or other effect or it fails to affect you. Area effects aren't subject to this flat check.

A creature might be able to use the Seek action to try to observe you.
