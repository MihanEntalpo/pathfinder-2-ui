# [Step 3: Apply Immunities, Weaknesses, and Resistances](./Rules.aspx-ID=2309.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 407](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Defenses against certain types of damage or effects are called immunities or resistances, while vulnerabilities are called weaknesses. Apply immunities first, then weaknesses, and resistances third. See [Immunity, Weakness, and Resistance](./Rules.aspx-ID=2312.md).
