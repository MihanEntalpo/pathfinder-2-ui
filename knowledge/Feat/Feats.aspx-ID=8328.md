# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Mental Static](./Feats.aspx-ID=8328.md) [reaction] Feat 8

[Mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) [Nonlethal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=661.md) [Occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md) [Psychic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=894.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 27](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

Trigger You roll a critical success on a Will save against a [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) effect from a creature.

---

Your thoughts sting back when a lesser mind tries to invade your own. You deal mental damage equal to your level to the triggering creature. If you so desire, you can also send the creature a single mental image to coincide with the damage, such as a watchful eye or the dread of floating in an infinite sea.

## Traits

Mental: A mental effect can alter the target’s mind. It has no effect on an object or a mindless creature.

Nonlethal: Attacks with this weapon are [nonlethal](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2311.md), and are used to knock creatures unconscious instead of kill them. You can use a nonlethal weapon to make a lethal attack with a –2 circumstance penalty.

Occult: This magic comes from the occult tradition, calling upon bizarre and ephemeral mysteries. Anything with this trait is magical.

A creature with this trait is primarily constituted of or has a strong connection to occult magic.

Psychic: This indicates abilities from the [psychic](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=68.md) class.
