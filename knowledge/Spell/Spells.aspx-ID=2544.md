# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Sacred Nimbus [two-actions] Spell 4

## This Spell may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Rare [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Holy](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=517.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 309](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Traditions [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Range 30 feet; Area 10-foot square
Defense Reflex; Duration [sustained](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2317.md) up to 1 minute

---

You summon a small, fluffy white nimbus cloud that floats 10 feet above the ground and rains down pleasantly warm sanctified water. The rain deals 5d6 spirit damage to [fiends](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=600.md), [undead](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=722.md), and creatures that have a weakness to spirit damage in the area at the end of your turn. When you [Sustain](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2317.md) the spell, you can move the cloud at a fly speed of 15 feet to anywhere in the spell’s range. The water dissipates as it makes contact with any creatures or surfaces, leaving only a bit of humidity in its place.

---

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 1d6.
