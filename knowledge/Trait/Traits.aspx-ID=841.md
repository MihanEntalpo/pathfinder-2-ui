# [Tearing](./Traits.aspx-ID=841.md)

Source [War of Immortals pg. 222](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=232.md)
This weapon is edged with curved teeth that leave bleeding wounds. When you hit a creature with this weapon, it deals an additional 1 [persistent bleed damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86&Redirected=1.md). This increases to 2 persistent bleed damage if the weapon has a greater striking rune.
