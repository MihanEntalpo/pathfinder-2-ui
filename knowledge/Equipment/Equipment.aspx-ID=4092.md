## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Uncommon Magical
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 303](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 1,300 gp
Usage held in 2 hands; Bulk 1

---

This hourglass-shaped drum has two heads, each of which produces a different sound when struck. When played together, the resonance of the two sounds is effective in repelling evil spirits. This drum can be played as an instrument, granting a +2 item bonus to [Performance](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=45.md).

Activate—Memories of Life [two-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md), [vitality](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=509.md)); Frequency once per hour; Effect You play both heads of the drums, generating a pulse of energy that targets one creature within 30 feet that has the [undead](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=722.md) trait. This deals 5d8 [vitality](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=509.md) damage (DC 28 Fortitude save) to that creature; if the undead also has the [ghost](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=753.md) trait, the ghost becomes [sickened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=91.md) 1 if it fails the save.

Activate—Memories of Joy [three-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md), [vitality](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=509.md)); Frequency once per hour; Effect You raucously play both heads of the drums, accompanied with shouting and dancing. This display generates a pulse of energy in a 30-foot [emanation](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2387.md) from you. All [undead](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=722.md) creatures within this area must attempt a DC 28 Fortitude save.

Critical Success The creature takes no damage.
Success The creature takes 2d8 vitality damage.
Failure The creature takes 5d8 vitality damage; if the creature has the [ghost](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=753.md) trait, it becomes [stupefied](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=94.md) 1 for 1 minute.
Critical Failure The creature takes 10d8 vitality damage; if the creature has the ghost trait, it becomes stupefied 2 for 1 minute.
