# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Control Water [two-actions] Spell 5

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Water
Source [Player Core pg. 321](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Deities Aminara, Bokrug, Embaral, [Gozreh](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=284.md), Hanspur, Hataam, Isis, Lady Nanbyo, Lysianassa, Neith, Oaur-Ooung, Orgesh, [Pharimia](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=704.md), Seafarer's Hope, Srikalis, Sritaming, and Sribaril, Waves of the Boundless Sea, Zogmugot
Range 500 feet; Area 50 feet long by 50 feet wide
Defense Fortitude (see text); Duration 1 hour

---

By imposing your will upon the water, you can raise or lower the level of water in the chosen area by 10 feet. Creatures that have the water trait and that are in the area when you Cast the Spell must attempt a Fortitude save, with the effects of the slow spell.
