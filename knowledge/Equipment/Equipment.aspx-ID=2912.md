[Alchemical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=528.md) [Consumable](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=564.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 251](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md), [Player Core 2 pg. 296](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md); There is a more recent version of this item. Click here to view.
Price 6 gp
Usage held in 2 hands; Bulk &mdash;
Activate [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md))

---

You can slather this silvery paste onto one melee weapon, one thrown weapon, or 10 pieces of ammunition. Silver salve spoils quickly, so once you open a vial, you must use it all at once, rather than saving it. For the next hour, the weapon or ammunition counts as silver instead of its normal material (such as cold iron) for any physical damage it deals.
