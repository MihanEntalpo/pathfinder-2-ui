# [Aquadynamic](./Traits.aspx-ID=792.md)

Source [Howl of the Wild pg. 216](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=226.md)
This armor is made for use underwater, with streamlined design and buoyant materials used in strategic places. You don’t apply the armor’s check penalty to [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md) or [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) checks in water or similar liquids.
