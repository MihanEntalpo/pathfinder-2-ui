# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Jealous Grip](./Feats.aspx-ID=8055.md) [reaction] Feat 5

[Dragonet](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=893.md)
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 212](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

Trigger A creature in your reach that you’re aware of attempts to [Disarm](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2383.md) you, or damage or [Steal](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2410.md) an item in your possession.

---

You are protective of your hoard, and woe betide anyone who tries to take what’s yours. Make a melee [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) against the triggering creature. If your attack is a critical hit and the triggering action was a [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md) action, you disrupt that action. This Strike doesn’t count toward your multiple attack penalty, and your multiple attack penalty doesn’t apply to this Strike.

## Traits

Dragonet: Dragonets are miniature dragons, complete with wings, dagger-like teeth, and magical breath. They can be fiercely independent and proud, at times protective or avaricious, and they delight in the finer things in life.
