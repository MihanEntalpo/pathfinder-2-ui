# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Draconic Familiar](./Feats.aspx-ID=8093.md) Feat 6

Archetype
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 219](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Archetype [Draconic Acolyte](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=333.md)
Prerequisites [Draconic Acolyte Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8090.md)

---

Your devotion to dragons has attracted a loyal servant. You gain a [familiar](./Feats.aspx-ID=5024.md). You can select four familiar or master abilities each day, instead of two, but one of them must always be the dragon familiar ability. When you Channel Draconic Essence, you can also [Command](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2400.md) your familiar as a free action.

## Traits

Archetype: This feat belongs to an archetype.
