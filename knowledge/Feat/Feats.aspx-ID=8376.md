# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Intensify Investiture](./Feats.aspx-ID=8376.md) [free-action] Feat 12

[Thaumaturge](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=895.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 46](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

Trigger once per 10 minutes

---

Your bond to your invested items enables you to put more of yourself into them. If you next action is to activate an invested item that has a saving throw DC, you can use your thaumaturge class DC instead of the item's DC if it is higher.

## Traits

Thaumaturge: This indicates abilities from the [thaumaturge](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=69.md) class.
