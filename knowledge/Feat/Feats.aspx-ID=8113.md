# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Basic Skysage Divination](./Feats.aspx-ID=8113.md) Feat 4

Archetype
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 238](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Archetype [Oatia Skysage](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=335.md)
Prerequisites [Oatia Skysage Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8112.md)

---

The stars move, granting you a sliver of their magic. Choose one of the following 1st-rank spells to your spell repertoire: object reading or [sure strike](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1709.md). You can [Cast this Spell](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2734.md) as an occult Oatia skysage spell.

At 6th level, choose one of the following 2nd-rank spells to add to your spell repertoire: [augury](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1445.md) or [see the unseen](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1663.md). At 8th level, you choose one of the following 3rd-rank spells to add to your spell repertoire: [clairaudience](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1465.md) or [locate](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1588.md).
