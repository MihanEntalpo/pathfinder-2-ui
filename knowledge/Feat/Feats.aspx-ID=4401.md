# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[March the Mines](./Feats.aspx-ID=4401.md) [two-actions] Feat 13

Dwarf
Source [Player Core pg. 45](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

You march through the earth and lead an ally along. You gain a burrow Speed of 15 feet when you use this activity (if you don't have a higher one), and then you either [Stride](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2305&Redirected=1.md) twice or [Burrow](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2310&Redirected=1.md) twice. You can choose one willing ally adjacent to you at the start of your movement to move with you. That ally ends its movement one square behind you.

## Traits

Dwarf: A creature with this trait is a member of the dwarf ancestry. Dwarves are stout folk who often live underground and typically have darkvision. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by dwarves. An item with this trait is created and used by dwarves.
