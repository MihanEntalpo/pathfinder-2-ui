# [Agathion](./Traits.aspx-ID=866.md)

Source [Monster Core 2 pg. 363](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=276.md)
This family of animal-featured celestials is native to the plane of [Nirvana](../Plane/Planes.aspx-ID=44.md). Most agathions are [holy](./Traits.aspx-ID=517.md), have [darkvision](../MonsterAbility/MonsterAbilities.aspx-ID=59.md), and have a weakness to [unholy](./Traits.aspx-ID=521.md).
