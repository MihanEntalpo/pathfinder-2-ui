# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Perfect Distraction](./Feats.aspx-ID=4983.md) [one-action] Feat 16

[Rogue](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=687.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 177](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites legendary in [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38&Redirected=1.md)

---

You use clever tactics to mislead your foes as you sneak away. You Sneak while leaving a decoy behind. The decoy acts as the spell [mislead](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1605.md), though you aren’t invisible, just undetected. You can continue to concentrate to move your decoy, as with the spell, whether or not you remain hidden throughout the duration. Once you use Perfect Distraction, you need to spend 10 minutes to set up another decoy before you can use it again.
