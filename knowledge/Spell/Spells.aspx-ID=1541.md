# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Gecko Grip [two-actions] Spell 2

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 333](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Deities [Chinostes (Nightwarden)](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=701.md), [Chinostes (Redeemer)](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=700.md)
Range touch; Targets 1 creature
Duration 10 minutes

---

Tiny clinging hairs sprout across the creature's hands and feet, offering purchase on nearly any surface. The target gains a climb Speed equal to its Speed.

---

Heightened (5th) The duration increases to 1 hour.
