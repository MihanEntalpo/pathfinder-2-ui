Class [Bard](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=32.md)

You gain the basic spellcasting benefits.. Each time you gain a spell slot of a new rank from the bard archetype, add a common occult spell of the appropriate rank, or another appropriate occult spell you learned or discovered, to your repertoire.
