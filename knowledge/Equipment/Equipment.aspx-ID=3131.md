# Monkey's PawItem 20

[Rare](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=683.md) [Cursed](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=567.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Misfortune](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=654.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 306](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L

---

This dried, gnarled hand is clenched in a fist, waiting for a creature to pick it up. When you pick up the monkey's paw, the hand opens, revealing three withered fingers. The monkey's paw grants you three wishes (with the effects of a success on the [wish](../Ritual/Rituals.aspx-ID=125.md) ritual), curling one finger after every one. Once you pick up the monkey's paw, you cannot discard the hand until it returns to a clenched fist by granting its three wishes. Any attempts to discard the hand, even with the effects of a wish ritual, are unsuccessful as the monkey's paw reappears among your possessions within 1d4 hours; it doesn't work for any other creature in the intervening time. The hand returns even if another creature steals it from you. Once you make all three wishes, the monkey's paw uses [interplanar teleport](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1576.md) to travel to a random point in the multiverse.

Whenever the monkey's paw hears you utter a statement that sounds like a wish, even if you don't use the words “I wish,” it activates and grants a twisted, horrifying version of your wish, producing any effect within the possibility of wish ritual, and potentially a greater effect at the GM's discretion.
