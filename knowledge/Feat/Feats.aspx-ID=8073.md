# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Dracomancer](./Feats.aspx-ID=8073.md) Feat 9

Kobold
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 215](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md), [Advanced Player's Guide pg. 15](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=39.md)

Prerequisites dragonscaled kobold heritage

---

The power infused into you by your draconic benefactor’s presence allows you to manifest their most iconic spells. Choose one 1st-rank spell and one 2nd-rank spell from those listed for a dragon spellcaster of your draconic benefactor’s type, such as those presented here (for example, [fear](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1524.md) and [invisibility](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1577.md) for a conspirator dragon benefactor). You can cast each of these spells once per day as innate spells of the same tradition as your draconic benefactor. You gain the trained proficiency rank in the spell attack modifier and spell DC statistics, and your key spellcasting attribute is Charisma.

## Traits

Kobold: A creature with this trait is a member of the [kobold](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=80.md) ancestry, reptilian humanoids who are usually Small and typically have [darkvision](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2410.md). An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by kobolds.
