# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Knock [two-actions] Spell 2

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 340](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Deities Alseta, Barzahk, Dranngvit, Lao Shu Po, The Deliberate Journey, Urban Prosperity
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 door, lock, or container
Duration 1 minute

---

You make the target easier to open. Knock grants a +4 status bonus to any creature that tries to open the target door, lock, or container with an [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md) or a [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50&Redirected=1.md) check. You can attempt a Thievery check to open the target as part of casting knock, and you add your level even if you're untrained.

Knock counteracts lock.
