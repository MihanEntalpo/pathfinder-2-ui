# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Breath Unleashed](./Feats.aspx-ID=8072.md) Feat 9

Kobold
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 215](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

---

You’re able to channel more of your draconic benefactor’s power and focus it into your Kobold Breath, increasing its effectiveness but requiring more time to recover. When you use Kobold Breath, you can increase the damage dice to d8s and increase the area to 30 feet for a cone or 60 feet for a line. If you do, you can’t use Kobold Breath again for 1 hour. During this time, Kobold Breath cannot be recharged using [Kobold Momentum](./Feats.aspx-ID=8070.md).

## Traits

Kobold: A creature with this trait is a member of the [kobold](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=80.md) ancestry, reptilian humanoids who are usually Small and typically have [darkvision](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2410.md). An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by kobolds.
