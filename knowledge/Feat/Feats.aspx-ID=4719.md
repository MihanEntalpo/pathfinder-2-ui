# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Poison Resistance](./Feats.aspx-ID=4719.md) Feat 2

Alchemist [Druid](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=583.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 129](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetypes [Assassin](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=239.md)*, [Poisoner](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=259.md)*
* This archetype offers Poison Resistance at a different level than displayed here.

---

Your affinity for the natural world grants you protection against some of its dangers. You gain poison resistance equal to half your level, and you gain a +1 status bonus to saving throws against poisons.

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more archetypes in addition to the listed classes. When selected this way, the feat does not have its class traits.
