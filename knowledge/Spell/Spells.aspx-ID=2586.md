# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Frenzied Revelry [two-actions] Focus 1

[Rare](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=683.md) [Cleric](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=554.md) [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) [Emotion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=590.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md) [Mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 142](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Domain [indulgence](../Domain/Domains.aspx-ID=79.md) (apocryphal)
Area 5-foot emanation; Targets you and allies in the area
Duration 1 minute

---

You recall memories of hedonistic rites to send yourself into an infectious frenzy, dancing and howling wildly to encourage your companions to join in. You and your allies gain a +1 status bonus to saving throws against [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) effects while in the area. Whenever someone benefiting from this bonus critically succeeds at a saving throw against an enemy's mental effect, its revelry increases, granting it a +1 status bonus to attack rolls and damage rolls for 1 round.

---

Heightened (4th) The emanation’s radius is 10 feet, and the status bonus to saves is +2.
Heightened (7th) The emanation’s radius is 15 feet, and the status bonus to saves is +3.
