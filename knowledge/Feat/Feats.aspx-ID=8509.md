# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Mind Shards](./Feats.aspx-ID=8509.md) [two-actions] Feat 6

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 205](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Archetype [Mind Smith](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=351.md)
Prerequisites [Mind Smith Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8504.md)

---

With a swing and a thought, you detonate your mind weapon into a burst of psychic shards that shred the mind. You concentrate and unleash a 15-foot cone that deals 3d6 mental damage to all creatures in the area, with a [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Will save against the higher of your class DC or spell DC. After the attack, your mind weapon automatically re-forms, and you can't use this ability again for 1 minute. Mind Shards' damage increases by 1d6 at level 7 and every two levels thereafter.

## Traits

Archetype: This feat belongs to an archetype.

Concentrate: An action with this trait requires a degree of mental concentration and discipline.

Magical: Something with the magical trait is imbued with magical energies not tied to a specific tradition of magic. Some items or effects are closely tied to a particular tradition of magic. In these cases, the item has the arcane, divine, occult, or primal trait instead of the magical trait. Any of these traits indicate that the item is magical.

Mental: A mental effect can alter the target’s mind. It has no effect on an object or a mindless creature.
