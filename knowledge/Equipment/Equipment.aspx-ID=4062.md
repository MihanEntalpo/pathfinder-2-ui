## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Unique Magical
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 296](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 100 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Base Weapon [Dagger](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=358.md)

---

The blade of this [+1](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2830.md) [low-grade silver](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2920.md) [dagger](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=358.md) has a sickly red tinge. Though once used to send souls to empower [Kugaptee](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=725.md), the fury of those slain by the blade now allows its wielder to periodically strike back against [fiends](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=600.md).

Activate—Avenging Soul [free-action] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md), [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md)); Frequency once per day; Trigger You score a critical hit against a [fiend](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=600.md); Effect Vengeful echoes of sacrificed souls lance into the psyche of the fiend struck. After the normal effects of the critical hit, the target must roll a DC 18 Fortitude save.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
Success The creature becomes distracted by vengeful spirits and becomes [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) until the start of your next turn.
Failure As success, but the creature is also [slowed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=92.md) 1 for 1 round.
Critical Failure As failure, but the creature is slowed 1 for 1 minute.
