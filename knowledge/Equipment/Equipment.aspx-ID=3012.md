# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Necklace of AllureItem 17

[Apex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=532.md) [Invested](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=637.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 271](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Price 15,000 gp
Usage worn; Bulk &mdash;

---

Several sapphires are set along the length of this brilliant silver necklace. The necklace features an intricately carved pendant in the shape of a wolf's head. You gain a +2 item bonus to Deception and Diplomacy checks. When you invest the necklace, you either increase your Charisma modifier by 1 or increase it to +4, whichever would give you a higher value.

Activate—Win Them Over [two-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md)); Frequency once per hour; Effect You cast a 4th-rank charm spell (DC 38).
