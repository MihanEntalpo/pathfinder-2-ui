Uncommon [Druid](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=583.md) [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md) Healing [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) [Vitality](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=509.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 382](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Range touch or 30 feet (see text); Targets 1 willing living animal

---

You heal an animal's wounds, restoring 1d8 Hit Points to the target. The number of actions spent Casting this Spell determines its effect.

[one-action] The spell has a range of touch.
[two-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md)) The spell has a range of 30 feet and restores an additional 8 Hit Points to the target.

---

Heightened (+1) The amount of healing increases by 1d8, and the additional healing for the 2-action version increases by 8.
