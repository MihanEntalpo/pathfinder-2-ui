# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Kugaptee](./Deities.aspx-ID=725.md)

Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 292](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Nethys Note: This deity entry contains spoilers for the Season of Ghosts campaign. Read at your own risk.

Kugaptee was one of the most formidable nindorus of the last millennium. Said to have four heads and four arms, each representing one of the seasons of the year, his reign over a remote reach of central Tian Xia was brought to an end in 6223 ic by the heroine Tan Sui-Jing, although she perished in the same battle where she defeated him. Her soul, reincarnated as an immense sugi tree, grows above Kugaptee’s grave, ensuring the dead fiend will never reincarnate and return to this world while the tree remains.

Category Nindoru Ascetics
Edicts curse living creatures, defile graveyards and pastoral natural regions
Anathema perform a burial ritual meant to provide peace to the dead, remove a curse from anyone other than yourself, restore a non-nindoru creature to life
Areas of Concern curses, defiled graves, haunted wildlands, and seasonal disasters
Religious Symbol twisted knot of wooden roots
