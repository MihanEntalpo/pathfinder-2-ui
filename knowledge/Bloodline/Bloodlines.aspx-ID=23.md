# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Draconic](./Bloodlines.aspx-ID=23.md)

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 150](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
The blood of dragons flows through your veins. These beasts are both fearsome in combat and skilled at magic.

## Tradition

variable (see Draconic Exemplars below)

## Bloodline Skills

[Intimidation](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=40.md) and one other skill (see Draconic Exemplars below)

## Sorcerous Gifts

cantrip: [shield](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1671.md), 1st: [fear](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1524.md), 2nd: see Draconic Exemplars below, 3rd: [haste](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1553.md), 4th: [fly](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1534.md), 5th: see Draconic Exemplars below, 6th: [dragon form](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1502.md) (dragon must match your draconic exemplar), 7th: [mask of terror](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1595.md), 8th: see Draconic Exemplars below, 9th: [overwhelming presence](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1621.md)

## Bloodline Spells

initial: [flurry of claws](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=2102.md), advanced: [dragon breath](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=2103.md), greater: [dragon wings](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=2104.md)

## Blood Magic—Scaly Hide

Draconic scales grow briefly on you or one target, granting a +1 status bonus to AC for 1 round.

## Draconic Exemplars

At 1st level, choose the tradition related to the dragon exemplar who influenced your bloodline. You can't change your dragon exemplar later. As noted, this affects both your bloodline's features and your sorcerous gift spells.

### Specific Draconic Exemplars

If you want choose a specific type of dragon as your draconic exemplar instead of a category, you can work with your GM to replace the sorcerous gifts listed in Draconic Exemplars with appropriate-rank spells found in the various Dragon Spellcasters sidebars for that type of dragon found in Monster Core.
