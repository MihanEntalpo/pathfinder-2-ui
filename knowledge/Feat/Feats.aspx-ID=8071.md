# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Tip the Scales](./Feats.aspx-ID=8071.md) Feat 5

Kobold
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 215](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

---

You’re a cunning adversary in close quarters and are well-versed in hit-and-run tactics that exploit the advantages of your small stature. As part of your training, you learned what to do when you’re [grabbed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=77.md) or [restrained](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=90.md). You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to checks to Escape, and after resolving a successful [Escape](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2296.md) action, you can immediately use your reaction to [Step](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2304.md) or to [Shove](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2380.md) or [Trip](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2382.md) the creature from whom you just Escaped.

## Traits

Kobold: A creature with this trait is a member of the [kobold](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=80.md) ancestry, reptilian humanoids who are usually Small and typically have [darkvision](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2410.md). An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by kobolds.
