Alchemist's fire is a combination of volatile liquids that ignite when exposed to air. Alchemist's fire deals the listed fire damage, [persistent fire damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86&Redirected=1.md), and splash damage. Many types grant an item bonus to attack rolls.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Alchemist's Fire (Lesser)Item 1

Source [GM Core pg. 244](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Price 3 gp
The bomb deals 1d8 fire damage, 1 persistent fire damage, and 1 fire splash damage.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Alchemist's Fire (Moderate)Item 3

Source [GM Core pg. 244](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Price 10 gp
You gain a +1 item bonus to attack rolls. The bomb deals 2d8 fire damage, 2 persistent fire damage, and 2 fire splash damage.
