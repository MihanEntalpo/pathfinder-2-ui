# [Cowards's Roots](./Curses.aspx-ID=80.md)Curse 5

[Curse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=566.md) [Emotion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=590.md) [Fear](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=598.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 86](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
You find all courage stolen from your heart. When faced with something frightening, you flee in terror or stand frozen in place.

Saving Throw DC 20 Will; Effect At the start of your turn, if you are frightened, you become your choice of [immobilized](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=81.md) or [fleeing](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=74.md) until the end of that turn.
