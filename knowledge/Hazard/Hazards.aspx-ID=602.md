# [Shroud of Silence](./Hazards.aspx-ID=602.md)Hazard 4

[Environmental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=591.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 76](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 25 (expert)
Description Sounds become muted in a 20-foot-wide, 100-foot-long segment of this street corridor.

---

Disable DC 25 [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md) (expert) to twist the magic of the ley line around you, disabling the hazard for only you; or [dispel magic](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1493.md) (4th rank; counteract DC 23) to disable the hazard permanently
Hush [reaction] ([illusion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=629.md), [occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md)) Trigger A creature reaches the street’s halfway point; Effect Each creature in the affected area must succeed at a DC 23 Will saving throw or be silenced as long as it remains in the area. A silenced creature makes no sound and can’t use abilities with the [sonic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=697.md) or [auditory](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=541.md) traits, including casting spells that don’t have the [subtle](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=522.md) trait.
