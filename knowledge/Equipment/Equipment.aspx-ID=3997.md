## This Item may contain spoilers from the Myth-Speakers Adventure Path

Rare Alchemical Consumable Injury [Mythic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=835.md) Poison
Source [Pathfinder #218: Titanbane pg. 69](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=274.md)
Price 100 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Activate [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md))

---

Mythotoxin is exceedingly difficult to produce, as it requires fresh venom from a mythic creature that must then be distilled into a more concentrated form. [Mythic creatures](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=3320.md) take a –2 circumstance penalty to saves against mythotoxin.

Saving Throw DC 26 Fortitude; Maximum Duration 6 rounds; Stage 1 3d6 poison damage (1 round); Stage 2 3d8 poison damage (1 round); Stage 3 3d10 damage and lose 1 Mythic Point, or become [doomed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=67.md) 1 if you have no Mythic Points to lose (1 round; doomed condition value has normal duration)
