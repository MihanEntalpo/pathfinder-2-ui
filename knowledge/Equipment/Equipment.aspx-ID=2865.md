# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Fighter's ForkItem 3

[Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 242](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Price 50 gp
Usage held in 1 or 2 hands; Bulk 1
Base Weapon [Trident](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=401.md)

---

This +1 trident, usually engraved with a decorative pattern resembling fish scales, is a common weapon among warriors of aquatic ancestries.

Activate—Fluid Length [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)); Effect You extend or shorten the trident's haft. When extended, the trident requires two hands to wield and gains the reach trait, but loses the trident's normal thrown trait.
