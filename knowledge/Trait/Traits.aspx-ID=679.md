# [Psychopomp](./Traits.aspx-ID=679.md)

Source [GM Core pg. 333](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
A family of monitors spawned within the Boneyard to convey souls to the Outer Planes, most psychopomps are true neutral. They typically have darkvision, lifesense, and spirit touch, and they’re immune to death effects.
