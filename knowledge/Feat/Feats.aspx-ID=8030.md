# [Sonic Dash](./Feats.aspx-ID=8030.md) [one-action] Feat 2

## This Feat may contain spoilers from the Gatewalkers Adventure Path

Rare Deviant Magical
Source [Gatewalkers Player's Guide (Remastered) pg. 10](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=287.md)

---

You bounce up and down once, then sprint forward. You [Stride](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2305.md) twice in a straight line. You can use Sonic Dash while [Burrowing](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2310.md), [Climbing](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2374.md), [Flying](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2312.md), or [Swimming](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2381.md) instead of Striding if you have the corresponding movement type.

Awakening Your Sonic Dash generates a sonic boom, dealing 1d4 sonic damage for every two levels you have to all creatures other than you in a 5-foot [emanation](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2387.md) at the point you end your Sonic Dash, with a [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Fortitude save. Creatures that fail are [deafened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=66.md) for 1 round, or 1 minute on a critical failure.
Awakening Your speed generates a slipstream that allows you to reposition your allies. You can dash through a willing ally’s space during your Sonic Dash, causing them to be drawn by winds alongside you. You deposit them in any unoccupied space adjacent to you at the end of your Sonic Dash.
