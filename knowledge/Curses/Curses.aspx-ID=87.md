# [Spirit Anchor](./Curses.aspx-ID=87.md)Curse 13

[Curse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=566.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 87](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
This curse prevents your soul from moving on after death.

Saving Throw DC 31 Fortitude; Effect If you die while affected, your spirit is anchored to the plane you currently inhabit, and you become a ghost or other incorporeal undead.
