# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Forbidden Thought [two-actions] Cantrip 1

[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Cantrip](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=550.md) [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md) [Mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) [Psychic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=894.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 21](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature
Defense Will

---

You place a psychic lock in a foe's mind that prevents it from a specific action. Choose “Strike,” “Stride,” “Cast a Spell,” or a specific action you know the creature to have (such as a [dragon's](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=581.md) magic breath). If the creature attempts that action on its next turn, it must surmount your lock to do so, causing it to take 2d6 mental damage (with a [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Will save). The target is then temporarily immune for 1 minute.

---

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 1d6.

---

Amp Your telepathic push is particularly shocking to those who resist it. If the target fails its save, it's also [stunned](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=93.md) 1.
