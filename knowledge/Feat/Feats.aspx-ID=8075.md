# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Elite Dracomancer](./Feats.aspx-ID=8075.md) Feat 13

Kobold
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 215](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md), [Advanced Player's Guide pg. 15](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=39.md)

Prerequisites [Dracomancer](./Feats.aspx-ID=8073.md)

---

Your draconic power equals that of a lesser dragon. Choose one 3rd-rank spell and one 4th-rank spell from those listed for a dragon spellcaster of your draconic benefactor’s type, such as those here (for example, [clairvoyance](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1466.md) and [paralyze](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1622.md) for a conspirator dragon benefactor). You can cast each of these spells once per day as innate spells of the same tradition as your draconic benefactor.

## Traits

Kobold: A creature with this trait is a member of the [kobold](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=80.md) ancestry, reptilian humanoids who are usually Small and typically have [darkvision](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2410.md). An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by kobolds.
