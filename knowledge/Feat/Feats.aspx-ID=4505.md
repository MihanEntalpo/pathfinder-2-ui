# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Lucky Keepsake](./Feats.aspx-ID=4505.md) Feat 9

Leshy
Source [Player Core pg. 69](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites [Leshy Superstition](./Feats.aspx-ID=4496.md)

---

You have a keepsake that grants you luck. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saves against spells and magical effects at all times, not just when you use Leshy Superstition. If you lose the keepsake, you lose the bonus until you designate a new keepsake, typically over the course of a week.

## Traits

Leshy: A creature with this trait is a member of the leshy ancestry. These small plant or fungus creatures are humanoid in form. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by leshies. A weapon with this trait is created and used by leshies.
