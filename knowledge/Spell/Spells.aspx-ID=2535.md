# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Briny Bolt [two-actions] Spell 1

[Attack](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=540&Redirected=1.md) [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Water
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 233](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Range 60 feet; Targets 1 creature
Defense AC

---

You hurl a bolt of salt water from your extended hand. Make a ranged spell attack against a target within range.

Critical Success The creature takes 4d6 bludgeoning damage and is [blinded](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=59.md) for 1 round and [dazzled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=65.md) for 1 minute as salt water sprays into its eyes. The creature can spend an [Interact](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2297.md) action to rub its eyes and end the blinded condition, but not the dazzled condition.
Success The creature takes 2d6 bludgeoning damage and is blinded for 1 round. The creature can spend an Interact action wiping the salt water from its eyes to end the blinded condition.

---

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 2d6.
