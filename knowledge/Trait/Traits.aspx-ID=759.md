# [Kobold](./Traits.aspx-ID=759.md)

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 317](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
A creature with this trait is a member of the [kobold](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=80.md) ancestry, reptilian humanoids who are usually Small and typically have [darkvision](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2410.md). An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by kobolds.
