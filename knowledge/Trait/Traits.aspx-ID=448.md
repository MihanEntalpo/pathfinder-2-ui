# [Infamous](./Traits.aspx-ID=448.md)

### Legacy Content

Source Kingmaker Adventure Path pg. 543
The Infamous trait increases your Infamy score when the structure is built. Some structures have both [Famous](./Traits.aspx-ID=447.md) and Infamous traits; in this case apply the one that matches your kingdom's preference (see Fame and Infamy).
