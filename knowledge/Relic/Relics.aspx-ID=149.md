# [Rolling Geode](./Relics.aspx-ID=149.md)Minor Gift

[Earth](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=585.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 312](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Aspect earth

---

Activate [two-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md)); Frequency once per hour; Effect Stone and crystal form a sphere that chases your foes. You create a geode in an unoccupied square within 30 feet. You can spend a single action, which has the [concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) trait, to move the geode up to 30 feet along the ground. If the geode enters a creature's space, the creature must succeed at a basic Reflex save or take 2d6 bludgeoning damage; a creature can take damage this way only once per turn, even if you roll the geode through its space multiple times. The geode persists for up to 1 minute or until destroyed or Dismissed; it has AC 5, Hardness 10, and 40 Hit Points, and is immune to critical hits and precision damage.
