# [Haunting Presence](./Hazards.aspx-ID=647.md)Hazard 0

[Unique](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=723.md) [Haunt](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=622.md) [Simple](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=865.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 32](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 19 to notice an unsettling sensation
Description Ghostly sounds or glimpses of apparitions move to startle or distract those who walk the streets.

---

Disable DC 16 [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md) to exorcise the haunt, or light the Eternal Lantern (automatically disables these haunts)
Ghostly Menace [reaction] ([divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md), [emotion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=590.md), [fear](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=598.md), [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md)) Trigger A haunt encounter occurs; Effect Kugaptee’s presence makes itself known in a frightening way—a sudden shriek of agony, an explosive burst of red butterflies, a momentarily overwhelming air of decay, or the like. A random PC takes 2d6+3 mental damage (DC 19 [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Will save), and then the haunting presence vanishes, leaving behind nothing but mental scars and trauma.
