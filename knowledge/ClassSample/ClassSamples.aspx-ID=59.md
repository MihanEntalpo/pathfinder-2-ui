# [Dancer](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=59.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 102](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Through myriad styles of dance, you inspire your allies and channel otherworldly magic.

## Ability Scores

Make Charisma highest, followed by Dexterity. Add to Constitution for more health and to Intelligence for skills.

## Skills

[Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md), [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md), [Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md), [Medicine](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=42.md), [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md), [Performance](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=45.md)

## Muse

[Maestro](../Muses/Muses.aspx-ID=6.md)

## Higher-Level Feats

[Uplifting Overture](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=4587.md) (2nd), [Triple Time](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=4594.md) (4th), [Allegro](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=4622.md) (14th)

## Spell Repertoire
