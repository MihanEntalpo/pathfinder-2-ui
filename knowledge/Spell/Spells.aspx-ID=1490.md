Deity Nyarlathotep (The Veiled Voice)
Range touch; Targets 1 target
Duration 10 minutes

---

You shroud a creature from others' senses. The target becomes [invisible](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=83.md), but not merely to vision. The invisibility granted by disappearance applies to all precise senses an observer might have. It's still possible for a creature to find the target by [Seeking](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2301.md) using various senses, looking for disturbed dust, hearing gaps in the sound spectrum, or finding some other way to discover the presence of a creature that is otherwise undetectable.
