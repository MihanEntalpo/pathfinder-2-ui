## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Rare Magical Sonic
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 297](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 2,000 gp
Usage held in 2 hands; Bulk 2
Base Weapon [Greatsword](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=379.md)

---

This massive, curved blade roars when it crashes down on opponents, seemingly intent on subduing them with sheer force. It’s said materials for the first thundering fury dadaos came from the hide of Yorak, the Horned Thunder, a legendary kaiju that roams the Shanguang desert. The lack of embellishments on this [+2 striking](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2861.md) [thundering](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2850.md) [greatsword](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=379.md) belies a weapon of deadly efficacy.

Activate—Thunder Dance [one-action] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect You infuse the thundering fury dadao with the speed of lightning and the force of thunder. The weapon gains the [agile](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=526.md) and [forceful](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=611.md) traits for one round. You can [Sustain](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2317.md) this activation for up to 1 minute.
