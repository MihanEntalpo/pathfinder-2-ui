# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Hardy Traveler](./Feats.aspx-ID=4486.md) Feat 9

Human
Source [Player Core pg. 65](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

There’s no journey too far or burden too heavy when you have friends at your side or hope within your heart. Increase your maximum and encumbered Bulk limits by 1. In addition, you gain a +10-foot circumstance bonus to your Speed during overland travel.

## Traits

Human: A creature with this trait is a member of the human ancestry. Humans are a diverse array of people known for their adaptability. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by humans.
