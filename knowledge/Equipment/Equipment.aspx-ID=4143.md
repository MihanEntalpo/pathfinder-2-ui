[Rare](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=683.md) [Consumable](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=564.md) [Cursed](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=567.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Potion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=672.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 161](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Activate [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md))

---

A pickled lizard's tongue, tied into a knot, floats in this oily potion. When you drink it, you can speak and understand all spoken (but not written) languages for 1 hour. However, if you attempt to translate any spoken language, your translation is always erroneous in a way likely to cause substantial confusion or anger, typically reducing the listener's [attitude](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2392.md) toward you by one step. You aren't aware of your error, and any attempt to correct the mistake only compounds it.
