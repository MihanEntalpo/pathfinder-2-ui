# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Feet to Fins [two-actions] Spell 3

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Morph
Source [Player Core pg. 331](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Deities Besmara, Dagon, Jin Li, Picoperi, Ragadahn, Seafarer's Hope, Sobek, Telastmar, Ylimancha, [Zeaki](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=707.md)
Lesson lesson of the shark
Range touch; Targets 1 willing creature
Duration 10 minutes

---

The target's feet transform into fins, improving mobility in the water but reducing it on land. The target gains a swim Speed equal to its normal land Speed, but its land Speed becomes 5 feet.

---

Heightened (6th) The spell lasts until your next daily preparations.
