## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Linguist Dedication Feat 2

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Dedication](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=572.md)
Source [Player Core 2 pg. 203](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetype [Linguist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=253.md)
Prerequisites You speak at least 3 languages

---

You have studied languages and their development. You become trained in [Society](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=47.md); if you were already trained in Society, you instead become an expert in Society. You gain the [Multilingual](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=5181.md) skill feat twice.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Multilingual Cipher Feat 4

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Skill](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=695.md)
Source [Player Core 2 pg. 203](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetype [Linguist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=253.md)
Prerequisites Linguist Dedication; expert in [Society](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=47.md)

---

You use multiple languages to create and break codes. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Decipher Writing. If another creature attempts to Decipher Writing you encoded, they take a –2 circumstance penalty unless they speak all the languages you used when you created the writing.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Phonetic Training Feat 4

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Skill](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=695.md)
Source [Player Core 2 pg. 203](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetype [Linguist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=253.md)
Prerequisites Linguist Dedication; expert in [Society](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=47.md)

---

You can pronounce and repeat words with near-perfect accuracy even if you don’t understand their meaning, and you can write them down in a phonetic script that allows you or another creature with Phonetic Training to read it later. Even if the words are in a language with sounds you can’t pronounce, you can faithfully transcribe them. Memorizing long sequences of words remains just as difficult as it is normally.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Spot Translate [reaction] Feat 4

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Auditory](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=541.md) [Linguistic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=642.md)
Source [Player Core 2 pg. 203](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetype [Linguist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=253.md)
Prerequisites Linguist Dedication
Trigger Another creature within 60 feet uses a [linguistic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=642.md) effect in a language you understand.

---

You bridge a language gap, repeating the message in a different language that you know. Choose a language you understand to repeat the message in. The [linguistic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=642.md) effect counts as both languages, rather than only the language the triggering creature is using, potentially allowing it to affect a wider range of creatures. As normal, you can translate normal speech without Spot Translate and without using a reaction, but this reaction allows you to apply the benefits of translation to spells and actions such as command or [Demoralize](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2395.md).

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Analyze Idiolect Feat 6

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Skill](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=695.md)
Source [Player Core 2 pg. 203](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetype [Linguist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=253.md)
Prerequisites Linguist Dedication; expert in [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38.md) and [Society](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=47.md)

---

You break down a specific individual’s idiolect, memorizing their speech mannerisms and habits. If you interact with someone for at least 10 minutes, when you later attempt to [Impersonate](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2388.md) that individual, you gain a +4 circumstance bonus to your [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38.md) checks and DCs. Due to the intense character study required, if you Analyze Idiolect again, it replaces any previous idiolect you memorized.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Read Shibboleths Feat 7

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Linguistic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=642.md) [Skill](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=695.md)
Source [Player Core 2 pg. 203](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetypes [Linguist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=253.md), [Venture-Gossip](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=311.md)
Prerequisites Linguist Dedication; master in [Society](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=47.md)

---

All communication is filled with slight tells and signals. If you interact with someone for at least 10 minutes, their regional words, pronunciation changes, and nonverbal cues provide you with a minor fact about their social environment, such as their hometown or certain groups they might belong to. If a target of this ability is being deceptive about their social environment, such as a commoner pretending to be a noble, they secretly attempt a [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38.md), [Society](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=47.md), or appropriate [Lore](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=41.md) check against your Society DC, providing you a false minor fact consistent with their assumed identity on a success.

Once you’ve gleaned one or more true minor facts about a person, you can then incorporate these mannerisms into your speech to present yourself in a way that they find familiar. This grants you a +1 circumstance bonus to your [Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md) and Deception checks with them.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Crude Communication Feat 8

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Player Core 2 pg. 203](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetypes [Linguist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=253.md), [Venture-Gossip](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=311.md)
Prerequisites Linguist Dedication

---

Even if you don’t speak a creature’s language, you can rely on inflection, root words, and body language to infer rudimentary meaning. If you interact with a creature for at least 10 minutes and that creature can speak a language, the GM rolls a secret [Society](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=47.md) check for you, with a DC appropriate for the language’s rarity. On a success, you understand the gist of the meaning and can communicate basic concepts back to the creature; on a failure, you are mistaken or communicate incorrect concepts. If you’re legendary in Society, you can communicate instantly without needing to attempt a Society check; even if you didn’t know the medium of communication the creature uses (speech, sign language, and so on), you intuit this information as soon as they try to communicate.
