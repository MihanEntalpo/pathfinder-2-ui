# [Sportlebore](./Hazards.aspx-ID=625.md)Hazard 7

Source [Monster Core 2 pg. 306](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=276.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 26 (expert)
Description A sportlebore hides amid victuals on a plate of food or within a pack of trail rations.

---

Disable A creature that notices the sportlebore can remove it from the food it’s [hidden](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=79.md) within automatically as an [Interact](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2297.md) action.
Mimic Food [reaction] Trigger A creature eats food the sportlebore is [hidden](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=79.md) within; Effect The sportlebore rapidly multiplies in the host’s stomach, exposing the host to sportlebore infestation.
Sportlebore Infestation ([disease](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=578.md)) Saving Throw DC 22 Fortitude; Stage 1 carrier with no ill effect (1 day); Stage 2 [enfeebled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=71.md) 1 (1 hour); Stage 3 enfeebled 2 (1 hour); Stage 4 4d6 bludgeoning damage (DC 25 [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Fortitude save) as the host painfully vomits out a sportlebore swarm and returns to stage 1
