# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Blood and Spirit](./Feats.aspx-ID=8079.md) Feat 5

[Dragonblood](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=794.md)
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 216](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

Prerequisites Divine Dragonblood

---

Your connection to the divine has sanctified your blood, but the power only manifests when you are shedding it. Choose [holy](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=517.md) or [unholy](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=521.md). While you are taking [persistent bleed damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86.md), you can [Interact](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2297.md) to coat a piercing or slashing weapon you’re wielding with your blood. Until the end of your turn, your Strikes with that weapon deal an additional 1d6 [spirit](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=737.md) damage with the chosen trait to creatures with the opposing trait. Alternatively, you can deal 1d6 slashing damage to yourself (which can’t be resisted in any way) as part of that Interact action if you aren’t taking persistent bleed damage.

## Traits

Dragonblood: A creature with this trait has the dragonblood versatile heritage. These creatures are born of draconic lineage and often have draconic appearances.
