# Censer

Source [Rage of Elements pg. 123](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=205.md)
Censers are decorative containers, often metal or ceramic, in which incense is burned. Some censers are used solely in religious ceremonies, but many are made for domestic use. They range in shape and size, from lidded pots to egg-shaped vessels attached to chains that allow them to be swung about. Most are designed with ornate openings to increase airflow.
