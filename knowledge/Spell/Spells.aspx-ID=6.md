# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Air Walk [two-actions] Spell 4

### Legacy Content

Air [Transmutation](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=157.md)
Source [Core Rulebook pg. 317](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)
Traditions [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Cast [two-actions] somatic, verbal
Range touch; Targets 1 creature
Duration 5 minutes

---
