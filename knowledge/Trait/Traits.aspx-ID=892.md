# [Wyrwood](./Traits.aspx-ID=892.md)

Source [Monster Core 2 pg. 366](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=276.md)
A wyrwood is a small, sapient living construct crafted of wood and powered with an [aeon stone](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=3055.md) or a similar magical stone.
