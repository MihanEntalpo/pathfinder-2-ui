# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Mineral Deposits](./Feats.aspx-ID=8085.md) [one-action] Feat 9

[Dragonblood](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=794.md)
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 217](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

Frequency once per hour
Prerequisites [Terra Dragonblood](./Feats.aspx-ID=8078.md)

---

Precious metals from your bloodstream line your claws, jaws, or the tip of your tail, and you’re able call upon your blood to surge and empower your attacks. Choose [cold iron](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2916.md) or [silver](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2920.md) and a claw, jaws, or tail unarmed attack that you have. Until the end of your turn, your Strikes with the chosen unarmed attack count as being made of the chosen metal. At 17th level, you can choose [adamantine](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2915.md).

## Traits

Dragonblood: A creature with this trait has the dragonblood versatile heritage. These creatures are born of draconic lineage and often have draconic appearances.
