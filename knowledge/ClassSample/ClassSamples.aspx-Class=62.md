# Sorcerer Sample Builds

# Draconic Scion

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 156](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Through fate or design, you count a powerful dragon as one of your ancestors. You consider this a mark of great honor and believe that you can use your power to better the world. Woe betide any villains who stand in your way!

## Ability Scores

Prioritize Charisma. Intelligence will help you learn more skills, and a good Dexterity will help keep you safe from trouble.

## Skills

[Arcana](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=35.md), [Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md), [Intimidation](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=40.md), [Society](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=47.md)

## Bloodline

[Draconic (Fortune Dragon)](../Bloodline/Bloodlines.aspx-ID=23.md)

## Higher-Level Feats

Entreat with Forebears (2nd), Split Shot (4th), Advanced Bloodline (6th), Explosion of Power (8th), Greater Bloodline (10th), Bloodline Focus (12th)

## Spell Repertoire
