# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Ghostly Shift [one-action] Cantrip 3

[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Cantrip](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=550.md) [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) [Psychic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=894.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 23](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature
Duration sustained up to 1 minute

---

You gain resistance 2 to all damage (except force).

---

Heightened (+2) The resistance increases by 1.

---

Amp Your insubstantiality is complete enough that you can pass through other creatures, causing painful interference effects when you do so. Once each round when you Stride, [Burrow](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2310.md), [Swim](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2381.md), or [Fly](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2312.md), you can pass through one creature's space during that action without needing to attempt a check to [Tumble Through](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2370.md), though you can't end your turn in its space. When you pass through a creature's space in this way, you deal 2d6 force damage to that creature, with a basic Fortitude save.
Amp Heightened (+2) Increase the damage dealt by phasing through a creature by 1d6.
