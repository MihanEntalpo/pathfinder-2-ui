# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Enkindled QuintessenceItem 14

Rare [Contract](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=329.md) Invested Magical
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 227](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)
Bulk &mdash;

---

You have fully subsumed your soul within that of a dragon’s soul, overloading your very essence with eldritch potential. You immediately retrain your class to that of a [sorcerer](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=62.md) with the [draconic bloodline](../Bloodline/Bloodlines.aspx-ID=23.md), with your draconic exemplar matching your bound dragon. You are able to [cast spells](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2734.md) immediately as if you had a full 8 hours of rest and completed your [daily preparations](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2575.md); however, you become [drained](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=68.md) 2 from the strain of igniting your soul’s magical potential.

Once per day, from any distance, the dragon can either [teleport](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1720.md) you to their lair or teleport to switch places with you. This is a 3-action activity with the teleportation trait and the dragon’s tradition trait.

---

Oathbreaker’s Calamity Should either party break the oath, both your souls begin to immolate through your physical forms. If either of you casts a spell, they immediately become [drained](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=68.md) 3 unless they’re casting it to harm the other party. If either of you die as a result of this calamity, you rise as a wraith (if it’s you) or a wyrmwraith (if it’s the dragon).
