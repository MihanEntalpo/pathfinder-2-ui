# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Staff of the Unblinking EyeItem 6+

[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Staff](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=700.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 281](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
[PFS Note](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md) All Pathfinder agents have access to the this item

Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk 1

---

The stone head piece of this smooth wooden staff is carved to look like a lidless eye. While wielding it, you gain a +1 status bonus to Perception checks made for initiative.

Activate Cast a Spell; Effect You expend a number of charges from the staff to cast a spell from its list.

Craft Requirements Supply one casting of all listed ranks of all listed spells.
