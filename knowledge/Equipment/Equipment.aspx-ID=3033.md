# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Thurible of RevelationItem 3+

[Divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 277](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk 1

---

This brass censer dangles on a length of chain. Most thuribles of revelation are adorned with swirling [Empyrean](../Language/Languages.aspx-ID=115.md) text, though some are iron and feature [Diabolic](../Language/Languages.aspx-ID=117.md) or [Chthonian](../Language/Languages.aspx-ID=114.md) text.

Activate—Burn Incense [two-actions] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)); Cost incense worth
at least 5 gp; Effect You light the incense inside the censer, and it burns for 1 hour. During that time, as long you are holding the thurible, you gain a +1 item bonus to Religion checks, and any critical failure you roll when you Decipher Writing of a religious nature is a failure instead.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Thurible of Revelation (Moderate)Item 10

Source [GM Core pg. 277](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Price 900 gp
The thurible grants a +2 bonus. Once per day, when you activate the thurible, you can increase its revelations. During that activation, you can hold the thurible up to your eyes with an Interact action to gain the effects of [see the unseen](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1663.md) for 1 round by peering through the smoke.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Thurible of Revelation (Greater)Item 18

Source [GM Core pg. 277](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Price 19,000 gp
The thurible grants a +3 bonus. The greater version shares the once-per-day enhanced revelations of the moderate version, except peering through the smoke also grants you the effects of [truesight](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1727.md).
