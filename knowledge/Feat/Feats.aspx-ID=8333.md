# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Emotional Surge](./Feats.aspx-ID=8333.md) [one-action] Feat 10

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) [Emotion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=590.md) [Psyche](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=901.md) [Psychic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=894.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 27](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

Requirements [emotional acceptance](../SubconsciousMinds/SubconsciousMinds.aspx-ID=5.md) subconscious mind

---

Your heart soars, increasing your power for a moment. You gain a +2 status bonus to attack rolls and skill checks until the start of your next turn.

## Traits

Concentrate: An action with this trait requires a degree of mental concentration and discipline.

Emotion: This effect alters a creature’s emotions. Effects with this trait always have the mental trait as well. Creatures with special training or that have mechanical or artificial intelligence are immune to emotion effects.

Psyche: Your psyche must be unleashed to use abilities that have the psyche trait, and they end automatically when your unleashed psyche subsides.

Psychic: This indicates abilities from the [psychic](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=68.md) class.
