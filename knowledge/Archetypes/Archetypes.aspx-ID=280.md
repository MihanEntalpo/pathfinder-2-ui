### Multiclass Animist Characters

The animist archetype is a great way to create a character of another class who has a bond to a spiritual entity that they can draw powers from. The apparitions available to animists can open up opportunities to gain a variety of [Lore](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=41.md) skills and unique [divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md) spells.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Animist Dedication Feat 2

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Dedication](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=572.md) [Multiclass](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=659.md)
Source [War of Immortals pg. 56](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=232.md)
Archetype [Animist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=280.md)
Prerequisites Wisdom +2

---

You have formed a bond with an apparition and can cast [divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md) spells. Choose a single [apparition](../Apparition/Apparitions.aspx.md) from those available to the animist. You become bound to that apparition and can attune to it each day during your [daily preparations](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2575.md) to become trained in its apparition skills. If you are 8th level, you are expert in its apparition skills while attuned to it, and if you are 16th level, you are a master in its apparition skills while attuned to it. You can spend 1 day of downtime ending your relationship with this apparition and bonding to a new one.

You gain the [Cast a Spell](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2734.md) activity. You can prepare two common cantrips each day from the [divine](../SpellLists/SpellLists.aspx-Tradition=2.md) spell list or any other divine cantrips you have access to, including the cantrip listed in your apparition's apparition spells. You're trained in the spell attack modifier and spell DC statistics. Your key spellcasting attribute for animist archetype spells is Wisdom, and they are divine animist spells.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Basic Animist Spellcasting Feat 4

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [War of Immortals pg. 56](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=232.md)
Archetype [Animist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=280.md)
Prerequisites Animist Dedication

---

You gain the basic spellcasting benefits as a prepared divine caster. In addition to standard [divine](../SpellLists/SpellLists.aspx-Tradition=2.md) tradition spells, you can prepare your bonded apparition’s apparition spells in your spell slots of the appropriate level from the animist archetype.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Animist's Power Feat 6

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [War of Immortals pg. 56](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=232.md)
Archetype [Animist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=280.md)
Prerequisites Spiritual Awakening

---

You gain one animist feat. For the purpose of meeting its prerequisites, your animist level is equal to half your character level. You can’t use this feat to gain animist feats with the [wandering](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=843.md) trait.

Special You can select this feat more than once. Each time you select it, you gain another animist feat.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Apparition Magic Feat 8

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [War of Immortals pg. 56](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=232.md)
Archetype [Animist](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=280.md)
Prerequisites Basic Animist Spellcasting

---

You can cast more [divine](../SpellLists/SpellLists.aspx-Tradition=2.md) spells each day. You gain 1 additional spell slot from animist archetype feats for each spell rank other than your two highest animist spell slots. These additional slots can only be used to prepare spells from your apparition’s apparition spells.
