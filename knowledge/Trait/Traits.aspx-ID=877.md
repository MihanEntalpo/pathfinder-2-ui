# [Grioth](./Traits.aspx-ID=877.md)

Source [Monster Core 2 pg. 364](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=276.md)
A grioth is a batlike alien that wields occult powers and can exist in the dark vacuum of space. They have [darkvision](../MonsterAbility/MonsterAbilities.aspx-ID=59.md) and echolocation.
