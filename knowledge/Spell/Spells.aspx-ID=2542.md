# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Life's Fresh Bloom [two-actions] Spell 4

## This Spell may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Rare [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Healing [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) [Vitality](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=509.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 308](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Traditions [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Range 30 feet; Area 20-foot burst
Duration [sustained](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2317.md) up to 5 rounds

---

The ground around you blooms with life, sprouting sparkling grass, flowers, and small shrubs. Each living and non-nindoru creature that starts its turn standing on the ground in the area recovers 1d6 Hit Points and gains a +5‑foot status bonus to its Speed until the end of its next turn. Nindoru fiends and [undead](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=722.md) that start their turn in the area of life’s fresh bloom instead become [sickened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=91.md) 1 unless they succeed at a Fortitude saving throw.

---

Heightened (7th) Increase the Hit Points restored to 1d8 and the sickened condition value to 2.
Heightened (10th) Increase the Hit Points restored to 1d10 and the sickened condition value to 3.
