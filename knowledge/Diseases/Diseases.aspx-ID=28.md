# [Tetanus](./Diseases.aspx-ID=28.md)Disease 1

[Disease](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=578.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 88](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
An infection introduced through open wounds, tetanus can produce stiffness, muscle spasms strong enough to break bones, and ultimately death.

Saving Throw DC 14 Fortitude; Onset 10 days; Stage 1 [clumsy](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=61.md) 1 (1 week); Stage 2 clumsy 2 and can’t speak (1 day); Stage 3 [paralyzed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=85.md) with spasms (1 day); Stage 4 death
