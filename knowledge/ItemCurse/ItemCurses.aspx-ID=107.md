# Infected SilverCurse 13

[Rare](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=683.md) [Cursed](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=567.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 337](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
After 1 minute of carrying infected silver, it fuses to you. You become [clumsy](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=61.md) 1 and remain so as long as the curse persists. As long as you remain cursed, you take a –2 status penalties to all saving throws against [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) effects created by nindorus.
