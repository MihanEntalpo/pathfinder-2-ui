# [Catalyst](./Traits.aspx-ID=791.md)

Source [Howl of the Wild pg. 216](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=226.md)
Items with the catalyst trait are consumables that alter or magnify specific spells. Using a catalyst adds the [manipulate](./Traits.aspx-ID=645.md) trait to the spell. Activating a catalyst is part of Casting the Spell. The catalyst might increase the number of actions required to Cast the Spell, as indicated in the catalyst's Activate entry. You can draw the catalyst as part of Casting the Spell.
