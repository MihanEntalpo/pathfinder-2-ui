# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Dazzling Dragonet Disappearance](./Feats.aspx-ID=8062.md) [reaction] Feat 17

[Dragonet](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=893.md)
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 213](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

Frequency once per day
Trigger An adjacent enemy hits you with an [attack](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=540.md).

---

A brilliant sheen on your scales flashes when you’re hit, then you’re nowhere to be found. The triggering enemy becomes [dazzled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=65.md) for 1 round, and you become affected by a 4th-rank [invisibility](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1577.md) spell.

## Traits

Dragonet: Dragonets are miniature dragons, complete with wings, dagger-like teeth, and magical breath. They can be fiercely independent and proud, at times protective or avaricious, and they delight in the finer things in life.
