Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Base Weapon [Sling](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=430.md)

---

Made of shiny brown leather, this +1 striking sling has a single white thread interwoven into its cord.

Activate—Unleash Roar [two-actions] (manipulate,sonic); Frequency once per day; Effect You pull the white thread free, then whirl the sling in circles at high speed. It lets out an ear-piercing wave of sound. Each creature in a 30-foot cone takes 4d6 sonic damage (DC 21 basic Fortitude save). Any creature that fails is deafened for 1 round, or 1 hour on a critical failure.
