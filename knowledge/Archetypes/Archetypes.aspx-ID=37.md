## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Staff Acrobat Dedication Feat 2

### This Feat may contain spoilers from the Extinction Curse Adventure Path

### Legacy Content

Uncommon Archetype Dedication
Source [Pathfinder #151: The Show Must Go On pg. 75](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=21.md)
Archetype [Staff Acrobat](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=37.md)
Prerequisites Dexterity 16; trained in [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34&Redirected=1.md) and [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md); trained with at least one of the following weapons: staff, bo staff, halfling sling staff, or any weapon in the [spear](../WeaponGroups/WeaponGroups.aspx-ID=14.md) or [polearm](../WeaponGroups/WeaponGroups.aspx-ID=11.md) group (referred to in this archetype as “your staff”)

---

When you [High Jump](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2377&Redirected=1.md), Leap, or [Long Jump](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2378&Redirected=1.md) while wielding your staff, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to any [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md) check required and add 5 feet to the distance you can Leap vertically and horizontally. You can Shove and Trip even if you don’t have a free hand, provided you are wielding your staff. Finally, when you roll a success on a check to Balance while wielding your staff, you get a critical success instead.

Special You cannot select another dedication feat until you have gained two other feats from the staff acrobat archetype.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Bullying Staff Feat 4

### This Feat may contain spoilers from the Extinction Curse Adventure Path

### Legacy Content

Uncommon Archetype
Source [Pathfinder #151: The Show Must Go On pg. 75](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=21.md)
Archetype [Staff Acrobat](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=37.md)
Prerequisites Staff Acrobat Dedication
Requirements You are wielding your staff.

---

You can attempt to Shove or Trip creatures up to two sizes larger than you. If you have master proficiency with your staff, you can attempt to Shove or Trip creatures up to three sizes larger than you.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Staff Sweep [one-action] Feat 6

### This Feat may contain spoilers from the Extinction Curse Adventure Path

### Legacy Content

Uncommon Archetype Flourish
Source [Pathfinder #151: The Show Must Go On pg. 75](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=21.md)
Archetype [Staff Acrobat](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=37.md)
Prerequisites Staff Acrobat Dedication
Requirements You are wielding your staff.

---

You sweep your foes away with your staff. Roll an Athletics check to Shove or Trip, and compare the result to the appropriate DCs of up to two foes, each of whom must be within your reach and no more than 5 feet apart.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Levering Strike [two-actions] Feat 8

### This Feat may contain spoilers from the Extinction Curse Adventure Path

### Legacy Content

Uncommon Archetype Press
Source [Pathfinder #151: The Show Must Go On pg. 75](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=21.md)
Archetype [Staff Acrobat](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=37.md)
Prerequisites Staff Acrobat Dedication
Requirements You are wielding your staff.

---

Make a Strike with your staff. As long as you don’t critically fail, the target becomes flat-footed and takes a –2 circumstance penalty to its DC against Shove, Trip, and Tumble Through attempts until the start of your next turn.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Whirlwind Stance [one-action] Feat 10

### This Feat may contain spoilers from the Extinction Curse Adventure Path

### Legacy Content

Uncommon Archetype Flourish Stance

Archetype [Staff Acrobat](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=37.md)

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Pivot Strike [two-actions] Feat 14

### This Feat may contain spoilers from the Extinction Curse Adventure Path
