# [Ripples and Waves](./Relics.aspx-ID=195.md)Grand Gift

[Water](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=732.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 319](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Aspect water

---

You become attuned to the ebb and flow of all things, as if embodying water itself. You gain wavesense 60 feet as a precise sense and can cast 5th-rank hydraulic push at will as an innate spell.

Activate [two-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect Ankle-deep water floods outward from you, filling a 60-foot emanation centered on you for 1 minute. Enemies within the area without a swim Speed treat the area as [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366&Redirected=1.md) for movement on land. While the effect persists, you can have any water effects you generate originate from any point within the emanation, in addition to their normal range and area.
