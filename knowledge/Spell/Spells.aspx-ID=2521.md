# Crown of Prophets [one-action] Focus 1

## This Spell may contain spoilers from the Myth-Speakers Adventure Path

[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Animist](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=836.md) [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md) [Morph](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=657.md)
Source [Pathfinder #216: The Acropolis Pyre pg. 83](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=271.md)
Saving Throw Fortitude; Duration sustained up to 1 minute

---

Serpents sprout from your head, equally ready to bite your foes or offer you counsel. You gain a fangs unarmed attack that deals 1d6 piercing damage and has the [agile](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=526.md), [finesse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=602.md), and [versatile poison](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=724.md) traits. The first time you Cast or Sustain this spell each round, you can perform one of the following as a free action.

- Serpentine Advice The snakes compete to offer you counsel. You gain the [Dubious Knowledge](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=5142.md) feat until the end of your turn, then you Recall Knowledge.
- Toxic Prophacy ([poison](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=669.md)) Requirements A creature within 15 feet damaged you since the end of your last turn; Effect The snakes spit toxic curses at the required creature, dealing 2d4 poison damage (basic Fortitude save).

---

Heightened (+1) The poison damage dealt by Toxic Prophecy increases by 1d4.
