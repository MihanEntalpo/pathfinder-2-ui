# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Pest Form [two-actions] Spell 1

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Polymorph
Source [Player Core pg. 348](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Deities Bastet, Daikitsu, Grandmother Spider, [Sigek](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=729.md), Ydersius, Zevgavizeb
Patron Theme mosquito witch
Duration 10 minutes

---

You transform into the battle form of a Tiny animal, such as
a cat, insect, lizard, or rat. You can decide the specific type of
animal (such as a rat or praying mantis), but this has no effect
on your Size or statistics. While in this form, you gain the animal trait, and you can't make Strikes. You can Dismiss the spell.

You gain the following statistics and abilities:

- AC = 15 + your level. Ignore your armor's check penalty and Speed reduction.
- Speed 20 feet.
- Weakness 5 to physical damage. (If you take physical damage in this form, you take 5 additional damage.)
- Low-light vision and [imprecise](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2407&Redirected=1.md) scent 30 feet.
- [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34&Redirected=1.md) and [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48&Redirected=1.md) modifiers of +10, unless your own is higher; [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md) modifier –4.

---

Heightened (4th) You can turn into a flying creature, such as a bird, which grants you a fly Speed of 20 feet.
