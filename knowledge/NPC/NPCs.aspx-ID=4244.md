# Deniigi

Brawny, quiet, and stoic, Deniigi is one of Aaminiut's finest hunters. Among Sakuachi's companions, she's the unofficial “muscle” of the group. Deniigi has never traveled far outside of her village and prefers to speak in her native Erutaki. She's likely to be suspicious of the PCs, though she respects characters who show bravery in battle, especially those who put themselves in harm's way to protect Sakuachi, acknowledging such acts with a silent nod of approval. Her bear-claw daggers were gifts from her older siblings, each made from a trophy taken from one of their many kills.
