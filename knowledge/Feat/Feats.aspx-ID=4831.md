# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Twin Riposte](./Feats.aspx-ID=4831.md) [reaction] Feat 10

[Fighter](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=601.md) [Ranger](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=682.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 148](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetypes [Avenger](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=282.md)*, [Dual-Weapon Warrior](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=247.md)*
Trigger A creature within your reach critically fails to Strike you
Requirements You are benefiting from Twin Parry
* This archetype offers Twin Riposte at a different level than displayed here.

---

A clever parry with one weapon leaves your opponent open to an attack with the other weapon. Make a melee Strike or Disarm action against the triggering opponent.

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more archetypes in addition to the listed classes. When selected this way, the feat does not have its class traits.
