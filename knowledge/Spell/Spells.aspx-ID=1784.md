# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Face in the Crowd [one-action] Focus 1

Uncommon [Cleric](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=554.md) [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Visual
Source [Player Core pg. 372](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Domain [cities](../Domain/Domains.aspx-ID=66.md)
Duration 1 minute

---

While in a crowd of roughly similar creatures, your appearance becomes bland and nondescript. You gain a +2 status bonus to [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38&Redirected=1.md) and [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48&Redirected=1.md) checks to go incognito among the crowd, and you ignore [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366&Redirected=1.md) caused by the crowd. This counts as setting up a disguise for the Impersonate use of Deception and you add your level even if you're untrained.

---

Heightened (3rd) The spell gains a range of 10 feet and can target up to 10 creatures.
