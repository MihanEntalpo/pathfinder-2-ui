# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Pervasive Superstition](./Feats.aspx-ID=4524.md) Feat 9

Orc
Source [Player Core pg. 73](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites [Orc Superstition](./Feats.aspx-ID=4516.md)

---

You steep yourself in superstition and practice ancient orc mental exercises for shrugging off the effects of magic. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to saving throws against spells and magical effects at all times.

## Pervasive Superstition Leads To...

## Traits

Orc: A creature with this trait is a member of the orc ancestry. These green-skinned people tend to have darkvision. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by orcs. An item with this trait is created and used by orcs.
