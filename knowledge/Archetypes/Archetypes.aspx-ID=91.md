### Legacy Content

Source [Pathfinder #166: Despair on Danger Island pg. 78](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=85.md)
You've trained in one of Goka's oldest traditions of espionage, intimidation, and combat mastery. You use your skills to learn about your enemies and cut them down with your mastery of the [butterfly sword](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=162.md).

# Additional Feats

Source [Player Core pg. 215](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Some archetypes include a list of “Additional Feats” that appear in other sources. The list includes each feat’s level, which might be different than normal when gained from the archetype. You can take the feat as an archetype feat of that level, meaning it counts toward the number of feats required by the archetype’s dedication feat. When selected this way, a feat that normally has a class’s trait (such as the fighter trait) doesn’t have that class trait.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Butterfly Blade Dedication Feat 4

### This Feat may contain spoilers from the Fists of the Ruby Phoenix Adventure Path

### Legacy Content

Uncommon Archetype Dedication
Source [Pathfinder #166: Despair on Danger Island pg. 78](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=85.md)
Archetype [Butterfly Blade](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=91.md)
Prerequisites trained in [butterfly swords](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=162.md)
Access Defeat a member of the Butterfly Blades in a sanctioned bout.

---

You become trained in [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38&Redirected=1.md) and [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48&Redirected=1.md); if you were already trained, you become an expert instead. Whenever your proficiency rank in any weapon increases to expert or beyond, you also gain that new proficiency rank with [butterfly swords](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=162.md). You gain access to butterfly swords.

Special You cannot select another dedication feat until you have gained two other feats from this archetype.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Combat Assessment [one-action] Feat 4

### Legacy Content

Fighter
Source [Advanced Player's Guide pg. 126](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=39.md)
Archetype [Butterfly Blade](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=91.md)

---

You make a telegraphed attack to learn about your foe. Make a melee Strike. On a hit, you can immediately attempt a check to Recall Knowledge about the target. On a critical hit, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to the check to Recall Knowledge. The target is temporarily immune to Combat Assessment for 1 day.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Deadly Butterfly Feat 8

### Legacy Content

Archetype
Source [Pathfinder #166: Despair on Danger Island pg. 78](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=85.md)
Archetype [Butterfly Blade](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=91.md)
Prerequisites Butterfly Blade Dedication

---

You gain access to the butterfly sword's critical specialization effect. If you already had access to the critical specialization effect or you gain the effect at a later time, you also gain the critical specialization effect for knives when you critically hit with a butterfly sword. You can benefit from only one critical specialization effect at a time.
