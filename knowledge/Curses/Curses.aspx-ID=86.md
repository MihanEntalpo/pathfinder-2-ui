# [Reviling Earth](./Curses.aspx-ID=86.md)Curse 12

[Curse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=566.md) [Death](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=571.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 87](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
A reviling earth curse usually appears across a specific geographical region, such as a ruined town, a necromancer's domain, or a similar area.

Saving Throw DC 30 Fortitude; Effect When you enter the area, you become [doomed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=67.md) 1, or doomed 2 on a critical failure.
