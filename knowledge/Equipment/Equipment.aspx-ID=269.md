# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Triton's ConchItem 9

### Legacy Content

Magical [Transmutation](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=157.md)
Source [Core Rulebook pg. 576](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)
Price 640 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L

---

If you put this large opalescent conch shell to your ear, you can hear the sound of the roaring sea crashing against the shore.

Activate [one-action] Interact ([auditory](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=541&Redirected=1.md)); Effect You can raise the conch to your lips and blow into it, letting out a long, rumbling note. For the next minute, you and all allies who were within 30 feet of you when you activated the conch gain a +2 item bonus to Athletics checks to Swim and can breathe under water.
