An indestructible shield is a high-grade greater reinforcing adamantine shield (Hardness 17, HP 136) that can withstand just about any damage. It can be damaged only by a [disintegrate](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1492.md) spell (roll damage as if against a creature that failed its save) or by an artifact tied to destruction.

Craft Requirements The raw materials must include at least 4,400 gp of adamantine.
