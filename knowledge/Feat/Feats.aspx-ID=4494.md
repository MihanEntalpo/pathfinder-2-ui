# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Harmlessly Cute](./Feats.aspx-ID=4494.md) Feat 1

Leshy
Source [Player Core pg. 68](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

Your size and demeanor make it easy for you to convince others that you mean no harm. You gain the Shameless Request skill feat. Additionally, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to initiative checks when you roll [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38&Redirected=1.md) for initiative.

## Traits

Leshy: A creature with this trait is a member of the leshy ancestry. These small plant or fungus creatures are humanoid in form. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by leshies. A weapon with this trait is created and used by leshies.
