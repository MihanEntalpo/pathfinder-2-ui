# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Oil of PotencyItem 2+

[Consumable](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=564.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Oil](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=663.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 257](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage held in 2 hands; Bulk L
Activate [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md))

---

When you apply this thick, viscous oil to a weapon or suit of armor, that item immediately becomes magically potent. If the item is a weapon, it temporarily becomes a +1 striking weapon, or, if it's armor, it temporarily becomes +1 resilient armor. This lasts for 1 minute.
