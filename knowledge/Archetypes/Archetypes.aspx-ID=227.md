# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Winged Warrior](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=227.md)

Source [Howl of the Wild pg. 82](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=226.md)
Nature provided wings to certain creatures to help them soar above predators or reach food hanging from tall trees or the side of a cliff. When those graced with wings no longer have to depend on flight for survival, their appendages can be turned to a different use: combat. In addition to granting strategic elevation over foes, wings provide a variety of combat advantages.

Winged warriors are found independently among several ancestries. While awakened animals seem able to use these techniques as if by instinct, tengu of the Inner Sea region are the most commonly seen practitioners of this fighting style. Naval and pirate crews in the Shackles particularly value a tengu’s skill at boarding actions. Warriors able to take flight, even temporarily, can often name their price when joining a crew on the Fever Sea. Despite the ranged armaments available on most ships, many sailors are hard-pressed to defend against skilled aerial assailants.

Even those who develop wings later in life, like certain surkis and those with grafted wings, can learn the fighting style of a winged warrior. Although it’s a long path, many of the techniques involved are reinvented, rediscovered, and even updated by individual warriors.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Winged Warrior Dedication Feat 2

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Dedication](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=572.md)
Source [Howl of the Wild pg. 82](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=226.md)
Archetype [Winged Warrior](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=227.md)
Prerequisites you have permanent wings (such as from an ancestry or graft)

---

Through rigorous training, you have strengthened your wings, granting you enough thrust to gain additional altitude. When you make a horizontal [Leap](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2298.md), increase the distance by 10 feet up to a maximum of your Speed. Any fly Speed granted by ancestry feats and other permanent wings increases by 5 feet.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Battering Wings Feat 4

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Howl of the Wild pg. 82](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=226.md)
Archetype [Winged Warrior](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=227.md)

---

Though not powerful enough for flight, your wings can deliver a thrashing to anyone who gets close. You gain a wing unarmed attack that deals 1d4 bludgeoning damage. Your wing attack is in the brawling group and has the [agile](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=526.md), [finesse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=602.md), and [unarmed](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=719.md) traits.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Wing Bounce [two-actions] Feat 4

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Howl of the Wild pg. 82](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=226.md)
Archetype [Winged Warrior](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=227.md)
Prerequisites Winged Warrior Dedication

---

You rush forward, flapping your wings for a bit of lift, and can use that momentum to knock down a foe. [Leap](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2298.md), and if you land within melee reach of at least one enemy, you can attempt to [Trip](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2382.md) that enemy. You don't need to have one hand free. If you successfully knock the target [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88.md), you can Leap again. If you have a fly Speed, you can [Fly](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2312.md) instead of Leap.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Sunbird Glare [one-action] Feat 6

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)

Archetype [Winged Warrior](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=227.md)

Requirements You’re in bright light

---

By keeping your feathers clean and sleek, you can catch the light across your wings to create a startling glare in the eyes of a creature within 20 feet. The target must succeed at a Fortitude save against your class DC or be [dazzled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=65.md) for 1 round (1 minute on a critical failure). ## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Pluck From the Sky [two-actions] Feat 8

[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Flourish](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=606.md)
Source [Howl of the Wild pg. 83](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=226.md)
Archetype [Winged Warrior](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=227.md)
Prerequisites Winged Warrior Dedication
Access tengu, [strix](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=40.md), or [awakened animal](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=72.md) (flying animal) ancestry

---

The skies are your rightful place, and you will not suffer another to occupy them. Make a Strike against a flying creature. If the attack deals damage, the target must attempt a Reflex save against your class DC.

Critical Success No effect.
Success The target falls 15 feet but doesn't take damage if it reaches the ground.
Failure The target falls 30 feet but doesn't take damage if it reaches the ground.
Critical Failure As failure, but the target takes falling damage.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Wing Shove [one-action] Feat 8

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Howl of the Wild pg. 83](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=226.md)
Archetype [Winged Warrior](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=227.md)
Prerequisites Winged Warrior Dedication
Requirements You’re flanked by at least two enemies

---

You spread your wings wide, pushing foes away from you. Attempt to [Shove](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2380.md) one of the flanking enemies, and then make a second Shove attempt against a different enemy that is flanking you. You don't need one hand free. The second Shove attempt has the same multiple attack penalty as the first and doesn't count toward your multiple attack penalty. On a success or critical success, you can't Stride after a target, but that target is also [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) until the beginning of their next turn. On a critical failure, you're off-guard until the beginning of your next turn instead of falling prone.
