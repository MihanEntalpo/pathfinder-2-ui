# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Messenger's RingItem 9+

[Invested](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=637.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 292](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage worn; Bulk &mdash;

---

This silver signet ring changes to match the insignia of a leader or organization you serve (or your own face, if you serve no one else). It grants you a +2 item bonus to [Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md) checks and lets you cast [message](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1598.md) as an arcane innate spell at will.

Activate—Dispatch Messenger 1 minute ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect The ring casts [animal messenger](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1441.md) to your specification. The animal is a magical creature that springs from the ring, and its appearance suits the iconography or heraldry of the leader or organization represented by the ring.
