Prerequisites [Drake Rider Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8102.md); expert in martial weapons; dragon companion with a support benefit that includes damage

Your dragon’s constant breath threatens your foes. While in this stance, your dragon companion has an aura in a 5-foot [emanation](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2387.md). Each creature that starts its turn in the aura takes the damage listed under the dragon’s support benefit with a [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Reflex save against your mount’s [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) DC.

## Traits

Archetype: This feat belongs to an archetype.

Aura: An aura is an emanation that continually ebbs out from you, affecting creatures within a certain radius. Aura can also refer to the magical signature of an item.

Stance: A stance is a general combat strategy that you enter by using an action with the stance trait, and you remain in for some time. A stance lasts until you get knocked out, until its requirements (if any) are violated, until the encounter ends, or until you use a stance action again, whichever comes first. After you take an action with the stance trait, you can’t take another one for 1 round. You can enter or be in a stance only in encounter mode. You can Dismiss a stance.
