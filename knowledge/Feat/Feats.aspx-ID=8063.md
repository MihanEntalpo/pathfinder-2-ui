# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Dragonet Immunities](./Feats.aspx-ID=8063.md) Feat 17

[Dragonet](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=893.md)
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 213](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

Prerequisites [Dragonet Resistances](./Feats.aspx-ID=8048.md)

---

You will not fall asleep unless you choose to allow it, nor will you sit still. Your resistances to sleep and paralysis increase, letting you completely ignore such effects, as befits a dragon. You gain [immunity](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2312.md) to [sleep](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=696.md) effects and effects that would make you [paralyzed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=85.md).

## Traits

Dragonet: Dragonets are miniature dragons, complete with wings, dagger-like teeth, and magical breath. They can be fiercely independent and proud, at times protective or avaricious, and they delight in the finer things in life.
