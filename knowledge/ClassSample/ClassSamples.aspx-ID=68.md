# [Animal Ranger](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=68.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 160](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Sharing a powerful bond, you and your beloved animal explore the wilds and fight in tandem.

## Ability Scores

Prioritize Dexterity, followed by Constitution and Wisdom. Take Strength if you want to deal more damage.

[Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md), [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md), [Medicine](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=42.md), [Nature](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=43.md), [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48.md), [Survival](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=49.md)
