# [Dancer](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=4.md)

Source [Core Rulebook pg. 102](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)
Through myriad styles of dance, you inspire your allies and channel otherworldly magic.

## Ability Scores

Make Charisma highest, followed by Dexterity. Add to Constitution for more health and to Intelligence for skills.

## Skills

[Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34&Redirected=1.md), [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md), Diplomacy, Medicine, Occultism, Performance

## Muse

[Maestro](../Muses/Muses.aspx-ID=2.md)

## Higher-Level Feats

Inspire Competence (2nd), Triple Time (4th), Allegro (14th)

## Spell Repertoire

1st color spray, illusory disguise, soothe; Cantrips detect magic, ghost sound, light, mage hand, read aura
