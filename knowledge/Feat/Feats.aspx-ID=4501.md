# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Leshy Glide](./Feats.aspx-ID=4501.md) [one-action] Feat 5

Leshy
Source [Player Core pg. 68](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites [Leaf Leshy](../Heritage/Heritages.aspx-ID=267.md), or [Cat Fall](./Feats.aspx-ID=5131.md)

---

Using your own leaves, you can control your descent. You glide slowly toward the ground, 5 feet down and up to 25 feet forward through the air. As long as you spend at least 1 action gliding each round and have not yet reached the ground, you remain in the air at the end of your turn.

## Traits

Leshy: A creature with this trait is a member of the leshy ancestry. These small plant or fungus creatures are humanoid in form. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by leshies. A weapon with this trait is created and used by leshies.
