# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Execute [two-actions] Spell 7

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Death [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) [Void](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=510.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 329](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Bloodlines undead, aesir
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature
Defense basic Fortitude

---

You point at a creature and invoke the demise of all things. The target takes 70 void damage with a basic Fortitude save. If the target is undead or otherwise has void healing, the spell loses the death and [void](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=510.md) traits and gains the [vitality](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=509.md) trait, and the target takes 70 vitality damage with a basic Fortitude save.

---

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 10.
