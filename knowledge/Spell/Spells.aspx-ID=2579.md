# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Sea of Thought [two-actions] Spell 3

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 106](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Deity Thoth
Range 60 feet; Area 15-foot burst
Defense Fortitude; Duration 1 minute

---

You cover the area in a sloshing torrent of semi-solidified thought, roughly ankle high. The area becomes [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366.md), similarly to a shallow bog. Each round that a creature starts its turn in the area, it must attempt a Fortitude save against the shifting waves of thought. On a failure, it takes a –10-foot circumstance penalty to its Speeds until it leaves the area, and on a critical failure, it is also knocked [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88.md).
