# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Aether MarblesItem 4+

## This Item may contain spoilers from the Gatewalkers Adventure Path

Uncommon Bomb Consumable Force Splash
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 235](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Activate [one-action] Strike

---

Aether marbles create violent, lingering eddies of force that extend partially into unseen planes. When the bomb explodes, it deals the listed [force](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=610.md) damage and force splash damage. Many aether marbles grant an item bonus to attack rolls.

Surfaces in the splash area are filled with twisting eddies of force, which are [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366.md) for [incorporeal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=632.md) creatures but not for corporeal ones. The force eddies in a square disperse after an incorporeal creature enters the square.
