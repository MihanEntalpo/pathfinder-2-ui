# [Pharasmin](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=61.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 120](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
A follower of the Lady of Graves, you respect the sanctity of both life and death.

## Ability Scores

Prioritize Wisdom. Charisma helps you spread the word of your faith.

## Skills

[Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md), [Medicine](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=42.md), [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md), [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md)

## Deity

[cloistered cleric](../Doctrines/Doctrines.aspx-ID=4.md) ([death](../Domain/Domains.aspx-ID=70.md) domain)

## Doctrine

[Pharasma](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=290.md) (divine font: [heal](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1554.md))

## Higher-Level Feats

[Panic the Dead](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=4650.md) (2nd), [Selective Energy](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=4665.md) (6th)

## Prepared Spells

1st [fear](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1524.md), [heal spells from divine font](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1554.md), [mindlink](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1603.md); Cantrips [daze](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1482.md), [detect magic](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1485.md), [light](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1585.md), [stabilize](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1689.md), [vitality lash](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1744.md)
