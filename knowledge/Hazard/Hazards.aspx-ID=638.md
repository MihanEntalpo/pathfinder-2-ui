# [Clone Mirrors](./Hazards.aspx-ID=638.md)Hazard 6

[Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Trap](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=715.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 124](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth DC 21
Description Two opposing mirrors spawn illusory duplicates of creatures in the hall in an unending tide.

---

Disable DC 28 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) (expert) to reposition each mirror, or [dispel magic](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1493.md) (3rd level; counteract DC 25) to counteract each mirror
AC 21, Fort +17, Ref +11
Hardness 13 per mirror, HP 54 (BT 27); Immunities object immunities
Spawn Reflection [reaction] ([illusion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=629.md)) Trigger A creature is reflected in the mirror; Effect The mirror creates a reflection of the triggering creature, which Steps out of the mirror and into the hall. The trap then rolls initiative.

---

Routine (2 actions) This trap loses 1 action for each mirror disabled. Each mirror uses 1 action to Spawn a Reflection of a creature reflected in the mirror. The hazard can have a maximum of four reflections spawned at once.

---

Reflection AC 24; Fort +11, Ref +17
Reflection HP 30; Immunities object immunities
Reflection Routine (2 actions) Each reflection has 2 actions which it uses to Step, Stride, or Strike (in any combination). Reflections can act on the same turn they're created.
Reflection Speed 50 feet
Melee [one-action] reflected weapon +17 (versatile P, versatile S), Damage 2d6+8 bludgeoning

---

Reset 1 day
