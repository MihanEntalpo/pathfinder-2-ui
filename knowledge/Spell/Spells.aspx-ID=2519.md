# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Funeral Flames [two-actions] Spell 1

## This Spell may contain spoilers from the Myth-Speakers Adventure Path

[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) [Fire](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=604.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)
Source [Pathfinder #216: The Acropolis Pyre pg. 82](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=271.md)
Traditions [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Range touch; Targets one [club](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=357.md) or [staff](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=367.md) you're wielding
Duration 1 minute

---

You transform a bludgeon into a fearsome torch suited to casting [undead](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=722.md) into the afterlife. One end of the weapon ignites, becoming wreathed in blue flame. While you wield the target weapon, it becomes a +1 weapon, gains the [versatile fire](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=724.md) trait, and sheds light as brightly as a [torch](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2760.md). Whenever you successfully Strike a creature with the weapon, you can Dismiss the spell as a free action to deal 1d6 [persistent fire damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86&Redirected=1.md) to the target; if the target is undead, increase the persistent damage dice to d8s.

---

Heightened (+2) The persistent fire damage increases by 1d6.
