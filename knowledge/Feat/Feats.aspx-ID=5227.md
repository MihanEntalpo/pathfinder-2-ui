# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Toughness](./Feats.aspx-ID=5227.md) Feat 1

General
Source [Player Core pg. 263](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

Your body can withstand more punishment than most before succumbing. Increase your maximum Hit Points by your level. You reduce the DC of [recovery checks](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2326.md) by 1.

## Traits

General: A type of feat that any character can select, regardless of ancestry and class, as long as they meet the prerequisites. You can select a feat with this trait when your class grants a general feat.
