# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Forbidding Ward [two-actions] Cantrip 1

Cantrip [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 332](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 ally and 1 enemy
Duration sustained up to 1 minute

---

You ward an ally against the attacks and hostile spells from the target enemy. The target ally gains a +1 status bonus to Armor Class and saving throws against the target enemy's attacks, spells, and other effects.

---

Heightened (6th) The status bonus increases to +2.
