# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Ammunition Thaumaturgy](./Feats.aspx-ID=8353.md) Feat 1

[Thaumaturge](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=895.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 42](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

---

You're so used to handling your [implement](../Implements/Implements.aspx.md), weapon, and esoterica in the heat of combat that adding a few bullets or arrows to the mix is no extra burden. You can Interact to reload a weapon using the hand holding your implement.

## Traits

Thaumaturge: This indicates abilities from the [thaumaturge](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=69.md) class.
