### Ciliospg. 77

Variant male mummy guardian
Blinding Rot (curse, disease, divine, [necromancy](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=117.md), negative) Cilios's fist Strikes expose those they damage to blinding rot. This disease and any damage from it can't be healed until this curse is removed. A creature killed by blinding rot turns to dust and can't be resurrected except by a 7th-level resurrect ritual or similar magic. Saving Throw DC 22 Fortitude; Stage 1 carrier with no ill effect (1 minute); Stage 2 4d6 negative damage and dazzled (1 day); Stage 3 4d6 negative damage and blinded (1 day)
