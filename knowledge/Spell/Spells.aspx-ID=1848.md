# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Tidal Surge [one-action] Focus 1

Uncommon [Cleric](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=554.md) [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Water
Source [Player Core pg. 381](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Domain [water](../Domain/Domains.aspx-ID=98.md)
Range 60 feet; Targets 1 creature

---

You call forth a tremendous wave to move the target either in a body of water or on the ground. You move the target up to 10 feet in any direction along the ground or 20 feet through a body of water. An unwilling target can attempt a Fortitude save, and it avoids being moved if it succeeds.
