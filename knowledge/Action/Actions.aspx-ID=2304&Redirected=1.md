# [Step](./Actions.aspx-ID=2304.md) [one-action]

[Move](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=658&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 418](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Requirements Your Speed is at least 10 feet.

---

You carefully move 5 feet. Unlike most types of movement, Stepping doesn't trigger reactions, such as [Reactive Strike](./Actions.aspx-ID=2256.md), that can be triggered by move actions or upon leaving or entering a square.

You can't Step into [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366&Redirected=1.md), and you can't Step using a Speed other than your land Speed.
