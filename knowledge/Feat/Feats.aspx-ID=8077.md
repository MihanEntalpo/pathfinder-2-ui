# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Summiting Dragonblood](./Feats.aspx-ID=8077.md) Feat 1

[Dragonblood](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=794.md) Lineage
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 216](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

---

Thanks to your draconic blood, there are no mountains or hills you can’t surmount. You gain a climb Speed of 20 feet; if you already have a base climb Speed, it increases by 5 feet. You also gain the [Combat Climber](./Feats.aspx-ID=5134.md) skill feat, even if you aren’t trained in [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md). If you choose a draconic exemplar, you must choose a dragon with a climb Speed.

## Traits

Dragonblood: A creature with this trait has the dragonblood versatile heritage. These creatures are born of draconic lineage and often have draconic appearances.

Lineage: A feat with this trait indicates a character’s descendance from a particular type of creature. You can have only one lineage feat. You can select a lineage feat only at 1st level, and you can’t retrain into or out of these feats.
