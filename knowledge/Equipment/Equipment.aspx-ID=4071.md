## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Uncommon Consumable Magical Snare Trap
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 298](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 5 gp
Bulk L

---

A spirit trap consists of a net dipped in water that has been steeped with sacred herbs to better combat phantoms. This snare’s components function as a net when not set up. You set this snare up in a 10-foot-by-10-foot area. The first creature with the [spirit](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=737.md) trait that steps into the area must attempt a DC 16 Fortitude save. Additional rules for snares appear here.

Critical Success The creature is unaffected.
Success The creature is [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) for 1 round.
Failure The creature is [immobilized](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=81.md) for 1 round.
Critical Failure The creature is immobilized until it [Escapes](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2296.md) (DC 16).
