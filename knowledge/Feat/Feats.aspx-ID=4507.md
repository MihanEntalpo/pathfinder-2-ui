# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Thorned Seedpod](./Feats.aspx-ID=4507.md) Feat 9

Leshy
Source [Player Core pg. 69](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites [Seedpod](./Feats.aspx-ID=4497.md)

---

When your seedpods [strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) a felling blow, they burst into viciously thorny vines. When you critically succeed at a seedpod Strike, vines twist and stab your foe, dealing 1d4 [persistent](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86&Redirected=1.md) piercing damage.

## Traits

Leshy: A creature with this trait is a member of the leshy ancestry. These small plant or fungus creatures are humanoid in form. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by leshies. A weapon with this trait is created and used by leshies.
