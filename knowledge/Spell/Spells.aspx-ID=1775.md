# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Song of Strength [one-action] Cantrip 1

Uncommon [Bard](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=546.md) Cantrip Composition [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Emotion Mental
Source [Player Core pg. 371](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Area 60-foot emanation
Duration 1 round

---

You bolster your allies' physical strength with a hearty exhortation. You and your allies gain a +1 status bonus to [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md) checks and to their DCs against Athletics skill actions such as Disarm, Reposition, Shove, and Trip.
