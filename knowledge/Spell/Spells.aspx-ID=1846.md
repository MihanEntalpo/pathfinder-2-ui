# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Touch of Undeath [one-action] Focus 1

Uncommon [Cleric](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=554.md) [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) [Void](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=510.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 380](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Domain [undeath](../Domain/Domains.aspx-ID=97.md)
Range touch; Targets 1 living creature
Defense Fortitude; Duration varies

---

You attack the target's life force with undeath, dealing 1d6 void damage. The target must attempt a Fortitude save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.
Success The target takes half damage.
Failure The target takes full damage, and [vitality](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=509.md) effects heal it only half as much as normal for 1 round.
Critical Failure The target takes double damage, and vitality effects heal it only half as much as normal for 1 minute.

---

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 1d6.
