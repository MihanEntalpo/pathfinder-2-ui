# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Dragon Shape](./Feats.aspx-ID=4749.md) Feat 12

[Druid](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=583.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 133](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites [Soaring Shape](./Feats.aspx-ID=4739.md)

---

You can take on the shape of some of the world's most fearsome creatures. Add the shapes listed in [dragon form](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1502.md) to your untamed form list. Whenever you're polymorphed into another form using [untamed form](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1861.md), you gain resistance 5 to your choice of bludgeoning or poison.
