# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Potion of UndetectabilityItem 18

[Consumable](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=564.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Potion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=672.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 260](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Price 4,400 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Activate [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md))

---

Drinking this dull-black liquid makes you undetectable to detection effects. This grants the same effects as hidden mind but without the bonus against mental effects. You also gain the effects of a 4th-rank [invisibility](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1577&Redirected=1.md) spell, which protects against see the unseen spells of 8th rank and lower and has a DC of 36 against [truesight](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1727.md). The potion's effects last for 10 minutes.
