# Ability Scores

Source [Secrets of Magic pg. 58](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=96.md)

An eidolon's ability scores depend on which array you choose, such as marauding dragon or cunning dragon for a dragon eidolon. As noted in the [summoner's ability boosts class feature](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=18.md), your eidolon gets boosts to its ability scores at the same time you do. It also increases one score by 2 when it gains its transcendence ability.
