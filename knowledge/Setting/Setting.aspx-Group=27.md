### Giant Leechpg. 17

Parasitizing While burrowed into Lady Shimmersnip, a giant leech has cover, can't make attacks, and can Squirm. If a giant leech is [grappled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=77&Redirected=1.md), [restrained](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=90&Redirected=1.md), or stunned, a creature can attempt to remove the leech by spending a single action, which has the [attack](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=540&Redirected=1.md) and manipulate traits, attempting an [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md) or Medicine check against the giant leech's Fortitude DC. On a success, the creature extracts the leech, reduces Lady Shimmersnip's clumsy condition by 1, and places the leech in a space adjacent to the creature and Lady Shimmersnip.
Gnash [one-action] Frequency once per round; Requirements The giant leech is parasitizing Lady Shimmersnip; Effect Lady Shimmersnip takes 1 slashing damage that bypasses her resistances.
