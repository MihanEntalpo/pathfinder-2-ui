# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Bloodseeker BeakItem 4+

[Consumable](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=564.md) [Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Talisman](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=709.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 263](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage affixed to a weapon; Bulk &mdash;
Activate [free-action] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md)); Trigger You hit a [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) creature with the affixed weapon

---

This long, hollow proboscis is harvested from the notorious bloodseeker beast and drips a trickle of blood. When you activate the beak, you deal an extra 1d4 precision damage on your damage roll. If you deal sneak attack damage to the creature, you also deal 1d4 [persistent bleed damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86&Redirected=1.md).
