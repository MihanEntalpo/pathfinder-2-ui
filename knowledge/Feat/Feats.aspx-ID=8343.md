Your power has unbound you from the earth below. You're affected by a constant [fly](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1534.md) spell, and when your Psyche is Unleashed, you gain a +10-foot status bonus to your fly Speed.

Psychic: This indicates abilities from the [psychic](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=68.md) class.
