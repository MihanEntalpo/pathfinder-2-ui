# [Electric Latch Rune](./Hazards.aspx-ID=468.md)Hazard 3

Electricity Magical Trap
Source [GM Core pg. 101](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 20 (trained)
Description An invisible rune imprinted on a door latch releases a powerful electric discharge.

---

Disable DC 20 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) (expert) to scratch out the rune without allowing electricity to flow, or [dispel magic](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1493.md) (2nd rank; counteract DC 18) to counteract the rune
Electrocution [reaction] ([arcane](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=534.md), [electricity](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=586.md)) Trigger A creature grasps the door latch directly or with a tool; Effect The trap deals 3d12 electricity damage to the triggering creature (DC 22 [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Reflex save).
