# [Feint](./Actions.aspx-ID=2390.md) [one-action]

Mental
Source [Player Core pg. 239](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Skill [Deception (Trained)](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38.md)
Requirements You are within melee reach of the target you attempt to Feint.

---

With a misleading flourish, you leave an opponent unprepared for your real attack. Attempt a [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38&Redirected=1.md) check against your target's Perception DC.

Critical Success You throw your enemy's defenses against you entirely off. The target is [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) against melee attacks that you attempt against it until the end of your next turn.
Success Your foe is fooled, but only momentarily. The target is off-guard against the next melee attack that you attempt against it before the end of your current turn.
Critical Failure Your feint backfires. You are off-guard against melee attacks the target attempts against you until the end of your next turn.
