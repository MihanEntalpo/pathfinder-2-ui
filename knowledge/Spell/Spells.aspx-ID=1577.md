# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Invisibility [two-actions] Spell 2

Illusion [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) [Subtle](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=522.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 339](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Deities [Achaekek](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=325.md), Erecura, Fortune's Fate, Ketephys, Nalinivati, Ng, Nivi Rhombodazzle, [Norgorber](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=289.md), Raumya, Shax, The Tides of Chaos, Yaezhing, Zugero
Range touch; Targets 1 creature
Duration 10 minutes

---

Illusions bend light around the target, rendering it invisible. This makes it [undetected](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=96&Redirected=1.md) to all creatures, though the creatures can attempt to find the target, making it [hidden](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=79&Redirected=1.md) to them instead. If the target uses a hostile action, the spell ends after that hostile action is completed.

---

Heightened (4th) The spell lasts 1 minute, but it doesn't end if the target uses a hostile action.
