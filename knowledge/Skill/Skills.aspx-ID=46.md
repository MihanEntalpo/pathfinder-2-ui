# [Religion (Wis)](./Skills.aspx-ID=46.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 244](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
The secrets of deities, dogma, faith, and the realms of divine creatures both sublime and sinister are open to you. You also understand how magic works, though your training imparts a religious slant to that knowledge.

- [Recall Knowledge](./Skills.aspx-ID=24&General=true.md) about divine agents, the finer points of theology, obscure myths regarding a faith, and creatures of religious significance (like [celestials](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=551.md), [fiends](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=600.md), and undead), [Creation's Forge](../Plane/Planes.aspx-ID=27.md), [the Void](../Plane/Planes.aspx-ID=28.md), and the Outer Sphere.

## Religion Trained Actions

- [Decipher Writing](./Skills.aspx-ID=20&General=true.md) of a religious nature, including allegories, homilies, and proverbs.
- [Identify Magic](./Skills.aspx-ID=22&General=true.md), particularly magic of the [divine](../SpellLists/SpellLists.aspx-Tradition=2.md) tradition.
- [Learn a Spell](./Skills.aspx-ID=23&General=true.md) from the [divine](../SpellLists/SpellLists.aspx-Tradition=2.md) tradition.
