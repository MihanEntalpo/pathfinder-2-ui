# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Psychic Rapport](./Feats.aspx-ID=8313.md) Feat 1

[Psychic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=894.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 24](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

---

Your telepathic sensitivity intensifies when you're affected by mental magic, making it easier for you to link to other creatures. After you Cast a Spell with the [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) trait, until the start of your next turn, you gain a +1 circumstance bonus to Deception, Diplomacy, and Intimidation checks, and to Perception checks to [Sense Motive](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2302.md).

## Traits

Psychic: This indicates abilities from the [psychic](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=68.md) class.
