# [Spear Launcher](./Hazards.aspx-ID=483.md)Hazard 2

Mechanical Trap
Source [GM Core pg. 104](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 20 (trained)
Description A wall socket loaded with a spear connects to a floor tile in one 5-foot square.

---

AC 18, Fort +11, Ref +3
Hardness 8, HP 32 (BT 16); Immunities critical hits, object immunities, precision damage
Spear [reaction] Trigger Pressure is applied to the floor tile; Effect The trap shoots a spear, making a [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) against the creature or object on the floor tile.

Ranged spear +14, Damage 2d6+6 piercing
