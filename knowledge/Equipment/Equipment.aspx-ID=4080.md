## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Uncommon Consumable Magical Potion [Tea](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=738.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 300](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 250 gp
Usage held in 1 hand; Bulk L
Activate [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md))

---

Resurrection tea was named for its repugnant taste, said to be “strong enough to kill the living,” and its incredible medicinal properties “powerful enough to raise the dead.” The primary ingredients in this medicinal tea are ginseng and jujubes, enhanced with additional herbs, shaved deer antlers, sugi tree bark, and a variety of mushrooms stewed for several hours in a large pot to form a brown, pulpy, and viscous liquid. The bitterness and earthy taste make the tea rather unappetizing. The tea restores 2d8+5 Hit Points, and you reduce the DC of [recovery checks](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2326.md) by 1 for 8 hours.

---

Activate—Tea Ceremony 10 minutes ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)) The tea reduces the DC of recovery checks by 3.
