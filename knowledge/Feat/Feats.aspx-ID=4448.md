# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Loud Singer](./Feats.aspx-ID=4448.md) Feat 5

Goblin
Source [Player Core pg. 57](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites [Goblin Song](./Feats.aspx-ID=4442.md)

---

Staying on pitch, proper breath control, and remembering the words are all less important than the real measure of a good singer: volume! The range of your [Goblin Song](./Feats.aspx-ID=4442.md) is increased to 60 feet, and you can target one additional enemy when you use it.

## Traits

Goblin: A creature with this trait is a small, frantic goblinoid. Goblins tend to have darkvision. An ability with this trait can be used or chosen only by goblins. A weapon with this trait is created and used by goblins.
