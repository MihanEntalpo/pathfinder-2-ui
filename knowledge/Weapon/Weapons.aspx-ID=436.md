# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Longbow](./Weapons.aspx-ID=436.md)

Deadly d10 Volley 30 ft.
Source [Player Core pg. 280](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Favored Weapon Breath of the Endless Sky, Cernunnos, Eiseth, Elion, [Erastil](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=282.md), General Susumu, Hshurha, Isph-Aun-Vuln, Jukha, Ketephys, Phlegyas, Skode, Sky Keepers, Sovyrian Conclave, Ylimancha
Price 6 gp; Damage 1d8 P; Bulk 2
Hands 1+; Range 100 ft.; Reload 0
Type Ranged; Category Martial; Group [Bow](../WeaponGroups/WeaponGroups.aspx-ID=3.md)

---

This 5-foot-tall bow, usually made of a single piece of elm, hickory, or yew, has a powerful draw and is excellent at propelling arrows with great force and at an extreme distance. You must use two hands to fire a longbow, and it can't be used while mounted.

## Traits

Deadly d10: On a critical hit, the weapon adds a weapon damage die of the listed size. Roll this after doubling the weapon’s damage. This increases to two dice if the weapon has a greater striking rune and three dice if the weapon has a major striking rune. For instance, a rapier with a greater striking rune deals 2d8 extra piercing damage on a critical hit. An ability that changes the size of the weapon’s normal damage dice doesn’t change the size of its deadly die.

Volley 30 ft.: This ranged weapon is less effective at close distances. Your attacks against targets that are at a distance within the range listed take a –2 penalty.

## Critical Specialization Effects

Source [Core Rulebook pg. 283](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)
Certain feats, class features, weapon runes, and other effects can grant you additional benefits when you make a Strike with certain weapons and get a critical success. This is called a critical specialization effect. The exact effect depends on which weapon group your weapon belongs to, as listed below. You can always decide not to add the critical specialization effect of your weapon.

Bow: If the target of the critical hit is adjacent to a surface, it gets stuck to that surface by the missile. The target is [immobilized](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=81&Redirected=1.md) and must spend an Interact action to attempt a DC 10 [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md) check to pull the missile free; it can't move from its space until it succeeds. The creature doesn't become stuck if it is incorporeal, is liquid (like a water elemental or some oozes), or could otherwise escape without effort.

## Specific Magic Weapons

Horselord's Longbow, Hunter's Anthem, Splithead Bow
