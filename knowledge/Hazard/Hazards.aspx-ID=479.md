# [Polymorph Trap](./Hazards.aspx-ID=479.md)Hazard 12

Magical Trap
Source [GM Core pg. 103](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 34 (trained)
Description A Druidic glyph attempts to transform a trespasser into an animal.

---

Disable DC 32 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) (master) to drain the glyph’s power harmlessly, or [dispel magic](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1493.md) (6th rank; counteract DC 30) to counteract the glyph
Cursed Metamorphosis [reaction] ([primal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=676.md)) Trigger A creature moves within 30 feet of the glyph without speaking the passphrase in Druidic; Effect The creature is targeted by [cursed metamorphosis](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1479.md) (DC 32 Will save).
