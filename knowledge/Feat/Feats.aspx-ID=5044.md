# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Magic Sense](./Feats.aspx-ID=5044.md) Feat 12

Arcane Detection [Magus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=389.md) [Oracle](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=817.md) [Sorcerer](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=824.md) [Wizard](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=735.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 204](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md), [Player Core 2 pg. 142](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Archetype [Lich](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=179.md)*
* This archetype offers Magic Sense at a different level than displayed here.

---

You have a literal sixth sense for ambient magic in your vicinity. You can sense the presence of magic auras as though you were always using a 1st-rank detect magic spell. This detects magic in your field of vision only. When you Seek, you gain the benefits of a 3rd-rank detect magic spell on things you see (in addition to the normal benefits of Seeking). You can turn this sense off and on with a free action at the start or the end of your turn.

## Archetype Use

This feat can be used for one or more archetypes in addition to the listed classes. When selected this way, the feat does not have its class traits.

## Traits

Arcane: This magic comes from the arcane tradition, which is built on logic and rationality. Anything with this trait is magical.

A creature with this trait is primarily constituted of or has a strong connection to arcane magic.

Detection: Effects with this trait attempt to determine the presence or location of a person, object, or aura.
