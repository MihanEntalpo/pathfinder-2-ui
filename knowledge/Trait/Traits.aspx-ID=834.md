# [Calling](./Traits.aspx-ID=834.md)

Source [War of Immortals pg. 69](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=232.md)
All mythic characters gain a Calling when they first gain mythic power, usually at either 1st or 2nd level. Callings define the purpose of a character’s mythic power and grant a unique way to regain Mythic Points.
