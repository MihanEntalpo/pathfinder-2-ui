# [Mutagen](./Traits.aspx-ID=808.md)

Source [Player Core 2 pg. 289](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md)
Mutagens are a special type of [elixir](./Traits.aspx-ID=589.md) that temporarily transmogrify the subject's body and mind. Typically, only [alchemists](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=56.md) have the expertise to craft mutagens.

A mutagen always conveys one or more beneficial effects (listed in the Benefit entry) and one or more detrimental effects (shown in the Drawback entry). Mutagens are [polymorph](./Traits.aspx-ID=670.md) effects, and a subsequent polymorph effect attempts to counteract an existing effect; the counteract check for a mutagen uses the item's level and a modifier equal to that level's DC – 10.
