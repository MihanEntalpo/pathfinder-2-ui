# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Spiral of Horrors [two-actions] Focus 4

Uncommon Aura [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Emotion Fear [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Mental [Wizard](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=735.md)

Area 30-foot emanation
Duration sustained up to 1 minute

Shades and spirits howl and whirl around you in a display that strikes fear into the hearts of all who witness it. Enemies in the area are frightened 1 and can't reduce their frightened value below 1 for the spell's duration.
