# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Devoted VestmentsItem 11

[Divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md) [Focused](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=609.md) [Invested](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=637.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 288](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Price 1,250 gp
Usage worn garment; Bulk L

---

These vestments are made of panels showing various scenes from the legends of a particular deity. It serves as a religious symbol of that deity, and you gain a +2 item bonus to Religion checks. When you cast harm or heal, healing granted to followers of that deity is increased by the rank of the spell.

Activate—Domain Devotion [free-action] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect Gain 1 Focus Point, which you can spend only to cast a cleric domain spell for a domain belonging to the deity the vestments are dedicated to. If you don't spend this point by the end of this turn, it is lost.

Craft Requirements You are a cleric who worships the deity tied to the vestments.
