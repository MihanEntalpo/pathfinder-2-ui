# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Benefactor's Strike](./Feats.aspx-ID=8069.md) Feat 5

Magical
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 215](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

Prerequisites dragonscaled kobold heritage

---

Your [benefactor’s](../DraconicBenefactors/DraconicBenefactors.aspx.md) blood flows through your veins, and you channel their power into your body. When you critically hit with an unarmed Strike, the target takes an additional 1 [persistent damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86.md) of the same type as the damage dealt by the breath of your draconic benefactor. Some examples are shown on the Draconic Benefactors table [here.](../DraconicBenefactors/DraconicBenefactors.aspx.md)

## Traits

Magical: Something with the magical trait is imbued with magical energies not tied to a specific tradition of magic. Some items or effects are closely tied to a particular tradition of magic. In these cases, the item has the arcane, divine, occult, or primal trait instead of the magical trait. Any of these traits indicate that the item is magical.
