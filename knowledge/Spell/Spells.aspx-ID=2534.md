# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Advanced Scurvy [two-actions] Spell 2

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Disease [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 233](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Traditions [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Range touch; Targets 1 creature
Defense Fortitude

---

Your touch afflicts the target with an advanced form of scurvy, a mariner’s disease stemming from improper nutrition. The symptoms of scurvy include fatigue, joint pain, loose teeth, and internal hemorrhaging.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.
Success The target is [enfeebled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=71.md) 1 for 1 minute.
Failure The target is afflicted with advanced scurvy at stage 1.
Critical Failure The target is afflicted with advanced scurvy at stage 2.

Advanced Scurvy ([disease](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=578.md)) Level 4; for 1 day after eating fresh fruit, a creature gains a +2 circumstance bonus to their next saving throw against this affliction; Stage 1 enfeebled 1 and the damage dealt by [persistent bleed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86.md) effects is increased by 1 (1 day); Stage 2 enfeebled 1, [fatigued](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=73.md), and the damage dealt by persistent bleed effects is increased by 1d4 (2 days); Stage 3 enfeebled 2, fatigued, and the damage dealt by persistent bleed effects is increased by 1d6 (4 days)
