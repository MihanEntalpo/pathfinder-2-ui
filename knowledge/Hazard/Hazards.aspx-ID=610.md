# [Dream-Poisoned Doors](./Hazards.aspx-ID=610.md)Hazard 12

[Magical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=644.md) [Trap](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=715.md)
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 186](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 35 (master)
Description Eerie shapes manifest within the doorway as it’s opened, conjuring a group of four animate dreams into being.

---

Disable DC 30 [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md) (expert) to concentrate on the eldritch energies and diffuse them through meditation, DC 35 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) (master) to scratch warding runes along the door’s hinges and block dreams from manifesting, or [dispel magic](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1493.md) (6th rank; counteract DC 30) to counteract the trap
Manifest Dream Guardians [reaction] ([occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md)) Trigger The door is opened; Effect A group of four animate dreams manifest in the area just beyond the door. These four animate dreams each appear as vaguely whale‑shaped but human-sized masses of tendrils. They swim through the air and attack the intruders at once, fighting to the death and pursuing any who flee the temple.
