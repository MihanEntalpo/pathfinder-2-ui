# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Etheric Shards [two-actions] Spell 5

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) [Force](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=610.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 105](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Range 60 feet; Area 20-foot burst
Defense [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Reflex; Duration 1 minute

---

You reach out and sow glittering splinters of force throughout the area, otherworldly caltrops of will and ectoplasm. The area is [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366.md), even to flying or incorporeal creatures. Any creature that moves through the area takes 1d4 piercing damage per square traversed, with a basic Reflex save made at the end of each move action they take through the area.

---

Heightened (+2) The spell’s area increases by 5 feet, and the piercing damage increases by 1.
