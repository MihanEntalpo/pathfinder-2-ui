# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Cooperative Nature](./Feats.aspx-ID=4475.md) Feat 1

Human
Source [Player Core pg. 64](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

The short human life span lends perspective and has taught you from a young age to set aside differences and work with others to achieve greatness. You gain a +4 circumstance bonus on checks to Aid.

## Cooperative Nature Leads To...

Cooperative Soul

## Traits

Human: A creature with this trait is a member of the human ancestry. Humans are a diverse array of people known for their adaptability. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by humans.
