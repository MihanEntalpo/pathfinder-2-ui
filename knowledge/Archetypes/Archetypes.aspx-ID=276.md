Fan dancers display exceptional elegance and control as they drift across the stage, specializing in spinning movements that are, at times, like a trickling brook and, at others, a swift and powerful river. While some fan dancers use their skills to gain the advantage in combat, others adeptly manipulate their fans to distract their enemies and create openings for their allies.

Some fan dancer abilities require you to be wielding one or more fans, which can be a [fighting fan](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=284.md) or a normal fan used as an improvised weapon.

Characters with Tian Xia origin have access to the fan dancer archetype.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Fan Dancer Dedication Feat 2

[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Dedication](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=572.md)
Source [Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 98](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=230.md)
Archetype [Fan Dancer](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=276.md)
Prerequisites trained in [Performance](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=45.md)
Access Tian Xia origin

---

You sweep across the battlefield, manifesting both the gentle spring breeze and the crisp autumn gusts. You become an expert in [Performance](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=45.md). At 7th level you become a master in Performance, and at 15th level, you become legendary in Performance. Whenever you [Feint](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2390.md) while holding a fan, you can give your Feint the [air](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=527.md) trait. If you do, you can Stride 10 feet before or after as part of the same action.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Petal Step Feat 4

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 98](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=230.md)
Archetype [Fan Dancer](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=276.md)

You have a mastery over your own movement and can distribute your weight to dance lightly over the ground like petals drifting on the water. You gain a +1 circumstance bonus to your [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48.md) checks to [Sneak](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2405.md) and aren’t detectable by tremorsense.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Solo Dancer Feat 4

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Skill](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=695.md)

You often dance alone with a grace exceeding that of most other performers, carrying yourself with a poise and confidence that draws the attention of those around you. You can always roll [Performance](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=45.md) for initiative, and during the first round of combat, creatures that act after you are [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) to you.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Twirl Through Feat 4

You sweep across the battlefield in a fluttering of movement honed from years of coordinating perfectly spaced movements alongside fellow dancers. When you attempt to [Tumble Through](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2370.md) an enemy’s space, you can use [Performance](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=45.md) instead of [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md).

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Fluttering Misdirection Feat 6

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Skill](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=695.md)
Source [Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 98](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=230.md)
Archetype [Fan Dancer](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=276.md)
Prerequisites Fan Dancer Dedication

---

You’ve trained in drawing the eyes of your audience to specific aspects of your performance through the careful manipulation of your fans and body. While wielding a fan, you can snap open, flutter, or otherwise manipulate the fan to briefly distract observers around you, giving you and adjacent allies a constant +1 circumstance bonus to [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48.md) checks to secretly [Conceal an Object](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2403.md) and to [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) checks to [Steal](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2410.md) or [Palm an Object](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2409.md).

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Sweeping Fan Block [reaction] Feat 6

[Air](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=527.md) [Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 98](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=230.md)
Archetype [Fan Dancer](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=276.md)
Prerequisites Fan Dancer Dedication
Trigger You're targeted by a ranged attack that uses ammunition.
Requirements You're wielding two fans, one in each hand.

---

You leap up on one leg, snapping your fans open alongside your head before sweeping them across your body. When you’re the target of an attack using ammunition (such as [arrows](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=443.md), [bolts](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=441.md), [sling bullets](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=442.md), and other objects of similar size) while wielding two fans, whirl your fans to disrupt the incoming attack with gusts of air, gaining a +2 circumstance bonus to AC against the triggering attack. If the triggering attack misses, you can redirect the ammunition into a nearby pocket or container where it can be retrieved and reused.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Pushing Wind Feat 8

[Air](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=527.md) [Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Aura](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=542.md)
Source [Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 98](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=230.md)
Archetype [Fan Dancer](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=276.md)
Prerequisites Fan Dancer Dedication
Requirements You’re wielding a fan.

---

As you spin and glide your fans alongside your allies, you kick up a mild wind that gently carries you all forward. So long as you’re holding a fan, you and allies who start their turn in a 30- foot aura [emanating](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2387.md) around you gain a +5-foot circumstance bonus to land Speed for 1 round; you also gain this bonus to your fly Speed if you possess one, but it can’t grant you the ability to fly if you wouldn’t otherwise be able to.

Additionally, the air impedes the movements of your foes. While holding a fan, the area in a 10-foot aura emanating around you is [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366.md) for all enemies.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Twirling Strike [two-actions] Feat 8

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 98](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=230.md)
Archetype [Fan Dancer](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=276.md)
Prerequisites Fan Dancer Dedication; Twirl Through
Requirements You’re wielding a fan.

---

Your fans, one raised up alongside your head and the other alongside your hip, become a blur as you twirl across the battlefield. Attempt to [Tumble Through](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2370.md) an enemy’s space using [Performance](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=45.md); on a success, you can make a melee [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) against the enemy with a fan you’re wielding at any point during the movement. On a critical success, the enemy is [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) against this attack.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Close Formation Feat 10

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Dizzying Spin Dance [three-actions] Feat 10

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 99](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=230.md)
Archetype [Fan Dancer](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=276.md)
Prerequisites Fan Dancer Dedication; Twirling Strike
Requirements You’re wielding a fan.

---

You’re practiced in using your fans to spin with dizzying speed. [Tumble Through](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2370.md) an opponent’s space, and then [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) that opponent. If both the Tumble Through and Strike are successful, the opponent is [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) to the next attack made against it before the start of your next turn, and you can attempt to Tumble Through the space of a different opponent and Strike again with the same effect. If this second Tumble Through and Strike are successful, you can repeat these actions a third time against a third opponent.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Sweeping Fan Redirection Feat 10

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 99](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=230.md)
Archetype [Fan Dancer](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=276.md)
Prerequisites Fan Dancer Dedication; Sweeping Fan Block
Requirements You're wielding two fans, one in each hand.

---

You can redirect ammunition back at the creature that fired it with sweeping gusts of wind. When Sweeping Fan Block prevents an attack from hitting you, instead of recovering the ammunition, you can make a ranged [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) against the triggering target using the normal attack bonus and damage of your fans as part of the same reaction. You also apply any special effects the ammunition might have.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Dragon's Journey [one-action] Feat 14

[Air](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=527.md) [Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Flourish](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=606.md) [Move](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=658.md)
Source [Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 99](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=230.md)
Archetype [Fan Dancer](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=276.md)
Prerequisites Fan Dancer Dedication
Requirements You're wielding two fans, one in each hand.

---

Your movements are like that of a dragon weaving a serpentine path between your enemies and allies. Using your fans, you slice the air and create a path of least resistance, allowing you to move across the battlefield with an ethereal grace. You Stride, your movement doesn’t trigger enemy reactions, and any allies you pass within 5 feet of can immediately use their reaction to Stride, moving in the same direction. Allies must end their movement as close as possible to you or another ally who benefited from this ability. During Dragon’s Journey, ally movement doesn’t trigger reactions.

## [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Peony's Flourish [three-actions] Feat 14

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md) [Incapacitation](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=631.md) [Visual](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=727.md)
Source [Tian Xia Character Guide pg. 99](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=230.md)
Archetype [Fan Dancer](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=276.md)
Prerequisites Fan Dancer Dedication
Requirements You're wielding two fans, one in each hand.

---

As you spin around and create wide arching circles with your fans, you manifest a mosaic of peonies that confuses your enemies. Stride twice and then attempt a [Performance](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=45.md) check against the Will DC of each creature you passed adjacent to.

Critical Success The creature is [stunned](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=93.md) 3 and [dazzled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=65.md) for as long as they’re stunned.
Success The creature is stunned 1 and dazzled for 1 round.
Failure The creature is dazzled for 1 round.
Critical Failure The creature is unaffected and temporarily immune to further uses of Peony’s Flourish for 24 hours.
