The blessings of your god make your feet faster and your movements more fluid. You gain a +5-foot status bonus to your Speed and ignore [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366&Redirected=1.md). As part of casting agile feet, you can Step, Stride, or Tumble Through; you can instead [Burrow](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2310&Redirected=1.md), Climb, [Fly](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2312&Redirected=1.md), or Swim if you have the appropriate Speed.
