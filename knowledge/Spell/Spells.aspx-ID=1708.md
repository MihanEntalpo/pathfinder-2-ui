# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Sure Footing [two-actions] Spell 2

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Healing [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 361](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Range touch; Targets 1 willing creature

---

You free the target's limbs from ailments that impede mobility. Attempt to counteract an effect of your choice imposing one of these conditions on the target: clumsy, [grabbed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=77&Redirected=1.md), or paralyzed. If you didn't counteract the effect, but you would have if its counteract rank were 2 lower, instead suppress the effect until the beginning of your next turn. The effect's duration doesn't elapse while it's suppressed.

This spell can't counteract or suppress curses, diseases, or conditions that are part of the target's normal state.
