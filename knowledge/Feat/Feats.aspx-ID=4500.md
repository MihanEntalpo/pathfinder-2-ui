# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Anchoring Roots](./Feats.aspx-ID=4500.md) Feat 5

Leshy
Source [Player Core pg. 68](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

Small roots sprout from your feet, steadying you as you move. You gain the Steady Balance skill feat, even if you aren’t trained in [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34&Redirected=1.md), and you can use the Anchor action.

Anchor [one-action] Requirements You’re on a solid surface; Effect You send your roots into or across the ground, making it harder for you to stumble. Until you move, you gain a +2 circumstance bonus to your Fortitude or Reflex DC against attempts to [Reposition](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2379.md), Shove, or Trip you (or a +4 circumstance bonus if you’re a [root leshy](../Heritage/Heritages.aspx-ID=269.md)). This bonus also applies to saving throws against spells or effects that attempt to move you or knock you [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88&Redirected=1.md). If an effect forces you to move, you move only half the normal distance, as some of the effort goes to tearing loose the roots.

## Traits

Leshy: A creature with this trait is a member of the leshy ancestry. These small plant or fungus creatures are humanoid in form. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by leshies. A weapon with this trait is created and used by leshies.
