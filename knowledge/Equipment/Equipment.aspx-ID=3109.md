# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Shining SymbolItem 3+

[Divine](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=579.md) [Invested](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=637.md) [Light](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=640.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 295](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage worn; Bulk &mdash;

---

If you worship a deity, this golden amulet transforms into your deity's religious symbol when you invest it. You gain a +1 item bonus to Religion. The symbol casts dim light in a 20-foot emanation.

Activate—Spiritual Light [two-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [light](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=640.md), [revelation](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=686.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect The light cast by the symbol becomes bright light for 10 minutes and shines through bodies to reveal hints of the spirits within. Creatures in the light receive a –1 status penalty to Deception and Stealth checks. You can Dismiss this activation.
