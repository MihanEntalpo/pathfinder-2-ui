# [Untamed Druid](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=64.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 130](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Taking on the shapes of dangerous creatures, you fight with feral intensity. Though you trust your instincts, you might mistrust the ways of polite society.

## Ability Scores

Prioritize Wisdom and Strength so you can cast spells and enter melee combat. You’ll also want Dexterity and Constitution to improve your defenses and survivability.

## Skills

[Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34.md), [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md), [Intimidation](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=40.md), [Nature](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=43.md)

## Order

[untamed](../DruidicOrders/DruidicOrders.aspx-ID=11.md)
