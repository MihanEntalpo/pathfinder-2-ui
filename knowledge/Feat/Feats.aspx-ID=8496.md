# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Combat Premonition](./Feats.aspx-ID=8496.md) Feat 12

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 187](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Archetype [Chronoskimmer](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=349.md)
Prerequisites [Chronoskimmer Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8489.md)

---

By narrowing your sense of the future, you can improve that of your allies. When you roll initiative, instead of stabilizing or destabilizing your own timestream, you can grant your allies a flash of insight into their future. Choose two allies. Those allies roll their initiative roll twice and take the better result; this is a [fortune](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=612.md) effect. You roll your initiative roll twice and take the worse result; this is a [misfortune](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=654.md) effect. The two effects are tied together: if you would avoid the misfortune effect for any reason, or if any of your allies would negate their fortune effect, your Combat Premonition does nothing.
