# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Shatter Mind [two-actions] Cantrip 3

[Uncommon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=721.md) [Cantrip](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=550.md) [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md) [Mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) [Psychic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=894.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 21](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Area 15-foot cone

---

You telepathically assail the minds of your foes. You deal 3d4 mental damage to all enemies in the area, with a [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Will save.

---

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 1d4.

---

Amp You increase the area of the spell to a 30-foot cone or 60- foot cone, and the damage dice for the spell change to d10s. Creatures that fail are [stupefied](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=94.md) 1 until the start of your next turn (or stupefied 2 on a critical failure).
