# [Backstabber](./Traits.aspx-ID=544.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 282](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
When you hit an [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) creature, this weapon deals 1 precision damage in addition to its normal damage. The precision damage increases to 2 if the weapon is a +3 weapon.
