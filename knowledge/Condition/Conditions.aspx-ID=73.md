# [Fatigued](./Conditions.aspx-ID=73.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 444](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
You're tired and can't summon much energy. You take a –1 status penalty to AC and saving throws. You can't use [exploration activities](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2442.md) performed while traveling, such as those on pages 438–439.

You recover from fatigue after a full night's rest.
