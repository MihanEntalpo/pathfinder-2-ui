# [Grasping Currents](./Hazards.aspx-ID=648.md)Hazard 3

[Unique](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=723.md) [Complex](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=30.md) [Haunt](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=622.md)
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 36](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Complexity Complex
Stealth +20 (trained) to notice a weird, faintly glowing ripple of the current beneath the water
Description A swath of drowned spirits tries to pull someone down into the water to join them.

---

Disable DC 23 [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md) to calm the unquiet spirits in the waters for an hour, or light the Eternal Lantern (automatically disables the haunt)
Ghostly Grasp [reaction] Trigger A creature attempts to cross the lake by swimming or piloting a boat; Effect Ghostly arms reach up to grab and clutch at the creature or the boat, causing a –2 penalty to the [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) or Piloting check that triggered the haunt. The haunt then rolls initiative.

---

Routine (1 action) On its initiative, the grasping currents attempt to [Grapple](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2376.md) a swimming creature or capsize a rowboat by attempting an [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) check with a +12 modifier. A creature that’s restrained by the grasping currents is pulled underwater and risks drowning. To capsize a rowboat, the haunt’s Athletics check is rolled against the pilot’s Athletics or [Sailing Lore](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=41.md) DC, whichever is higher

---

Reset The grasping currents quickly build up power and can trigger again after 10 minutes have passed.
