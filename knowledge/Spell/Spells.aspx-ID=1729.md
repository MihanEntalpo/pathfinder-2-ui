# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Umbral Journey Spell 5

Uncommon [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Shadow Teleportation
Source [Player Core pg. 364](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Deities [Camazotz](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=334.md), Count Ranalc, Enkaar, Eyes That Watch, Grasping Iovett, Husk, Lady Razor, Luhar, Reshmit of the Heavy Voice, Thalaphyrr, [Zon-Kuthon](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=296.md)
Cast 1 minute
Range touch; Targets you and up to 10 willing creatures touched
Duration 8 hours

---

You move partially into the Netherworld, using its warped nature to speed your travels. Each hour, you cover roughly as much ground as you normally would in 3 days. The landmarks are vague and symbolic rather than concrete, leaving you within a mile of your intended destination when you Dismiss the spell or its duration ends.
