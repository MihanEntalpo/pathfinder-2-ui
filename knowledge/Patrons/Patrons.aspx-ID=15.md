# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Silence in Snow](./Patrons.aspx-ID=15.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 185](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Bitter cold heralded your patron's appearance, in the depths of the winter solstice or on a frozen peak at the end of the world. Your patron might be a winter hag, ice yai, or other spirit of the cold, but one thing is clear as ice—their power is not to be underestimated.

Spell List [primal](../Spell/Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Patron Skill Nature

Lesson of Winter's Chill Your patron taught you the power of winter firsthand by subjecting you to frost and snow. You learn the [clinging ice](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1887.md) hex cantrip and your familiar learns [gust of wind](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1550.md).
Familiar of Freezing Rime Your familiar is cold to the touch, its breath always visible. When you Cast or Sustain a hex, you can cause ice to form in a 5-foot burst centered on a square of your familiar's space. Those squares are [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366.md) until the start of your next turn.
