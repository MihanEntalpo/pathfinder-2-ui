# Sihedron MedallionItem 5+

## This Item may contain spoilers from the Revenge of the Runelords Adventure Path

Rare Invested Magical
Source [Pathfinder Adventure Path #219: Lord of the Trinity Star pg. 69](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=283.md)
Usage worn; Bulk &mdash;

---

These amulets once served as tokens of loyalty between the runelords and their favored agents. Though they vary in appearance, they all share a common design—a leather or metallic cord attached to a metal disc inscribed with the seven-pointed star known as the Sihedron. For original Sihedron medallions created before Earthfall, a runelord of old Thassilon (not a character with the runelord archetype) doesn’t reduce the DC of any [scrying](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1662.md) spell they cast against a creature that wears a Sihedron medallion even if they have never met the target or are unaware of the target’s identity. If such a runelord successfully scries on a creature wearing an original Sihedron medallion, they can use the wearer’s voice to issue messages to those in the vicinity. Sihedron medallions created in more modern times don’t possess this disadvantage.

A dead body that wears a Sihedron medallion doesn’t decay, and bugs or other pests (such as maggots) are prevented from consuming the remains, but unlike the effects of [peaceful rest](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1625.md), this doesn’t offer any protection against undeath, nor does it have an impact on spells that require the corpse to have died within a certain amount of time.

Activate—Bolster Flesh [two-actions] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md), [manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect Your flesh is augmented with magical vitality. You gain 10 temporary Hit Points for 8 hours.
