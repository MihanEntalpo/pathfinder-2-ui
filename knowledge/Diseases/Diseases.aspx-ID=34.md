# [Choking Death](./Diseases.aspx-ID=34.md)Disease 6

[Disease](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=578.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 88](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
This disease is capable of wiping out nations or even entire continents. A few pockets of the disease still remain in Iobaria, keeping that region’s population sparse.

Saving Throw DC 22 Fortitude; Onset 1 day; Stage 1 hoarse voice but no other symptoms (1 day); Stage 2 [drained](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=68.md) 1 (1 day); Stage 3 drained 2 and can’t speak (1 day); Stage 4 death
