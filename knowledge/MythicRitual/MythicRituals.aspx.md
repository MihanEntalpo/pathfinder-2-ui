# Mythic Rituals

Source [War of Immortals pg. 158](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=232.md)
Rituals already offer incredible power to those who master their demanding secrets. These most powerful rituals can defend or desolate entire cities, call down a divine plague, or turn fertile acres into a ravaged wasteland. Mythic rituals differ from most others in that the primary caster must spend a Mythic Point to begin the ritual and performs the primary check at mythic proficiency; they must be trained in the corresponding skill. As normal, secondary checks have a standard DC for a level twice the ritual's spell rank.
