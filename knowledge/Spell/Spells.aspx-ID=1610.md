# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Mountain Resilience [two-actions] Spell 4

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) Earth [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 346](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [primal](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=4.md)
Catalysts [Dragon Scute](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=4015.md)
Deities Arqueros, [Belech](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=709.md), Cihua Coatl, Dammerich, Imbrex, [Irori](../Deity/Deities.aspx-ID=286.md), Kazutal, Keepers of the Hearth, Myr, Sairazul, Soralyon, Talons of the Godclaw, Trudd, Urazra, Vavaalrav, Xoveron
Range touch; Targets 1 creature
Duration 20 minutes

---

The target's skin hardens like the stone of a mountain face. It gains resistance 5 to physical damage, except adamantine. Each time the target is hit by a bludgeoning, piercing, or slashing attack, mountain resilience's duration decreases by 1 minute.

---

Heightened (6th) The resistance increases to 10.
Heightened (8th) The resistance increases to 15.
Heightened (10th) The resistance increases to 20.
