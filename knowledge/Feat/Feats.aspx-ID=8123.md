# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Master Skysage Divination](./Feats.aspx-ID=8123.md) Feat 16

Archetype
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 239](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Archetype [Oatia Skysage](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=335.md)
Prerequisites [Expert Skysage Divination](./Feats.aspx-ID=8119.md)

---

You unlock the most powerful secrets hidden in the stars above. You choose one of the following 7th-rank spells to add to your spell repertoire: [retrocognition](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1652.md) or [true target](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1726.md). At 18th level, add [pinpoint](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1633.md) to your spell repertoire, and at 20th level, add [foresight](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1537.md) to your spell repertoire. You gain master proficiency with spell attack rolls and spell DCs for occult spells.
