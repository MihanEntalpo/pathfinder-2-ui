# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Draconic Sycophant](./Feats.aspx-ID=8065.md) Feat 1

Kobold
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 214](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

---

You possess features that dragons find particularly cute or charming, making it easier to endear yourself to them, or at least cause them to momentarily forgive your insolence. You gain a +2 circumstance bonus to Perception checks and saving throws against dragons. In addition, whenever you meet a creature with the [dragon](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=581.md) trait in a social situation, you can attempt a [Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md) check to [Make an Impression](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2392.md) on that creature immediately, rather than after conversing for 1 minute; you take a –5 circumstance penalty to this check. If you fail, you can choose to engage in 1 minute of conversation and then attempt a new check rather than accept the failure or critical failure result.

## Traits

Kobold: A creature with this trait is a member of the [kobold](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=80.md) ancestry, reptilian humanoids who are usually Small and typically have [darkvision](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2410.md). An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by kobolds.
