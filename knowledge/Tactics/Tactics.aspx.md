# Tactics

Source [Battlecry! pg. 25](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)
The fundamental strategies of war used by many commanders are recorded below. When you first gain your commander’s folio at 1st level, choose five mobility or offensive tactics from the list below; as you increase in level (or if you take the Tactical Expansion feat), you will gain additional tactics. Mobility tactics are basic tactics that make it easier for your party to move across the battlefield or negotiate unusual terrain, while offensive tactics are basic tactics that help you attack enemies, control enemy positioning, or endure enemy attacks.

## Mobility Tactics

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Defensive Retreat [two-actions] Mobility

[Brandish](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=857.md) [Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md)
Source [Battlecry! pg. 25](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)

---

You call for a careful retreat. Signal all squadmates within the aura of your banner; each can immediately [Step](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2304.md) up to three times as a free action. Each Step must take them farther away from at least one hostile creature they are observing and can only take them closer to a hostile creature if doing so is the only way for them to move toward safety (such as if they’re surrounded).

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Gather to Me! [one-action] Mobility

[Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md)
Source [Battlecry! pg. 25](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)

---

You signal your team to move into position together. Signal all squadmates; each can immediately Stride as a reaction, though each must end their movement inside your banner’s aura, or as close to your banner’s aura as their movement Speed allows.

Squadmates can use Gather to Me! while [Burrowing](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2310.md), [Climbing](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2374.md), [Flying](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2312.md), or [Swimming](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2381.md) instead of Striding if they have the corresponding movement type.

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Mountaineering Training [one-action] Mobility

[Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md)
Source [Battlecry! pg. 25](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)

---

Your instructions make it easier for you and your allies to scale dangerous surfaces. Signal all squadmates; until the end of your next turn, you and each ally gain a climb Speed of 20 feet.

Special If you have this tactic prepared, you can use [Warfare Lore](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=41.md) in place of [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) for checks you make to Climb.

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Naval Training [one-action] Mobility

[Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md)
Source [Battlecry! pg. 25](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)

---

Your instructions make it easier for you and your allies to swim through dangerous waters. Signal all squadmates; until the end of your next turn, you and each ally gain a swim Speed of 20 feet.

Special If you have this tactic prepared, you can use [Warfare Lore](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=41.md) in place of [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md) for checks you make to Swim.

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Passage of Lines [one-action] Mobility

[Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md)
Source [Battlecry! pg. 25](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)
[PFS Note](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md) This tactic can be used by [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88.md) PCs, but not PCs who are [grappled](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2376.md), [immobilized](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=81.md), or otherwise unable to move of their own volition.

---

You command your allies to regroup, allowing endangered units to fall back while rested units press the advantage. Signal all squadmates within the aura of your commander’s banner; each can swap positions with another willing ally adjacent to them. An ally can’t swap into a space it can’t fit into, and if one of the allies occupies more squares than the other, the smaller ally can move into any part of the larger ally’s space as long as the two remain adjacent after the swap.

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Protective Screen [one-action] Mobility

[Brandish](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=857.md) [Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md)
Source [Battlecry! pg. 25](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)

---

You’ve trained your allies in a technique designed to protect war mages. Signal one squadmate; as a reaction, that squadmate Strides directly toward any other squadmate who is within the aura of your banner. If the first squadmate ends their movement adjacent to another squadmate, the second squadmate does not trigger reactions when casting spells or making ranged attacks until the end of their next turn or until they are no longer adjacent to the first squadmate, whichever comes first. If the first squadmate ends their movement adjacent to more than one other squadmate, the first squadmate must choose which of the adjacent squadmates gains this benefit.

## Offensive Tactics

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Coordinating Maneuvers [one-action] Offensive

[Brandish](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=857.md) [Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md)
Source [Battlecry! pg. 26](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)

---

Your team works to slip enemies into a disadvantageous position. Signal one squadmate within the aura of your banner; that squadmate can immediately [Step](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2304.md) as a free action. If they end this movement next to an opponent, they can attempt to [Reposition](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2379.md) that target as a reaction.

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Double Team [two-actions] Offensive

[Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md)
Source [Battlecry! pg. 26](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)

---

Your team works together to set an enemy up for a vicious attack. Signal one squadmate who has an opponent within their reach. That ally can [Shove](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2380.md) or [Reposition](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2379.md) an opponent as a free action. If their maneuver is successful and the target ends their movement adjacent to a different squadmate, the second squadmate can attempt a melee Strike against that target as a reaction.

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)End it! [two-actions] Offensive

[Brandish](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=857.md) [Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) Incapacitation [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md)
Source [Battlecry! pg. 26](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)
Requirements You and your allies currently outnumber enemies on the battlefield, and you or a squadmate have reduced an enemy to 0 Hit Points since the start of your last turn.

---

At your proclamation that victory is already at hand, your allies march forward with an authoritative stomp, scattering your enemies in terror. Signal all squadmates within the aura of your banner; you and each ally can [Step](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2304.md) as a free action directly toward a hostile creature. Any hostile creatures within 10 feet of a squadmate after this movement must attempt a Will save against your class DC; on a failure they become [fleeing](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=74.md) for 1 round, and on a critical failure they become fleeing for 1 round and [frightened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=76.md) 2. This is an [emotion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=590.md), [fear](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=598.md), and [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) effect.

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Pincer Attack [one-action] Offensive

Source [Battlecry! pg. 26](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)

---

You call for an aggressive formation designed for exploiting enemies’ vulnerabilities. Signal all squadmates; each can [Step](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2304.md) as a reaction. If any of your allies ends this movement adjacent to an opponent, that opponent is [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) to melee attacks from you and all other squadmates who responded to Pincer Attack until the start of your next turn.

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Reload! [one-action] Offensive

[Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md)
Source [Battlecry! pg. 26](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)

---

Your drill instruction kicks in, and your allies rapidly reload their weapons to prepare for the next volley. Signal all squadmates; each can immediately [Interact](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2297.md) to reload as a reaction.

Special When leading a [siege weapon](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=1622.md) crew, you can use this tactic to command your squadmates to Load a siege weapon instead.

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Shields Up! [one-action] Offensive

Source [Battlecry! pg. 26](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)

---

You signal your allies to ready their defenses. Signal all squadmates within the aura of your commander’s banner; each can immediately [Raise a Shield](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2316.md) as a reaction. Squadmates who are wielding a [parry](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=667.md) weapon can instead position that weapon defensively as a reaction.

Special If one of your squadmates knows or has prepared the [shield](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1671.md) cantrip, they can cast it as a reaction instead of taking the actions normally granted by this tactic.

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Strike Hard! [two-actions] Offensive

Source [Battlecry! pg. 26](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)

---

You command an ally to attack. Signal a squadmate within the aura of your commander’s banner. That ally immediately attempts a Strike as a reaction.

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Tactical Takedown [two-actions] Offensive

[Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md)
Source [Battlecry! pg. 26](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)

You direct a coordinated maneuver that sends an enemy tumbling down. Signal up to two squadmates within the aura of your commander’s banner. Each of those allies can [Stride](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2305.md) up to half their Speed as a reaction. If they both end this movement adjacent to an enemy, that enemy must succeed at a Reflex save against your class DC or fall [prone](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=88.md).

## Expert Tactics

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Alley-oop [one-action] Expert

[Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md)
Source [Battlecry! pg. 26](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)

---

Your team excels at sharing resources and delivering them exactly where they need to be. Signal a squadmate within the aura of your banner who is holding or wearing a [consumable](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=564.md) that can be activated as a single action. That squadmate can toss their consumable to any other squadmate within the aura of your banner as a free action, and the receiving squadmate can catch and activate the consumable as a reaction. If the receiving squadmate chooses not to catch the consumable or if they don’t have a free hand to catch it with, it lands on the ground in their space.

Special If the consumable is a piece of activated ammunition and the receiving squadmate is wielding a compatible weapon that takes 1 or fewer actions to reload, they can load the ammunition into their weapon as part of the reaction used to catch and activate it. The ammunition remains activated until the end of their next turn.

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Buckle-cut blitz [two-actions] Expert

Each enemy they are adjacent to at any point during this movement must attempt a Reflex save against your class DC or become [clumsy](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=61.md) 1 for 1 round (clumsy 2 on a critical failure).

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Demoralizing Charge [two-actions] Expert

[Brandish](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=857.md) [Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md)
Source [Battlecry! pg. 27](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)

---

Your team’s coordinated assault strikes fear into your enemies’ hearts. Signal up to two squadmates within the aura of your commander’s banner; as a free action, those squadmates can immediately [Stride](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2305.md) toward an enemy they are observing. If they end this movement adjacent to an enemy, they can attempt to [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) that enemy as a reaction. For each of these Strikes that are successful, the target enemy must succeed at a Will save against your class DC or become [frightened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=76.md) 1 (frightened 2 on a critical failure); this is an [emotion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=590.md), [fear](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=598.md), and [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) effect. If both Strikes target the same enemy, that enemy attempts the save only once after the final attack and takes a –1 circumstance penalty to their Will save to resist this effect (this penalty increases to –2 if both Strikes are successful or to –3 if both Strikes are successful and either is a critical hit.)

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Slip and Sizzle [two-actions] Expert

[Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md)
Source [Battlecry! pg. 27](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)

---

Your team executes a brutal technique designed to knock down an opponent and blast them with magical devastation. Signal up to two squadmates within the aura of your commander’s banner; one of these squadmates must be adjacent to an opponent and the other must be capable of casting a spell that deals damage. The first squadmate can attempt to [Trip](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2382.md) the adjacent opponent as a reaction. If this Trip is successful, the second squadmate can cast a ranged spell that deals damage and takes 2 or fewer actions to cast. This spell is cast as a reaction and must either target the tripped opponent or include the tripped opponent in the spell’s area.

If the second squadmate cast a spell using slots or Focus Points as part of this tactic, they are [slowed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=92.md) 1 until the end of their next turn and do not gain a reaction when they regain actions at the start of their next turn.

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Stupefying Raid [two-actions] Expert

---

Your team dashes about in a series of maneuvers that leave the enemy befuddled. Signal up to two squadmates within the aura of your commander’s banner; these squadmates can [Stride](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2305.md) up to their Speed as a reaction. Each enemy they are adjacent to at any point during this movement must attempt a Will save against your class DC or become [stupefied](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=94.md) 1 for 1 round (stupefied 2 on a critical failure); this is a [mental](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=647.md) effect.

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Take the High Ground [one-action] Expert

Signal a squadmate within the aura of your commander’s banner; as a free action, that squadmate can [Stride](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2305.md) directly toward any other squadmate you are both observing. If the first squadmate ends this movement adjacent to another squadmate, the first squadmate can immediately [Leap](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2298.md) up to 25 feet horizontally or 15 feet vertically as a reaction, boosted by the other squadmate. This distance increases to 40 feet horizontally or 25 feet vertically if you have legendary proficiency in [Warfare Lore](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=41.md).

## Master Tactics

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Mirrored Wall [two-actions] Master

[Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md) Visual
Source [Battlecry! pg. 27](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)
Requirements At least one of your squadmates is in bright light or has a focused light source available, such as a [bull's eye lantern.](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2730.md)
Frequency once per 10 minutes

---

Your squadmates have polished their shields to a reflective sheen and now position them to reflect a blinding light into your enemy’s eyes. All of your squadmates can [Raise a Shield](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2316.md) or cast [shield](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1671.md) as a reaction. Then, signal a squadmate within the aura of your commander’s banner who currently has a shield raised (including spellcasting allies with an active casting of the shield cantrip), and choose an enemy within 60 feet. The formation bounces light off the raised shield and into the enemy’s eyes; the target must succeed at a Fortitude saving throw against your class DC or become [blinded](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=59.md) for 1 round (on a critical failure, the creature remains [dazzled](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=65.md) for 3 rounds after the blindness ends).

You can signal additional allies with raised shields to participate in this tactic; the target takes a circumstance penalty on this save equal to the number of additional participating squadmates (to a maximum –4 circumstance penalty to the target’s save).

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Piranha Assault [one-action] Master

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Pop, Drop, and Lock [two-actions] Master

Each squadmate can only attempt one specific action granted by this tactic, the actions can be attempted in any order and a specific action can only be attempted once as part of this tactic.

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Ready, Aim, Fire! [two-actions] Master

Special If one of your squadmates knows or has prepared a cantrip with a range of 30 feet or more that deals damage and requires 2 or fewer actions to cast, they can cast it targeting the enemy instead of taking the other actions normally granted by this tactic.

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Roaring Charge [two-actions] Master

Any creature within 10 feet of a squadmate once all movement from this tactic has been completed must attempt a Will save against your class DC with the following results. ### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)The Bigger They Are [one-action] Master

[Brandish](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=857.md) [Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md)
Source [Battlecry! pg. 27](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)

---

Regardless of your individual strengths, collectively your squad has the power to move mountains and topple giants. Signal a squadmate within the aura of your commander’s banner. That squadmate can attempt to [Reposition](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2379.md), [Shove](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2380.md), or [Trip](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2382.md) a target within their reach. Each other squadmate who is adjacent to the original squadmate or the target can attempt to assist with the maneuver as a reaction. For each squadmate who assists in this way, the original squadmate increases the maximum size of creature they can target (for example, if a total of two squadmates participated in this maneuver, the initial squadmate could target a creature up to two sizes larger than them.) The original squadmate gains a circumstance bonus on their check to Reposition, Shove, or Trip equal to the number of additional squadmates who assisted in the maneuver (maximum +4).

## Legendary Tactics

### [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Bloody Guillotine [two-actions] Legendary

[Commander](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=855.md) [Tactic](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=858.md)
Source [Battlecry! pg. 28](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=268.md)
Frequency once per day

---

Your squad performs a brutal combination attack that levels an enemy and attempts to put them down permanently. Choose and signal up to three squadmates within the aura of your commander’s banner.
