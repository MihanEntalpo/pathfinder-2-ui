# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[My Claws are Daggers](./Feats.aspx-ID=8050.md) Feat 1

[Dragonet](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=893.md)
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 211](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

---

Your claws are as sharp as your teeth. You gain a [claw](../Weapon/Weapons.aspx-ID=356.md) unarmed attack that deals 1d6 slashing damage. Your claws are in the knife group and have the [agile](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=526.md), [finesse](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=602.md), and [unarmed](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=719.md) traits.

At 5th level, whenever you get a critical hit with your claws or jaws, you get its critical specialization effect.

Special If your dragonet heritage is house drake, your claws count as [silver](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2920.md).

## Traits

Dragonet: Dragonets are miniature dragons, complete with wings, dagger-like teeth, and magical breath. They can be fiercely independent and proud, at times protective or avaricious, and they delight in the finer things in life.
