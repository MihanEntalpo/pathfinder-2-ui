# [Redeemer](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=7.md)

Source [Core Rulebook pg. 112](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=1.md)
Your truth and compassion glow strong as the sun, bringing the evil into the light of your goddess, the Dawnflower.

## Ability Scores

Take a high Strength for your combat abilities, and a good Charisma for your devotion spells.

## Skills

[Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md), Diplomacy, Medicine, Religion

## Deity and Cause

Sarenrae, redeemer

## Starting Feat

Deity's Domain (truth)

## Higher-Level Feats

[Shining Oath](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=221.md) (2nd), Mercy (4th), [Litany Against Wrath](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=227.md) (6th), Sense Evil (8th), Devoted Focus (10th), [Lasting Doubt](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=247.md) (12th), Ultimate Mercy (18th)
