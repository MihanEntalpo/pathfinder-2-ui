Archetype [Mind Smith](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=351.md)
Prerequisites [Mind Smith Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8504.md)

You always account for every weakness, allowing your mind to imagine just the right physical form to take advantage of your opponents. Choose between [cold iron](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2916.md) or [silver](../Equipment/Equipment.aspx-ID=2920.md); all your mind weapon Strikes are treated as the chosen type of metal.
