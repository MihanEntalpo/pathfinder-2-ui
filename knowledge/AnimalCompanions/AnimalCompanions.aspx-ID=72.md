# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Boar](./AnimalCompanions.aspx-ID=72.md)

[Animal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=531.md) [Minion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=653.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 208](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Your companion is a boar or pig.
Size Small
Melee [one-action] tusk, Damage 1d8 piercing
Str +3, Dex +1, Con +2, Int -4, Wis +2, Cha +0
Hit Points 8
Skill Survival
Senses low-light vision, scent (imprecise, 30 feet)
Speed 35 feet
Support Benefit Your boar gores your foes. Until the start of your next turn, your Strikes that damage a creature in your boar's reach also deal 1d6 [persistent bleed damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86&Redirected=1.md). If your boar is nimble or savage, the persistent bleed damage increases to 2d6.
Advanced Maneuver Boar Charge

### Boar Charge [two-actions]

Source [Player Core pg. 208](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

The boar Strides twice in a straight line and then makes a tusk Strike. As long as it moved at least 20 feet, it gains a +2 circumstance bonus to its attack roll.
