# [Edgewatch Detective](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=85.md)

### Legacy Content

Source [Pathfinder #157: Devil at the Dreaming Palace pg. 79](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=45.md)
You're a specially trained detective for the Edgewatch guard precinct in Absalom.

## Edgewatch Detective Dedication Feat 2

### This Feat may contain spoilers from the Agents of Edgewatch Adventure Path

### Legacy Content

Uncommon Archetype Dedication
Source [Pathfinder #157: Devil at the Dreaming Palace pg. 79](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=45.md)
Archetype [Edgewatch Detective](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=85.md)
Prerequisites Intelligence 14, or Wisdom 14

---

You become trained in Society or [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50&Redirected=1.md); if you are already trained in both of these skills, you instead become trained in a skill of your choice. You can use Perception instead of Survival to Track, and you gain the Experienced Tracker skill feat.

Special You can't select another dedication feat until you have gained two other feats from this archetype.

## Sense Alignment [three-actions] Feat 4

### Legacy Content

Archetype [Secret](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=690&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Pathfinder #157: Devil at the Dreaming Palace pg. 79](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=45.md)
Archetype [Edgewatch Detective](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=85.md)
Prerequisites Edgewatch Detective Dedication

---

You read the body language of an intelligent creature within 30 feet and pick up on subtle cues to detect its general propensity for good or evil and law or chaos. The GM rolls a [secret](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=690&Redirected=1.md) Perception check, comparing the result against the target's [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38&Redirected=1.md) DC.

Critical Success You ascertain the target's alignment.
Success You ascertain the target's alignment, but only along one axis (your choice of either the good-evil axis or the lawful-chaotic axis).
Failure You do not learn the target's alignment.
Critical Failure You incorrectly ascertain the target's alignment, misidentifying their alignment along either one axis or both (GM's choice).

## Arcane Sensitivity Feat 6

### Legacy Content

Archetype
Source [Pathfinder #157: Devil at the Dreaming Palace pg. 79](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=45.md)
Archetype [Edgewatch Detective](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=85.md)
Prerequisites Edgewatch Detective Dedication

---

You can sense the presence of the supernatural, even if you don't have any magical ability yourself. You can spend a single action (this action has the [concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) trait) to determine if any magical auras are present in the area. This has the effects of a 1st-level detect magic spell but isn't magical. By straining, you can also discover the source of the magic and its school (with the effect of a 4th-level detect magic spell), but doing so prevents you from using this ability again until you've rested for 8 hours.

## Bolera's Interrogation [three-actions] Feat 6

### Legacy Content

Archetype
Source [Pathfinder #157: Devil at the Dreaming Palace pg. 79](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=45.md)
Archetype [Edgewatch Detective](./Archetypes.aspx-ID=85.md)
Frequency three times per day
Prerequisites Edgewatch Detective Dedication

---

You focus your attention on a single creature within 30 feet. That creature must attempt a Will save against your Perception DC. On a failure, the creature finds itself unable to speak any deliberate and intentional lies and takes a –2 penalty to [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38&Redirected=1.md) checks. On a critical failure, the penalty to Deception checks is –4. This effect lasts for 10 minutes as long as you are within 30 feet of the target and the target is aware that you are using the ability against it. If you begin a new interrogation, this effect ends for any previous target. After your interrogation ends, the target is immune to this ability for 24 hours.
