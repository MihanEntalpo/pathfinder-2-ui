# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Animal-Turning FuluItem 1

## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Rare Consumable [Fulu](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=378.md) Magical Talisman
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 301](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 4 gp
Usage affixed to armor; Bulk L
Activate [reaction] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md)) Trigger A creature with the [animal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=531.md) trait successfully Strikes you.

---

Frightened animals depicted on this fulu flee in all directions from a central figure (traditionally represented by a human hunter, but sometimes depicted as a skeletal [undead](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=722.md) creature or even a [fiend](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=600.md) with long, broken arms). You activate this fulu, gaining a +2 item bonus to AC against the triggering Strike. If this causes the Strike to miss, you become [concealed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=62.md) from the triggering creature until the start of your next turn.
