Source [Player Core pg. 216](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Archetype [Bard](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=211.md)
Class [Bard](../Class/Classes.aspx-ID=32.md)
Prerequisites [Basic Muse's Whispers](./Feats.aspx-ID=5059.md)

You gain one bard feat. For the purpose of meeting its prerequisites, your bard level is equal to half your character level.

Special You can select this feat more than once. Each time you select it, you gain another bard feat.

Archetype: This feat belongs to an archetype.
