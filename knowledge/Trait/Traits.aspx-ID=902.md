# [Reflection](./Traits.aspx-ID=902.md)

Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 221](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
A creature with this trait has the [reflection](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=97.md) versatile heritage. Reflections are beings who are copies of living people, whether through cloning, planar duplication, or another mechanism. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by reflections.
