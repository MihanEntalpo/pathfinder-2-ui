# [Eerie Flicker](./Feats.aspx-ID=8042.md) [reaction] Feat 2

## This Feat may contain spoilers from the Gatewalkers Adventure Path

Rare Deviant Magical
Source [Gatewalkers Player's Guide (Remastered) pg. 13](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=287.md), [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 102](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)

Trigger An enemy’s Strike would hit you and you weren’t already [concealed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=62.md), [hidden](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=79.md), or [undetected](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=96.md) by that enemy.

---

Your body flickers momentarily into the [Ethereal Plane](../Plane/Planes.aspx-ID=34.md). You become [concealed](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=62.md) for 1 round, and the [flat check](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=333.md) for concealment applies to the Strike that would have hit you. If the flat check fails, the [Strike](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2306.md) misses you.

Awakening When your body flickers, you momentarily assume a terrifying form. If a creature fails the flat check against concealment from your Eerie Flicker, it becomes [frightened](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=76.md) 1, and it doesn’t reduce the frightened condition from this effect at the end of the same turn it gained the condition.
Awakening You can choose to compress your flickering movement into a single moment, increasing your chance to avoid the triggering attack in exchange for a shorter- lived effect. If you choose to do so, the [flat check](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=333.md) for [concealment](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=62.md) against the triggering attack increases to DC 9, but the concealment affects only the triggering Strike.
