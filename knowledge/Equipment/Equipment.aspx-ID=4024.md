# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Stalk GogglesItem 1+

## This Item may contain spoilers from the Gatewalkers Adventure Path

Rare Invested Magical Morph
Source [Gatewalkers (Hardcover) pg. 230](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=290.md)
Usage worn eyepiece; Bulk &mdash;

---

These black leather goggle frames have no lenses. Instead, when a character puts them on and invests them, the wearer’s eyes transform and lengthen into snail-like eyestalks. The wobbly eyestalks stretch out through the lens holes and up over the wearer’s head on lengthened optic nerves.

Activate—Looksee [one-action] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect By focusing hard, you can watch for enemies in all directions. You gain all-around vision for 1 minute; during this time, you can’t be [flanked](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2375.md).
