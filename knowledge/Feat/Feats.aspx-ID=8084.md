# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Home in the Deep](./Feats.aspx-ID=8084.md) Feat 9

[Dragonblood](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=794.md)
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 217](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

Prerequisites [Aqueous Dragonblood](./Feats.aspx-ID=8076.md)

---

You cut through the water, and dwell beneath the surface with ease. Increase your swim Speed by 15 feet; you also gain the [amphibious](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=529.md) trait.

## Traits

Dragonblood: A creature with this trait has the dragonblood versatile heritage. These creatures are born of draconic lineage and often have draconic appearances.
