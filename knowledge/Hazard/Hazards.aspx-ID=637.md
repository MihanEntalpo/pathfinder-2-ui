# [Spirit Window](./Hazards.aspx-ID=637.md)Hazard 20

[Haunt](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=622.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 124](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 51
Description Spirits trapped inside a haunted window harm those who touch the window.

---

Disable DC 48 Occultism (legendary) to free the spirits from the mirror, or DC 48 [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md) (legendary) to banish the spirits
AC 45, Fort +36, Ref +30
Hardness 35 (window), HP 132 (BT 66); Immunities object immunities
Siphon Soul [reaction] ([occult](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=662.md), [void](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=510.md)) Trigger A creature touches the window; Effect The triggering creature takes 8d10+44 void damage with a DC 42 basic Will save as the window attempts to steal their soul. If this damage would kill the target, the target's soul is trapped inside the window with the effects of [seize soul](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1664.md).

---

Reset 1 round
