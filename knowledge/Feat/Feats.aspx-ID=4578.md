# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Versatile Performance](./Feats.aspx-ID=4578.md) Feat 1

[Bard](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=546.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 101](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

Prerequisites [polymath muse](../Muses/Muses.aspx-ID=3.md)

---

You can rely on the grandeur of your performances rather than ordinary social skills. You can use [Performance](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=45.md) instead of [Diplomacy](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=39.md) to [Make an Impression](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2392.md) and instead of [Intimidation](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=40.md) to [Demoralize](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2395.md). You can also use an acting Performance instead of [Deception](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=38.md) to [Impersonate](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2388.md). In addition, you can use your proficiency rank in Performance to meet the prerequisites of skill feats that require a particular proficiency rank in Deception, Diplomacy, or Intimidation.
