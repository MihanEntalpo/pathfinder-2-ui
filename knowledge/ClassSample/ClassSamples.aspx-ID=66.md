# [Bully](./ClassSamples.aspx-ID=66.md)

Source [Player Core pg. 144](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Using a two-handed weapon, you push your foes about the battlefield and deal grievous wounds. You excel at taking down the biggest enemy standing in your way.

## Ability Scores

Prioritizing Strength and Constitution. Wisdom helps with survivability and increases your damage; Dexterity enables extra maneuverability.

## Skills

[Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36.md), [Crafting](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=37.md), [Intimidation](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=40.md), [Medicine](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=42.md)

## Starting Feat

[Vicious Swing](../Feat/Feats.aspx-ID=4775.md)
