# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Sure Feet](./Feats.aspx-ID=4461.md) Feat 1

Halfling
Source [Player Core pg. 60](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

Whether keeping your balance or climbing, your feet easily find purchase. If you roll a success on an [Acrobatics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=34&Redirected=1.md) check to Balance or an [Athletics](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=36&Redirected=1.md) check to Climb, you get a critical success instead. You're not [off-guard](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=58.md) when you attempt to Balance or Climb.

## Traits

Halfling: A creature with this trait is a member of the halfling ancestry. These small people are friendly wanderers considered to be lucky. An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by halflings. A weapon with this trait is created and used by halflings.
