Duration 1 minute

---

Your body sprouts brambly thorns that thrive on life magic. Adjacent creatures that hit you with a melee attack, as well as creatures that hit you with unarmed attacks, take 1 piercing damage each time they do. Anytime you cast a [vitality](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=509.md) spell, the thorns' damage increases to 1d6 until the start of your next turn.

---

Heightened (+1) The damage increases by 1, or 1d6 after you cast a vitality spell.
