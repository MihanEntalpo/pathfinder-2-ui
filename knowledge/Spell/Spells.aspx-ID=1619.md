Deities Granduncle Taproot, Onos, Rhan-Tegoth, Soralyon, Yuelral
Duration 10 minutes or 8 hours

---

You can either transform into a stone or merge with stone. This spell has the same effects as [one with plants](./Spells.aspx-ID=1618.md), but lets you merge with or turn into stone. A stone you turn into has AC 23. You can Dismiss this spell.
