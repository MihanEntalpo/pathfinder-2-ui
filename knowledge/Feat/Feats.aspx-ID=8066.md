# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Dragonscaled Lore](./Feats.aspx-ID=8066.md) Feat 1

Kobold
Source [Draconic Codex pg. 214](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=277.md)

Prerequisites dragonscaled kobold heritage

---

The study of dragons is not merely academic to you but part of your family tree. Tracking lineages, benefactors, and communities has become second nature. You become trained in [Intimidation](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=40.md) and your choice of [Arcana](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=35.md), [Nature](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=43.md), [Occultism](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=44.md), or [Religion](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=46.md). For each of these skills in which you were already trained, you become trained in a skill of your choice. You also gain the [Additional Lore](./Feats.aspx-ID=5114.md) general feat for [Dragon Lore](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=41.md).

## Traits

Kobold: A creature with this trait is a member of the [kobold](../Ancestry/Ancestries.aspx-ID=80.md) ancestry, reptilian humanoids who are usually Small and typically have [darkvision](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2410.md). An ability with this trait can be used or selected only by kobolds.
