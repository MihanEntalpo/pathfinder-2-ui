# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Soothing Mist [two-actions] Focus 2

Uncommon [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md) Healing [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) [Ranger](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=682.md) [Vitality](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=509.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 383](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 willing living creature or 1 undead creature

---

You call forth a magical mist that envelops a creature. The mist restores 2d8 Hit Points to a target living creature and ends one source of [persistent acid, bleed, fire, poison, or void damage](../Condition/Conditions.aspx-ID=86&Redirected=1.md) affecting it. If the creature is taking persistent damage from multiple sources, you select which one is removed. Against an undead target, you deal 2d8 vitality damage (basic Fortitude save); if it fails the save, it also takes 2 persistent vitality damage.

---

Heightened (+1) The amount of healing (or damage to an undead target) increases by 1d8, and the persistent vitality damage to an undead creature increases by 1.
