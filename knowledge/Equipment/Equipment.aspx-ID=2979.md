Source [GM Core pg. 265](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Usage affixed to armor; Bulk &mdash;
Activate [one-action] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md))

---

This talisman comes in many forms, most often a severed piece of a humanoid creature displayed in some gruesome manner. When you activate the trophy, make an [Intimidation](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=40.md) check to [Demoralize](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2395.md) up to two targets, comparing your Intimidation check result to both of their DCs
