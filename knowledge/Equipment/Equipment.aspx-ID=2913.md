# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Smoke BallItem 1+

[Alchemical](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=528.md) [Consumable](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=564.md)
Source [GM Core pg. 251](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md), [Player Core 2 pg. 296](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=227.md); There is a more recent version of this item. Click here to view.
Usage held in 2 hands; Bulk L
Activate [one-action] ([manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md))

---

Upon smashing this ball on the ground, you instantly create a screen of thick, opaque smoke in a burst centered on one corner of your space. All creatures within that area are concealed, and all other creatures are concealed to them. The smoke lasts for 1 minute or until dispersed by a strong wind.
