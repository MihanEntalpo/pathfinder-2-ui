# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Spell Acceleration](./Feats.aspx-ID=8486.md) Feat 12

[Archetype](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=535.md)
Source [Dark Archives (Remastered) pg. 185](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=293.md)
Archetype [Time Mage](../Archetypes/Archetypes.aspx-ID=348.md)
Prerequisites [Time Mage Dedication](./Feats.aspx-ID=8480.md)

---

Special time-weaving techniques let you compress your spellcasting gestures and incantations into a short burst so you can cast with incredible speed—though it causes temporal strain. You gain the [Quickened Casting](./Feats.aspx-ID=4614.md) feat. It applies to your cantrips and spells from the class you used to qualify for the Time Mage Dedication.

Archetype: This feat belongs to an archetype.
