# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)[Arboreal Sapling](./AnimalCompanions.aspx-ID=67.md)

Uncommon [Minion](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=653.md) Plant
Source [Player Core pg. 206](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Your companion is a walking tree, a cousin of the great arboreal wardens and regents. An arboreal sapling has the plant trait instead of the animal trait, but it otherwise functions normally as an animal companion. They are typically only selected by animal order druids who are also members of the leaf order.
Access You are a member of the leaf order.
Size Small
Melee [one-action] branch, Damage 1d8 bludgeoning
Str +3, Dex +1, Con +2, Int -4, Wis +2, Cha +0
Hit Points 8
Skill [Stealth](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=48&Redirected=1.md)
Senses low-light vision
Speed 25 feet
Support Benefit Your arboreal sapling plants roots around your foe, impeding its movement. Until the start of your next turn, if you hit and deal damage to a creature in your arboreal sapling's reach, the first square the creature moves into after taking that damage is [difficult terrain](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2366&Redirected=1.md).
Advanced Maneuver Throw Rock

### Throw Rock [one-action]

Source [Player Core pg. 206](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)

---

The arboreal sapling Interacts to pick up a rock within reach or retrieve a stowed rock, then throws it with a ranged rock Strike that deals 2d6 bludgeoning (3d6 for a specialized companion) damage with a range increment of 30 feet.
