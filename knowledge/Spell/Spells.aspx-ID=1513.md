# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Enfeeble [two-actions] Spell 1

[Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md)
Source [Player Core pg. 329](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Traditions [arcane](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=1.md), [divine](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=2.md), [occult](./Spells.aspx-Tradition=3.md)
Catalysts Vulture's Wing
Patron Themes [the resentment](../Patrons/Patrons.aspx-ID=14.md), devourer of decay
Range 30 feet; Targets 1 creature
Defense Fortitude; Duration varies

---

You sap the target's strength, depending on its Fortitude save.

Critical Success The target is unaffected.
Success The target is enfeebled 1 until the start of your next turn.
Failure The target is enfeebled 2 for 1 minute.
Critical Failure The target is enfeebled 3 for 1 minute.
