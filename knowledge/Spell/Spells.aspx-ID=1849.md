# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Downpour [two-actions] Focus 4

Uncommon [Cleric](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=554.md) [Concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561&Redirected=1.md) [Focus](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=511.md) [Manipulate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=645&Redirected=1.md) Water
Source [Player Core pg. 381](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=216.md)
Domain [water](../Domain/Domains.aspx-ID=98.md)
Range 120 feet; Area 30-foot burst
Duration 1 minute

---

You call forth a torrential downpour, which extinguishes non-magical flames. Creatures in the area are concealed and gain fire resistance 10. Creatures outside the area are concealed to those inside the area. If a creature with weakness to water ends its turn in the area, the downpour triggers its weakness.

---

Heightened (+1) The fire resistance increases by 2.
