# [Fireball Rune](./Hazards.aspx-ID=469.md)Hazard 5

Fire Magical Trap
Source [GM Core pg. 101](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=218.md)
Complexity Simple
Stealth DC 24 (expert)
Description An invisible rune creates an invisible, spherical magical sensor with a 20-foot radius.

---

Disable DC 22 [Thievery](../Skill/Skills.aspx-ID=50.md) (expert) to erase the rune without triggering the sensor, or [dispel magic](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1493.md) (3rd rank; counteract DC 20) to counteract the rune
Fireball [reaction] ([arcane](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=534.md), [fire](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=604.md)) Trigger A living creature enters the sensor area; Effect The rune detonates a fireball centered on the triggering creature’s square. This is a 3rd-rank [fireball](../Spell/Spells.aspx-ID=1530.md) spell that deals 6d6 fire damage (DC 22 [basic](../Rules/Rules.aspx-ID=2297.md) Reflex save).
