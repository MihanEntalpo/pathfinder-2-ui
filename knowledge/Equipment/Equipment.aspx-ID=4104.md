# [PFS.aspx.htm](../PFS/PFS.aspx.md)Ghost ScarfItem 7

## This Item may contain spoilers from the Season of Ghosts Adventure Path

Rare Invested Magical
Source [Season of Ghosts (Hardcover) pg. 307](../Source/Sources.aspx-ID=294.md)
Price 360 gp
Usage worn belt or scarf; Bulk &mdash;

---

This 6-foot-long scarf shimmers with the silvery light of the River of Souls while worn, providing illumination equivalent to that of a candle. You can deactivate or activate this radiance as an [Interact](../Action/Actions.aspx-ID=2297.md) action. The dangling lengths of a ghost scarf softly billow in the presence of haunts, granting the wearer a +1 item bonus to all Perception checks and Perception DCs to resolve discovering a [haunt](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=622.md) or rolling initiative when a haunt triggers.

Activate—Ghost Slayer's Caress [one-action] ([concentrate](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=561.md)); Frequency once per day; Effect The scarf extends silvery threads that wrap around a weapon you carry, granting the effects of a [ghost touch](./Equipment.aspx-ID=2840.md) property rune to that weapon for 5 minutes. If the weapon already bears a ghost touch rune, you instead gain a +1 item bonus to Fortitude saves against effects from [incorporeal](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=632.md) [undead](../Trait/Traits.aspx-ID=722.md) for 5 minutes.
